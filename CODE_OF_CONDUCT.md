# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Fluent is built by people who value clarity, respect, and thoughtful work.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to help create a welcoming, focused, and constructive environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Give criticism with context and care.
- Welcome contributors with different levels of experience.
- Credit ideas, research, and contributions fairly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith arguments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Dismissing contributors based on identity, background, or experience level

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Enforcement should be proportionate, consistent, and focused on restoring a healthy project environment.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.

Reports will be reviewed with discretion.
