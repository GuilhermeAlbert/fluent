# Advice

## Meaning

Advice is a suggestion about what someone should do.

## Examples

- She gave me good advice.
- I need your advice.
- Let me give you some advice.

## Notes

Advice is usually uncountable in English. Say "some advice" or "a piece of advice," not "an advice."
