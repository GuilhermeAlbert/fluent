# Adviser

## Meaning

Adviser is an expert who gives advice.

## Examples

- An adviser helped students select their courses.
- The United States sent military advisors to Guatemala.
- The adviser asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
