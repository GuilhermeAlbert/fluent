# Advocate

## Meaning

Advocate means someone who pleads for a cause or propounds an idea.

## Examples

- The advocate asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the advocate yesterday.
- The advocate joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
