# Affect

## Meaning

To affect means to change or influence someone or something.

## Examples

- Will the new rules affect me?
- Stress can affect your sleep.
- The weather affected our plans.

## Notes

Usually a verb. Do not confuse it with "effect," which is usually a noun.
