# Afraid

## Meaning

Afraid means feeling fear, worry, or nervousness about something.

## Examples

- I was afraid to ask.
- She looked afraid at first.
- He is afraid of heights.

## Notes

Usually used after verbs like "be," "feel," "look," or "seem."
