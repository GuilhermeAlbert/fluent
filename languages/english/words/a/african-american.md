# African-American

## Meaning

African-American describes a person or community in the United States with African ancestry.

## Examples

- She is an African-American writer.
- The museum highlights African-American history.
- They discussed African-American culture in class.

## Notes

Use it respectfully as an identity term when it fits the person or context.
