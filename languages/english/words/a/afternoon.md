# Afternoon

## Meaning

Afternoon is the part of the day between noon and evening.

## Examples

- He spent a quiet afternoon in the park.
- The afternoon went by quickly.
- We chose a better afternoon.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
