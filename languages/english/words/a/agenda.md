# Agenda

## Meaning

Agenda is a temporally organized plan for matters to be attended to.

## Examples

- We talked about the agenda.
- The agenda matters here.
- I noticed the agenda right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
