# Aggressive

## Meaning

Aggressive describes someone or something that is having or showing determination and energetic pursuit of your ends.

## Examples

- An aggressive businessman.
- An aggressive basketball player.
- He was aggressive and imperious; positive in his convictions.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
