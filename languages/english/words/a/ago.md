# Ago

## Meaning

Ago describes someone or something that is gone by.

## Examples

- Two years ago.
- 'agone' is an archaic word for 'ago'.
- It feels ago.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
