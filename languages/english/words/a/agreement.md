# Agreement

## Meaning

Agreement is the statement of an exchange of promises.

## Examples

- They had an agreement that they would not interfere in each other's business.
- There was an understanding between management and the workers.
- We talked about the agreement.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
