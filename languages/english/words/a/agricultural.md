# Agricultural

## Meaning

Agricultural describes something connected with or used in or promoting agriculture or farming.

## Examples

- Agricultural engineering.
- Modern agricultural (or farming) methods.
- Agricultural (or farm) equipment.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
