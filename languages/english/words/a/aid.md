# Aid

## Meaning

Aid is a person or thing that is a resource that helps make something easier or possible to do.

## Examples

- Visual aids in teaching.
- His job was to give technical assistance over the phone.
- We talked about the aid.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
