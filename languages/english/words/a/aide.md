# Aide

## Meaning

Aide is an officer who acts as military helpant to a more senior officer.

## Examples

- The aide asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the aide yesterday.
- The aide joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
