# Air

## Meaning

Air is a mixture of gases required for breathing.

## Examples

- Air pollution.
- A smell of chemicals in the air.
- Open a window and let in some air.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
