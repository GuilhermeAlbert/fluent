# Alcohol

## Meaning

Alcohol is a liquor or brew containing alcohol as the active agent.

## Examples

- Alcohol (or drink) ruined him.
- I need more alcohol before I decide.
- We talked about alcohol after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
