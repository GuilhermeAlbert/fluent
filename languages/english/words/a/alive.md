# Alive

## Meaning

Alive describes someone or something that is haveing life.

## Examples

- The happiest person alive.
- The nerve is alive.
- Doctors are working hard to keep him alive.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
