# All

## Meaning

All describes someone or something that is used with either mass or count nouns to indicate the whole number or amount of or every one of a class.

## Examples

- We sat up all night.
- Ate all the food.
- All men are mortal.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
