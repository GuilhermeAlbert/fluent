# Alone

## Meaning

Alone describes someone or something that is isolated from others.

## Examples

- Could be alone in a crowded room.
- Was alone with her thoughts.
- I want to be alone.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
