# Alternative

## Meaning

Alternative describes someone or something that is serving or used in place of another.

## Examples

- An alternative plan.
- It feels alternative.
- That was an alternative choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
