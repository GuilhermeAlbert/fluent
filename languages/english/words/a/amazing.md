# Amazing

## Meaning

Amazing describes someone or something that is surprising greatly.

## Examples

- She does an amazing amount of work.
- The dog was capable of astonishing tricks.
- It feels amazing.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
