# Analyst

## Meaning

Analyst means someone who is skilled at analyzing data.

## Examples

- The analyst asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the analyst yesterday.
- The analyst joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
