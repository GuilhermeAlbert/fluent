# Animal

## Meaning

Animal describes someone or something that is marked by the appetites and passions of the body.

## Examples

- Animal instincts.
- Carnal knowledge.
- Fleshly desire.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
