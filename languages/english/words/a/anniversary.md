# Anniversary

## Meaning

Anniversary is the date on which an event occurred in some previous year.

## Examples

- The anniversary went by quickly.
- We chose a better anniversary.
- That anniversary worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
