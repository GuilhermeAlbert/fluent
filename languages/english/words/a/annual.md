# Annual

## Meaning

Annual describes someone or something that is completing its life cycle within a year.

## Examples

- A border of annual flowering plants.
- It feels annual.
- That was an annual choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
