# Another

## Meaning

Another describes someone or something that is any of various alternatives.

## Examples

- Put it off to another (or some other) day.
- It feels another.
- That was an another choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
