# Anxiety

## Meaning

Anxiety is a relatively permanent state of worry and nervousness occurring in a variety of mental disorders, usually accompanied by compulsive behavior or attacks of panic.

## Examples

- We talked about the anxiety.
- The anxiety matters here.
- I noticed the anxiety right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
