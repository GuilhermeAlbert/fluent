# Anymore

## Meaning

Anymore means doing something in a way that is at the present or from now on.

## Examples

- Alice doesn't live here anymore.
- The children promised not to quarrel any more.
- She answered anymore.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
