# Anyway

## Meaning

Anyway means doing something in a way that is used to indicate that a statement explains or supports a previous statement.

## Examples

- Anyhow, he is dead now.
- I think they're asleep; anyhow, they're quiet.
- I don't know what happened to it; anyway, it's gone.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
