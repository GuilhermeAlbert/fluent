# Anywhere

## Meaning

Anywhere means doing something in a way that is at or in or to any place.

## Examples

- You can find this food anywhere.
- She answered anywhere.
- It happened anywhere.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
