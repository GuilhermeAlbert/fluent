# Apart

## Meaning

Apart means doing something in a way that is separated or at a distance in place or position or time.

## Examples

- These towns are many miles apart.
- Stood with his legs apart.
- Born two years apart.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
