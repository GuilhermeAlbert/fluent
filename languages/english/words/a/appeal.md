# Appeal

## Meaning

To appeal means to take a court case to a higher court for review.

## Examples

- He was found guilty but appealed immediately.
- I need to appeal soon.
- They want to appeal together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
