# Appearance

## Meaning

Appearance means outward or visible aspect of a person or thing.

## Examples

- We talked about the appearance.
- The appearance matters here.
- I noticed the appearance right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
