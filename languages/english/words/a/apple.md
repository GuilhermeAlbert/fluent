# Apple

## Meaning

An apple is a common fruit with crisp flesh and red, yellow, or green skin.

## Examples

- I ate an apple for breakfast.
- She bought apples at the store.
- This apple tastes fresh.

## Notes

Use "an apple" for one fruit and "apples" for more than one.
