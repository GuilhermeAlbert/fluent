# Application

## Meaning

Application is the action of bringing something to bear.

## Examples

- He advocated the application of statistics to the problem.
- A novel application of electronics to medical diagnosis.
- The application starts at nine.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
