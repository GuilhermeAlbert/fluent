# Appointment

## Meaning

Appointment is the action of putting a person into a non-elective position.

## Examples

- The appointment had to be approved by the whole committee.
- The appointment starts at nine.
- We planned the appointment together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
