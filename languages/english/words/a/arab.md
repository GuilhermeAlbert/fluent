# Arab

## Meaning

Arab is a member of a Semitic people originally from the Arabian peninsula and surrounding territories who speaks Arabic and who inhabits much of the Middle East and northern Africa.

## Examples

- The arab asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the arab yesterday.
- The arab joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
