# Architect

## Meaning

Architect means someone who creates plans to be used in making something.

## Examples

- The architect asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the architect yesterday.
- The architect joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
