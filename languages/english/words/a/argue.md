# Argue

## Meaning

To argue means to present reasons and arguments.

## Examples

- I need to argue this clearly.
- She tried to argue the idea.
- They plan to argue after the meeting.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
