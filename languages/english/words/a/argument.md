# Argument

## Meaning

Argument is a fact or assertion offered as evidence that something is true.

## Examples

- It was a strong argument that his hypothesis was true.
- We talked about the argument.
- The argument matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
