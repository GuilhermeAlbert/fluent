# Armed

## Meaning

Armed describes someone or something that is characterized by having or bearing arms.

## Examples

- Armed robbery.
- It feels armed.
- That was an armed choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
