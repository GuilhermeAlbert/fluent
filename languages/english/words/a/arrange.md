# Arrange

## Meaning

To arrange means to put into a proper or systematic order.

## Examples

- They arrange the bags on the table.
- The men arrange the bookshelves.
- Arrange the books on the shelves in chronological order.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
