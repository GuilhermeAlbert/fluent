# Arrangement

## Meaning

Arrangement is the thing arranged or agreed to.

## Examples

- They made arrangements to meet in Chicago.
- We talked about the arrangement.
- The arrangement matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
