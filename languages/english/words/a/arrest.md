# Arrest

## Meaning

To arrest means to take into custody.

## Examples

- The police nabbed the suspected criminals.
- I need to arrest this today.
- They plan to arrest it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
