# Article

## Meaning

Article means nonfictional prose forming an independent part of a publication.

## Examples

- We talked about the article.
- The article matters here.
- I noticed the article right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
