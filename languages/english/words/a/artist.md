# Artist

## Meaning

Artist means someone whose creative work shows sensitivity and imagination.

## Examples

- The artist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the artist yesterday.
- The artist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
