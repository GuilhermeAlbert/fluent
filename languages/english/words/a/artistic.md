# Artistic

## Meaning

Artistic describes something connected with or characteristic of art or artists.

## Examples

- His artistic background.
- It feels artistic.
- That was an artistic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
