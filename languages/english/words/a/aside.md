# Aside

## Meaning

Aside means doing something in a way that is on or to one side.

## Examples

- Step aside.
- Stood aside to let him pass.
- Threw the book aside.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
