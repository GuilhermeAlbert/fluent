# Ask

## Meaning

To ask means to make a request or demand for something to somebody.

## Examples

- They ask him to write the letter.
- She asked him for a loan.
- I need to ask this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
