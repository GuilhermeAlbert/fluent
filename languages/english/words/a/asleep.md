# Asleep

## Meaning

Asleep describes someone or something that is in a situation of sleep.

## Examples

- Were all asleep when the phone rang.
- Fell asleep at the wheel.
- It feels asleep.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
