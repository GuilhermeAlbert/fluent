# Aspect

## Meaning

Aspect is a distinct feature or element in a problem.

## Examples

- He studied every facet of the question.
- We talked about the aspect.
- The aspect matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
