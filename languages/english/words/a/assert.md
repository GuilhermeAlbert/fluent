# Assert

## Meaning

To assert means to state categorically.

## Examples

- They assert that there was a traffic accident.
- I need to assert this clearly.
- She tried to assert the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
