# Assign

## Meaning

To assign means to give an assignment to to a post, or assign a task to.

## Examples

- They assign him to write the letter.
- I need to assign soon.
- They want to assign together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
