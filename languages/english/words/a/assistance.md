# Assistance

## Meaning

Assistance is the activity of contributing to the fulfillment of a need or furtherance of an effort or purpose.

## Examples

- He gave me an assist with the housework.
- Could not walk without assistance.
- Rescue party went to their aid.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
