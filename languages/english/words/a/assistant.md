# Assistant

## Meaning

Assistant means someone who contributes to the fulfillment of a need or furtherance of an effort or purpose.

## Examples

- My invaluable assistant.
- They hired additional help to finish the work.
- The assistant asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
