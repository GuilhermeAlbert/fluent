# Assumption

## Meaning

Assumption is a statement that is assumed to be true and from which a conclusion can be drawn.

## Examples

- On the assumption that he has been injured, we can infer that he will not play.
- We talked about the assumption.
- The assumption matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
