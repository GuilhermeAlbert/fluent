# Athlete

## Meaning

Athlete is a person trained to compete in sports.

## Examples

- The athlete asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the athlete yesterday.
- The athlete joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
