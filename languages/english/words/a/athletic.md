# Athletic

## Meaning

Athletic describes something connected with or befitting athletics or athletes.

## Examples

- Athletic facilities.
- It feels athletic.
- That was an athletic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
