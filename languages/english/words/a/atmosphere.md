# Atmosphere

## Meaning

Atmosphere is a particular environment or surrounding influence.

## Examples

- There was an atmosphere of excitement.
- We talked about the atmosphere.
- The atmosphere matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
