# Attention

## Meaning

Attention is the process whereby a person concentrates on some features of the environment to the exclusion of others.

## Examples

- I need more attention before I decide.
- We talked about attention after lunch.
- That attention really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
