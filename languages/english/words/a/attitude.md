# Attitude

## Meaning

Attitude is a complex mental state involving beliefs and feelings and values and dispositions to act in certain ways.

## Examples

- He had the attitude that work was fun.
- We talked about the attitude.
- The attitude matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
