# Attractive

## Meaning

Attractive describes someone or something that is pleasing to the eye or mind especially through beauty or charm.

## Examples

- A remarkably attractive young man.
- An attractive personality.
- Attractive clothes.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
