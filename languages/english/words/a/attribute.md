# Attribute

## Meaning

Attribute is a construct whereby objects or people can be distinguished.

## Examples

- Self-confidence is not an endearing property.
- We talked about the attribute.
- The attribute matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
