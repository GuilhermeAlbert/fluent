# Audience

## Meaning

Audience is a gathering of spectators or listeners at a performance.

## Examples

- The audience applauded.
- Someone in the audience began to cough.
- We talked about the audience.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
