# Avoid

## Meaning

To avoid means to stay clear from.

## Examples

- Her former friends now avoid her.
- I need to avoid this clearly.
- She tried to avoid the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
