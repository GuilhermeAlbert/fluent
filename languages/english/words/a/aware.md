# Aware

## Meaning

Aware describes someone or something that is having or showing knowledge or understanding or realization or perception.

## Examples

- Was aware of his opponent's hostility.
- Became aware of her surroundings.
- Aware that he had exceeded the speed limit.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
