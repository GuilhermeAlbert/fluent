# Away

## Meaning

Away describes someone or something that is not present.

## Examples

- He's away right now.
- You must not allow a stranger into the house when your mother is away.
- It feels away.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
