# Awful

## Meaning

Awful describes someone or something that is exceptionally bad or displeasing.

## Examples

- Atrocious taste.
- Abominable workmanship.
- An awful voice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
