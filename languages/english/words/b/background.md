# Background

## Meaning

Background is a person's social heritage: previous experience or training.

## Examples

- He is a lawyer with a sports background.
- We talked about the background.
- The background matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
