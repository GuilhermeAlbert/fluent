# Bake

## Meaning

To bake means to cook and make edible by putting in a hot oven.

## Examples

- The chefs bake the vegetables.
- Bake the potatoes.
- I need to bake this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
