# Bar

## Meaning

Bar is a room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served over a counter.

## Examples

- He drowned his sorrows in whiskey at the bar.
- I left the bar on the table.
- She picked up the bar carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
