# Battle

## Meaning

Battle is a hostile meeting of opposing military forces in the course of a war.

## Examples

- Grant won a decisive victory in the battle of Chattanooga.
- He lost his romantic ideas about war when he got into a real engagement.
- The battle starts at nine.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
