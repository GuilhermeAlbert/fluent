# Bean

## Meaning

Bean means any of various edible seeds of plants of the family Leguminosae used for food.

## Examples

- I had bean with breakfast.
- She bought bean at the store.
- This bean tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
