# Beauty

## Meaning

Beauty is the qualities that give pleasure to the senses.

## Examples

- We talked about the beauty.
- The beauty matters here.
- I noticed the beauty right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
