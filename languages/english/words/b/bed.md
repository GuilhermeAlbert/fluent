# Bed

## Meaning

Bed is a piece of furniture that provides a place to sleep.

## Examples

- He sat on the edge of the bed.
- The room had only a bed and chair.
- I left the bed on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
