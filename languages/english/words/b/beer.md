# Beer

## Meaning

Beer is a general name for alcoholic beverages made by fermenting a cereal flavored with hops.

## Examples

- I had beer with breakfast.
- She bought beer at the store.
- This beer tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
