# Belt

## Meaning

Belt means endless loop of flexible material between two rotating shafts or pulleys.

## Examples

- I left the belt on the table.
- She picked up the belt carefully.
- The belt is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
