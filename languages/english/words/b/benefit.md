# Benefit

## Meaning

To benefit means to derive a benefit from.

## Examples

- She profited from his vast experience.
- I need to benefit this today.
- They plan to benefit it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
