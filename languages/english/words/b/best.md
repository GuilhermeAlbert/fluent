# Best

## Meaning

Best means doing something in a way that is in a most excellent way or manner.

## Examples

- He played best after a couple of martinis.
- She answered best.
- It happened best.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
