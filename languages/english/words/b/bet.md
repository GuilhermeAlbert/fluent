# Bet

## Meaning

To bet means to maintain with or as if with a bet.

## Examples

- I bet she will be there!
- I need to bet this clearly.
- She tried to bet the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
