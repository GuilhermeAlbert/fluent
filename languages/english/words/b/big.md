# Big

## Meaning

Big describes someone or something that is above average in size or number or quantity or magnitude or extent.

## Examples

- A large city.
- Set out for the big city.
- A large sum.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
