# Bill

## Meaning

Bill is a statute in draft before it becomes law.

## Examples

- They held a public hearing on the bill.
- We talked about the bill.
- The bill matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
