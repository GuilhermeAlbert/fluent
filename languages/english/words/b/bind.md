# Bind

## Meaning

To bind means to stick to firmly.

## Examples

- Will this wallpaper adhere to the wall?
- I need to bind this today.
- They plan to bind it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
