# Bird

## Meaning

Bird means warm-blooded egg-laying vertebrates characterized by feathers and forelimbs modified as wings.

## Examples

- The bird moved across the road.
- We saw a bird near the house.
- The bird looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
