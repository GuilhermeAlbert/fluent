# Birth

## Meaning

Birth is the time when something begins.

## Examples

- They divorced after the birth of the child.
- His election signaled the birth of a new age.
- The birth went by quickly.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
