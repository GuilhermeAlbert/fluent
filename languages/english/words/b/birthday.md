# Birthday

## Meaning

Birthday is an anniversary of the day on which a person was born.

## Examples

- The birthday went by quickly.
- We chose a better birthday.
- That birthday worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
