# Bite

## Meaning

To bite means to grip, cut off, or tear with or as if with the teeth or jaws.

## Examples

- Gunny invariably tried to bite her.
- I need to bite this today.
- They plan to bite it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
