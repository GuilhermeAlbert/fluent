# Black

## Meaning

Black describes someone or something that is being of the achromatic color of maximum darkness.

## Examples

- Black leather jackets.
- As black as coal.
- Rich black soil.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
