# Blade

## Meaning

Blade means especially a leaf of grass or the broad portion of a leaf as distinct from the petiole.

## Examples

- We talked about the blade.
- The blade matters here.
- I noticed the blade right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
