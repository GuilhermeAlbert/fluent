# Blanket

## Meaning

Blanket means bedding that keeps a person warm in bed.

## Examples

- He pulled the covers over his head and went to sleep.
- I left the blanket on the table.
- She picked up the blanket carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
