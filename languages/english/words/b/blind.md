# Blind

## Meaning

Blind means people who have severe visual impairments, considered as a group.

## Examples

- He spent hours reading to the blind.
- We talked about the blind.
- The blind matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
