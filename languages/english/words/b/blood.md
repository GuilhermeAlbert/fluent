# Blood

## Meaning

Blood is the fluid that is pumped through the body by the heart and contains plasma, blood cells, and platelets.

## Examples

- Blood carries oxygen and nutrients to the tissues and carries away waste products.
- The ancients believed that blood was the seat of the emotions.
- I need more blood before I decide.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
