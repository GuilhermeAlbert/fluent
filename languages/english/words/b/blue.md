# Blue

## Meaning

Blue describes someone or something that is of the color intermediate between green and violet.

## Examples

- October's bright blue weather.
- A blue flame.
- Blue haze of tobacco smoke.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
