# Bomb

## Meaning

Bomb is an explosive device fused to explode under specific conditions.

## Examples

- I left the bomb on the table.
- She picked up the bomb carefully.
- The bomb is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
