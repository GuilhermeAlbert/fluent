# Bone

## Meaning

Bone means rigid connective tissue that makes up the skeleton of vertebrates.

## Examples

- My bone hurts a little.
- She touched her bone.
- Keep your bone relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
