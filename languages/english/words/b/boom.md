# Boom

## Meaning

To boom means to make a resonant sound, like artillery.

## Examples

- Cars boom in the streets.
- The streets boom with cars.
- His deep voice boomed through the hall.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
