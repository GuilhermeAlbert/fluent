# Borrow

## Meaning

To borrow means to get temporarily.

## Examples

- May I borrow your lawn mower?
- I need to borrow this today.
- They plan to borrow it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
