# Boss

## Meaning

Boss means someone who exercises control over workers.

## Examples

- If you want to leave early you have to ask the foreman.
- The boss asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the boss yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
