# Bother

## Meaning

To bother means to take the trouble to do something.

## Examples

- He did not trouble to call his mother on her birthday.
- Don't bother, please.
- I need to bother soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
