# Bottle

## Meaning

Bottle is a glass or plastic vessel used for storing drinks or other liquids.

## Examples

- I left the bottle on the table.
- She picked up the bottle carefully.
- The bottle is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
