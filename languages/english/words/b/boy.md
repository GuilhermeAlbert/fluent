# Boy

## Meaning

Boy is a youthful male person.

## Examples

- The baby was a boy.
- She made the boy brush his teeth every night.
- Most soldiers are only boys in uniform.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
