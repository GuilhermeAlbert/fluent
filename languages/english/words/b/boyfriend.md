# Boyfriend

## Meaning

Boyfriend is a man who is the lover of a man or woman.

## Examples

- If I'd known he was her boyfriend, I wouldn't have asked.
- When the law changed, Pet could finally marry his long-time boyfriend Jim.
- The boyfriend asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
