# Brain

## Meaning

Brain means that part of the central nervous system that includes all the higher nervous centers.

## Examples

- My brain hurts a little.
- She touched her brain.
- Keep your brain relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
