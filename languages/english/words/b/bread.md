# Bread

## Meaning

Bread means food made from dough of flour or meal and usually raised with yeast or baking powder and then baked.

## Examples

- I need more bread before I decide.
- We talked about bread after lunch.
- That bread really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
