# Brick

## Meaning

Brick means rectangular block of clay baked by the sun or in a kiln.

## Examples

- I left the brick on the table.
- She picked up the brick carefully.
- The brick is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
