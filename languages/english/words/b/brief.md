# Brief

## Meaning

Brief describes someone or something that is of short duration or distance.

## Examples

- A brief stay in the country.
- It feels brief.
- That was a brief choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
