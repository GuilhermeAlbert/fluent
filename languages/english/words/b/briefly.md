# Briefly

## Meaning

Briefly means doing something in a way that is in a concise manner.

## Examples

- The history is summed up concisely in this book.
- She replied briefly.
- Briefly, we have a problem.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
