# Bright

## Meaning

Bright describes someone or something that is emitting or reflecting light readily or in large amounts.

## Examples

- The sun was bright and hot.
- A bright sunlit room.
- It feels bright.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
