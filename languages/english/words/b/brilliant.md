# Brilliant

## Meaning

Brilliant describes someone or something that is of surpassing excellence.

## Examples

- A brilliant performance.
- A superb actor.
- It feels brilliant.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
