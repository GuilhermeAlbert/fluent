# Bring

## Meaning

To bring means to take something or somebody with oneself somewhere.

## Examples

- They bring the food to the people.
- They bring the people the food.
- Bring me the box from the other room.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
