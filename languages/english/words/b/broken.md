# Broken

## Meaning

Broken describes someone or something that is physically and forcibly separated into pieces or cracked or split.

## Examples

- A broken mirror.
- A broken tooth.
- A broken leg.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
