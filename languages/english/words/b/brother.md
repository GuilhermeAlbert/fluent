# Brother

## Meaning

Brother is a male with the same parents as someone else.

## Examples

- My brother still lives with our parents.
- The brother asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the brother yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
