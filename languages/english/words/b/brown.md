# Brown

## Meaning

To brown means to fry in a pan until it changes color.

## Examples

- The chefs brown the vegetables.
- Brown the meat in the pan.
- I need to brown this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
