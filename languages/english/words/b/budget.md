# Budget

## Meaning

Budget is a sum of money allocated for a particular purpose.

## Examples

- The laboratory runs on a budget of a million a year.
- We talked about the budget.
- The budget matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
