# Building

## Meaning

Building is a structure that has a roof and walls and stands more or less permanently in one place.

## Examples

- There was a three-story building on the corner.
- It was an imposing edifice.
- I left the building on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
