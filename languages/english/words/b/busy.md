# Busy

## Meaning

Busy describes someone or something that is actively or fully engaged or occupied.

## Examples

- Busy with her work.
- A busy man.
- Too busy to eat lunch.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
