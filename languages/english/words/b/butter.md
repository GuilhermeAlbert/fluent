# Butter

## Meaning

Butter is an edible emulsion of fat globules made by churning milk or cream.

## Examples

- I had butter with breakfast.
- She bought butter at the store.
- This butter tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
