# Button

## Meaning

Button is a round fastener sewn to shirts and coats etc. to fit through buttonholes.

## Examples

- I left the button on the table.
- She picked up the button carefully.
- The button is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
