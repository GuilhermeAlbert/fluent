# Buyer

## Meaning

Buyer means someone who buys.

## Examples

- The buyer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the buyer yesterday.
- The buyer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
