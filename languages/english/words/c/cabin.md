# Cabin

## Meaning

Cabin means small room on a ship or boat where people sleep.

## Examples

- I left the cabin on the table.
- She picked up the cabin carefully.
- The cabin is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
