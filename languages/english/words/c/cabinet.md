# Cabinet

## Meaning

Cabinet is a piece of furniture resembling a cupboard with doors and shelves and drawers.

## Examples

- I left the cabinet on the table.
- She picked up the cabinet carefully.
- The cabinet is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
