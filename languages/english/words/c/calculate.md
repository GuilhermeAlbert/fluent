# Calculate

## Meaning

To calculate means to make a mathematical calculation or computation.

## Examples

- I need to calculate the problem.
- They tried to calculate what happened.
- Can you calculate this for me?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
