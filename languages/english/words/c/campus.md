# Campus

## Meaning

Campus is a field on which the buildings of a university are situated.

## Examples

- We met near the campus.
- The campus was quiet this morning.
- I walked past the campus yesterday.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
