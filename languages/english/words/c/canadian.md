# Canadian

## Meaning

Canadian is a native or inhabitant of Canada.

## Examples

- The canadian asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the canadian yesterday.
- The canadian joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
