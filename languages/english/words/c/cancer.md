# Cancer

## Meaning

Cancer means any malignant growth or tumor caused by abnormal and uncontrolled cell division.

## Examples

- We talked about the cancer.
- The cancer matters here.
- I noticed the cancer right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
