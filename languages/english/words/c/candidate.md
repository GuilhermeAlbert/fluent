# Candidate

## Meaning

Candidate is a politician who is running for public office.

## Examples

- The candidate asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the candidate yesterday.
- The candidate joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
