# Capability

## Meaning

Capability is the quality of being capable - physically or intellectually or legally.

## Examples

- He worked to the limits of his capability.
- We talked about the capability.
- The capability matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
