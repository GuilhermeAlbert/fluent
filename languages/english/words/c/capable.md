# Capable

## Meaning

Capable describes someone or something that is having capacity or ability.

## Examples

- Capable of winning.
- Capable of hard work.
- Capable of walking on two feet.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
