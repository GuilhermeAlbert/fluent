# Capacity

## Meaning

Capacity means capability to perform or produce.

## Examples

- Among his gifts is his capacity for true altruism.
- Limited runway capacity.
- A great capacity for growth.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
