# Captain

## Meaning

Captain is an officer holding a rank below a major but above a lieutenant.

## Examples

- The captain asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the captain yesterday.
- The captain joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
