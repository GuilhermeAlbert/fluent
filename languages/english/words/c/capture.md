# Capture

## Meaning

To capture means to succeed in representing or expressing something intangible.

## Examples

- Capture the essence of Spring.
- Capture an idea.
- I need to capture this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
