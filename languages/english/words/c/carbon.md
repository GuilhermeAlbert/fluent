# Carbon

## Meaning

Carbon is an abundant nonmetallic tetravalent element occurring in three allotropic forms: amorphous carbon and graphite and diamond.

## Examples

- We talked about the carbon.
- The carbon matters here.
- I noticed the carbon right away.

## Notes
