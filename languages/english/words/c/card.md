# Card

## Meaning

Card means one of a set of small pieces of stiff paper marked in various ways and used for playing games or for telling fortunes.

## Examples

- He collected cards and traded them with the other boys.
- I left the card on the table.
- She picked up the card carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
