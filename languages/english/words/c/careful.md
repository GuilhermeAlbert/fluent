# Careful

## Meaning

Careful describes someone or something that is exercising caution or showing care or attention.

## Examples

- They were careful when crossing the busy street.
- Be careful to keep her shoes clean.
- Did very careful research.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
