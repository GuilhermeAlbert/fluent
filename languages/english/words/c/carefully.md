# Carefully

## Meaning

Carefully means doing something in a way that is taking care or paying attention.

## Examples

- They watched carefully.
- She answered carefully.
- It happened carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
