# Carrier

## Meaning

Carrier means someone whose employment involves carrying something.

## Examples

- The bonds were transmitted by carrier.
- The carrier asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the carrier yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
