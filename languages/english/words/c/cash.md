# Cash

## Meaning

Cash means money in the form of bills or coins.

## Examples

- There is a desperate shortage of hard cash.
- I need more cash before I decide.
- We talked about cash after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
