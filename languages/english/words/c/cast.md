# Cast

## Meaning

To cast means to put or send forth.

## Examples

- She threw the flashlight beam into the corner.
- The setting sun threw long shadows.
- Cast a spell.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
