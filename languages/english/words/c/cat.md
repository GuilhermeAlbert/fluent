# Cat

## Meaning

Cat means feline mammal usually having thick soft fur and no ability to roar.

## Examples

- The cat moved across the road.
- We saw a cat near the house.
- The cat looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
