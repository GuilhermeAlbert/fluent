# Catholic

## Meaning

Catholic describes someone or something that is free from provincial prejudices or attachments.

## Examples

- Catholic in one's tastes.
- It feels catholic.
- That was a catholic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
