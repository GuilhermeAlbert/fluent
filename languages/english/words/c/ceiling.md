# Ceiling

## Meaning

Ceiling is the overhead upper surface of a covered space.

## Examples

- He hated painting the ceiling.
- I left the ceiling on the table.
- She picked up the ceiling carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
