# Celebrate

## Meaning

To celebrate means to behave as expected during holidays or rites.

## Examples

- Keep the commandments.
- Celebrate Christmas.
- Observe Yom Kippur.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
