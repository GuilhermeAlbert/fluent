# Celebration

## Meaning

Celebration is a joyful occasion for special festivities to mark some happy event.

## Examples

- The celebration starts at nine.
- We planned the celebration together.
- That celebration changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
