# Celebrity

## Meaning

Celebrity is a widely known person.

## Examples

- He was a baseball celebrity.
- The celebrity asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the celebrity yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
