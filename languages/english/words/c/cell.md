# Cell

## Meaning

Cell means any small compartment.

## Examples

- The cells of a honeycomb.
- I left the cell on the table.
- She picked up the cell carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
