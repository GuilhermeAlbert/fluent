# Chair

## Meaning

Chair is a seat for one person, with a support for the back.

## Examples

- He put his coat over the back of the chair and sat down.
- I left the chair on the table.
- She picked up the chair carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
