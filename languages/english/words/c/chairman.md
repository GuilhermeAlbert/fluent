# Chairman

## Meaning

Chairman is the male officer who plives at the meetings of an organization.

## Examples

- The chairman asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the chairman yesterday.
- The chairman joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
