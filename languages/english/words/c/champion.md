# Champion

## Meaning

Champion means someone who has won first place in a competition.

## Examples

- The champion asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the champion yesterday.
- The champion joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
