# Chance

## Meaning

Chance is a possibility due to a favorable combination of circumstances.

## Examples

- The holiday gave us the opportunity to visit Washington.
- Now is your chance.
- We talked about the chance.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
