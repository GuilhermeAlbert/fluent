# Changing

## Meaning

Changing describes someone or something that is marked by continuous change or effective action.

## Examples

- It feels changing.
- That was a changing choice.
- The room looked changing.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
