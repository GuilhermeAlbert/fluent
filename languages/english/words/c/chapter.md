# Chapter

## Meaning

Chapter is a subdivision of a written work.

## Examples

- He read a chapter every night before falling asleep.
- We talked about the chapter.
- The chapter matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
