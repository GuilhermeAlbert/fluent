# Characterize

## Meaning

To characterize means to describe or portray the character or the qualities or peculiarities of.

## Examples

- You can characterize his behavior as that of an egotist.
- This poem can be characterized as a lament for a dead lover.
- I need to characterize this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
