# Charge

## Meaning

To charge means to make a rush at or sudden attack upon, as in battle.

## Examples

- He saw Jess charging at him with a pitchfork.
- I need to charge soon.
- They want to charge together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
