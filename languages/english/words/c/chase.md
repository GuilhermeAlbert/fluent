# Chase

## Meaning

To chase means to go after with the intent to catch.

## Examples

- They chase the car down the avenue.
- The policeman chased the mugger down the alley.
- The fox chased the rabbit.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
