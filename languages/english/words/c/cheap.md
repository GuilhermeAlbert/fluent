# Cheap

## Meaning

Cheap describes someone or something that is relatively low in price or charging low prices.

## Examples

- It would have been cheap at twice the price.
- Inexpensive family restaurants.
- It feels cheap.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
