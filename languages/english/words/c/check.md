# Check

## Meaning

To check means to examine so as to determine accuracy, quality, or condition.

## Examples

- The men check the area for animals.
- The men check for animals in the area.
- Check the brakes.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
