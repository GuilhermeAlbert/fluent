# Chest

## Meaning

Chest is the part of the human torso between the neck and the diaphragm or the corresponding part in other vertebrates.

## Examples

- My chest hurts a little.
- She touched her chest.
- Keep your chest relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
