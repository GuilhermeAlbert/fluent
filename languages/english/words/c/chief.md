# Chief

## Meaning

Chief means someone who is in charge.

## Examples

- The head of the whole operation.
- The chief asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the chief yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
