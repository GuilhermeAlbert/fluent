# Chip

## Meaning

Chip is a small fragment of something broken off from the whole.

## Examples

- A bit of rock caught him in the eye.
- We talked about the chip.
- The chip matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
