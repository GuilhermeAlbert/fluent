# Christian

## Meaning

Christian describes someone or something that is following the teachings or manifesting the qualities or spirit of Jesus Christ.

## Examples

- It feels christian.
- That was a christian choice.
- The room looked christian.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
