# Circle

## Meaning

Circle means ellipse in which the two axes are of equal length.

## Examples

- He calculated the circumference of the circle.
- We talked about the circle.
- The circle matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
