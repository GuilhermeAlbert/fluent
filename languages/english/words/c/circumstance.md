# Circumstance

## Meaning

Circumstance is a condition that accompanies or influences some event or activity.

## Examples

- We talked about the circumstance.
- The circumstance matters here.
- I noticed the circumstance right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
