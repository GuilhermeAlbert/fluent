# Citizen

## Meaning

Citizen is a native or naturalized member of a state or other political community.

## Examples

- The citizen asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the citizen yesterday.
- The citizen joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
