# Civil

## Meaning

Civil describes someone or something that is applying to ordinary citizens as contrasted with the military.

## Examples

- Civil authorities.
- It feels civil.
- That was a civil choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
