# Civilian

## Meaning

Civilian is a nonmilitary citizen.

## Examples

- The civilian asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the civilian yesterday.
- The civilian joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
