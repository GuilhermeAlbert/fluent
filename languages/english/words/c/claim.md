# Claim

## Meaning

To claim means to assert or affirm strongly.

## Examples

- They claim to move.
- He claimed that he killed the burglar.
- I need to claim this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
