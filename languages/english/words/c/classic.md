# Classic

## Meaning

Classic describes something connected with the first significant period of a civilization, culture, area of study, etc.

## Examples

- Classic Chinese pottery.
- Classical Marxism.
- It feels classic.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
