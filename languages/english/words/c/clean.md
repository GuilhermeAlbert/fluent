# Clean

## Meaning

To clean means to make clean by removing dirt, filth, or unwanted substances from.

## Examples

- They clean the cape.
- Clean the stove!
- The dentist cleaned my teeth.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
