# Clearly

## Meaning

Clearly means doing something in a way that is without doubt or question.

## Examples

- They were clearly lost.
- History has clearly shown the folly of that policy.
- She answered clearly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
