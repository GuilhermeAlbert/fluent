# Client

## Meaning

Client means someone who seeks the advice of a lawyer.

## Examples

- The client asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the client yesterday.
- The client joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
