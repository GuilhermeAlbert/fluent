# Climate

## Meaning

Climate is the weather in some location averaged over some long period of time.

## Examples

- The dank climate of southern Wales.
- Plants from a cold clime travel best in winter.
- We talked about the climate.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
