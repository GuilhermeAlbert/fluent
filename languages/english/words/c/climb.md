# Climb

## Meaning

To climb means to go upward with gradual or continuous progress.

## Examples

- They climb the hill.
- They climb up the hill.
- Did you ever climb up the hill behind your house?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
