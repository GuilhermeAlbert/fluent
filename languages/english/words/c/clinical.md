# Clinical

## Meaning

Clinical describes something connected with a clinic or conducted in or as if in a clinic and depending on direct observation of patients.

## Examples

- Clinical observation.
- Clinical case study.
- It feels clinical.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
