# Closely

## Meaning

Closely means doing something in a way that is in a close relation or position in time or space.

## Examples

- The onsets were closely timed.
- Houses set closely together.
- Was closely involved in monitoring daily progress.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
