# Closer

## Meaning

Closer means someone who closes something.

## Examples

- Whoever is the closer has to turn out the lights and lock up.
- The closer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the closer yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
