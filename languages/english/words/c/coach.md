# Coach

## Meaning

Coach means someone in charge of training an athlete or a team.

## Examples

- The coach asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the coach yesterday.
- The coach joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
