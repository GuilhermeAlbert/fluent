# Coat

## Meaning

To coat means to put a coat on.

## Examples

- The wind storms coat the area with dust and dirt.
- Coat the cake with chocolate.
- I need to coat this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
