# Coffee

## Meaning

Coffee is a beverage consisting of an infusion of ground coffee beans.

## Examples

- He ordered a cup of coffee.
- I need more coffee before I decide.
- We talked about coffee after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
