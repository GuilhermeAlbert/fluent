# Cognitive

## Meaning

Cognitive describes someone or something that is of or being or relating to or involving cognition.

## Examples

- Cognitive psychology.
- Cognitive style.
- It feels cognitive.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
