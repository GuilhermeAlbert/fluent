# Cold

## Meaning

Cold describes someone or something that is having a low or inadequate temperature or feeling a sensation of coldness or having been made cold by e.g. ice or refrigeration.

## Examples

- A cold climate.
- A cold room.
- Dinner has gotten cold.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
