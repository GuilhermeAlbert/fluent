# Collapse

## Meaning

To collapse means to break down, literally or metaphorically.

## Examples

- The business is going to collapse.
- The wall collapsed.
- The business collapsed.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
