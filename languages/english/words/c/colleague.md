# Colleague

## Meaning

Colleague is an associate that one works with.

## Examples

- The colleague asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the colleague yesterday.
- The colleague joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
