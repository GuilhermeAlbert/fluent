# Collective

## Meaning

Collective describes someone or something that is done by or characteristic of people acting together.

## Examples

- The collective mind.
- The corporate good.
- It feels collective.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
