# Colonial

## Meaning

Colonial describes something connected with or characteristic of or inhabiting a colony.

## Examples

- It feels colonial.
- That was a colonial choice.
- The room looked colonial.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
