# Color

## Meaning

Color is a visual attribute of things that results from the light they emit or transmit or reflect.

## Examples

- A white color is made up of many different wavelengths of light.
- We talked about the color.
- The color matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
