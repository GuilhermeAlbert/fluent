# Come

## Meaning

To come means to move toward, travel toward something or somebody or approach something or somebody.

## Examples

- They come down the river.
- He came singing down the road.
- Come with me to the Casbah.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
