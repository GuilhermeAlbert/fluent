# Comfort

## Meaning

Comfort is a situation of being relaxed and feeling no pain.

## Examples

- He is a man who enjoys his comfort.
- She longed for the comfortableness of her armchair.
- We talked about the comfort.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
