# Comfortable

## Meaning

Comfortable describes someone or something that is providing or experiencing physical well-being or relief.

## Examples

- Comfortable clothes.
- Comfortable suburban houses.
- Made himself comfortable in an armchair.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
