# Commander

## Meaning

Commander is an officer in command of a military unit.

## Examples

- The commander asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the commander yesterday.
- The commander joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
