# Comment

## Meaning

To comment means to make or write a comment on.

## Examples

- He commented the paper of his colleague.
- I need to comment this clearly.
- She tried to comment the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
