# Commercial

## Meaning

Commercial describes someone or something that is connected with or engaged in or sponsored by or used in commerce or commercial enterprises.

## Examples

- Commercial trucker.
- Commercial TV.
- Commercial diamonds.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
