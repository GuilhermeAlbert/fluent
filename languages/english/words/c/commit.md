# Commit

## Meaning

To commit means to perform an act, usually with a negative connotation.

## Examples

- They commit him to write the letter.
- Perpetrate a crime.
- Pull a bank robbery.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
