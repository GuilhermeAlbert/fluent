# Commitment

## Meaning

Commitment is the trait of sincere and steadfast fixity of purpose.

## Examples

- A man of energy and commitment.
- We talked about the commitment.
- The commitment matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
