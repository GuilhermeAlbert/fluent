# Committee

## Meaning

Committee is a special group delegated to consider some matter.

## Examples

- A committee is a group that keeps minutes and loses hours.
- We talked about the committee.
- The committee matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
