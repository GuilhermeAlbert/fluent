# Compare

## Meaning

To compare means to examine and note the similarities or differences of.

## Examples

- John compared his haircut to his friend's.
- We compared notes after we had both seen the movie.
- I need to compare the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
