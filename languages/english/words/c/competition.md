# Competition

## Meaning

Competition is a business relation in which two parties compete to gain customers.

## Examples

- Business competition can be fiendish at times.
- We talked about the competition.
- The competition matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
