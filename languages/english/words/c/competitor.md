# Competitor

## Meaning

Competitor is the contestant you hope to defeat.

## Examples

- He had respect for his rivals.
- He wanted to know what the competition was doing.
- The competitor asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
