# Complain

## Meaning

To complain means to express complaints, discontent, displeasure, or unhappiness.

## Examples

- They complain that there was a traffic accident.
- My mother complains all day.
- She has a lot to kick about.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
