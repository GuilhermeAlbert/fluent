# Complex

## Meaning

Complex is a conceptual whole made up of complicated and related parts.

## Examples

- The complex of shopping malls, houses, and roads created a new town.
- We talked about the complex.
- The complex matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
