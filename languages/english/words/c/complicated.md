# Complicated

## Meaning

Complicated describes someone or something that is difficult to analyze or understand.

## Examples

- A complicated problem.
- Complicated Middle East politics.
- He's more complex than he seems on the surface.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
