# Composition

## Meaning

Composition is the spatial property resulting from the arrangement of parts in relation to each other and to the whole.

## Examples

- Harmonious composition is essential in a serious work of art.
- We talked about the composition.
- The composition matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
