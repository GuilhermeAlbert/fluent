# Comprehensive

## Meaning

Comprehensive describes someone or something that is including all or everything.

## Examples

- Comprehensive coverage.
- A comprehensive history of the revolution.
- A comprehensive survey.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
