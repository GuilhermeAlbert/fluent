# Concentrate

## Meaning

To concentrate means to make denser, stronger, or purer.

## Examples

- Concentrate juice.
- I need to concentrate this today.
- They plan to concentrate it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
