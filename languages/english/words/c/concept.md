# Concept

## Meaning

Concept is an abstract or general idea inferred or derived from specific instances.

## Examples

- We talked about the concept.
- The concept matters here.
- I noticed the concept right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
