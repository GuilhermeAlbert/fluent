# Concerned

## Meaning

Concerned describes someone or something that is feeling or showing worry or solicitude.

## Examples

- Concerned parents of youthful offenders.
- Was concerned about the future.
- We feel concerned about accomplishing the task at hand.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
