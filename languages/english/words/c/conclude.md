# Conclude

## Meaning

To conclude means to decide by reasoning.

## Examples

- They conclude that there was a traffic accident.
- We reasoned that it was cheaper to rent than to buy a house.
- I need to conclude the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
