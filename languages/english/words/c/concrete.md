# Concrete

## Meaning

Concrete describes someone or something that is capable of being perceived by the senses.

## Examples

- Concrete objects such as trees.
- It feels concrete.
- That was a concrete choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
