# Confidence

## Meaning

Confidence means freedom from doubt.

## Examples

- His assurance in his superiority did not make him popular.
- After that failure he lost his confidence.
- She spoke with authority.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
