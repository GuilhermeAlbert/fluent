# Confident

## Meaning

Confident describes someone or something that is having or marked by confidence or assurance.

## Examples

- A confident speaker.
- A confident reply.
- His manner is more confident these days.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
