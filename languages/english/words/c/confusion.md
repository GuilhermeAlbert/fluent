# Confusion

## Meaning

Confusion means disorder resulting from a failure to behave predictably.

## Examples

- The army retreated in confusion.
- I need more confusion before I decide.
- We talked about confusion after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
