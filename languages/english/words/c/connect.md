# Connect

## Meaning

To connect means to connect, fasten, or put together two or more pieces.

## Examples

- Can you connect the two loudspeakers?
- Tie the ropes together.
- Link arms.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
