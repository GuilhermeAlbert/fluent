# Consciousness

## Meaning

Consciousness is an alert cognitive state in which you are aware of yourself and your situation.

## Examples

- He lost consciousness.
- We talked about the consciousness.
- The consciousness matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
