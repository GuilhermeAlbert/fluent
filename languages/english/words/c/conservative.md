# Conservative

## Meaning

Conservative describes someone or something that is resistant to change, particularly in relation to politics or religion.

## Examples

- It feels conservative.
- That was a conservative choice.
- The room looked conservative.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
