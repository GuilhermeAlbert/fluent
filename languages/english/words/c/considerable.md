# Considerable

## Meaning

Considerable describes someone or something that is large or relatively large in number or amount or extent or degree.

## Examples

- A considerable quantity.
- The economy was a considerable issue in the campaign.
- Went to considerable trouble for us.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
