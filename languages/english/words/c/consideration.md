# Consideration

## Meaning

Consideration is the process of giving careful thought to something.

## Examples

- We talked about the consideration.
- The consideration matters here.
- I noticed the consideration right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
