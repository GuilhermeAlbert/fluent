# Consistent

## Meaning

Consistent describes someone or something that is in agreement or consistent or reliable.

## Examples

- Testimony consistent with the known facts.
- It feels consistent.
- That was a consistent choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
