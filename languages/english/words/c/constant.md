# Constant

## Meaning

Constant describes someone or something that is unvarying in nature.

## Examples

- Maintained a constant temperature.
- Principles of unvarying validity.
- It feels constant.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
