# Constantly

## Meaning

Constantly means doing something in a way that is without variation or change, in every case.

## Examples

- Constantly kind and gracious.
- He always arrives on time.
- She answered constantly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
