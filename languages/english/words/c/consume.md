# Consume

## Meaning

To consume means to eat up completely, as with great appetite.

## Examples

- They consume more bread.
- Some people can down a pound of meat in the course of one meal.
- The teenagers demolished four pizzas among them.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
