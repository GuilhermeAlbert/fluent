# Consumer

## Meaning

Consumer means someone who uses goods or services.

## Examples

- The consumer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the consumer yesterday.
- The consumer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
