# Contemporary

## Meaning

Contemporary describes someone or something that is characteristic of the present.

## Examples

- Contemporary trends in design.
- The role of computers in modern-day medicine.
- It feels contemporary.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
