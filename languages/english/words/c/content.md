# Content

## Meaning

Content means everything that is included in a collection and that is held or included in something.

## Examples

- He emptied the contents of his pockets.
- The two groups were similar in content.
- We talked about the content.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
