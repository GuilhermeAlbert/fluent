# Context

## Meaning

Context means discourse that surrounds a language unit and helps to determine its interpretation.

## Examples

- We talked about the context.
- The context matters here.
- I noticed the context right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
