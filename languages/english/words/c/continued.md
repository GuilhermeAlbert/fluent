# Continued

## Meaning

Continued describes someone or something that is without stop or interruption.

## Examples

- To insure the continued success of the war.
- The continued existence of nationalism.
- The continued popularity of Westerns.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
