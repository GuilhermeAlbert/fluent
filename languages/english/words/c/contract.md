# Contract

## Meaning

To contract means to enter into a contractual arrangement.

## Examples

- They contract to move.
- I need to contract this clearly.
- She tried to contract the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
