# Contrast

## Meaning

To contrast means to put in opposition to show or emphasize differences.

## Examples

- The middle school teacher contrasted her best student's work with that of her weakest student.
- I need to contrast the problem.
- They tried to contrast what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
