# Controversial

## Meaning

Controversial describes someone or something that is marked by or capable of arousing controversy.

## Examples

- The issue of the death penalty is highly controversial.
- Rushdie's controversial book.
- A controversial decision on affirmative action.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
