# Controversy

## Meaning

Controversy is a contentious speech act.

## Examples

- They were involved in a violent argument.
- We talked about the controversy.
- The controversy matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
