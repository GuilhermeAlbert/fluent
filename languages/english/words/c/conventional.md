# Conventional

## Meaning

Conventional describes someone or something that is following accepted customs and proprieties.

## Examples

- Conventional wisdom.
- She had strayed from the path of conventional behavior.
- Conventional forms of address.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
