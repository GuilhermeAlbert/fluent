# Conversation

## Meaning

Conversation is the use of speech for informal exchange of views or ideas or information etc.

## Examples

- We talked about the conversation.
- The conversation matters here.
- I noticed the conversation right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
