# Convert

## Meaning

To convert means to change from one system to another or to a new plan or policy.

## Examples

- We converted from 220 to 110 Volt.
- I need to convert this today.
- They plan to convert it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
