# Conviction

## Meaning

Conviction is an unshakable belief in something without need for proof or evidence.

## Examples

- We talked about the conviction.
- The conviction matters here.
- I noticed the conviction right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
