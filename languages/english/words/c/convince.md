# Convince

## Meaning

To convince means to make agree, understand, or realize the truth or validity of something.

## Examples

- They convince him to write the letter.
- He had finally convinced several customers of the advantages of his product.
- I need to convince this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
