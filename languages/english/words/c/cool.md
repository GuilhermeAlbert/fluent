# Cool

## Meaning

Cool describes someone or something that is neither warm nor very cold.

## Examples

- A cool autumn day.
- A cool room.
- Cool summer dresses.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
