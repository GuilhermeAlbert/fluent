# Corporate

## Meaning

Corporate describes someone or something that is of or belonging to a corporation.

## Examples

- Corporate rates.
- Corporate structure.
- It feels corporate.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
