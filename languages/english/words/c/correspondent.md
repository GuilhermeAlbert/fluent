# Correspondent

## Meaning

Correspondent means someone who communicates by means of letters.

## Examples

- The correspondent asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the correspondent yesterday.
- The correspondent joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
