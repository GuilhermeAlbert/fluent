# Couch

## Meaning

Couch is an upholstered seat for more than one person.

## Examples

- I left the couch on the table.
- She picked up the couch carefully.
- The couch is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
