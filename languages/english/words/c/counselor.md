# Counselor

## Meaning

Counselor means someone who gives advice about problems.

## Examples

- The counselor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the counselor yesterday.
- The counselor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
