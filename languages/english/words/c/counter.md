# Counter

## Meaning

Counter means table consisting of a horizontal surface over which business is transacted.

## Examples

- I left the counter on the table.
- She picked up the counter carefully.
- The counter is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
