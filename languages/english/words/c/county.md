# County

## Meaning

County is a region created by territorial division for the purpose of local government.

## Examples

- The county has a population of 12,345 people.
- We met near the county.
- The county was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
