# Courage

## Meaning

Courage is a quality of spirit that enables you to face danger or pain without showing fear.

## Examples

- I need more courage before I decide.
- We talked about courage after lunch.
- That courage really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
