# Cousin

## Meaning

Cousin is the child of your aunt or uncle.

## Examples

- The cousin asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the cousin yesterday.
- The cousin joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
