# Craft

## Meaning

Craft is the skilled practice of a practical occupation.

## Examples

- He learned his trade as an apprentice.
- The craft starts at nine.
- We planned the craft together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
