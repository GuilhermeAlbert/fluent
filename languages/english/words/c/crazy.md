# Crazy

## Meaning

Crazy describes someone or something that is affected with madness or insanity.

## Examples

- A man who had gone mad.
- It feels crazy.
- That was a crazy choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
