# Crisis

## Meaning

Crisis is an unstable situation of extreme danger or difficulty.

## Examples

- They went bankrupt during the economic crisis.
- We talked about the crisis.
- The crisis matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
