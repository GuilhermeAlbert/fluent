# Critic

## Meaning

Critic means someone who is professionally engaged in the analysis and interpretation of works of art.

## Examples

- The critic asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the critic yesterday.
- The critic joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
