# Critical

## Meaning

Critical describes someone or something that is marked by a tendency to find and call attention to errors and flaws.

## Examples

- A critical attitude.
- It feels critical.
- That was a critical choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
