# Criticism

## Meaning

Criticism means disapproval expressed by pointing out faults or shortcomings.

## Examples

- The senator received severe criticism from his opponent.
- We talked about the criticism.
- The criticism matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
