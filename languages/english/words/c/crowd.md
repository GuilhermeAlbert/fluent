# Crowd

## Meaning

To crowd means to cause to herd, drive, or crowd together.

## Examples

- We herded the children into a spare classroom.
- We need to crowd soon.
- They started to crowd after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
