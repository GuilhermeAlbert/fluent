# Crucial

## Meaning

Crucial describes someone or something that is of extreme importance.

## Examples

- A crucial moment in his career.
- A crucial election.
- A crucial issue for women.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
