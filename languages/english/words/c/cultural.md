# Cultural

## Meaning

Cultural describes something connected with the arts and manners that a group favors.

## Examples

- Cultural events.
- A person of broad cultural interests.
- It feels cultural.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
