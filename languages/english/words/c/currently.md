# Currently

## Meaning

Currently means doing something in a way that is at this time or period.

## Examples

- He is presently our ambassador to the United Nations.
- Currently they live in Connecticut.
- She answered currently.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
