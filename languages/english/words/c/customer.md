# Customer

## Meaning

Customer means someone who pays for goods or services.

## Examples

- The customer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the customer yesterday.
- The customer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
