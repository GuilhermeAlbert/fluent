# Cycle

## Meaning

Cycle is an interval during which a recurring sequence of events occurs.

## Examples

- The never-ending cycle of the seasons.
- The cycle went by quickly.
- We chose a better cycle.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
