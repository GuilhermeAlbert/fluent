# Dance

## Meaning

To dance means to move in a graceful and rhythmical way.

## Examples

- The crowds dance in the streets.
- The streets dance with crowds.
- The young girl danced into the room.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
