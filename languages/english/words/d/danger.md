# Danger

## Meaning

Danger is the condition of being susceptible to harm or injury.

## Examples

- You are in no danger.
- There was widespread danger of disease.
- We talked about the danger.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
