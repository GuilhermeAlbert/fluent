# Dangerous

## Meaning

Dangerous describes someone or something that is involving or causing danger or risk.

## Examples

- A dangerous criminal.
- A dangerous bridge.
- Unemployment reached dangerous proportions.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
