# Dark

## Meaning

Dark describes someone or something that is devoid of or deficient in light or brightness.

## Examples

- Sitting in a dark corner.
- A dark day.
- Dark shadows.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
