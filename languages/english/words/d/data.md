# Data

## Meaning

Data is a collection of facts from which conclusions may be drawn.

## Examples

- Statistical data.
- I need more data before I decide.
- We talked about data after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
