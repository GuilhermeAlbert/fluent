# Daughter

## Meaning

Daughter is a female human offspring.

## Examples

- Her daughter cared for her in her old age.
- The daughter asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the daughter yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
