# Dead

## Meaning

Dead describes someone or something that is no longer having or seeming to have or expecting to have life.

## Examples

- The nerve is dead.
- A dead pallor.
- He was marked as a dead man by the assassin.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
