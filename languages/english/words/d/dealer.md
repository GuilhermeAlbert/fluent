# Dealer

## Meaning

Dealer means someone who buys and maintains an inventory of goods to be sold.

## Examples

- The dealer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the dealer yesterday.
- The dealer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
