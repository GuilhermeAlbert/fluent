# Death

## Meaning

Death is the event of dying or departure from life.

## Examples

- Her death came as a terrible shock.
- Upon your decease the capital will pass to your grandchildren.
- I need more death before I decide.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
