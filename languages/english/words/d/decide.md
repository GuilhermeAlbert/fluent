# Decide

## Meaning

To decide means to reach, make, or come to a decision about something.

## Examples

- They decide to move.
- We finally decided after lengthy deliberations.
- I need to decide the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
