# Deeply

## Meaning

Deeply means doing something in a way that is to a great depth psychologically or emotionally.

## Examples

- They felt the loss deeply.
- She loved him intensely.
- She answered deeply.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
