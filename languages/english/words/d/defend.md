# Defend

## Meaning

To defend means to argue or speak in defense of.

## Examples

- She supported the motion to strike.
- I need to defend this clearly.
- She tried to defend the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
