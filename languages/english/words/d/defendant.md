# Defendant

## Meaning

Defendant is a person or institution against whom an action is brought in a court of law.

## Examples

- The defendant asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the defendant yesterday.
- The defendant joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
