# Defensive

## Meaning

Defensive describes someone or something that is intended or appropriate for defending against or deterring aggression or attack.

## Examples

- Defensive weapons.
- A defensive stance.
- It feels defensive.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
