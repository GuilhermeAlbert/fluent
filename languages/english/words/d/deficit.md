# Deficit

## Meaning

Deficit is the property of being an amount by which something is less than expected or required.

## Examples

- We talked about the deficit.
- The deficit matters here.
- I noticed the deficit right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
