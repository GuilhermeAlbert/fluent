# Degree

## Meaning

Degree is a position on a scale of intensity or amount or quality.

## Examples

- A moderate grade of intelligence.
- A high level of care is required.
- It is all a matter of degree.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
