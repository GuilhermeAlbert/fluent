# Delivery

## Meaning

Delivery is the action of delivering or distributing something.

## Examples

- His reluctant delivery of bad news.
- The delivery starts at nine.
- We planned the delivery together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
