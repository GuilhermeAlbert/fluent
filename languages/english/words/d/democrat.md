# Democrat

## Meaning

Democrat is an advocate of democratic principles.

## Examples

- The democrat asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the democrat yesterday.
- The democrat joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
