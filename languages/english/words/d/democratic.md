# Democratic

## Meaning

Democratic describes someone or something that is characterized by or advocating or based upon the principles of democracy or social equality.

## Examples

- Democratic government.
- A democratic country.
- A democratic scorn for bloated dukes and lords.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
