# Dependent

## Meaning

Dependent describes someone or something that is relying on or requiring a person or thing for support, supply, or what is needed.

## Examples

- Dependent children.
- Dependent on moisture.
- It feels dependent.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
