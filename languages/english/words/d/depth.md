# Depth

## Meaning

Depth is the extent downward or backward or inward.

## Examples

- The depth of the water.
- Depth of a shelf.
- Depth of a closet.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
