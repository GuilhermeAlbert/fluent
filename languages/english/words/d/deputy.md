# Deputy

## Meaning

Deputy means someone authorized to exercise the powers of sheriff in emergencies.

## Examples

- The deputy asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the deputy yesterday.
- The deputy joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
