# Desert

## Meaning

To desert means to leave someone who needs or counts on you.

## Examples

- The mother deserted her children.
- I need to desert the problem.
- They tried to desert what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
