# Design

## Meaning

To design means to make or work out a plan for.

## Examples

- Did he design his major works over a short period of time?
- They design the cape.
- They contrived to murder their boss.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
