# Designer

## Meaning

Designer means someone who specializes in interior design.

## Examples

- The designer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the designer yesterday.
- The designer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
