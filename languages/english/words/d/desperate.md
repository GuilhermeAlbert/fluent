# Desperate

## Meaning

Desperate describes someone or something that is arising from or marked by despair or loss of hope.

## Examples

- A despairing view of the world situation.
- The last despairing plea of the condemned criminal.
- A desperate cry for help.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
