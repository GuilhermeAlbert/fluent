# Despite

## Meaning

Despite means that something happens even though another thing could prevent it.

## Examples

- Despite the rain, we went out.
- She smiled despite the bad news.
- The project succeeded despite delays.

## Notes

Follow it with a noun phrase, not a full sentence.
