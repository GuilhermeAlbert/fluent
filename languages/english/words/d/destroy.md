# Destroy

## Meaning

To destroy means to do away with, cause the destruction or undoing of.

## Examples

- The fire destroyed the house.
- I need to destroy this today.
- They plan to destroy it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
