# Destruction

## Meaning

Destruction is the termination of something by causing so much damage to it that it cannot be repaired or no longer exists.

## Examples

- The destruction starts at nine.
- We planned the destruction together.
- That destruction changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
