# Detail

## Meaning

Detail is an isolated fact that is considered separately from the whole.

## Examples

- Several of the details are similar.
- A point of information.
- We talked about the detail.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
