# Detailed

## Meaning

Detailed describes someone or something that is developed or executed with care and in minute detail.

## Examples

- A detailed plan.
- The elaborate register of the inhabitants prevented tax evasion.
- The carefully elaborated theme.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
