# Development

## Meaning

Development means act of improving by expanding or enlarging or refining.

## Examples

- He congratulated them on their development of a plan to meet the emergency.
- They funded research and development.
- The development starts at nine.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
