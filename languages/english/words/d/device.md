# Device

## Meaning

Device is an instrumentality invented for a particular purpose.

## Examples

- The device is small enough to wear on your wrist.
- A device intended to conserve water.
- I left the device on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
