# Difference

## Meaning

Difference is the quality of being unlike or dissimilar.

## Examples

- There are many differences between jazz and rock.
- We talked about the difference.
- The difference matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
