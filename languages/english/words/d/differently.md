# Differently

## Meaning

Differently means doing something in a way that is in another and different manner.

## Examples

- Very soon you will know differently.
- She thought otherwise.
- There is no way out other than the fire escape.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
