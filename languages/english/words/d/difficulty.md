# Difficulty

## Meaning

Difficulty is an effort that is inconvenient.

## Examples

- I went to a lot of trouble.
- He won without any trouble.
- Had difficulty walking.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
