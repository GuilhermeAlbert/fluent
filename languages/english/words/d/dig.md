# Dig

## Meaning

To dig means to turn up, loosen, or remove earth.

## Examples

- Dig we must.
- Turn over the soil for aeration.
- I need to dig this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
