# Digital

## Meaning

Digital describes someone or something that is displaying numbers rather than scale positions.

## Examples

- Digital clock.
- Digital readout.
- It feels digital.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
