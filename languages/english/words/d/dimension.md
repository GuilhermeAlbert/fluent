# Dimension

## Meaning

Dimension is the magnitude of something in a particular direction.

## Examples

- We talked about the dimension.
- The dimension matters here.
- I noticed the dimension right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
