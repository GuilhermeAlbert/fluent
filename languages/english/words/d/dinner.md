# Dinner

## Meaning

Dinner is the main meal of the day served in the evening or at midday.

## Examples

- Dinner will be at 8.
- On Sundays they had a large dinner when they returned from church.
- I had dinner with breakfast.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
