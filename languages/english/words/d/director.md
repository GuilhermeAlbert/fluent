# Director

## Meaning

Director means someone who controls resources and expenditures.

## Examples

- The director asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the director yesterday.
- The director joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
