# Dirty

## Meaning

Dirty describes someone or something that is soiled or likely to soil with dirt or grime.

## Examples

- Dirty unswept sidewalks.
- A child in dirty overalls.
- Dirty slums.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
