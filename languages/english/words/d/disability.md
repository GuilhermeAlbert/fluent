# Disability

## Meaning

Disability is the condition of being unable to perform as a consequence of physical or mental unfitness.

## Examples

- Reading disability.
- Hearing impairment.
- We talked about the disability.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
