# Disagree

## Meaning

To disagree means to be of different opinions.

## Examples

- I beg to differ!
- She disagrees with her husband on many questions.
- I need to disagree this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
