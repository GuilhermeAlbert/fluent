# Disappear

## Meaning

To disappear means to get lost, as without warning or explanation.

## Examples

- These cars won't disappear.
- He disappeared without a trace.
- I need to disappear this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
