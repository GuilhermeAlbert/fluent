# Discussion

## Meaning

Discussion is an extended communication dealing with some particular topic.

## Examples

- The book contains an excellent discussion of modal logic.
- His treatment of the race question is badly biased.
- We talked about the discussion.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
