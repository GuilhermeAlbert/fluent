# Disease

## Meaning

Disease is an impairment of health or a condition of abnormal functioning.

## Examples

- We talked about the disease.
- The disease matters here.
- I noticed the disease right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
