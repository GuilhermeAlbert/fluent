# Dismiss

## Meaning

To dismiss means to bar from attention or consideration.

## Examples

- She dismissed his advances.
- I need to dismiss this clearly.
- She tried to dismiss the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
