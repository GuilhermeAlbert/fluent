# Distance

## Meaning

Distance is the property created by the space between two objects or points.

## Examples

- We talked about the distance.
- The distance matters here.
- I noticed the distance right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
