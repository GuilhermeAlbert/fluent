# Distant

## Meaning

Distant describes someone or something that is separated in space or coming from or going to a distance.

## Examples

- Distant villages.
- The sound of distant traffic.
- A distant sound.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
