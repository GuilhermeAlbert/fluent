# Distinction

## Meaning

Distinction is a discrimination between things as different and distinct on the basis of their characteristics or attributes.

## Examples

- It is necessary to make a distinction between love and infatuation.
- We talked about the distinction.
- The distinction matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
