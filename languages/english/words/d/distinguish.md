# Distinguish

## Meaning

To distinguish means to mark as different.

## Examples

- We distinguish several kinds of maple.
- I need to distinguish the problem.
- They tried to distinguish what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
