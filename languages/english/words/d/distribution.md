# Distribution

## Meaning

Distribution is an arrangement of values of a variable showing their observed or theoretical frequency of occurrence.

## Examples

- We talked about the distribution.
- The distribution matters here.
- I noticed the distribution right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
