# Diverse

## Meaning

Diverse describes someone or something that is many and different.

## Examples

- Tourist offices of divers nationalities.
- A person of diverse talents.
- It feels diverse.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
