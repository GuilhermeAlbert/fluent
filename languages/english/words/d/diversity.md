# Diversity

## Meaning

Diversity means noticeable heterogeneity.

## Examples

- A diversity of possibilities.
- The range and variety of his work is amazing.
- We talked about the diversity.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
