# Domestic

## Meaning

Domestic describes someone or something that is of concern to or about the internal affairs of a nation.

## Examples

- Domestic issues such as tax rate and highway construction.
- It feels domestic.
- That was a domestic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
