# Dominant

## Meaning

Dominant describes someone or something that is exercising influence or control.

## Examples

- Television plays a dominant role in molding public opinion.
- The dominant partner in the marriage.
- It feels dominant.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
