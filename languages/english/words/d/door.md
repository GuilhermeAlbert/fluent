# Door

## Meaning

Door is a swinging or sliding barrier that will close the entrance to a room or building or vehicle.

## Examples

- He knocked on the door.
- He slammed the door as he left.
- I left the door on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
