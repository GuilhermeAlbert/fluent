# Downtown

## Meaning

Downtown is the central area or commercial center of a town or city.

## Examples

- The heart of Birmingham's downtown.
- We met near the downtown.
- The downtown was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
