# Dozen

## Meaning

Dozen is the cardinal number that is the sum of eleven and one.

## Examples

- We talked about the dozen.
- The dozen matters here.
- I noticed the dozen right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
