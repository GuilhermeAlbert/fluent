# Draft

## Meaning

To draft means to draw up an outline or sketch for something.

## Examples

- Did he draft his major works over a short period of time?
- Draft a speech.
- I need to draft this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
