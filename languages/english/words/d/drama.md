# Drama

## Meaning

Drama is a dramatic work intended for performance by actors on a stage.

## Examples

- He wrote several plays but only one was produced on Broadway.
- We talked about the drama.
- The drama matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
