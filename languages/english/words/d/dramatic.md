# Dramatic

## Meaning

Dramatic describes someone or something that is suitable to or characteristic of drama.

## Examples

- A dramatic entrance in a swirling cape.
- A dramatic rescue at sea.
- It feels dramatic.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
