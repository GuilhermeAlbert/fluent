# Dramatically

## Meaning

Dramatically means doing something in a way that is in a very impressive manner.

## Examples

- Your performance will improve dramatically.
- She answered dramatically.
- It happened dramatically.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
