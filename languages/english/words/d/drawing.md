# Drawing

## Meaning

Drawing is an illustration that is drawn by hand and published in a book, magazine, or newspaper.

## Examples

- It is shown by the drawing in Fig. 7.
- We talked about the drawing.
- The drawing matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
