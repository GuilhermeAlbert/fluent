# Drive

## Meaning

To drive means to operate or control a vehicle.

## Examples

- They drive the car down the avenue.
- Drive a car or bus.
- Can you drive this four-wheel truck?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
