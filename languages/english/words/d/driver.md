# Driver

## Meaning

Driver is the operator of a motor vehicle.

## Examples

- The driver asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the driver yesterday.
- The driver joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
