# Drop

## Meaning

To drop means to let fall to the ground.

## Examples

- The girls drop the wooden sticks.
- Don't drop the dishes.
- We need to drop soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
