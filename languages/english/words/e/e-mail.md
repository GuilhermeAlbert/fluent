# E-Mail

## Meaning

E-Mail is a system of world-wide electronic communication in which a computer user can compose a message at one terminal that can be regenerated at the recipient's terminal when the recipient logs in.

## Examples

- You cannot send packages by electronic mail.
- We talked about the e-mail.
- The e-mail matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
