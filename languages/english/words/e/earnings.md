# Earnings

## Meaning

Earnings is the excess of revenues over outlays in a given period of time.

## Examples

- We talked about the earnings.
- The earnings matters here.
- I noticed the earnings right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
