# Earth

## Meaning

Earth is the loose soft material that makes up a large part of the land surface.

## Examples

- They dug into the earth outside the church.
- We talked about the earth.
- The earth matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
