# Eastern

## Meaning

Eastern describes someone or something that is lying toward or situated in the east.

## Examples

- The eastern end of the island.
- It feels eastern.
- That was an eastern choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
