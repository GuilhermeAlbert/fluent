# Economic

## Meaning

Economic describes something connected with an economy, the system of production and management of material wealth.

## Examples

- Economic growth.
- Aspects of social, political, and economical life.
- It feels economic.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
