# Economist

## Meaning

Economist is an expert in the science of economics.

## Examples

- The economist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the economist yesterday.
- The economist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
