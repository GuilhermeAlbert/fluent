# Education

## Meaning

Education is the activities of educating or instructing.

## Examples

- He received no formal education.
- Our instruction was carefully programmed.
- Good classroom teaching is seldom rewarded.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
