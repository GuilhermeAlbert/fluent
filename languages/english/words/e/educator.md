# Educator

## Meaning

Educator means someone who educates young people.

## Examples

- The educator asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the educator yesterday.
- The educator joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
