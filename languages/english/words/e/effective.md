# Effective

## Meaning

Effective describes someone or something that is producing or capable of producing an intended result or having a striking effect.

## Examples

- An air-cooled motor was more effective than a witch's broomstick for rapid long-distance transportation.
- Effective teaching methods.
- Effective steps toward peace.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
