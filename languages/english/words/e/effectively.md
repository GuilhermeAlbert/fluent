# Effectively

## Meaning

Effectively means doing something in a way that is in an effective manner.

## Examples

- These are real problems that can be dealt with most effectively by rational discussion.
- She answered effectively.
- It happened effectively.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
