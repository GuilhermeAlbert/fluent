# Efficient

## Meaning

Efficient describes someone or something that is being effective without wasting time or effort or expense.

## Examples

- An efficient production manager.
- Efficient engines save gas.
- It feels efficient.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
