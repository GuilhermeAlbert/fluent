# Egg

## Meaning

Egg means animal reproductive body consisting of an ovum or embryo together with nutritive and protective envelopes.

## Examples

- The egg moved across the road.
- We saw a egg near the house.
- The egg looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
