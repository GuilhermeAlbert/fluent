# Either

## Meaning

Either means doing something in a way that is after a negative statement used as an intensive meaning something like "likewise" or "also".

## Examples

- He isn't stupid, but he isn't exactly a genius either.
- I don't know either.
- If you don't order dessert I won't either.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
