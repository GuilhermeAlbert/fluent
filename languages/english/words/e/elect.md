# Elect

## Meaning

To elect means to select by a vote for an office or membership.

## Examples

- They elect him as chairman.
- We elected him chairman of the board.
- I need to elect soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
