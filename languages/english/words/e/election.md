# Election

## Meaning

Election is a vote to select the winner of a position or political office.

## Examples

- The results of the election will be announced tonight.
- The election starts at nine.
- We planned the election together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
