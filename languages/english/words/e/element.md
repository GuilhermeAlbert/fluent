# Element

## Meaning

Element is an abstract part of something.

## Examples

- Jealousy was a component of his character.
- Two constituents of a musical composition are melody and harmony.
- The grammatical elements of a sentence.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
