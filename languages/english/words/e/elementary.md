# Elementary

## Meaning

Elementary describes someone or something that is easy and not involved or complicated.

## Examples

- An elementary problem in statistics.
- Elementary, my dear Watson.
- A simple game.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
