# Elsewhere

## Meaning

Elsewhere means doing something in a way that is in or to another place.

## Examples

- He went elsewhere.
- Look elsewhere for the answer.
- She answered elsewhere.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
