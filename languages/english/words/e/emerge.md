# Emerge

## Meaning

To emerge means to come out into view, as from concealment.

## Examples

- Suddenly, the proprietor emerged from his office.
- I need to emerge this today.
- They plan to emerge it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
