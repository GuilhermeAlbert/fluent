# Emergency

## Meaning

Emergency is a sudden unforeseen crisis that requires immediate action.

## Examples

- He never knew what to do in an emergency.
- The emergency starts at nine.
- We planned the emergency together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
