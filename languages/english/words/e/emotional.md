# Emotional

## Meaning

Emotional describes someone or something that is determined or actuated by emotion rather than reason.

## Examples

- It was an emotional judgment.
- It feels emotional.
- That was an emotional choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
