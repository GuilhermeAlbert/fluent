# Emphasize

## Meaning

To emphasize means to stress, single out as important.

## Examples

- Dr. Jones emphasizes exercise in addition to a change in diet.
- I need to emphasize this clearly.
- She tried to emphasize the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
