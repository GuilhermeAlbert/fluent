# Employee

## Meaning

Employee is a worker who is hired to perform a job.

## Examples

- The employee asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the employee yesterday.
- The employee joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
