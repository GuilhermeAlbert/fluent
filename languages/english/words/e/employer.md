# Employer

## Meaning

Employer is a person or firm that employs workers.

## Examples

- The employer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the employer yesterday.
- The employer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
