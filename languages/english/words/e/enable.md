# Enable

## Meaning

To enable means to render capable or able for some task.

## Examples

- This skill will enable you to find a job on Wall Street.
- The rope enables you to secure yourself when you climb the mountain.
- I need to enable this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
