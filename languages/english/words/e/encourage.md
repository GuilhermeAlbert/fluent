# Encourage

## Meaning

To encourage means to contribute to the progress or growth of.

## Examples

- They encourage him to write the letter.
- I am promoting the use of computers in the classroom.
- I need to encourage soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
