# Energy

## Meaning

Energy is a thermodynamic quantity equivalent to the capacity of a physical system to do work.

## Examples

- Energy can take a wide variety of forms.
- I need more energy before I decide.
- We talked about energy after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
