# Engineer

## Meaning

Engineer means someone who uses scientific knowledge to solve practical problems.

## Examples

- The engineer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the engineer yesterday.
- The engineer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
