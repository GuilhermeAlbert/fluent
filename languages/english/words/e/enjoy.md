# Enjoy

## Meaning

To enjoy means to derive or receive pleasure from.

## Examples

- She relished her fame and basked in her glory.
- I need to enjoy soon.
- They want to enjoy together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
