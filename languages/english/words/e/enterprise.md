# Enterprise

## Meaning

Enterprise is a purposeful or industrious undertaking.

## Examples

- He had doubts about the whole enterprise.
- The enterprise starts at nine.
- We planned the enterprise together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
