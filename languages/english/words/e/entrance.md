# Entrance

## Meaning

Entrance means something that provides access.

## Examples

- They waited at the entrance to the garden.
- Beggars waited just outside the entryway to the cathedral.
- I left the entrance on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
