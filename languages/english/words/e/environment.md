# Environment

## Meaning

Environment is the totality of surrounding conditions.

## Examples

- He longed for the comfortable environment of his living room.
- We talked about the environment.
- The environment matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
