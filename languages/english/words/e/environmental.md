# Environmental

## Meaning

Environmental describes something connected with the external conditions or surroundings.

## Examples

- Environmental factors.
- It feels environmental.
- That was an environmental choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
