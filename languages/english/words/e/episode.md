# Episode

## Meaning

Episode is a happening that is distinctive in a series of related events.

## Examples

- The episode starts at nine.
- We planned the episode together.
- That episode changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
