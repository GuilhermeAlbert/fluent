# Equal

## Meaning

Equal describes someone or something that is having the same quantity, value, or measure as another.

## Examples

- On equal terms.
- All men are equal before the law.
- It feels equal.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
