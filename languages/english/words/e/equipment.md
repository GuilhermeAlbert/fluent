# Equipment

## Meaning

Equipment is an instrumentality needed for an undertaking or to perform a service.

## Examples

- I need more equipment before I decide.
- We talked about equipment after lunch.
- That equipment really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
