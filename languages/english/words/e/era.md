# Era

## Meaning

Era is a period marked by distinctive character or reckoned from a fixed point or event.

## Examples

- The era went by quickly.
- We chose a better era.
- That era worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
