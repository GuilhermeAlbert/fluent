# Estimate

## Meaning

To estimate means to judge tentatively or form an estimate of.

## Examples

- I estimate this chicken to weigh three pounds.
- I need to estimate the problem.
- They tried to estimate what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
