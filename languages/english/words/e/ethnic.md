# Ethnic

## Meaning

Ethnic describes someone or something that is denoting or deriving from or distinctive of the ways of living built up by a group of people.

## Examples

- Influenced by ethnic and cultural ties.
- Ethnic food.
- It feels ethnic.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
