# European

## Meaning

European is a native or inhabitant of Europe.

## Examples

- The european asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the european yesterday.
- The european joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
