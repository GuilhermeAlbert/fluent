# Eventually

## Meaning

Eventually means doing something in a way that is after an unspecified period of time or an especially long delay.

## Examples

- She answered eventually.
- It happened eventually.
- We finished eventually.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
