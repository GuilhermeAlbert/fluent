# Every

## Meaning

Every describes someone or something that is each and all of the members of a group considered singly and without exception.

## Examples

- Every person is mortal.
- Every party is welcome.
- Had every hope of success.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
