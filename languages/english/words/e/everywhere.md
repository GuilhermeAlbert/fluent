# Everywhere

## Meaning

Everywhere means doing something in a way that is to or in any or all places.

## Examples

- You find fast food stores everywhere.
- People everywhere are becoming aware of the problem.
- He carried a gun everywhere he went.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
