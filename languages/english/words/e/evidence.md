# Evidence

## Meaning

Evidence means your basis for belief or disbelief.

## Examples

- The evidence that smoking causes lung cancer is very compelling.
- I need more evidence before I decide.
- We talked about evidence after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
