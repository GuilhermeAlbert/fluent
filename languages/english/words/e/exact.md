# Exact

## Meaning

Exact describes someone or something that is marked by strict and particular and complete accordance with fact.

## Examples

- An exact mind.
- An exact copy.
- Hit the exact center of the target.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
