# Example

## Meaning

Example is an item of information that is typical of a class or group.

## Examples

- This patient provides a typical example of the syndrome.
- There is an example on page 10.
- We talked about the example.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
