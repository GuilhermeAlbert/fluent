# Exceed

## Meaning

To exceed means to be greater in scope or size than some standard.

## Examples

- Their loyalty exceeds their national bonds.
- I need to exceed soon.
- They want to exceed together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
