# Excellent

## Meaning

Excellent describes someone or something that is very good.

## Examples

- Made an excellent speech.
- He was a splendid teacher.
- A first-class mind.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
