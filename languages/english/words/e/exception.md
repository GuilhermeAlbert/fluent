# Exception

## Meaning

Exception is a deliberate act of omission.

## Examples

- With the exception of the children, everyone was told the news.
- We talked about the exception.
- The exception matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
