# Exciting

## Meaning

Exciting describes someone or something that is creating or arousing excitement.

## Examples

- An exciting account of her trip.
- It feels exciting.
- That was an exciting choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
