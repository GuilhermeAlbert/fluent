# Executive

## Meaning

Executive describes someone or something that is having the function of carrying out plans or orders etc.

## Examples

- The executive branch.
- It feels executive.
- That was an executive choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
