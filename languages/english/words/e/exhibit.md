# Exhibit

## Meaning

To exhibit means to show an attribute, property, knowledge, or skill.

## Examples

- He exhibits a great talent.
- I need to exhibit soon.
- They want to exhibit together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
