# Expect

## Meaning

To expect means to regard something as probable or likely.

## Examples

- They expect to move.
- The meteorologists are expecting rain for tomorrow.
- I need to expect the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
