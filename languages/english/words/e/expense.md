# Expense

## Meaning

Expense means amounts paid for goods and services that may be currently tax deductible.

## Examples

- We talked about the expense.
- The expense matters here.
- I noticed the expense right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
