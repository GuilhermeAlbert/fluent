# Expensive

## Meaning

Expensive describes someone or something that is high in price or charging high prices.

## Examples

- Expensive clothes.
- An expensive shop.
- It feels expensive.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
