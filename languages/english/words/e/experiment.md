# Experiment

## Meaning

To experiment means to conduct a test or investigation.

## Examples

- We are experimenting with the new drug in order to fight this disease.
- I need to experiment soon.
- They want to experiment together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
