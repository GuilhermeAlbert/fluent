# Expert

## Meaning

Expert describes someone or something that is having or showing knowledge and skill and aptitude.

## Examples

- Adept in handicrafts.
- An adept juggler.
- An expert job.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
