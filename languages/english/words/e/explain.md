# Explain

## Meaning

To explain means to make plain and comprehensible.

## Examples

- He explained the laws of physics to his students.
- I need to explain this clearly.
- She tried to explain the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
