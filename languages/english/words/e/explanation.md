# Explanation

## Meaning

Explanation is a statement that makes something comprehensible by describing the relevant structure or operation or circumstances etc.

## Examples

- The explanation was very simple.
- I expected a brief account.
- We talked about the explanation.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
