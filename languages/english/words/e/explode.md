# Explode

## Meaning

To explode means to cause to burst with a violent release of energy.

## Examples

- We exploded the nuclear bomb.
- I need to explode this today.
- They plan to explode it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
