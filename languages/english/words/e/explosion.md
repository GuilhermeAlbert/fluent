# Explosion

## Meaning

Explosion is a violent release of energy caused by a chemical or nuclear reaction.

## Examples

- The explosion starts at nine.
- We planned the explosion together.
- That explosion changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
