# Extension

## Meaning

Extension is a mutually agreed delay in the date set for the completion of a job or payment of a debt.

## Examples

- They applied for an extension of the loan.
- The extension went by quickly.
- We chose a better extension.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
