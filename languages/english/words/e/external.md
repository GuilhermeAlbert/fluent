# External

## Meaning

External describes someone or something that is happening or arising or located outside or beyond some limits or especially surface.

## Examples

- The external auditory canal.
- External pressures.
- It feels external.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
