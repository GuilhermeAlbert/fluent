# Extreme

## Meaning

Extreme describes someone or something that is of the greatest possible degree or extent or intensity.

## Examples

- Extreme cold.
- Extreme caution.
- Extreme pleasure.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
