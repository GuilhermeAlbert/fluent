# Fabric

## Meaning

Fabric means artifact made by weaving or felting or knitting or crocheting natural or synthetic fibers.

## Examples

- The fabric in the curtains was light and semitransparent.
- Woven cloth originated in Mesopotamia around 5000 BC.
- She measured off enough material for a dress.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
