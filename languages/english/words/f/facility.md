# Facility

## Meaning

Facility is a building or place that provides a particular service or is used for a particular industry.

## Examples

- The assembly plant is an enormous facility.
- I left the facility on the table.
- She picked up the facility carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
