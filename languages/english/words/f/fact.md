# Fact

## Meaning

Fact is a piece of information about circumstances that exist or events that have occurred.

## Examples

- First you must collect all the facts of the case.
- We talked about the fact.
- The fact matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
