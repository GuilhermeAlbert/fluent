# Factor

## Meaning

Factor means anything that contributes causally to a result.

## Examples

- A number of factors determined the outcome.
- The factor starts at nine.
- We planned the factor together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
