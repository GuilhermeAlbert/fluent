# Faculty

## Meaning

Faculty means one of the inherent cognitive or perceptual powers of the mind.

## Examples

- We talked about the faculty.
- The faculty matters here.
- I noticed the faculty right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
