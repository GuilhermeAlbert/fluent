# Fair

## Meaning

Fair describes someone or something that is free from favoritism or self-interest or bias or deception.

## Examples

- A fair referee.
- Fair deal.
- On a fair footing.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
