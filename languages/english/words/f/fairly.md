# Fairly

## Meaning

Fairly means doing something in a way that is to certain extent or degree.

## Examples

- Pretty big.
- Pretty bad.
- Jolly decent of him.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
