# Faith

## Meaning

Faith is a strong belief in a supernatural power or powers that control human destiny.

## Examples

- He lost his faith but not his morality.
- I need more faith before I decide.
- We talked about faith after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
