# False

## Meaning

False describes someone or something that is not in accordance with the fact or reality or actuality.

## Examples

- Gave false testimony under oath.
- False tales of bravery.
- It feels false.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
