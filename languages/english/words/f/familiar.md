# Familiar

## Meaning

Familiar describes someone or something that is well known or easily recognized.

## Examples

- A familiar figure.
- Familiar songs.
- Familiar guests.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
