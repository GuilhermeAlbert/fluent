# Fantasy

## Meaning

Fantasy means imagination unrestricted by reality.

## Examples

- A schoolgirl fantasy.
- We talked about the fantasy.
- The fantasy matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
