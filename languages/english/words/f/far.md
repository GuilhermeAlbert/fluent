# Far

## Meaning

Far means doing something in a way that is to a considerable degree.

## Examples

- A far far better thing that I do.
- Felt far worse than yesterday.
- Eyes far too close together.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
