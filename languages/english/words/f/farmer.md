# Farmer

## Meaning

Farmer means someone who operates a farm.

## Examples

- The farmer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the farmer yesterday.
- The farmer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
