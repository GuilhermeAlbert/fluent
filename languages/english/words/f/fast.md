# Fast

## Meaning

Fast describes someone or something that is acting or moving or capable of acting or moving quickly.

## Examples

- Fast film.
- On the fast track in school.
- Set a fast pace.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
