# Fat

## Meaning

Fat describes someone or something that is having an abundance of flesh.

## Examples

- He hadn't remembered how fat she was.
- It feels fat.
- That was a fat choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
