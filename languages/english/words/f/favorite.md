# Favorite

## Meaning

Favorite means something regarded with special favor or liking.

## Examples

- That book is one of my favorites.
- We talked about the favorite.
- The favorite matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
