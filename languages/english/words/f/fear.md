# Fear

## Meaning

To fear means to be afraid or feel anxious or apprehensive about a possible or probable situation or event.

## Examples

- They fear that there was a traffic accident.
- I fear she might get aggressive.
- I need to fear soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
