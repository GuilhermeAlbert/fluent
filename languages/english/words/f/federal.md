# Federal

## Meaning

Federal describes something connected with the central government of a federation.

## Examples

- A federal district is one set aside as the seat of the national government.
- It feels federal.
- That was a federal choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
