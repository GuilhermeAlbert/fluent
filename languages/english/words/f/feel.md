# Feel

## Meaning

To feel means to undergo an emotional sensation or be in a particular state of mind.

## Examples

- John will feel angry.
- She felt resentful.
- He felt regret.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
