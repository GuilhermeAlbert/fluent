# Female

## Meaning

Female describes someone or something that is being the sex that produces fertilizable gametes from which offspring develop.

## Examples

- A female heir.
- Female holly trees bear the berries.
- It feels female.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
