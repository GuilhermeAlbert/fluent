# Fence

## Meaning

Fence is a barrier that serves to enclose an area.

## Examples

- I left the fence on the table.
- She picked up the fence carefully.
- The fence is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
