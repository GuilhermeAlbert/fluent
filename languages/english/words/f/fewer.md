# Fewer

## Meaning

Fewer describes someone or something that is quantifier meaning a smaller number of.

## Examples

- Fewer birds came this year.
- The birds are fewer this year.
- Fewer trains were late.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
