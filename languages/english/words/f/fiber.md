# Fiber

## Meaning

Fiber is a slender and greatly elongated substance capable of being spun into yarn.

## Examples

- We talked about the fiber.
- The fiber matters here.
- I noticed the fiber right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
