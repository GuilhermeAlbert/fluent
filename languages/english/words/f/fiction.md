# Fiction

## Meaning

Fiction is a literary work based on the imagination and not necessarily on fact.

## Examples

- I need more fiction before I decide.
- We talked about fiction after lunch.
- That fiction really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
