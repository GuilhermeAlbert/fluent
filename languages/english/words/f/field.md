# Field

## Meaning

Field is a piece of land cleared of trees and usually enclosed.

## Examples

- He planted a field of wheat.
- We met near the field.
- The field was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
