# Fill

## Meaning

To fill means to make full, also in a metaphorical sense.

## Examples

- They fill the cart with boxes.
- Fill a container.
- Fill the child with pride.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
