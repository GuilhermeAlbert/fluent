# Finally

## Meaning

Finally means doing something in a way that is after an unspecified period of time or an especially long delay.

## Examples

- She answered finally.
- It happened finally.
- We finished finally.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
