# Financial

## Meaning

Financial describes someone or something that is involving financial matters.

## Examples

- Fiscal responsibility.
- It feels financial.
- That was a financial choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
