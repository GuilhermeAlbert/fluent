# Firm

## Meaning

Firm describes someone or something that is marked by firm determination or resolution.

## Examples

- Firm convictions.
- A firm mouth.
- Steadfast resolve.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
