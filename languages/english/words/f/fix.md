# Fix

## Meaning

To fix means to restore by replacing a part or putting together what is torn or broken.

## Examples

- The men fix the chairs.
- She repaired her TV set.
- Repair my shoes please.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
