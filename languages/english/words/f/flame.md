# Flame

## Meaning

To flame means to shine with a sudden light.

## Examples

- Lights flame on the horizon.
- The horizon is flameing with lights.
- The night sky flared with the massive bombardment.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
