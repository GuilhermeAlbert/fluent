# Flat

## Meaning

Flat describes someone or something that is having a surface without slope, tilt in which no part is higher or lower than another.

## Examples

- A flat desk.
- Acres of level farmland.
- A plane surface.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
