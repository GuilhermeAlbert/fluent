# Floor

## Meaning

Floor is the inside lower horizontal surface.

## Examples

- They needed rugs to cover the bare floors.
- We spread our sleeping bags on the dry floor of the tent.
- I left the floor on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
