# Flow

## Meaning

To flow means to move or progress freely as if in a stream.

## Examples

- The crowd flowed out of the stadium.
- We need to flow soon.
- They started to flow after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
