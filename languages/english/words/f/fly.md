# Fly

## Meaning

To fly means to travel through the air.

## Examples

- Some big birds fly in the tree.
- There fly some big birds in the tree.
- Man cannot fly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
