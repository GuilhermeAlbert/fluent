# Focus

## Meaning

To focus means to direct one's attention on something.

## Examples

- Please focus on your studies and not on your hobbies.
- I need to focus the problem.
- They tried to focus what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
