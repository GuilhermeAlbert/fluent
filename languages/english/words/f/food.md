# Food

## Meaning

Food means any substance that can be metabolized by an animal to give energy and build tissue.

## Examples

- I need more food before I decide.
- We talked about food after lunch.
- That food really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
