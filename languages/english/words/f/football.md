# Football

## Meaning

Football means any of various games played with a ball in which two teams try to kick or carry or propel the ball into each other's goal.

## Examples

- The football starts at nine.
- We planned the football together.
- That football changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
