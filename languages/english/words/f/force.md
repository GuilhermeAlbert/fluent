# Force

## Meaning

To force means to cause to do through pressure or necessity, by physical, moral or intellectual means.

## Examples

- They force him to write the letter.
- She forced him to take a job in the city.
- He squeezed her for information.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
