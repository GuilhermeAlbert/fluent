# Foreign

## Meaning

Foreign describes someone or something that is of concern to or about the affairs of other nations.

## Examples

- Foreign trade.
- A foreign office.
- It feels foreign.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
