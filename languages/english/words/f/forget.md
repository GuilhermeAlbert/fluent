# Forget

## Meaning

To forget means to dismiss from the mind.

## Examples

- They won't forget the story.
- I tried to bury these unpleasant memories.
- I need to forget the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
