# Formal

## Meaning

Formal describes someone or something that is being in accord with established forms and conventions and requirements.

## Examples

- Pay one's formal respects.
- Formal dress.
- A formal ball.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
