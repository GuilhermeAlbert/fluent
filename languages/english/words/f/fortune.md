# Fortune

## Meaning

Fortune is an unknown and unpredictable phenomenon that causes an event to result one way rather than another.

## Examples

- Bad luck caused his downfall.
- We ran into each other by pure chance.
- We talked about the fortune.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
