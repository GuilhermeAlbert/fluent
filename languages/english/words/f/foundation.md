# Foundation

## Meaning

Foundation is the basis on which something is grounded.

## Examples

- There is little foundation for his objections.
- We talked about the foundation.
- The foundation matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
