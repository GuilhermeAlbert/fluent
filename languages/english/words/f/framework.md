# Framework

## Meaning

Framework is a hypothetical description of a complex entity or process.

## Examples

- The computer program was based on a model of the circulatory and respiratory systems.
- We talked about the framework.
- The framework matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
