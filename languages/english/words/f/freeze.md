# Freeze

## Meaning

To freeze means to stop moving or become immobilized.

## Examples

- When he saw the police car he froze.
- We need to freeze soon.
- They started to freeze after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
