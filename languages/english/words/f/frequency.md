# Frequency

## Meaning

Frequency is the number of occurrences within a given time period.

## Examples

- The frequency of modulation was 40 cycles per second.
- The frequency of his seizures increased as he grew older.
- The frequency went by quickly.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
