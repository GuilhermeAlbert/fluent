# Frequent

## Meaning

Frequent describes someone or something that is coming at short intervals or habitually.

## Examples

- A frequent guest.
- Frequent complaints.
- It feels frequent.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
