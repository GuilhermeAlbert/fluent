# Fresh

## Meaning

Fresh describes someone or something that is recently made, produced, or harvested.

## Examples

- Fresh bread.
- A fresh scent.
- Fresh lettuce.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
