# Friend

## Meaning

Friend is a person you know well and regard with affection and trust.

## Examples

- He was my best friend at the university.
- The friend asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the friend yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
