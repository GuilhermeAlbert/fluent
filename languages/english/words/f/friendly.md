# Friendly

## Meaning

Friendly describes someone or something that is characteristic of or befitting a friend.

## Examples

- Friendly advice.
- A friendly neighborhood.
- The only friendly person here.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
