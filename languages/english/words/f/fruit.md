# Fruit

## Meaning

Fruit is the ripened reproductive body of a seed plant.

## Examples

- I need more fruit before I decide.
- We talked about fruit after lunch.
- That fruit really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
