# Frustration

## Meaning

Frustration is the feeling that accompanies an experience of being thwarted in attaining your goals.

## Examples

- We talked about the frustration.
- The frustration matters here.
- I noticed the frustration right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
