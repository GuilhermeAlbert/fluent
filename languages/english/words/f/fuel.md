# Fuel

## Meaning

To fuel means to provide with a combustible substance that provides energy.

## Examples

- Fuel aircraft, ships, and cars.
- I need to fuel this today.
- They plan to fuel it together.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
