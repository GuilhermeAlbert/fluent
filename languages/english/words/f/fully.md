# Fully

## Meaning

Fully means doing something in a way that is to the greatest degree or extent.

## Examples

- Fully grown.
- He didn't fully understand.
- Knew full well.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
