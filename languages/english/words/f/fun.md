# Fun

## Meaning

Fun means activities that are enjoyable or amusing.

## Examples

- I do it for the fun of it.
- He is fun to have around.
- I need more fun before I decide.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
