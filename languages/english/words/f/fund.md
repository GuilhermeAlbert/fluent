# Fund

## Meaning

To fund means to convert into long-term debt that bears fixed interest and is represented by bonds.

## Examples

- I need to fund this today.
- They plan to fund it together.
- Can you fund this for me?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
