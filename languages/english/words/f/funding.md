# Funding

## Meaning

Funding means financial resources provided to make some project possible.

## Examples

- The foundation provided support for the experiment.
- We talked about the funding.
- The funding matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
