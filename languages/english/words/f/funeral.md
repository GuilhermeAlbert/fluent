# Funeral

## Meaning

Funeral is a ceremony at which a dead person is buried or cremated.

## Examples

- Hundreds of people attended his funeral.
- The funeral starts at nine.
- We planned the funeral together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
