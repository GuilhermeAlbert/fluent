# Funny

## Meaning

Funny describes someone or something that is arousing or provoking laughter.

## Examples

- An amusing film with a steady stream of pranks and pratfalls.
- An amusing fellow.
- A comic hat.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
