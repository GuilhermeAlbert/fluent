# Furniture

## Meaning

Furniture means furnishings that make a room or other area ready for occupancy.

## Examples

- They had too much furniture for the small apartment.
- There was only one piece of furniture in the room.
- I need more furniture before I decide.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
