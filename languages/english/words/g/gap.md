# Gap

## Meaning

Gap is a conspicuous disparity or difference as between two figures.

## Examples

- Gap between income and outgo.
- The spread between lending and borrowing costs.
- We talked about the gap.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
