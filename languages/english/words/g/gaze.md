# Gaze

## Meaning

To gaze means to look at with fixed eyes.

## Examples

- They gaze up the hill.
- The students stared at the teacher with amazement.
- I need to gaze soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
