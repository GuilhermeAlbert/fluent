# Gear

## Meaning

Gear is a toothed wheel that engages another toothed mechanism in order to change the speed or direction of transmitted motion.

## Examples

- I left the gear on the table.
- She picked up the gear carefully.
- The gear is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
