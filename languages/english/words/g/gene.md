# Gene

## Meaning

Gene is a segment of DNA that is involved in producing a polypeptide chain.

## Examples

- Genes were formerly called factors.
- My gene hurts a little.
- She touched her gene.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
