# Generally

## Meaning

Generally means doing something in a way that is usually.

## Examples

- By and large it doesn't rain much here.
- She answered generally.
- It happened generally.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
