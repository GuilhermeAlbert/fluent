# Genetic

## Meaning

Genetic describes someone or something that is occurring among members of a family usually by heredity.

## Examples

- An inherited disease.
- Familial traits.
- Genetically transmitted features.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
