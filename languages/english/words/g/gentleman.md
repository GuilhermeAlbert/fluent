# Gentleman

## Meaning

Gentleman is a man of refinement.

## Examples

- The gentleman asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the gentleman yesterday.
- The gentleman joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
