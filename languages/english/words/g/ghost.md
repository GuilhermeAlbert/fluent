# Ghost

## Meaning

Ghost is a mental representation of some haunting experience.

## Examples

- He looked like he had seen a ghost.
- It aroused specters from his past.
- We talked about the ghost.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
