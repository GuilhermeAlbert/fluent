# Gifted

## Meaning

Gifted describes someone or something that is endowed with talent or talents.

## Examples

- A gifted writer.
- It feels gifted.
- That was a gifted choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
