# Girlfriend

## Meaning

Girlfriend means any female friend.

## Examples

- Mary and her girlfriend organized the party.
- The girlfriend asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the girlfriend yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
