# Give

## Meaning

To give means to cause to have, in the abstract sense or physical sense.

## Examples

- They give the food to the people.
- They give the people the food.
- She gave him a black eye.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
