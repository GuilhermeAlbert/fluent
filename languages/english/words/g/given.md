# Given

## Meaning

Given describes someone or something that is acknowledged as a supposition.

## Examples

- Given the engine's condition, it is a wonder that it started.
- It feels given.
- That was a given choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
