# Glad

## Meaning

Glad describes someone or something that is showing or causing joy and pleasure.

## Examples

- Glad you are here.
- Glad that they succeeded.
- Gave a glad shout.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
