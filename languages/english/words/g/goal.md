# Goal

## Meaning

Goal is the state of affairs that a plan is intended to achieve and that ends behavior intended to achieve it.

## Examples

- The ends justify the means.
- We talked about the goal.
- The goal matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
