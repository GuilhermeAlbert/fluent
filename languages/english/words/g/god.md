# God

## Meaning

God means any supernatural being worshipped as controlling some part of the world or some aspect of life or who is the personification of a force.

## Examples

- The god asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the god yesterday.
- The god joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
