# Golden

## Meaning

Golden describes someone or something that is having the deep slightly brownish color of gold.

## Examples

- Long aureate (or golden) hair.
- A gold carpet.
- It feels golden.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
