# Governor

## Meaning

Governor is the head of a state government.

## Examples

- The governor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the governor yesterday.
- The governor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
