# Grab

## Meaning

To grab means to take hold of so as to seize or restrain or stop the motion of.

## Examples

- Catch the ball!
- Grab the elevator door!
- I need to grab this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
