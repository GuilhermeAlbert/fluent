# Gradually

## Meaning

Gradually means doing something in a way that is in a gradual manner.

## Examples

- The snake moved gradually toward its victim.
- She answered gradually.
- It happened gradually.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
