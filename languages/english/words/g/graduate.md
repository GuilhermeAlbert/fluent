# Graduate

## Meaning

To graduate means to receive an academic degree upon completion of one's studies.

## Examples

- She graduated in 1990.
- I need to graduate this today.
- They plan to graduate it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
