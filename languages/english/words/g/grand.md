# Grand

## Meaning

Grand describes someone or something that is of behavior that is impressive and ambitious in scale or scope.

## Examples

- An expansive lifestyle.
- In the grand manner.
- Collecting on a grand scale.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
