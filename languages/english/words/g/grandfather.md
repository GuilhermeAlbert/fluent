# Grandfather

## Meaning

Grandfather is the father of your father or mother.

## Examples

- The grandfather asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the grandfather yesterday.
- The grandfather joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
