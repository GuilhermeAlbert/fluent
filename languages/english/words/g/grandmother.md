# Grandmother

## Meaning

Grandmother is the mother of your father or mother.

## Examples

- The grandmother asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the grandmother yesterday.
- The grandmother joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
