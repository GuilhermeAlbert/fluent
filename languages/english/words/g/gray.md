# Gray

## Meaning

Gray describes someone or something that is of an achromatic color of any lightness intermediate between the extremes of white and black.

## Examples

- The little grey cells.
- Gray flannel suit.
- A man with greyish hair.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
