# Greatest

## Meaning

Greatest describes someone or something that is highest in quality.

## Examples

- It feels greatest.
- That was a greatest choice.
- The room looked greatest.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
