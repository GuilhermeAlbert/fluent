# Grow

## Meaning

To grow means to pass into a condition gradually, take on a specific property or attribute.

## Examples

- John will grow angry.
- The weather turned nasty.
- She grew angry.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
