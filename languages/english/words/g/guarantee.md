# Guarantee

## Meaning

To guarantee means to give surety or assume responsibility.

## Examples

- I vouch for the quality of my products.
- I need to guarantee this clearly.
- She tried to guarantee the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
