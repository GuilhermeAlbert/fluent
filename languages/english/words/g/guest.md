# Guest

## Meaning

Guest is a visitor to whom hospitality is extended.

## Examples

- The guest asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the guest yesterday.
- The guest joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
