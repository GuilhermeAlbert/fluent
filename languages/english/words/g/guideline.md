# Guideline

## Meaning

Guideline is a light line that is used in lettering to help align the letters.

## Examples

- We talked about the guideline.
- The guideline matters here.
- I noticed the guideline right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
