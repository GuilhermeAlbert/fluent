# Guilty

## Meaning

Guilty describes someone or something that is responsible for or chargeable with a reprehensible act.

## Examples

- Guilty of murder.
- The guilty person.
- Secret guilty deeds.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
