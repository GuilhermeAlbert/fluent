# Guy

## Meaning

Guy is an informal term for a youth or man.

## Examples

- A nice guy.
- The guy's only doing it for some doll.
- The poor sod couldn't even buy a drink.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
