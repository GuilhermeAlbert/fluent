# Habitat

## Meaning

Habitat is the type of environment in which an organism or group normally lives or occurs.

## Examples

- A marine habitat.
- He felt safe on his home grounds.
- We met near the habitat.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
