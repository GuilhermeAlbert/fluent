# Hair

## Meaning

Hair is a covering for the body consisting of a dense growth of threadlike structures.

## Examples

- He combed his hair.
- Each hair consists of layers of dead keratinized cells.
- My hair hurts a little.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
