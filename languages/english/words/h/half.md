# Half

## Meaning

Half describes someone or something that is consisting of one of two equivalent parts in value or quantity.

## Examples

- A half chicken.
- Lasted a half hour.
- It feels half.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
