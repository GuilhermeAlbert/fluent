# Hall

## Meaning

Hall is an interior passage or corridor onto which rooms open.

## Examples

- The elevators were at the end of the hall.
- I left the hall on the table.
- She picked up the hall carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
