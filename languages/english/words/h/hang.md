# Hang

## Meaning

To hang means to be suspended or hanging.

## Examples

- The lights hang from the ceiling.
- The flag hung on the wall.
- I need to hang this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
