# Happy

## Meaning

Happy means feeling or showing pleasure, joy, or satisfaction.

## Examples

- She looked happy after the call.
- I am happy with the result.
- They had a happy childhood.

## Notes

Can describe a feeling, a person, or a pleasant situation.
