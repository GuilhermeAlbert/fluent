# Hard

## Meaning

Hard describes someone or something that is not easy.

## Examples

- A difficult task.
- Nesting places on the cliffs are difficult of access.
- Difficult times.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
