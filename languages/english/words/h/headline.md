# Headline

## Meaning

To headline means to publicize widely or highly, as if with a headline.

## Examples

- I need to headline this clearly.
- She tried to headline the idea.
- They plan to headline after the meeting.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
