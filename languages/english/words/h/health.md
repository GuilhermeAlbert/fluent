# Health

## Meaning

Health is a healthy state of wellbeing free from disease.

## Examples

- Physicians should be held responsible for the health of their patients.
- I need more health before I decide.
- We talked about health after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
