# Healthy

## Meaning

Healthy describes someone or something that is having or indicating good health in body or mind.

## Examples

- A rosy healthy baby.
- Staying fit and healthy.
- It feels healthy.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
