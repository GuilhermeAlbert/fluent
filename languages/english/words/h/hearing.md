# Hearing

## Meaning

Hearing is a proceeding where evidence is taken for the purpose of determining an issue of fact and reaching a decision based on that evidence.

## Examples

- The hearing starts at nine.
- We planned the hearing together.
- That hearing changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
