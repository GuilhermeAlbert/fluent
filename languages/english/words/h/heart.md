# Heart

## Meaning

Heart is the locus of feelings and intuitions.

## Examples

- In your heart you know it is true.
- Her story would melt your bosom.
- We talked about the heart.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
