# Heaven

## Meaning

Heaven means any place of complete bliss and delight and peace.

## Examples

- We met near the heaven.
- The heaven was quiet this morning.
- I walked past the heaven yesterday.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
