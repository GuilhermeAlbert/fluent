# Heavily

## Meaning

Heavily means doing something in a way that is to a considerable degree.

## Examples

- He relied heavily on others' data.
- She answered heavily.
- It happened heavily.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
