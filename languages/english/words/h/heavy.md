# Heavy

## Meaning

Heavy describes someone or something that is of comparatively great physical weight or density.

## Examples

- A heavy load.
- Lead is a heavy metal.
- Heavy mahogany furniture.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
