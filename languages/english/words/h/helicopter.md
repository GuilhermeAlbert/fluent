# Helicopter

## Meaning

Helicopter is an aircraft without wings that gets its lift from the rotation of overhead blades.

## Examples

- I left the helicopter on the table.
- She picked up the helicopter carefully.
- The helicopter is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
