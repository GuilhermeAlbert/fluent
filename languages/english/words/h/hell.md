# Hell

## Meaning

Hell means any place of pain and turmoil.

## Examples

- The hell of battle.
- The inferno of the engine room.
- When you're alone Christmas is the pits.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
