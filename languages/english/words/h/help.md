# Help

## Meaning

To help means to give help or helpance.

## Examples

- They help him to write the letter.
- Everyone helped out during the earthquake.
- Can you help me carry this table?

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
