# Helpful

## Meaning

Helpful describes someone or something that is providing helpance or serving a useful function.

## Examples

- It feels helpful.
- That was a helpful choice.
- The room looked helpful.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
