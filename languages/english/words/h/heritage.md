# Heritage

## Meaning

Heritage means practices that are handed down from the past by tradition.

## Examples

- A heritage of freedom.
- We talked about the heritage.
- The heritage matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
