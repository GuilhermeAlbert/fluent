# Hero

## Meaning

Hero is a person distinguished by exceptional courage and nobility and strength.

## Examples

- RAF pilots were the heroes of the Battle of Britain.
- The hero asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the hero yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
