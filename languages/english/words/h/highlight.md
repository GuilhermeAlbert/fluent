# Highlight

## Meaning

To highlight means to move into the foreground to make more visible or prominent.

## Examples

- The introduction highlighted the speaker's distinguished career in linguistics.
- I need to highlight this today.
- They plan to highlight it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
