# Historian

## Meaning

Historian means someone who is an authority on history and who studies it and writes about it.

## Examples

- The historian asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the historian yesterday.
- The historian joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
