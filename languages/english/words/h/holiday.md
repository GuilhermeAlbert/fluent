# Holiday

## Meaning

Holiday means leisure time away from work devoted to rest or pleasure.

## Examples

- We get two weeks of vacation every summer.
- We took a short holiday in Puerto Rico.
- The holiday went by quickly.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
