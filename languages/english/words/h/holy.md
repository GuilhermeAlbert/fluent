# Holy

## Meaning

Holy describes someone or something that is belonging to or derived from or associated with a divine power.

## Examples

- It feels holy.
- That was a holy choice.
- The room looked holy.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
