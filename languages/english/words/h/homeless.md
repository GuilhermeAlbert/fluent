# Homeless

## Meaning

Homeless describes someone or something that is without nationality or citizenship.

## Examples

- Stateless persons.
- It feels homeless.
- That was a homeless choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
