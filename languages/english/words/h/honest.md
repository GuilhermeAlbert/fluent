# Honest

## Meaning

Honest describes someone or something that is not disposed to cheat or defraud.

## Examples

- Honest lawyers.
- Honest reporting.
- Gave honest answers.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
