# Honor

## Meaning

To honor means to bestow honor or rewards upon.

## Examples

- Today we honor our soldiers.
- The scout was rewarded for courageous action.
- I need to honor soon.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
