# Hope

## Meaning

To hope means to expect and wish.

## Examples

- They hope to move.
- I trust you will behave better from now on.
- I hope she understands that she cannot expect a raise.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
