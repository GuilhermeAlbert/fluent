# Horizon

## Meaning

Horizon is the line at which the sky and Earth appear to meet.

## Examples

- We met near the horizon.
- The horizon was quiet this morning.
- I walked past the horizon yesterday.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
