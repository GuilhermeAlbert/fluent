# Horse

## Meaning

Horse means solid-hoofed herbivorous quadruped domesticated since prehistoric times.

## Examples

- The horse moved across the road.
- We saw a horse near the house.
- The horse looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
