# Hour

## Meaning

Hour is a period of time equal to 1/24th of a day.

## Examples

- The job will take more than an hour.
- The hour went by quickly.
- We chose a better hour.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
