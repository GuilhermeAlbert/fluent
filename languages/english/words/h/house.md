# House

## Meaning

House is a dwelling that serves as living quarters for one or more families.

## Examples

- He has a house on Cape Cod.
- She felt she had to get out of the house.
- I left the house on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
