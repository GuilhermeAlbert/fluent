# Humor

## Meaning

Humor is a message whose ingenuity or verbal skill or incongruity has the power to evoke laughter.

## Examples

- I need more humor before I decide.
- We talked about humor after lunch.
- That humor really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
