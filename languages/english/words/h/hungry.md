# Hungry

## Meaning

Hungry describes someone or something that is feeling hunger.

## Examples

- A world full of hungry people.
- It feels hungry.
- That was a hungry choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
