# Hypothesis

## Meaning

Hypothesis is a proposal intended to explain certain facts or observations.

## Examples

- We talked about the hypothesis.
- The hypothesis matters here.
- I noticed the hypothesis right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
