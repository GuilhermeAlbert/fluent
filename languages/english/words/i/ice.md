# Ice

## Meaning

Ice means water frozen in the solid state.

## Examples

- Americans like ice in their drinks.
- I need more ice before I decide.
- We talked about ice after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
