# Ideal

## Meaning

Ideal describes someone or something that is conforming to an ultimate standard of perfection or excellence.

## Examples

- It feels ideal.
- That was an ideal choice.
- The room looked ideal.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
