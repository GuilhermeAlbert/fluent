# Identify

## Meaning

To identify means to recognize as being.

## Examples

- She identified the man on the 'wanted' poster.
- I need to identify the problem.
- They tried to identify what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
