# Identity

## Meaning

Identity is the distinct personality of an person regarded as a persisting entity.

## Examples

- You can lose your identity when you join the army.
- We talked about the identity.
- The identity matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
