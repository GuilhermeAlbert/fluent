# Ie

## Meaning

Ie means "that is" and introduces a clearer or more exact explanation.

## Examples

- Use a neutral color, ie gray or black.
- The deadline is Friday, ie tomorrow.
- Choose one format, ie PDF.

## Notes

In polished writing, use "i.e." with periods.
