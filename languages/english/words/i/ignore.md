# Ignore

## Meaning

To ignore means to refuse to acknowledge.

## Examples

- She cut him dead at the meeting.
- I need to ignore this clearly.
- She tried to ignore the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
