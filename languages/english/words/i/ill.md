# Ill

## Meaning

Ill describes someone or something that is affected by an impairment of normal physical or mental function.

## Examples

- Ill from the monotony of his suffering.
- It feels ill.
- That was an ill choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
