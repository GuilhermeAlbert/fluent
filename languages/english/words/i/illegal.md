# Illegal

## Meaning

Illegal describes someone or something that is prohibited by law or by official or accepted rules.

## Examples

- An illegal chess move.
- It feels illegal.
- That was an illegal choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
