# Illness

## Meaning

Illness means impairment of normal physiological function affecting part or all of an organism.

## Examples

- We talked about the illness.
- The illness matters here.
- I noticed the illness right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
