# Illustrate

## Meaning

To illustrate means to clarify by giving an example of.

## Examples

- I need to illustrate this clearly.
- She tried to illustrate the idea.
- They plan to illustrate after the meeting.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
