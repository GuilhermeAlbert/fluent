# Image

## Meaning

Image is an iconic mental representation.

## Examples

- Her imagination forced images upon her too awful to contemplate.
- We talked about the image.
- The image matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
