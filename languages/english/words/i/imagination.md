# Imagination

## Meaning

Imagination is the formation of a mental image of something that is not perceived as real and is not present to the senses.

## Examples

- Popular imagination created a world of demons.
- Imagination reveals what the world could be.
- We talked about the imagination.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
