# Imagine

## Meaning

To imagine means to form a mental image of something that is not present or that is not the case.

## Examples

- Can you conceive of him as the president?
- I need to imagine this today.
- They plan to imagine it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
