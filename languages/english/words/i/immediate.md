# Immediate

## Meaning

Immediate describes someone or something that is of the present time and place.

## Examples

- The immediate revisions.
- It feels immediate.
- That was an immediate choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
