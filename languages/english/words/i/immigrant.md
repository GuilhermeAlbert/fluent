# Immigrant

## Meaning

Immigrant means someone who comes to a country where they were not born in order to settle there.

## Examples

- The immigrant asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the immigrant yesterday.
- The immigrant joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
