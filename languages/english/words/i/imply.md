# Imply

## Meaning

To imply means to express or state indirectly.

## Examples

- I need to imply this clearly.
- She tried to imply the idea.
- They plan to imply after the meeting.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
