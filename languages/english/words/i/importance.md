# Importance

## Meaning

Importance is the quality of being important and worthy of note.

## Examples

- The importance of a well-balanced diet.
- I need more importance before I decide.
- We talked about importance after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
