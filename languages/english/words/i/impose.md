# Impose

## Meaning

To impose means to compel to behave in a certain way.

## Examples

- Social relations impose courtesy.
- Duty constrains one to act often contrary to one's desires or inclinations.
- I need to impose soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
