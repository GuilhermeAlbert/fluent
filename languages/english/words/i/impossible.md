# Impossible

## Meaning

Impossible describes someone or something that is not capable of occurring or being accomplished or dealt with.

## Examples

- An impossible dream.
- An impossible situation.
- It feels impossible.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
