# Impress

## Meaning

To impress means to have an emotional or cognitive impact upon.

## Examples

- The bad news will impress him.
- The good news will impress her.
- This child impressed me as unusually mature.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
