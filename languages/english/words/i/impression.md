# Impression

## Meaning

Impression is a vague idea in which some confidence is placed.

## Examples

- His impression of her was favorable.
- What are your feelings about the crisis?
- It strengthened my belief in his sincerity.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
