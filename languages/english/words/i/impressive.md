# Impressive

## Meaning

Impressive describes someone or something that is making a strong or vivid impression.

## Examples

- An impressive ceremony.
- It feels impressive.
- That was an impressive choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
