# Incorporate

## Meaning

To incorporate means to make into a whole or make part of a whole.

## Examples

- She incorporated his suggestions into her proposal.
- I need to incorporate this today.
- They plan to incorporate it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
