# Increasing

## Meaning

Increasing describes someone or something that is becoming greater or larger.

## Examples

- Increasing prices.
- It feels increasing.
- That was an increasing choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
