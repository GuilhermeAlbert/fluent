# Increasingly

## Meaning

Increasingly means doing something in a way that is advancing in amount or intensity.

## Examples

- She became increasingly depressed.
- She answered increasingly.
- It happened increasingly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
