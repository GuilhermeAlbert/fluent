# Incredible

## Meaning

Incredible describes someone or something that is beyond belief or understanding.

## Examples

- At incredible speed.
- The book's plot is simply incredible.
- It feels incredible.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
