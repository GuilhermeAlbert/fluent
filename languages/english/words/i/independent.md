# Independent

## Meaning

Independent describes someone or something that is free from external control and constraint.

## Examples

- An independent mind.
- A series of independent judgments.
- Fiercely independent individualism.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
