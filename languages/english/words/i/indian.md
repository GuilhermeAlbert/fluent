# Indian

## Meaning

Indian is a native or inhabitant of India.

## Examples

- The indian asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the indian yesterday.
- The indian joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
