# Indication

## Meaning

Indication means something that serves to indicate or suggest.

## Examples

- An indication of foul play.
- Indications of strain.
- Symptoms are the prime indicants of disease.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
