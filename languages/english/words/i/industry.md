# Industry

## Meaning

Industry is the people or companies engaged in a particular kind of commercial enterprise.

## Examples

- Each industry has its own trade publications.
- We talked about the industry.
- The industry matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
