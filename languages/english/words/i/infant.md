# Infant

## Meaning

Infant is a very young child who has not yet begun to walk or talk.

## Examples

- The baby began to cry again.
- She held the baby in her arms.
- It sounds simple, but when you have your own baby it is all so different.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
