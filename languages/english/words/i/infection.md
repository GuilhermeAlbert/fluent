# Infection

## Meaning

Infection is the pathological state resulting from the invasion of the body by pathogenic microorganisms.

## Examples

- We talked about the infection.
- The infection matters here.
- I noticed the infection right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
