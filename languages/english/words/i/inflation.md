# Inflation

## Meaning

Inflation is a general and progressive increase in prices.

## Examples

- In inflation everything gets more valuable except money.
- We talked about the inflation.
- The inflation matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
