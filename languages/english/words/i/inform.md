# Inform

## Meaning

To inform means to impart knowledge of some fact, state of affairs, or event to.

## Examples

- I informed him of his rights.
- I need to inform this clearly.
- She tried to inform the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
