# Ingredient

## Meaning

Ingredient is a component of a mixture or compound.

## Examples

- I left the ingredient on the table.
- She picked up the ingredient carefully.
- The ingredient is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
