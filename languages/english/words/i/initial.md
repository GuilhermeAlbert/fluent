# Initial

## Meaning

Initial describes someone or something that is occurring at the beginning.

## Examples

- Took the initial step toward reconciliation.
- It feels initial.
- That was an initial choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
