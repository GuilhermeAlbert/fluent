# Initially

## Meaning

Initially means doing something in a way that is at the beginning.

## Examples

- At first he didn't notice anything strange.
- She answered initially.
- It happened initially.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
