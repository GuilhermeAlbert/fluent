# Injury

## Meaning

Injury means any physical damage to the body caused by violence or accident or fracture etc.

## Examples

- We talked about the injury.
- The injury matters here.
- I noticed the injury right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
