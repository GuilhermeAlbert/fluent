# Inner

## Meaning

Inner describes someone or something that is located inward.

## Examples

- Beethoven's manuscript looks like a bloody record of a tremendous inner battle.
- An internal sense of rightousness.
- It feels inner.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
