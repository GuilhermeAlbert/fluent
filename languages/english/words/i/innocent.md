# Innocent

## Meaning

Innocent describes someone or something that is free from evil or guilt.

## Examples

- An innocent child.
- The principle that one is innocent until proved guilty.
- It feels innocent.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
