# Inquiry

## Meaning

Inquiry is a search for knowledge.

## Examples

- Their pottery deserves more research than it has received.
- We talked about the inquiry.
- The inquiry matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
