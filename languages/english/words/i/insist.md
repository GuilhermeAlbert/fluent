# Insist

## Meaning

To insist means to be emphatic or resolute and refuse to budge.

## Examples

- I must insist!
- I need to insist this clearly.
- She tried to insist the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
