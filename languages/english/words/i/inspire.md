# Inspire

## Meaning

To inspire means to heighten or intensify.

## Examples

- The performance is likely to inspire Sue.
- These paintings exalt the imagination.
- I need to inspire soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
