# Instead

## Meaning

Instead means doing something in a way that is in place of, or as an alternative to.

## Examples

- Felix became a herpetologist instead.
- Alternatively we could buy a used car.
- She answered instead.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
