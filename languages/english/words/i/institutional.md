# Institutional

## Meaning

Institutional describes something connected with or constituting or involving an institution.

## Examples

- Institutional policy.
- It feels institutional.
- That was an institutional choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
