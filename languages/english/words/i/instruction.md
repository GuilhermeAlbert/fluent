# Instruction

## Meaning

Instruction is a message describing how something is to be done.

## Examples

- He gave directions faster than she could follow them.
- We talked about the instruction.
- The instruction matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
