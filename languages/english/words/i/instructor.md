# Instructor

## Meaning

Instructor means someone whose occupation is teaching.

## Examples

- The instructor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the instructor yesterday.
- The instructor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
