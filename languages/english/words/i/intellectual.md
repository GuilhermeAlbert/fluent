# Intellectual

## Meaning

Intellectual describes someone or something that is of or associated with or requiring the use of the mind.

## Examples

- Intellectual problems.
- The triumph of the rational over the animal side of man.
- It feels intellectual.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
