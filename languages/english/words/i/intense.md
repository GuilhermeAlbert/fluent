# Intense

## Meaning

Intense describes someone or something that is haveing or displaying a distinctive feature to a heightened degree.

## Examples

- Intense heat.
- Intense anxiety.
- Intense desire.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
