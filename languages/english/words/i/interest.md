# Interest

## Meaning

Interest is a sense of concern with and curiosity about someone or something.

## Examples

- An interest in music.
- We talked about the interest.
- The interest matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
