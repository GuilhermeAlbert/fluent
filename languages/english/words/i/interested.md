# Interested

## Meaning

Interested describes someone or something that is having or showing interest.

## Examples

- An interested audience.
- Interested in sports.
- Was interested to hear about her family.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
