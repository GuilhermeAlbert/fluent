# Interesting

## Meaning

Interesting describes someone or something that is arousing or holding the attention.

## Examples

- It feels interesting.
- That was an interesting choice.
- The room looked interesting.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
