# Internal

## Meaning

Internal describes someone or something that is happening or arising or located within some limits or especially surface.

## Examples

- Internal organs.
- Internal mechanism of a toy.
- Internal party maneuvering.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
