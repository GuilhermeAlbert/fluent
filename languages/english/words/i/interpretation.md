# Interpretation

## Meaning

Interpretation is a mental representation of the meaning or significance of something.

## Examples

- We talked about the interpretation.
- The interpretation matters here.
- I noticed the interpretation right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
