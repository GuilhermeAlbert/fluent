# Introduction

## Meaning

Introduction is the action of beginning something new.

## Examples

- They looked forward to the debut of their new product line.
- The introduction starts at nine.
- We planned the introduction together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
