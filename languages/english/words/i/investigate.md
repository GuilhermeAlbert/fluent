# Investigate

## Meaning

To investigate means to investigate scientifically.

## Examples

- The customs agents investigate the bags for drugs.
- Let's investigate the syntax of Chinese.
- I need to investigate this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
