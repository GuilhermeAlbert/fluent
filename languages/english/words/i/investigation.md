# Investigation

## Meaning

Investigation is an inquiry into unfamiliar or questionable activities.

## Examples

- There was a congressional probe into the scandal.
- We talked about the investigation.
- The investigation matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
