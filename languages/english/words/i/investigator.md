# Investigator

## Meaning

Investigator is a scientist who devotes themself to doing research.

## Examples

- The investigator asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the investigator yesterday.
- The investigator joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
