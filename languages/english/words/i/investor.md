# Investor

## Meaning

Investor means someone who commits capital in order to gain financial returns.

## Examples

- The investor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the investor yesterday.
- The investor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
