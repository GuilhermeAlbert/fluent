# Involve

## Meaning

To involve means to connect closely and often incriminatingly.

## Examples

- This new ruling affects your business.
- I need to involve soon.
- They want to involve together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
