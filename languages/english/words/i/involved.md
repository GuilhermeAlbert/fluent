# Involved

## Meaning

Involved describes someone or something that is connected by participation or association or use.

## Examples

- We accomplished nothing, simply because of the large number of people involved.
- The problems involved.
- The involved muscles.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
