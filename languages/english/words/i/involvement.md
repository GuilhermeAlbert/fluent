# Involvement

## Meaning

Involvement is the action of sharing in the activities of a group.

## Examples

- The teacher tried to increase his students' engagement in class activities.
- The involvement starts at nine.
- We planned the involvement together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
