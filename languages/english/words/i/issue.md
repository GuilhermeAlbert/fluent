# Issue

## Meaning

Issue is an important question that is in dispute and must be settled.

## Examples

- The issue could be settled by requiring public education for everyone.
- Politicians never discuss the real issues.
- We talked about the issue.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
