# Item

## Meaning

Item is a distinct part that can be specified separately in a group of things that could be enumerated on a list.

## Examples

- He noticed an item in the New York Times.
- She had several items on her shopping list.
- The main point on the agenda was taken up first.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
