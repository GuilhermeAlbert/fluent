# Jew

## Meaning

Jew is a person belonging to the worldwide group claiming descent from Jacob and connected by cultural or religious ties.

## Examples

- The jew asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the jew yesterday.
- The jew joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
