# Joke

## Meaning

Joke is a humorous anecdote or remark intended to provoke laughter.

## Examples

- He told a very funny joke.
- He knows a million gags.
- Thanks for the laugh.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
