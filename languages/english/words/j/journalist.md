# Journalist

## Meaning

Journalist is a writer for newspapers and magazines.

## Examples

- The journalist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the journalist yesterday.
- The journalist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
