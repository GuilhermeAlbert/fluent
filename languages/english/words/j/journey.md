# Journey

## Meaning

To journey means to undertake a journey or trip.

## Examples

- We need to journey soon.
- They started to journey after lunch.
- I watched him journey across the room.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
