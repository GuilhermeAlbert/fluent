# Judgment

## Meaning

Judgment is an opinion formed by judging something.

## Examples

- He was reluctant to make his judgment known.
- She changed her mind.
- We talked about the judgment.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
