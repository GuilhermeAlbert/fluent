# Juice

## Meaning

Juice is the liquid part that can be extracted from plant or animal tissue by squeezing or cooking.

## Examples

- I had juice with breakfast.
- She bought juice at the store.
- This juice tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
