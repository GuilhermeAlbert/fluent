# Junior

## Meaning

Junior means term of address for a disrespectful and annoying male.

## Examples

- Look here, junior, it's none of your business.
- The junior asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the junior yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
