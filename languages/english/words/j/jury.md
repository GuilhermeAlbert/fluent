# Jury

## Meaning

Jury is a body of citizens sworn to give a true verdict according to the evidence presented in a court of law.

## Examples

- We talked about the jury.
- The jury matters here.
- I noticed the jury right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
