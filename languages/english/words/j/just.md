# Just

## Meaning

Just means doing something in a way that is and nothing more.

## Examples

- I was merely asking.
- It is simply a matter of time.
- Just a scratch.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
