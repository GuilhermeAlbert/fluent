# Justify

## Meaning

To justify means to show to be right by providing justification or proof.

## Examples

- Vindicate a claim.
- I need to justify this clearly.
- She tried to justify the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
