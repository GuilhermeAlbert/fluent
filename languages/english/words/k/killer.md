# Killer

## Meaning

Killer means someone who causes the death of a person or animal.

## Examples

- The killer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the killer yesterday.
- The killer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
