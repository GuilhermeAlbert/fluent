# Kind

## Meaning

Kind describes someone or something that is having or showing a tender and considerate and helpful nature.

## Examples

- Kind to sick patients.
- A kind master.
- Kind words showing understanding and sympathy.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
