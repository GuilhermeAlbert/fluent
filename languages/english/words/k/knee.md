# Knee

## Meaning

Knee means hinge joint in the human leg connecting the tibia and fibula with the femur and protected in front by the patella.

## Examples

- My knee hurts a little.
- She touched her knee.
- Keep your knee relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
