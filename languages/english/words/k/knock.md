# Knock

## Meaning

To knock means to deliver a sharp blow or push.

## Examples

- He knocked the glass clear across the room.
- I need to knock this today.
- They plan to knock it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
