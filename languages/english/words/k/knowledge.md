# Knowledge

## Meaning

Knowledge is the psychological result of perception and learning and reasoning.

## Examples

- I need more knowledge before I decide.
- We talked about knowledge after lunch.
- That knowledge really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
