# Labor

## Meaning

Labor is a social class comprising those who do manual labor or work for wages.

## Examples

- There is a shortage of skilled labor in this field.
- We talked about the labor.
- The labor matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
