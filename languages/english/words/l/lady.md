# Lady

## Meaning

Lady is a polite name for any woman.

## Examples

- A nice lady at the library helped me.
- The lady asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the lady yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
