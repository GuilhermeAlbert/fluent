# Landscape

## Meaning

Landscape is an expanse of scenery that can be seen in a single view.

## Examples

- We met near the landscape.
- The landscape was quiet this morning.
- I walked past the landscape yesterday.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
