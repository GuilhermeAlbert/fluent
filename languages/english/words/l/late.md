# Late

## Meaning

Late describes someone or something that is being or occurring at an advanced period of time or after a usual or expected time.

## Examples

- Late evening.
- Late 18th century.
- A late movie.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
