# Latter

## Meaning

Latter is the second of two or the second mentioned of two.

## Examples

- Tom and Dick were both heroes but only the latter is remembered today.
- We talked about the latter.
- The latter matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
