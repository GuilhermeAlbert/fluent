# Lawsuit

## Meaning

Lawsuit is a comprehensive term for any proceeding in a court of law whereby an person seeks a legal remedy.

## Examples

- The family brought suit against the landlord.
- The lawsuit starts at nine.
- We planned the lawsuit together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
