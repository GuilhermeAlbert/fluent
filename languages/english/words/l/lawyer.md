# Lawyer

## Meaning

Lawyer is a professional person authorized to practice law.

## Examples

- The lawyer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the lawyer yesterday.
- The lawyer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
