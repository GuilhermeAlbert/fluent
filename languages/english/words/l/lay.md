# Lay

## Meaning

Lay describes someone or something that is characteristic of those who are not members of the clergy.

## Examples

- Set his collar in laic rather than clerical position.
- The lay ministry.
- It feels lay.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
