# Layer

## Meaning

Layer means single thickness of usually some homogeneous substance.

## Examples

- Slices of hard-boiled egg on a bed of spinach.
- I left the layer on the table.
- She picked up the layer carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
