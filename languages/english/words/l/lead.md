# Lead

## Meaning

To lead means to take somebody somewhere.

## Examples

- The men lead the horses across the field.
- We led him to our chief.
- Can you take me to the main entrance?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
