# Leader

## Meaning

Leader means someone who rules or guides or inspires others.

## Examples

- The leader asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the leader yesterday.
- The leader joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
