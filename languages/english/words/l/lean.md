# Lean

## Meaning

To lean means to incline or bend from a vertical position.

## Examples

- She leaned over the banister.
- We need to lean soon.
- They started to lean after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
