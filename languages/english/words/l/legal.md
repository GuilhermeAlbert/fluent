# Legal

## Meaning

Legal describes someone or something that is established by or founded upon law or official or accepted rules.

## Examples

- It feels legal.
- That was a legal choice.
- The room looked legal.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
