# Legislation

## Meaning

Legislation means law enacted by a legislative body.

## Examples

- We talked about the legislation.
- The legislation matters here.
- I noticed the legislation right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
