# Lemon

## Meaning

Lemon means yellow oval fruit with juicy acidic flesh.

## Examples

- I had lemon with breakfast.
- She bought lemon at the store.
- This lemon tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
