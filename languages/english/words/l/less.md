# Less

## Meaning

Less describes someone or something that is a quantifier meaning not as great in amount or degree.

## Examples

- Of less importance.
- Less time to spend with the family.
- A shower uses less water.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
