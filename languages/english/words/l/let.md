# Let

## Meaning

To let means to make it possible through a specific action or lack of action for something to happen.

## Examples

- This permits the water to rush in.
- This sealed door won't allow the water to come into the basement.
- This will permit the rain to run off.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
