# Letter

## Meaning

Letter is a written message addressed to a person or organization.

## Examples

- Mailed an indignant letter to the editor.
- We talked about the letter.
- The letter matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
