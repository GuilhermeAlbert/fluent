# Liberal

## Meaning

Liberal describes someone or something that is showing or characterized by broad-mindedness.

## Examples

- A broad political stance.
- Generous and broad sympathies.
- A liberal newspaper.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
