# Lie

## Meaning

To lie means to be located or situated somewhere.

## Examples

- The children lie in the rocking chair.
- There lie some children in the rocking chair.
- I need to lie soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
