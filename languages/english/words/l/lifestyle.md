# Lifestyle

## Meaning

Lifestyle is a manner of living that reflects the person's values and attitudes.

## Examples

- We talked about the lifestyle.
- The lifestyle matters here.
- I noticed the lifestyle right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
