# Limitation

## Meaning

Limitation is a principle that limits the extent of something.

## Examples

- I am willing to accept certain restrictions on my movements.
- We talked about the limitation.
- The limitation matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
