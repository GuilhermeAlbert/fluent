# Lip

## Meaning

Lip means either of two fleshy folds of tissue that surround the mouth and play a role in speaking.

## Examples

- My lip hurts a little.
- She touched her lip.
- Keep your lip relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
