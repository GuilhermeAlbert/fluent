# Literature

## Meaning

Literature means creative writing of recognized artistic value.

## Examples

- We talked about the literature.
- The literature matters here.
- I noticed the literature right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
