# Local

## Meaning

Local describes something connected with or applicable to or concerned with the administration of a city or town or district rather than a larger area.

## Examples

- Local taxes.
- Local authorities.
- It feels local.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
