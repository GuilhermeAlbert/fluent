# Locate

## Meaning

To locate means to discover the location of.

## Examples

- Can you locate your cousins in the Midwest?
- My search turned up nothing.
- I need to locate this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
