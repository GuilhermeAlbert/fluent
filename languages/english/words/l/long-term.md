# Long-Term

## Meaning

Long-Term describes something connected with or extending over a relatively long time.

## Examples

- The long-run significance of the elections.
- The long-term reconstruction of countries damaged by the war.
- A long-term investment.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
