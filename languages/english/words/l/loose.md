# Loose

## Meaning

Loose describes someone or something that is not compact or dense in structure or arrangement.

## Examples

- Loose gravel.
- It feels loose.
- That was a loose choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
