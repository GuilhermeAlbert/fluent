# Lose

## Meaning

To lose means to fail to keep or maintain.

## Examples

- They lose the money.
- I sprang from the ground and quickly lost balance.
- I need to lose this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
