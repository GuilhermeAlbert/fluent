# Loud

## Meaning

Loud describes someone or something that is characterized by or producing sound of great volume or intensity.

## Examples

- A group of loud children.
- Loud thunder.
- Her voice was too loud.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
