# Lovely

## Meaning

Lovely describes someone or something that is appealing to the emotions as well as the eye.

## Examples

- It feels lovely.
- That was a lovely choice.
- The room looked lovely.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
