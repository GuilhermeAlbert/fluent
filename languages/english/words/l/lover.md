# Lover

## Meaning

Lover means someone who loves someone or is loved by someone.

## Examples

- The lover asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the lover yesterday.
- The lover joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
