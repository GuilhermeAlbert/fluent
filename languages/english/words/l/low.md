# Low

## Meaning

Low describes someone or something that is less than normal in degree or intensity or amount.

## Examples

- Low prices.
- The reservoir is low.
- It feels low.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
