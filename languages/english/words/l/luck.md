# Luck

## Meaning

Luck means your overall circumstances or condition in life.

## Examples

- Whatever my fortune may be.
- Deserved a better fate.
- Has a happy lot.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
