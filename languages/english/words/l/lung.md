# Lung

## Meaning

Lung means either of two saclike respiratory organs in the chest of vertebrates.

## Examples

- My lung hurts a little.
- She touched her lung.
- Keep your lung relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
