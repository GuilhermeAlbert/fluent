# Machine

## Meaning

Machine means any mechanical or electrical device that transmits or modifies energy to perform or help in the performance of human tasks.

## Examples

- I left the machine on the table.
- She picked up the machine carefully.
- The machine is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
