# Mad

## Meaning

Mad describes someone or something that is roused to anger.

## Examples

- Stayed huffy a good while.
- She gets mad when you wake her up so early.
- Mad at his friend.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
