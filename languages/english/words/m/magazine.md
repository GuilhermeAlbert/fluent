# Magazine

## Meaning

Magazine is a periodic publication containing pictures and stories and articles of interest to those who buy it or subscribe to it.

## Examples

- It takes several years before a magazine starts to break even or make money.
- We talked about the magazine.
- The magazine matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
