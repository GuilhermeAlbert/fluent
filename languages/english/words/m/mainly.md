# Mainly

## Meaning

Mainly means doing something in a way that is for the most part.

## Examples

- He is mainly interested in butterflies.
- She answered mainly.
- It happened mainly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
