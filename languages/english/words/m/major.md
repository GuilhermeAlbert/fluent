# Major

## Meaning

Major is a commissioned military officer in the United States Army or Air Force or Marines.

## Examples

- The major asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the major yesterday.
- The major joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
