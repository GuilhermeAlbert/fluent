# Majority

## Meaning

Majority is the property resulting from being or relating to the greater in number of two parts.

## Examples

- The majority of his customers prefer it.
- The bulk of the work is finished.
- We talked about the majority.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
