# Maker

## Meaning

Maker means someone who makes things.

## Examples

- The maker asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the maker yesterday.
- The maker joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
