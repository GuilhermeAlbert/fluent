# Man

## Meaning

Man is an adult person who is male.

## Examples

- There were two women and six men on the bus.
- The man asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the man yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
