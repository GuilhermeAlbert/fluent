# Manner

## Meaning

Manner means how something is done or how it happens.

## Examples

- Her dignified manner.
- His rapid manner of talking.
- Their nomadic mode of existence.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
