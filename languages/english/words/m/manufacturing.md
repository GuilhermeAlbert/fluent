# Manufacturing

## Meaning

Manufacturing is the action of making something from raw materials.

## Examples

- The synthesis and fabrication of single crystals.
- An improvement in the manufacture of explosives.
- Manufacturing is vital to Great Britain.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
