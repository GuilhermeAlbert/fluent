# Many

## Meaning

Many describes someone or something that is a quantifier that can be used with count nouns and is often preceded by "as" or "too" or "so" or "that".

## Examples

- Many temptations.
- The temptations are many.
- A good many.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
