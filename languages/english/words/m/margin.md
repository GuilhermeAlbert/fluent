# Margin

## Meaning

Margin is the boundary line or the area immediately inside the boundary.

## Examples

- We talked about the margin.
- The margin matters here.
- I noticed the margin right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
