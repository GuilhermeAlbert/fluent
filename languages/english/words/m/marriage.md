# Marriage

## Meaning

Marriage is the state of being a married couple voluntarily joined for life.

## Examples

- A long and happy marriage.
- God bless this union.
- We talked about the marriage.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
