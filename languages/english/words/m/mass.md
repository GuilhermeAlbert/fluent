# Mass

## Meaning

Mass is the property of a body that causes it to have weight in a gravitational field.

## Examples

- We talked about the mass.
- The mass matters here.
- I noticed the mass right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
