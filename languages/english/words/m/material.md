# Material

## Meaning

Material describes someone or something that is concerned with worldly rather than spiritual interests.

## Examples

- Material possessions.
- Material wealth.
- Material comforts.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
