# Mayor

## Meaning

Mayor is the head of a city government.

## Examples

- The mayor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the mayor yesterday.
- The mayor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
