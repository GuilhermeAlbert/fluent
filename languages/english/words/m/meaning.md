# Meaning

## Meaning

Meaning is the message that is intended or expressed or signified.

## Examples

- What is the meaning of this sentence?
- The significance of a red traffic light.
- The signification of Chinese characters.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
