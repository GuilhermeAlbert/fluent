# Meanwhile

## Meaning

Meanwhile means doing something in a way that is at the same time but in another place.

## Examples

- Meanwhile, back at the ranch ....
- She answered meanwhile.
- It happened meanwhile.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
