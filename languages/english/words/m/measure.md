# Measure

## Meaning

To measure means to determine the measurements of something or somebody, take measurements of.

## Examples

- Measure the length of the wall.
- I need to measure the problem.
- They tried to measure what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
