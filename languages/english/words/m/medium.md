# Medium

## Meaning

Medium is a means or instrumentality for storing or communicating information.

## Examples

- We talked about the medium.
- The medium matters here.
- I noticed the medium right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
