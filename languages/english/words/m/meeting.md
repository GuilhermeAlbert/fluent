# Meeting

## Meaning

Meeting is a formally arranged gathering.

## Examples

- Next year the meeting will be in Chicago.
- The meeting elected a chairperson.
- We talked about the meeting.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
