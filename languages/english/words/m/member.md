# Member

## Meaning

Member means one of the people who compose a social group.

## Examples

- Only members will be admitted.
- A member of the faculty.
- She was introduced to all the members of his family.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
