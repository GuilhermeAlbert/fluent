# Mental

## Meaning

Mental describes someone or something that is involving the mind or an intellectual process.

## Examples

- Mental images of happy times.
- Mental calculations.
- In a terrible mental state.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
