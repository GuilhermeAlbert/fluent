# Metal

## Meaning

Metal describes someone or something that is containing or made of or resembling or characteristic of a metal.

## Examples

- A metallic compound.
- Metallic luster.
- The strange metallic note of the meadow lark, suggesting the clash of vibrant blades.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
