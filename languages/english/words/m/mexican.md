# Mexican

## Meaning

Mexican is a native or inhabitant of Mexico.

## Examples

- The mexican asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the mexican yesterday.
- The mexican joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
