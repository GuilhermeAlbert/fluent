# Milk

## Meaning

To milk means to take milk from female mammals.

## Examples

- Cows need to be milked every morning.
- I need to milk this today.
- They plan to milk it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
