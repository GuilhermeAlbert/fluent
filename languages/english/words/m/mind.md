# Mind

## Meaning

Mind means that which is responsible for one's thoughts, feelings, and conscious brain functions.

## Examples

- His mind wandered.
- I couldn't get his words out of my head.
- We talked about the mind.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
