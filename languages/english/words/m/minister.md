# Minister

## Meaning

Minister is a person authorized to conduct religious worship.

## Examples

- Clergymen are usually called ministers in Protestant churches.
- The minister asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the minister yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
