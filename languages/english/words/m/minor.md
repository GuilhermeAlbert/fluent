# Minor

## Meaning

Minor is a young person of either sex.

## Examples

- She writes books for children.
- They're just kids.
- 'tiddler' is a British term for youngster.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
