# Minority

## Meaning

Minority is a group of people who differ racially or politically from a larger group of which it is a part.

## Examples

- We talked about the minority.
- The minority matters here.
- I noticed the minority right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
