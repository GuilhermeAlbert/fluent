# Minute

## Meaning

Minute is a unit of time equal to 60 seconds or 1/60th of an hour.

## Examples

- He ran a 4 minute mile.
- The minute went by quickly.
- We chose a better minute.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
