# Miracle

## Meaning

Miracle means any amazing or wonderful occurrence.

## Examples

- The miracle starts at nine.
- We planned the miracle together.
- That miracle changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
