# Mirror

## Meaning

Mirror means polished surface that forms images by reflecting light.

## Examples

- I left the mirror on the table.
- She picked up the mirror carefully.
- The mirror is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
