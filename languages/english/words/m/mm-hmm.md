# Mm-Hmm

## Meaning

Mm-hmm is a casual sound people use to say yes or show they are listening.

## Examples

- "Do you understand?" "Mm-hmm."
- She nodded and said, "Mm-hmm."
- I said mm-hmm while taking notes.

## Notes

Very informal and mostly used in speech or dialogue.
