# Modest

## Meaning

Modest describes someone or something that is marked by simplicity.

## Examples

- A modest apartment.
- Too modest to wear his medals.
- It feels modest.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
