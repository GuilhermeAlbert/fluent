# Mom

## Meaning

Mom means informal terms for a mother.

## Examples

- The mom asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the mom yesterday.
- The mom joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
