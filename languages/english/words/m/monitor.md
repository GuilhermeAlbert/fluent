# Monitor

## Meaning

Monitor means someone who supervises.

## Examples

- The monitor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the monitor yesterday.
- The monitor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
