# Moral

## Meaning

Moral describes someone or something that is concerned with principles of right and wrong or conforming to standards of behavior and character based on those principles.

## Examples

- Moral sense.
- A moral scrutiny.
- A moral lesson.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
