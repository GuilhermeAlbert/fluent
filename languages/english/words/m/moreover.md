# Moreover

## Meaning

Moreover means doing something in a way that is denoting additional information.

## Examples

- Computer chess games are getting cheaper all the time; furthermore, their quality is improving.
- The cellar was dark; moreover, mice nested there.
- What is more, there's no sign of a change.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
