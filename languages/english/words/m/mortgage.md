# Mortgage

## Meaning

Mortgage is a conditional conveyance of property as security for the repayment of a loan.

## Examples

- We talked about the mortgage.
- The mortgage matters here.
- I noticed the mortgage right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
