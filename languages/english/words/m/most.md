# Most

## Meaning

Most means doing something in a way that is used to form the superlative, greatest in size or degree.

## Examples

- The king cobra is the most dangerous snake.
- She answered most.
- It happened most.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
