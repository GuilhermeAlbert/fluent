# Mostly

## Meaning

Mostly means doing something in a way that is in large part.

## Examples

- These accounts are largely inactive.
- She answered mostly.
- It happened mostly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
