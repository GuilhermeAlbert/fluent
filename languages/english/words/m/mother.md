# Mother

## Meaning

Mother is a woman who has given birth to a child.

## Examples

- The mother of three children.
- The mother asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the mother yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
