# Motion

## Meaning

Motion is the use of movements to communicate familiar or prearranged signals.

## Examples

- We talked about the motion.
- The motion matters here.
- I noticed the motion right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
