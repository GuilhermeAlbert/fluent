# Motivation

## Meaning

Motivation is the psychological feature that arouses an organism to action toward a desired goal.

## Examples

- We did not understand his motivation.
- He acted with the best of motives.
- We talked about the motivation.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
