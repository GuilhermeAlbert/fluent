# Motor

## Meaning

Motor means machine that converts other forms of energy into mechanical energy and so imparts motion.

## Examples

- I left the motor on the table.
- She picked up the motor carefully.
- The motor is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
