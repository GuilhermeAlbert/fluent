# Mount

## Meaning

To mount means to attach to a support.

## Examples

- The men mount the bookshelves.
- They mounted the aerator on a floating.
- I need to mount this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
