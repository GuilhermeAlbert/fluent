# Mountain

## Meaning

Mountain is a land mass that projects well above its surroundings.

## Examples

- We talked about the mountain.
- The mountain matters here.
- I noticed the mountain right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
