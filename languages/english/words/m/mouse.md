# Mouse

## Meaning

Mouse means any of numerous small rodents typically resembling diminutive rats having pointed snouts and small ears on elongated bodies with slender usually hairless tails.

## Examples

- The mouse moved across the road.
- We saw a mouse near the house.
- The mouse looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
