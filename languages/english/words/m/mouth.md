# Mouth

## Meaning

Mouth is the opening through which food is taken in and vocalizations emerge.

## Examples

- He stuffed his mouth with candy.
- My mouth hurts a little.
- She touched her mouth.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
