# Movie

## Meaning

Movie is a form of entertainment that enacts a story by sound and a sequence of images giving the illusion of continuous movement.

## Examples

- They went to a movie every Saturday night.
- The film was shot on location.
- We talked about the movie.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
