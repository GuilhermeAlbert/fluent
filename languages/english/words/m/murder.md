# Murder

## Meaning

Murder means unlawful premeditated killing of a human being by a human being.

## Examples

- The murder starts at nine.
- We planned the murder together.
- That murder changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
