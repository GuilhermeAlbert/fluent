# Music

## Meaning

Music is an artistic form of auditory communication incorporating instrumental or vocal tones in a structured and continuous manner.

## Examples

- I need more music before I decide.
- We talked about music after lunch.
- That music really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
