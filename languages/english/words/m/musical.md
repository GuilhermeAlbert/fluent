# Musical

## Meaning

Musical describes someone or something that is characterized by or capable of producing music.

## Examples

- A musical evening.
- Musical instruments.
- It feels musical.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
