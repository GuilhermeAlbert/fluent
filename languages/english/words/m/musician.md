# Musician

## Meaning

Musician means someone who plays a music as a profession.

## Examples

- The musician asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the musician yesterday.
- The musician joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
