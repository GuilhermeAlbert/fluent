# Mutual

## Meaning

Mutual describes someone or something that is common to or shared by two or more parties.

## Examples

- A common friend.
- The mutual interests of management and labor.
- It feels mutual.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
