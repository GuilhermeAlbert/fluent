# Mystery

## Meaning

Mystery means something that baffles understanding and cannot be explained.

## Examples

- How it got out is a mystery.
- It remains one of nature's secrets.
- We talked about the mystery.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
