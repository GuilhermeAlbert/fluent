# Narrative

## Meaning

Narrative describes someone or something that is consisting of or characterized by the telling of a story.

## Examples

- Narrative poetry.
- It feels narrative.
- That was a narrative choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
