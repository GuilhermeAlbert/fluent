# Native

## Meaning

Native describes someone or something that is characteristic of or existing by virtue of geographic origin.

## Examples

- The native North American sugar maple.
- Many native artists studied abroad.
- It feels native.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
