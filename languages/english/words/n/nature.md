# Nature

## Meaning

Nature is the essential qualities or characteristics by which something is recognized.

## Examples

- It is the nature of fire to burn.
- The true nature of jealousy.
- We talked about the nature.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
