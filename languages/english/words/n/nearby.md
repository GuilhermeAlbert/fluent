# Nearby

## Meaning

Nearby describes someone or something that is close at hand.

## Examples

- The nearby towns.
- Concentrated his study on the nearby planet Venus.
- It feels nearby.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
