# Necessarily

## Meaning

Necessarily means doing something in a way that is in an essential manner.

## Examples

- Such expenses are necessarily incurred.
- She answered necessarily.
- It happened necessarily.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
