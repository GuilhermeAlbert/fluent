# Necessary

## Meaning

Necessary describes someone or something that is absolutely essential.

## Examples

- It feels necessary.
- That was a necessary choice.
- The room looked necessary.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
