# Neck

## Meaning

Neck is the part of an organism that connects the head to the rest of the body.

## Examples

- He admired her long graceful neck.
- The horse won by a neck.
- My neck hurts a little.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
