# Negative

## Meaning

Negative describes someone or something that is characterized by or displaying negation or denial or opposition or resistance.

## Examples

- A negative outlook on life.
- A colorless negative personality.
- A negative evaluation.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
