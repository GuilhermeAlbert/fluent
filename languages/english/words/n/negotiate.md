# Negotiate

## Meaning

To negotiate means to discuss the terms of an arrangement.

## Examples

- They negotiated the sale of the house.
- I need to negotiate this clearly.
- She tried to negotiate the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
