# Neighbor

## Meaning

Neighbor means someone who lives near another.

## Examples

- The neighbor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the neighbor yesterday.
- The neighbor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
