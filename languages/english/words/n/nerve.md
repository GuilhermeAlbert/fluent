# Nerve

## Meaning

Nerve means any bundle of nerve fibers running to various organs and tissues of the body.

## Examples

- My nerve hurts a little.
- She touched her nerve.
- Keep your nerve relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
