# Nervous

## Meaning

Nervous describes someone or something that is easily agitated.

## Examples

- A nervous addict.
- A nervous thoroughbred.
- It feels nervous.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
