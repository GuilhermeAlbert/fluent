# Newspaper

## Meaning

Newspaper is a daily or weekly publication on folded sheets.

## Examples

- He read his newspaper at breakfast.
- We talked about the newspaper.
- The newspaper matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
