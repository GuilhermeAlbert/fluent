# Nice

## Meaning

Nice describes someone or something that is pleasant or pleasing or agreeable in nature or appearance.

## Examples

- What a nice fellow you are and we all thought you so nasty.
- Nice manners.
- A nice dress.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
