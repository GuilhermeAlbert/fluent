# Night

## Meaning

Night is the time after sunset and before sunrise while it is dark outside.

## Examples

- The night went by quickly.
- We chose a better night.
- That night worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
