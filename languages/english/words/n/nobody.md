# Nobody

## Meaning

Nobody is a person of no influence.

## Examples

- The nobody asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the nobody yesterday.
- The nobody joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
