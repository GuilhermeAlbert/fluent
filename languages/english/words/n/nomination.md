# Nomination

## Meaning

Nomination is the action of officially naming a candidate.

## Examples

- The Republican nomination for Governor.
- The nomination starts at nine.
- We planned the nomination together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
