# Normal

## Meaning

Normal describes someone or something that is conforming with or constituting a norm or standard or level or type or social norm.

## Examples

- Serve wine at normal room temperature.
- Normal diplomatic relations.
- Normal working hours.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
