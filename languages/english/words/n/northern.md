# Northern

## Meaning

Northern describes someone or something that is in or characteristic of a region of the United States north of the Mason-Dixon line.

## Examples

- Northern liberals.
- Northern industry.
- Northern cities.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
