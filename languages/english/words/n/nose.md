# Nose

## Meaning

Nose is the organ of smell and entrance to the respiratory tract.

## Examples

- He has a cold in the nose.
- My nose hurts a little.
- She touched her nose.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
