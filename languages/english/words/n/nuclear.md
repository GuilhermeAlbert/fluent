# Nuclear

## Meaning

Nuclear describes someone or something that is deriving destructive energy from the release of atomic energy.

## Examples

- Nuclear war.
- Nuclear weapons.
- Atomic bombs.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
