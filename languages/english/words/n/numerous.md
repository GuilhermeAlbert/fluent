# Numerous

## Meaning

Numerous describes someone or something that is amounting to a large indefinite number.

## Examples

- Numerous times.
- The family was numerous.
- Palomar's fans are legion.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
