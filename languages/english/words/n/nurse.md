# Nurse

## Meaning

To nurse means to try to cure by special care of treatment, of an illness or injury.

## Examples

- Did he nurse his foot?
- He nursed his cold with Chinese herbs.
- I need to nurse soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
