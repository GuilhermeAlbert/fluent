# Objective

## Meaning

Objective describes someone or something that is undistorted by emotion or personal bias.

## Examples

- An objective appraisal.
- Objective evidence.
- It feels objective.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
