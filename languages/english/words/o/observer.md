# Observer

## Meaning

Observer means someone who becomes aware through the senses.

## Examples

- The observer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the observer yesterday.
- The observer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
