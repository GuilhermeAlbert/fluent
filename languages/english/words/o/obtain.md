# Obtain

## Meaning

To obtain means to come into haveion of.

## Examples

- How did you obtain the visa?
- I need to obtain this today.
- They plan to obtain it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
