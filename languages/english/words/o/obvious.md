# Obvious

## Meaning

Obvious describes someone or something that is easily perceived and understood.

## Examples

- Obvious errors.
- It feels obvious.
- That was an obvious choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
