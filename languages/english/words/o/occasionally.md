# Occasionally

## Meaning

Occasionally means doing something in a way that is sporadically and infrequently.

## Examples

- He was arrogant and occasionally callous.
- Open areas are only occasionally interrupted by clumps of trees.
- They visit New York on occasion.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
