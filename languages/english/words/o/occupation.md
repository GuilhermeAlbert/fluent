# Occupation

## Meaning

Occupation is the principal activity in your life that you do to earn money.

## Examples

- He's not in my line of business.
- The occupation starts at nine.
- We planned the occupation together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
