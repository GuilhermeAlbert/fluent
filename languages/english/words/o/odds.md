# Odds

## Meaning

Odds is the likelihood of a thing occurring rather than not occurring.

## Examples

- We talked about the odds.
- The odds matters here.
- I noticed the odds right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
