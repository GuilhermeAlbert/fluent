# Offensive

## Meaning

Offensive describes someone or something that is violating or tending to violate or offend against.

## Examples

- Violative of the principles of liberty.
- Considered such depravity offensive against all laws of humanity.
- It feels offensive.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
