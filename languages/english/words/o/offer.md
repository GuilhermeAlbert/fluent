# Offer

## Meaning

To offer means to give something useful or necessary to.

## Examples

- They offer cars to the tourists.
- They offer the tourists their cars.
- We provided the room with an electrical heater.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
