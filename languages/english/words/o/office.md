# Office

## Meaning

Office means place of business where professional or clerical duties are performed.

## Examples

- He rented an office in the new building.
- I left the office on the table.
- She picked up the office carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
