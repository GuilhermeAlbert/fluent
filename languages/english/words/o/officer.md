# Officer

## Meaning

Officer means any person in the armed services who holds a position of authority or command.

## Examples

- An officer is responsible for the lives of his men.
- The officer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the officer yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
