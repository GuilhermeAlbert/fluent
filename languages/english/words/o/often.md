# Often

## Meaning

Often means doing something in a way that is many times at short intervals.

## Examples

- We often met over a cup of coffee.
- She answered often.
- It happened often.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
