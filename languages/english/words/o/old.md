# Old

## Meaning

Old describes someone or something that is having lived for a relatively long time or attained a specific age.

## Examples

- His mother is very old.
- A ripe old age.
- How old are you?

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
