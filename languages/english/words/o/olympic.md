# Olympic

## Meaning

Olympic describes something connected with the Olympic Games.

## Examples

- Olympic winners.
- It feels olympic.
- That was an olympic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
