# Ongoing

## Meaning

Ongoing describes someone or something that is currently happening.

## Examples

- An ongoing economic crisis.
- It feels ongoing.
- That was an ongoing choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
