# Online

## Meaning

Online describes someone or something that is on a regular route of a railroad or bus or airline system.

## Examples

- On-line industries.
- It feels online.
- That was an online choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
