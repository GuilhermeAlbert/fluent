# Opponent

## Meaning

Opponent is a contestant that you are matched against.

## Examples

- The opponent asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the opponent yesterday.
- The opponent joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
