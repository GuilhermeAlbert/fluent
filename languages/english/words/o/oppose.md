# Oppose

## Meaning

To oppose means to be against.

## Examples

- We oppose the ban on abortion.
- I need to oppose this clearly.
- She tried to oppose the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
