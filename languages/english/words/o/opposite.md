# Opposite

## Meaning

Opposite describes someone or something that is being directly across from each other.

## Examples

- And I on the opposite shore will be, ready to ride and spread the alarm.
- We lived on opposite sides of the street.
- At opposite poles.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
