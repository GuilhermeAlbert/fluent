# Orange

## Meaning

Orange means round yellow to orange fruit of any of several citrus trees.

## Examples

- I had orange with breakfast.
- She bought orange at the store.
- This orange tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
