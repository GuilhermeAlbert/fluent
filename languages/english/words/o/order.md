# Order

## Meaning

To order means to give instructions to or direct somebody to do something with authority.

## Examples

- They order him to write the letter.
- I said to him to go home.
- She ordered him to do the shopping.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
