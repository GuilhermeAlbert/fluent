# Ordinary

## Meaning

Ordinary describes someone or something that is not exceptional in any way especially in quality or ability or size or degree.

## Examples

- Ordinary everyday objects.
- Ordinary decency.
- An ordinary day.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
