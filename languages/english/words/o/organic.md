# Organic

## Meaning

Organic describes someone or something that is relating or belonging to the class of chemical compounds having a carbon basis.

## Examples

- Hydrocarbons are organic compounds.
- It feels organic.
- That was an organic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
