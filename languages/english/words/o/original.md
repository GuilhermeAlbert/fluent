# Original

## Meaning

Original describes someone or something that is preceding all others in time or being as first made or performed.

## Examples

- The original inhabitants of the Americas.
- The book still has its original binding.
- Restored the house to its original condition.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
