# Originally

## Meaning

Originally means doing something in a way that is in an manner suggesting originality.

## Examples

- She answered originally.
- It happened originally.
- We finished originally.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
