# Other

## Meaning

Other describes someone or something that is not the same one or ones already mentioned or implied.

## Examples

- Today isn't any other day.
- The construction of highways and other public works.
- He asked for other employment.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
