# Otherwise

## Meaning

Otherwise means doing something in a way that is in other respects or ways.

## Examples

- He is otherwise normal.
- The funds are not otherwise available.
- An otherwise hopeless situation.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
