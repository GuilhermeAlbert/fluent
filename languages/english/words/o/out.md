# Out

## Meaning

Out describes someone or something that is not allowed to continue to bat or run.

## Examples

- He was tagged out at second on a close play.
- He fanned out.
- It feels out.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
