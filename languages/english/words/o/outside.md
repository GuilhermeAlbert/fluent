# Outside

## Meaning

Outside describes something connected with or being on or near the outer side or limit.

## Examples

- An outside margin.
- It feels outside.
- That was an outside choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
