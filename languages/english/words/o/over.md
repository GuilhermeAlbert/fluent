# Over

## Meaning

Over is the division of play during which six balls are bowled at the batsman by one player from the other team from the same end of the pitch.

## Examples

- The over went by quickly.
- We chose a better over.
- That over worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
