# Overcome

## Meaning

To overcome means to win a victory over.

## Examples

- The fighter managed to overcome his opponent.
- You must overcome all difficulties.
- Defeat your enemies.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
