# Owner

## Meaning

Owner means someone who owns a business.

## Examples

- He is the owner of a chain of restaurants.
- The owner asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the owner yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
