# Page

## Meaning

Page means one side of one leaf or the written or pictorial matter it contains.

## Examples

- We talked about the page.
- The page matters here.
- I noticed the page right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
