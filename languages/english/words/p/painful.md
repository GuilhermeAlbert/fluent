# Painful

## Meaning

Painful describes someone or something that is causing physical or psychological pain.

## Examples

- Worked with painful slowness.
- It feels painful.
- That was a painful choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
