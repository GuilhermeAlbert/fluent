# Painting

## Meaning

Painting means graphic art consisting of an artistic composition made by applying paints to a surface.

## Examples

- A small painting by Picasso.
- He bought the painting as an investment.
- His pictures hang in the Louvre.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
