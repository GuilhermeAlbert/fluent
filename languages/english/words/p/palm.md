# Palm

## Meaning

Palm is the inner surface of the hand from the wrist to the base of the fingers.

## Examples

- My palm hurts a little.
- She touched her palm.
- Keep your palm relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
