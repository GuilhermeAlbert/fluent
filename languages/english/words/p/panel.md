# Panel

## Meaning

Panel means sheet that forms a distinct section or component of something.

## Examples

- I left the panel on the table.
- She picked up the panel carefully.
- The panel is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
