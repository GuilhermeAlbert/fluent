# Parent

## Meaning

Parent is a father or mother.

## Examples

- The parent asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the parent yesterday.
- The parent joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
