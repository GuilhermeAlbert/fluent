# Park

## Meaning

Park is a large area of land preserved in its natural state as public property.

## Examples

- There are laws that protect the wildlife in this park.
- We met near the park.
- The park was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
