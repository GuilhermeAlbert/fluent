# Participant

## Meaning

Participant means someone who takes part in an activity.

## Examples

- He was a major player in setting up the corporation.
- The participant asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the participant yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
