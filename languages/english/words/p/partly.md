# Partly

## Meaning

Partly means doing something in a way that is to some extent.

## Examples

- I felt partly to blame.
- He was partially paralyzed.
- She answered partly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
