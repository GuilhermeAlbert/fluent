# Partner

## Meaning

Partner is a person's partner in marriage.

## Examples

- The partner asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the partner yesterday.
- The partner joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
