# Passenger

## Meaning

Passenger is a traveler riding in a vehicle who is not operating it.

## Examples

- The passenger asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the passenger yesterday.
- The passenger joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
