# Patient

## Meaning

Patient means someone who requires medical care.

## Examples

- The number of emergency patients has grown rapidly.
- The patient asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the patient yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
