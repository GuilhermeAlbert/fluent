# Pause

## Meaning

To pause means to interrupt temporarily an activity before continuing.

## Examples

- They pause a long time.
- The speaker paused.
- I need to pause soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
