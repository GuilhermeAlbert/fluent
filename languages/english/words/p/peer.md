# Peer

## Meaning

Peer means someone who is of equal standing with another in a group.

## Examples

- The peer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the peer yesterday.
- The peer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
