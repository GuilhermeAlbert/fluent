# Perceive

## Meaning

To perceive means to become aware of through the senses.

## Examples

- I could perceive the ship coming over the horizon.
- I need to perceive soon.
- They want to perceive together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
