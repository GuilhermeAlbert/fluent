# Perfect

## Meaning

Perfect describes someone or something that is being complete of its kind and without defect or blemish.

## Examples

- A perfect circle.
- A perfect reproduction.
- Perfect happiness.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
