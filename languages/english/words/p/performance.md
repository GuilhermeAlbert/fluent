# Performance

## Meaning

Performance is a dramatic or musical entertainment.

## Examples

- They listened to ten different performances.
- The play ran for 100 performances.
- The frequent performances of the symphony testify to its popularity.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
