# Permission

## Meaning

Permission means approval to do something.

## Examples

- He asked permission to leave.
- I need more permission before I decide.
- We talked about permission after lunch.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
