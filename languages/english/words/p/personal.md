# Personal

## Meaning

Personal describes someone or something that is about or affecting a particular person or his or her private life and personality.

## Examples

- A personal favor.
- For your personal use.
- Personal papers.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
