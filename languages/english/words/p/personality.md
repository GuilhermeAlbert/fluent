# Personality

## Meaning

Personality is the complex of all the attributes - behavioral, temperamental, emotional and mental - that characterize a unique person.

## Examples

- Their different reactions reflected their very different personalities.
- We talked about the personality.
- The personality matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
