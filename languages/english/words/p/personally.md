# Personally

## Meaning

Personally means doing something in a way that is as yourself.

## Examples

- Speaking personally, I would not want to go.
- She answered personally.
- It happened personally.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
