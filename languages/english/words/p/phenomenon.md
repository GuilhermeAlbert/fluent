# Phenomenon

## Meaning

Phenomenon means any state or process known through the senses rather than by intuition or reasoning.

## Examples

- We talked about the phenomenon.
- The phenomenon matters here.
- I noticed the phenomenon right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
