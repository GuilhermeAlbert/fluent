# Philosophy

## Meaning

Philosophy is a belief accepted as authoritative by some group or school.

## Examples

- We talked about the philosophy.
- The philosophy matters here.
- I noticed the philosophy right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
