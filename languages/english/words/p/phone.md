# Phone

## Meaning

Phone means electronic equipment that converts sound into electrical signals that can be transmitted over distances and then converts received signals back into sounds.

## Examples

- I talked to him on the telephone.
- I left the phone on the table.
- She picked up the phone carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
