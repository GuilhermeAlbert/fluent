# Photo

## Meaning

Photo is a representation of a person or scene in the form of a print or transparent slide or in digital format.

## Examples

- I left the photo on the table.
- She picked up the photo carefully.
- The photo is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
