# Photographer

## Meaning

Photographer means someone who takes photographs professionally.

## Examples

- The photographer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the photographer yesterday.
- The photographer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
