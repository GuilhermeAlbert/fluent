# Phrase

## Meaning

Phrase is an expression consisting of one or more words forming a grammatical constituent of a sentence.

## Examples

- We talked about the phrase.
- The phrase matters here.
- I noticed the phrase right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
