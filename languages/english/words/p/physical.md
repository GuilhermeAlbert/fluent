# Physical

## Meaning

Physical describes someone or something that is involving the body as distinguished from the mind or spirit.

## Examples

- Physical exercise.
- Physical suffering.
- Was sloppy about everything but her physical appearance.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
