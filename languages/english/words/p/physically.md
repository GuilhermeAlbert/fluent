# Physically

## Meaning

Physically means doing something in a way that is in accord with physical laws.

## Examples

- It is physically impossible.
- She answered physically.
- It happened physically.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
