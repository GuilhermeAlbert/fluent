# Physician

## Meaning

Physician is a licensed medical practitioner.

## Examples

- I felt so bad I went to see my doctor.
- The physician asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the physician yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
