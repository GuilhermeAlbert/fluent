# Piano

## Meaning

Piano is a keyboard instrument that is played by depressing keys that cause hammers to strike tuned strings and produce sounds.

## Examples

- I left the piano on the table.
- She picked up the piano carefully.
- The piano is in the kitchen.

## Notes
