# Picture

## Meaning

Picture is a visual representation produced on a surface.

## Examples

- They showed us the pictures of their wedding.
- A movie is a series of images projected so rapidly that the eye integrates them.
- I left the picture on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
