# Pie

## Meaning

Pie means dish baked in pastry-lined pan often with a pastry top.

## Examples

- I had pie with breakfast.
- She bought pie at the store.
- This pie tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
