# Piece

## Meaning

Piece is a separate part of a whole.

## Examples

- An important piece of the evidence.
- I left the piece on the table.
- She picked up the piece carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
