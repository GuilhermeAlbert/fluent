# Pilot

## Meaning

Pilot means someone who is licensed to operate an aircraft in flight.

## Examples

- The pilot asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the pilot yesterday.
- The pilot joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
