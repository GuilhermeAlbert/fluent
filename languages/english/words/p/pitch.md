# Pitch

## Meaning

To pitch means to throw or toss with a light motion.

## Examples

- The children pitch the ball.
- Flip me the beachball.
- Toss me newspaper.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
