# Plane

## Meaning

Plane is an aircraft that has a fixed wing and is powered by propellers or jets.

## Examples

- The flight was delayed due to trouble with the airplane.
- I left the plane on the table.
- She picked up the plane carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
