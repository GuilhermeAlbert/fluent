# Planet

## Meaning

Planet means any of the nine large celestial bodies in the solar system that revolve around the sun and shine by reflected light.

## Examples

- We talked about the planet.
- The planet matters here.
- I noticed the planet right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
