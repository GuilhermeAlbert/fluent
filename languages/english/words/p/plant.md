# Plant

## Meaning

To plant means to put or set into the ground.

## Examples

- They plant rye in the field.
- Let's plant flowers in the garden.
- I need to plant this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
