# Plastic

## Meaning

Plastic describes someone or something that is capable of being molded or modeled.

## Examples

- Plastic substances such as wax or clay.
- It feels plastic.
- That was a plastic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
