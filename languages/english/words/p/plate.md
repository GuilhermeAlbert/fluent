# Plate

## Meaning

Plate means base consisting of a rubber slab where the batter stands.

## Examples

- He ruled that the runner failed to touch home.
- I left the plate on the table.
- She picked up the plate carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
