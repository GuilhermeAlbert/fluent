# Player

## Meaning

Player means someone who participates in or is skilled at some game.

## Examples

- The player asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the player yesterday.
- The player joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
