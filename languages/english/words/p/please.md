# Please

## Meaning

To please means to give pleasure to or be pleasing to.

## Examples

- The good news will please her.
- The performance is likely to please Sue.
- These colors please the senses.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
