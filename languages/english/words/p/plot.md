# Plot

## Meaning

To plot means to plan secretly, usually something illegal.

## Examples

- They plot to move.
- They plotted the overthrow of the government.
- I need to plot the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
