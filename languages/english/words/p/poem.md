# Poem

## Meaning

Poem is a composition written in metrical feet forming rhythmical lines.

## Examples

- We talked about the poem.
- The poem matters here.
- I noticed the poem right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
