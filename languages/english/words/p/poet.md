# Poet

## Meaning

Poet is a writer of poems.

## Examples

- The poet asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the poet yesterday.
- The poet joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
