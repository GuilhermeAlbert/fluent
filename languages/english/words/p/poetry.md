# Poetry

## Meaning

Poetry means literature in metrical form.

## Examples

- We talked about the poetry.
- The poetry matters here.
- I noticed the poetry right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
