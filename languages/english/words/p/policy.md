# Policy

## Meaning

Policy is a plan of action adopted by an person or social group.

## Examples

- It was a policy of retribution.
- A politician keeps changing his policies.
- We talked about the policy.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
