# Politically

## Meaning

Politically means doing something in a way that is with regard to social relationships involving authority.

## Examples

- Politically correct clothing.
- She answered politically.
- It happened politically.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
