# Politician

## Meaning

Politician is a leader engaged in civil administration.

## Examples

- The politician asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the politician yesterday.
- The politician joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
