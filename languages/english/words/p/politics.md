# Politics

## Meaning

Politics means social relations involving intrigue to gain authority or power.

## Examples

- Office politics is often counterproductive.
- We talked about the politics.
- The politics matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
