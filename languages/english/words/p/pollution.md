# Pollution

## Meaning

Pollution means undesirable state of the natural environment being contaminated with harmful substances as a consequence of human activities.

## Examples

- We talked about the pollution.
- The pollution matters here.
- I noticed the pollution right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
