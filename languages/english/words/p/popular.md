# Popular

## Meaning

Popular describes someone or something that is regarded with great favor, approval, or affection especially by the general public.

## Examples

- A popular tourist attraction.
- A popular girl.
- Cabbage patch dolls are no longer popular.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
