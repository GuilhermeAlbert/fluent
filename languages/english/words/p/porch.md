# Porch

## Meaning

Porch is a structure attached to the exterior of a building often forming a covered entrance.

## Examples

- I left the porch on the table.
- She picked up the porch carefully.
- The porch is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
