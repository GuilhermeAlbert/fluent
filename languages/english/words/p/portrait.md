# Portrait

## Meaning

Portrait is a word picture of a person's appearance and character.

## Examples

- We talked about the portrait.
- The portrait matters here.
- I noticed the portrait right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
