# Portray

## Meaning

To portray means to portray in words.

## Examples

- The book portrays the actor as a selfish person.
- I need to portray this clearly.
- She tried to portray the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
