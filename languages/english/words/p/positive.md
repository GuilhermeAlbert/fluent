# Positive

## Meaning

Positive describes someone or something that is characterized by or displaying affirmation or acceptance or certainty etc.

## Examples

- A positive attitude.
- The reviews were all positive.
- A positive benefit.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
