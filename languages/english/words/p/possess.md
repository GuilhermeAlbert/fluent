# Possess

## Meaning

To possess means to have as an attribute, knowledge, or skill.

## Examples

- He possesses great knowledge about the Middle East.
- I need to possess soon.
- They want to possess together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
