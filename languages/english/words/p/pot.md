# Pot

## Meaning

Pot means metal or earthenware cooking vessel that is usually round and deep.

## Examples

- I left the pot on the table.
- She picked up the pot carefully.
- The pot is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
