# Potential

## Meaning

Potential describes someone or something that is existing in possibility.

## Examples

- A potential problem.
- Possible uses of nuclear power.
- It feels potential.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
