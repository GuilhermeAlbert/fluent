# Potentially

## Meaning

Potentially means doing something in a way that is with a possibility of becoming actual.

## Examples

- He is potentially dangerous.
- Potentially useful.
- She answered potentially.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
