# Power

## Meaning

Power means haveion of controlling influence.

## Examples

- The deterrent power of nuclear weapons.
- The power of his love saved her.
- His powerfulness was concealed by a gentle facade.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
