# Practical

## Meaning

Practical describes someone or something that is concerned with actual use or practice.

## Examples

- He is a very practical person.
- The idea had no practical application.
- A practical knowledge of Japanese.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
