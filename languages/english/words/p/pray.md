# Pray

## Meaning

To pray means to address a deity, a prophet, a saint or an object of worship.

## Examples

- Pray to the Lord.
- I need to pray this clearly.
- She tried to pray the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
