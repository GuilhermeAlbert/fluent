# Prefer

## Meaning

To prefer means to like better.

## Examples

- They prefer him to write the letter.
- Some people prefer camping to staying in hotels.
- We prefer sleeping outside.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
