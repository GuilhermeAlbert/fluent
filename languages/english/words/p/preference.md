# Preference

## Meaning

Preference is a strong liking.

## Examples

- My own preference is for good literature.
- The Irish have a penchant for blarney.
- We talked about the preference.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
