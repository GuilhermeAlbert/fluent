# Pregnant

## Meaning

Pregnant describes someone or something that is carrying developing offspring within the body or being about to produce new life.

## Examples

- It feels pregnant.
- That was a pregnant choice.
- The room looked pregnant.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
