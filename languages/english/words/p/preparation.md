# Preparation

## Meaning

Preparation is the activity of putting or setting in order in advance of some act or purpose.

## Examples

- Preparations for the ceremony had begun.
- The preparation starts at nine.
- We planned the preparation together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
