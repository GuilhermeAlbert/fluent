# Prescription

## Meaning

Prescription means directions prescribed beforehand.

## Examples

- I tried to follow her prescription for success.
- We talked about the prescription.
- The prescription matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
