# Presentation

## Meaning

Presentation is the activity of formally presenting something.

## Examples

- She gave the trophy but he made the presentation.
- The presentation starts at nine.
- We planned the presentation together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
