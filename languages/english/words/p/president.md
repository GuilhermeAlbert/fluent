# President

## Meaning

President is an executive officer of a firm or corporation.

## Examples

- The president asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the president yesterday.
- The president joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
