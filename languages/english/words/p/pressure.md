# Pressure

## Meaning

Pressure is the force applied to a unit area of surface.

## Examples

- The compressed gas exerts an increased pressure.
- We talked about the pressure.
- The pressure matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
