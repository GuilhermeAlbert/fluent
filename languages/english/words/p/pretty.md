# Pretty

## Meaning

Pretty describes someone or something that is pleasing by delicacy or grace.

## Examples

- Pretty girl.
- Pretty song.
- Pretty room.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
