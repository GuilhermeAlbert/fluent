# Previously

## Meaning

Previously means doing something in a way that is at an earlier time or formerly.

## Examples

- She had previously lived in Chicago.
- He was previously president of a bank.
- Better than anything previously proposed.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
