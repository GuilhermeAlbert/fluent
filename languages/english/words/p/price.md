# Price

## Meaning

Price is the property of having material worth.

## Examples

- The fluctuating monetary value of gold and silver.
- He puts a high price on his services.
- He couldn't calculate the cost of the collection.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
