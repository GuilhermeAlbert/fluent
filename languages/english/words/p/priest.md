# Priest

## Meaning

Priest is a clergyman in Christian churches who has the authority to perform or administer various religious rites.

## Examples

- The priest asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the priest yesterday.
- The priest joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
