# Print

## Meaning

Print is the text appearing in a book, newspaper, or other printed publication.

## Examples

- I want to see it in print.
- We talked about the print.
- The print matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
