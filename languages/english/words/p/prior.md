# Prior

## Meaning

Prior is the head of a religious order.

## Examples

- The prior asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the prior yesterday.
- The prior joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
