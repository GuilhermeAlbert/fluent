# Priority

## Meaning

Priority means status established in order of importance or urgency.

## Examples

- ... its precedence as the world's leading manufacturer of pharmaceuticals.
- National independence takes priority over class struggle.
- We talked about the priority.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
