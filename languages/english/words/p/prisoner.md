# Prisoner

## Meaning

Prisoner means someone who is confined.

## Examples

- The prisoner asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the prisoner yesterday.
- The prisoner joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
