# Producer

## Meaning

Producer means someone who manufactures something.

## Examples

- The producer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the producer yesterday.
- The producer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
