# Product

## Meaning

Product means items for sale to the person consumer.

## Examples

- Good business depends on having good merchandise.
- That store offers a variety of products.
- I left the product on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
