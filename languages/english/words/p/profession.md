# Profession

## Meaning

Profession is the body of people in a learned occupation.

## Examples

- The news spread rapidly through the medical profession.
- We talked about the profession.
- The profession matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
