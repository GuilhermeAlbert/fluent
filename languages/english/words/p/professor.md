# Professor

## Meaning

Professor means someone who is a member of the faculty at a college or university.

## Examples

- The professor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the professor yesterday.
- The professor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
