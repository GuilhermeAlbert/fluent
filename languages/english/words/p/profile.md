# Profile

## Meaning

Profile is an analysis representing the extent to which something exhibits various characteristics.

## Examples

- A biochemical profile of blood.
- A psychological profile of serial killers.
- We talked about the profile.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
