# Profit

## Meaning

Profit is the excess of revenues over outlays in a given period of time.

## Examples

- We talked about the profit.
- The profit matters here.
- I noticed the profit right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
