# Program

## Meaning

Program is a series of steps to be carried out or goals to be accomplished.

## Examples

- They drew up a six-step plan.
- They discussed plans for a new bond issue.
- We talked about the program.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
