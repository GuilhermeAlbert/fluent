# Progress

## Meaning

Progress means gradual improvement or growth or development.

## Examples

- Advancement of knowledge.
- Great progress in the arts.
- I need more progress before I decide.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
