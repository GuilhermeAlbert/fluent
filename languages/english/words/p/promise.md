# Promise

## Meaning

To promise means to make a promise or commitment.

## Examples

- They promise him to write the letter.
- I need to promise this clearly.
- She tried to promise the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
