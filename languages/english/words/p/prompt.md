# Prompt

## Meaning

To prompt means to give an incentive for action.

## Examples

- They prompt him to write the letter.
- This moved me to sacrifice my career.
- I need to prompt this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
