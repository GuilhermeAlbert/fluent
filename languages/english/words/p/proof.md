# Proof

## Meaning

Proof means any factual evidence that helps to establish the truth of something.

## Examples

- If you have any proof for what you say, now is the time to produce it.
- We talked about the proof.
- The proof matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
