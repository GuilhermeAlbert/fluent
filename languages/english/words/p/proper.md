# Proper

## Meaning

Proper describes someone or something that is marked by suitability or rightness or appropriateness.

## Examples

- Proper medical treatment.
- Proper manners.
- It feels proper.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
