# Proportion

## Meaning

Proportion is the quotient geted when the magnitude of a part is divided by the magnitude of the whole.

## Examples

- We talked about the proportion.
- The proportion matters here.
- I noticed the proportion right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
