# Proposed

## Meaning

Proposed describes something suggested but not yet finally accepted.

## Examples

- The proposed plan looks expensive.
- We discussed the proposed changes.
- The proposed rule starts next year.

## Notes

Common before nouns like "plan," "law," "change," and "solution."
