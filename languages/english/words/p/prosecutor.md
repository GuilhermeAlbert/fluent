# Prosecutor

## Meaning

Prosecutor is a government official who conducts criminal prosecutions on behalf of the state.

## Examples

- The prosecutor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the prosecutor yesterday.
- The prosecutor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
