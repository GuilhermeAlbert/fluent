# Protect

## Meaning

To protect means to shield from danger, injury, destruction, or damage.

## Examples

- Weatherbeater protects your roof from the rain.
- I need to protect soon.
- They want to protect together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
