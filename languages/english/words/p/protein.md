# Protein

## Meaning

Protein means any of a large group of nitrogenous organic compounds that are essential constituents of living cells.

## Examples

- A diet high in protein.
- We talked about the protein.
- The protein matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
