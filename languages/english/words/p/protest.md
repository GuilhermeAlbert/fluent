# Protest

## Meaning

Protest is a formal and solemn declaration of objection.

## Examples

- They finished the game under protest to the league president.
- The senator rose to register his protest.
- The many protestations did not stay the execution.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
