# Proud

## Meaning

Proud describes someone or something that is feeling self-respect or pleasure in something by which you measure your self-worth.

## Examples

- Proud parents.
- A proud moment.
- Proud to serve his country.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
