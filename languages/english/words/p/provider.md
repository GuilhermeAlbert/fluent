# Provider

## Meaning

Provider means someone whose business is to supply a particular service or commodity.

## Examples

- The provider asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the provider yesterday.
- The provider joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
