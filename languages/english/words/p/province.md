# Province

## Meaning

Province is the territory occupied by one of the constituent administrative districts of a nation.

## Examples

- His state is in the deep south.
- We met near the province.
- The province was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
