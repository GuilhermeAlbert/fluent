# Psychological

## Meaning

Psychological describes someone or something that is mental or emotional as opposed to physical in nature.

## Examples

- Give psychological support.
- Psychological warfare.
- It feels psychological.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
