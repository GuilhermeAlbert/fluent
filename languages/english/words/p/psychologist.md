# Psychologist

## Meaning

Psychologist is a scientist trained in psychology.

## Examples

- The psychologist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the psychologist yesterday.
- The psychologist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
