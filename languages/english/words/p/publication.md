# Publication

## Meaning

Publication is a copy of a printed work offered for distribution.

## Examples

- We talked about the publication.
- The publication matters here.
- I noticed the publication right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
