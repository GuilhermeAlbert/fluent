# Publicly

## Meaning

Publicly means doing something in a way that is in a manner accessible to or observable by the public.

## Examples

- She admitted publicly to being a communist.
- She answered publicly.
- It happened publicly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
