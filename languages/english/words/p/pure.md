# Pure

## Meaning

Pure describes someone or something that is free of extraneous elements of any kind.

## Examples

- Pure air and water.
- Pure gold.
- Pure primary colors.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
