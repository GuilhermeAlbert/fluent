# Qualify

## Meaning

To qualify means to prove capable or fit.

## Examples

- I need to qualify soon.
- They want to qualify together.
- Please qualify when you are ready.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
