# Quarterback

## Meaning

Quarterback is the person who plays quarterback.

## Examples

- The quarterback asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the quarterback yesterday.
- The quarterback joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
