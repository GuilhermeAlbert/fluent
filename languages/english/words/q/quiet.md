# Quiet

## Meaning

Quiet describes someone or something that is characterized by an absence or near absence of agitation or activity.

## Examples

- A quiet life.
- A quiet throng of onlookers.
- Quiet peace-loving people.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
