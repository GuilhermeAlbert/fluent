# Quote

## Meaning

To quote means to repeat a passage from.

## Examples

- The parents quote a French poem to the children.
- The parents quote the children a French poem.
- He quoted the Bible to her.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
