# Rain

## Meaning

Rain means water falling in drops from vapor condensed in the atmosphere.

## Examples

- I need more rain before I decide.
- We talked about rain after lunch.
- That rain really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
