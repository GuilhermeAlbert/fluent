# Range

## Meaning

Range is an area in which something acts or operates or has power or control:.

## Examples

- A piano has a greater range than the human voice.
- The ambit of municipal legislation.
- Within the compass of this article.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
