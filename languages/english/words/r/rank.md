# Rank

## Meaning

Rank is a row or line of people standing abreast of one another.

## Examples

- The entrance was guarded by ranks of policemen.
- We talked about the rank.
- The rank matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
