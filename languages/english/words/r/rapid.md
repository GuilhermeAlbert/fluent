# Rapid

## Meaning

Rapid describes someone or something that is done or occurring in a brief period of time.

## Examples

- A rapid rise through the ranks.
- It feels rapid.
- That was a rapid choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
