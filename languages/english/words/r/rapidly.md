# Rapidly

## Meaning

Rapidly means doing something in a way that is with speed.

## Examples

- He works quickly.
- John got ready in short order.
- She answered rapidly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
