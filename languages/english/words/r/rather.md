# Rather

## Meaning

Rather means doing something in a way that is on the contrary.

## Examples

- Rather than disappoint the children, he did two quick tricks before he left.
- He didn't call; rather (or instead), he wrote her a letter.
- Used English terms instead of Latin ones.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
