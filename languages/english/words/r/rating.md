# Rating

## Meaning

Rating is an appraisal of the value of something.

## Examples

- He set a high valuation on friendship.
- We talked about the rating.
- The rating matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
