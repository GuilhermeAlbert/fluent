# Raw

## Meaning

Raw describes someone or something that is being unprocessed or manufactured using only simple or minimal processes.

## Examples

- Natural yogurt.
- Natural produce.
- Raw wool.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
