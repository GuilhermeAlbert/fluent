# Reach

## Meaning

To reach means to reach a destination, either real or abstract.

## Examples

- They reach the hill.
- We hit Detroit by noon.
- The water reached the doorstep.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
