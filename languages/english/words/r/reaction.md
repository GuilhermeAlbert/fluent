# Reaction

## Meaning

Reaction is a process in which one or more substances are changed into others.

## Examples

- There was a chemical reaction of the lime with the ground water.
- We talked about the reaction.
- The reaction matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
