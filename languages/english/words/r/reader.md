# Reader

## Meaning

Reader means someone who enjoys reading.

## Examples

- The reader asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the reader yesterday.
- The reader joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
