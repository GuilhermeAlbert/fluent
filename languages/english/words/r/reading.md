# Reading

## Meaning

Reading is the cognitive process of understanding a written linguistic message.

## Examples

- His main reading was detective stories.
- Suggestions for further reading.
- We talked about the reading.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
