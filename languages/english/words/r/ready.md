# Ready

## Meaning

Ready describes someone or something that is completely prepared or in condition for immediate action or use or progress.

## Examples

- Get ready.
- She is ready to resign.
- The bridge is ready to collapse.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
