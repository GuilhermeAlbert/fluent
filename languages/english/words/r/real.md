# Real

## Meaning

Real describes someone or something that is being or occurring in fact or actuality.

## Examples

- Real objects.
- Real people, not ghosts.
- A film based on real life.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
