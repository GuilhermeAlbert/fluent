# Reality

## Meaning

Reality means all of your experiences that determine how things appear to you.

## Examples

- His world was shattered.
- We live in different worlds.
- For them demons were as much a part of reality as trees were.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
