# Realize

## Meaning

To realize means to be fully aware or cognizant of.

## Examples

- I need to realize the problem.
- They tried to realize what happened.
- Can you realize this for me?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
