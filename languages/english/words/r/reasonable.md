# Reasonable

## Meaning

Reasonable describes someone or something that is showing reason or sound judgment.

## Examples

- A sensible choice.
- A sensible person.
- It feels reasonable.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
