# Recommendation

## Meaning

Recommendation means something that is recommended as advisable.

## Examples

- We talked about the recommendation.
- The recommendation matters here.
- I noticed the recommendation right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
