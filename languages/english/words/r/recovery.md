# Recovery

## Meaning

Recovery means return to an original state.

## Examples

- The recovery of the forest after the fire was surprisingly rapid.
- The recovery starts at nine.
- We planned the recovery together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
