# Recruit

## Meaning

To recruit means to register formally as a participant or member.

## Examples

- The party recruited many new members.
- I need to recruit soon.
- They want to recruit together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
