# Reference

## Meaning

Reference is a remark that calls attention to something or someone.

## Examples

- She made frequent mention of her promotion.
- There was no mention of it.
- The speaker made several references to his wife.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
