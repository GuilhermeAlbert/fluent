# Reflect

## Meaning

To reflect means to manifest or bring back.

## Examples

- This action reflects his true beliefs.
- I need to reflect soon.
- They want to reflect together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
