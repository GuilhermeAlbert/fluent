# Reflection

## Meaning

Reflection is a calm, lengthy, intent consideration.

## Examples

- We talked about the reflection.
- The reflection matters here.
- I noticed the reflection right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
