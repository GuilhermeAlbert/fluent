# Reform

## Meaning

To reform means to make changes for improvement in order to remove abuse and injustices.

## Examples

- Reform a political system.
- I need to reform this today.
- They plan to reform it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
