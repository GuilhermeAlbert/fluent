# Refugee

## Meaning

Refugee is an exile who flees for safety.

## Examples

- The refugee asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the refugee yesterday.
- The refugee joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
