# Refuse

## Meaning

To refuse means to show unwillingness towards.

## Examples

- They refuse to move.
- He declined to join the group on a hike.
- I need to refuse this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
