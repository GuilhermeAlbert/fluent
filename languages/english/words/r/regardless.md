# Regardless

## Meaning

Regardless describes someone or something that is without due thought or consideration.

## Examples

- Careless of the consequences.
- Crushing the blooms with regardless tread.
- It feels regardless.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
