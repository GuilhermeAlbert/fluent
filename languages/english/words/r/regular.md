# Regular

## Meaning

Regular describes someone or something that is in accordance with fixed order or procedure or principle.

## Examples

- His regular calls on his customers.
- Regular meals.
- Regular duties.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
