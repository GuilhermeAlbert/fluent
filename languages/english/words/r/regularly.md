# Regularly

## Meaning

Regularly means doing something in a way that is in a regular manner.

## Examples

- Letters arrived regularly from his children.
- She answered regularly.
- It happened regularly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
