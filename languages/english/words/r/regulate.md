# Regulate

## Meaning

To regulate means to fix or adjust the time, amount, degree, or rate of.

## Examples

- Regulate the temperature.
- Modulate the pitch.
- I need to regulate this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
