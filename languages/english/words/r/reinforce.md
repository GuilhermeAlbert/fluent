# Reinforce

## Meaning

To reinforce means to make stronger.

## Examples

- He reinforced the concrete.
- I need to reinforce this today.
- They plan to reinforce it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
