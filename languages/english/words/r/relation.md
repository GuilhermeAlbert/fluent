# Relation

## Meaning

Relation is an abstraction belonging to or characteristic of two entities or parts together.

## Examples

- We talked about the relation.
- The relation matters here.
- I noticed the relation right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
