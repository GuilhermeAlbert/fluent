# Relatively

## Meaning

Relatively means doing something in a way that is in a relative manner.

## Examples

- The situation is relatively calm now.
- She answered relatively.
- It happened relatively.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
