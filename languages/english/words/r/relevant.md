# Relevant

## Meaning

Relevant describes someone or something that is having a bearing on or connection with the subject at issue.

## Examples

- The scientist corresponds with colleagues in order to learn about matters relevant to her own research.
- It feels relevant.
- That was a relevant choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
