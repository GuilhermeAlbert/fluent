# Relief

## Meaning

Relief is the feeling that comes when something burdensome is removed or reduced.

## Examples

- As he heard the news he was suddenly flooded with relief.
- We talked about the relief.
- The relief matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
