# Religion

## Meaning

Religion is a strong belief in a supernatural power or powers that control human destiny.

## Examples

- He lost his faith but not his morality.
- We talked about the religion.
- The religion matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
