# Remarkable

## Meaning

Remarkable describes someone or something that is unusual or striking.

## Examples

- A remarkable sight.
- Such poise is singular in one so young.
- It feels remarkable.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
