# Remind

## Meaning

To remind means to put in the mind of someone.

## Examples

- They remind him to write the letter.
- Remind me to call Mother.
- I need to remind the problem.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
