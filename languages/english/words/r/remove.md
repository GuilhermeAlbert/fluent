# Remove

## Meaning

To remove means to remove something concrete, as by lifting, pushing, or taking off, or remove something abstract.

## Examples

- They want to remove the doors.
- Remove a threat.
- Remove a wrapper.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
