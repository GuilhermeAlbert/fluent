# Repeat

## Meaning

To repeat means to say, state, or perform again.

## Examples

- They won't repeat the story.
- She kept reiterating her request.
- I need to repeat this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
