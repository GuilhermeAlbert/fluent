# Repeatedly

## Meaning

Repeatedly means doing something in a way that is repeatedly.

## Examples

- The unknown word turned up over and over again in the text.
- She answered repeatedly.
- It happened repeatedly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
