# Report

## Meaning

To report means to give an account or representation of in words.

## Examples

- They report that there was a traffic accident.
- Discreet Italian police described it in a manner typically continental.
- I need to report this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
