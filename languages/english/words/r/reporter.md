# Reporter

## Meaning

Reporter means someone who investigates and reports or edits news stories.

## Examples

- The reporter asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the reporter yesterday.
- The reporter joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
