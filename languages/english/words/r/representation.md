# Representation

## Meaning

Representation is a presentation to the mind in the form of an idea or image.

## Examples

- We talked about the representation.
- The representation matters here.
- I noticed the representation right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
