# Representative

## Meaning

Representative describes someone or something that is serving to represent or typify.

## Examples

- Representative moviegoers.
- A representative modern play.
- It feels representative.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
