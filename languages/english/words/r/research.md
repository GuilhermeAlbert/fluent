# Research

## Meaning

To research means to attempt to find out in a systematically and scientific manner.

## Examples

- The student researched the history of that word.
- I need to research this clearly.
- She tried to research the idea.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
