# Researcher

## Meaning

Researcher is a scientist who devotes themself to doing research.

## Examples

- The researcher asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the researcher yesterday.
- The researcher joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
