# Resident

## Meaning

Resident means someone who lives at a particular place for a prolonged period or who was born there.

## Examples

- The resident asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the resident yesterday.
- The resident joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
