# Responsible

## Meaning

Responsible describes someone or something that is worthy of or requiring responsibility or trust.

## Examples

- A responsible adult.
- Responsible journalism.
- A responsible position.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
