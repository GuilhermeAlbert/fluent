# Restore

## Meaning

To restore means to return to its original or usable and functioning condition.

## Examples

- Restore the forest to its original pristine condition.
- I need to restore soon.
- They want to restore together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
