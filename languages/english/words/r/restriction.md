# Restriction

## Meaning

Restriction is a principle that limits the extent of something.

## Examples

- I am willing to accept certain restrictions on my movements.
- We talked about the restriction.
- The restriction matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
