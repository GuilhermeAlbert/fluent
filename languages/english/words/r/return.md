# Return

## Meaning

To return means to go or come back to place, condition, or activity where one has been before.

## Examples

- The children return to the playground.
- Return to your native land.
- The professor returned to his teaching position after serving as Dean.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
