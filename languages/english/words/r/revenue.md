# Revenue

## Meaning

Revenue is the entire amount of income before any deductions are made.

## Examples

- We talked about the revenue.
- The revenue matters here.
- I noticed the revenue right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
