# Revolution

## Meaning

Revolution is a drastic and far-reaching change in ways of thinking and behaving.

## Examples

- The industrial revolution was also a cultural revolution.
- The revolution starts at nine.
- We planned the revolution together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
