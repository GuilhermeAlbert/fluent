# Rhythm

## Meaning

Rhythm is the basic rhythmic unit in a piece of music.

## Examples

- The piece has a fast rhythm.
- The conductor set the beat.
- We talked about the rhythm.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
