# Rice

## Meaning

Rice means grains used as food either unpolished or more often polished.

## Examples

- I need more rice before I decide.
- We talked about rice after lunch.
- That rice really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
