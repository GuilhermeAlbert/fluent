# Right

## Meaning

Right describes someone or something that is being or located on or directed toward the side of the body to the east when facing north.

## Examples

- My right hand.
- Right center field.
- A right-hand turn.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
