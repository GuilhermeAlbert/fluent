# Risk

## Meaning

To risk means to expose to a chance of loss or damage.

## Examples

- We risked losing a lot of money in this venture.
- Why risk your life?
- She laid her job on the line when she told the boss that he was wrong.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
