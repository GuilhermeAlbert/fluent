# Romantic

## Meaning

Romantic describes someone or something that is belonging to or characteristic of Romanticism or the Romantic Movement in the arts.

## Examples

- Romantic poetry.
- It feels romantic.
- That was a romantic choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
