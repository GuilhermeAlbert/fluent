# Room

## Meaning

Room is an area within a building enclosed by walls and floor and ceiling.

## Examples

- The rooms were very small but they had a nice view.
- I left the room on the table.
- She picked up the room carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
