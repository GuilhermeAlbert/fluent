# Rose

## Meaning

Rose means any of many shrubs of the genus Rosa that bear roses.

## Examples

- We talked about the rose.
- The rose matters here.
- I noticed the rose right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
