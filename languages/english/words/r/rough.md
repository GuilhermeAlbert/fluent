# Rough

## Meaning

Rough describes someone or something that is having or caused by an irregular surface.

## Examples

- Trees with rough bark.
- Rough ground.
- Rough skin.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
