# Rule

## Meaning

Rule is a principle or condition that customarily governs behavior.

## Examples

- It was his rule to take a walk before breakfast.
- Short haircuts were the regulation.
- We talked about the rule.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
