# Running

## Meaning

Running describes someone or something that is moving or issuing in a stream.

## Examples

- As mountain stream with freely running water.
- Hovels without running water.
- It feels running.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
