# Rural

## Meaning

Rural describes something connected with the countryside as opposed to the city.

## Examples

- Rural people.
- Large rural households.
- Unpaved rural roads.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
