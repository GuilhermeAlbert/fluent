# Rush

## Meaning

To rush means to move hurridly.

## Examples

- They rush up the hill.
- He rushed down the hall to receive his guests.
- The cars raced down the street.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
