# Sacred

## Meaning

Sacred describes someone or something that is concerned with religion or religious purposes.

## Examples

- Sacred texts.
- Sacred rites.
- Sacred music.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
