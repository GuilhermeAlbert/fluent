# Sad

## Meaning

Sad describes someone or something that is experiencing or showing sorrow or unhappiness.

## Examples

- Feeling sad because his dog had died.
- It feels sad.
- That was a sad choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
