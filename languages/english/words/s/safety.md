# Safety

## Meaning

Safety is the state of being certain that adverse effects will not be caused by some agent under defined conditions.

## Examples

- Insure the safety of the children.
- The reciprocal of safety is risk.
- We talked about the safety.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
