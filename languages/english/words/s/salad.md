# Salad

## Meaning

Salad means food mixtures either arranged on a plate or tossed and served with a moist dressing.

## Examples

- I had salad with breakfast.
- She bought salad at the store.
- This salad tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
