# Sample

## Meaning

Sample is a small part of something intended as representative of the whole.

## Examples

- We talked about the sample.
- The sample matters here.
- I noticed the sample right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
