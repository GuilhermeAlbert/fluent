# Sand

## Meaning

Sand is a loose material consisting of grains of rock or coral.

## Examples

- I need more sand before I decide.
- We talked about sand after lunch.
- That sand really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
