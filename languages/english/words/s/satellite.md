# Satellite

## Meaning

Satellite means man-made equipment that orbits around the earth or the moon.

## Examples

- I left the satellite on the table.
- She picked up the satellite carefully.
- The satellite is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
