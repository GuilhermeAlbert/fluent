# Satisfaction

## Meaning

Satisfaction is the contentment one feels when one has fulfilled a desire, need, or expectation.

## Examples

- The chef tasted the sauce with great satisfaction.
- We talked about the satisfaction.
- The satisfaction matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
