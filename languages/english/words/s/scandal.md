# Scandal

## Meaning

Scandal means disgraceful gossip about the private lives of other people.

## Examples

- We talked about the scandal.
- The scandal matters here.
- I noticed the scandal right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
