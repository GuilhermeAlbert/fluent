# Scared

## Meaning

Scared describes someone or something that is made afraid.

## Examples

- The frightened child cowered in the corner.
- Too shocked and scared to move.
- It feels scared.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
