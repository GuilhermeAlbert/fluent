# Scholar

## Meaning

Scholar is a learned person.

## Examples

- The scholar asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the scholar yesterday.
- The scholar joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
