# Scholarship

## Meaning

Scholarship means financial aid provided to a student on the basis of academic merit.

## Examples

- We talked about the scholarship.
- The scholarship matters here.
- I noticed the scholarship right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
