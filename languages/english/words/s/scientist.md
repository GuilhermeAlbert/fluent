# Scientist

## Meaning

Scientist is a person with advanced knowledge of one or more sciences.

## Examples

- The scientist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the scientist yesterday.
- The scientist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
