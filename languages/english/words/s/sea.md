# Sea

## Meaning

Sea is a division of an ocean or a large body of salt water partially enclosed by land.

## Examples

- We talked about the sea.
- The sea matters here.
- I noticed the sea right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
