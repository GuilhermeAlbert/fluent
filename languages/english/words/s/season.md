# Season

## Meaning

Season is a period of the year marked by special events or activities in some field.

## Examples

- He celebrated his 10th season with the ballet company.
- She always looked forward to the avocado season.
- The season went by quickly.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
