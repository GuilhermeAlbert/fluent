# Secretary

## Meaning

Secretary means someone who is head of an administrative department of government.

## Examples

- The secretary asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the secretary yesterday.
- The secretary joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
