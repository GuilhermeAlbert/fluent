# Section

## Meaning

Section is a self-contained part of a larger composition.

## Examples

- He always turns first to the business section.
- The history of this work is discussed in the next section.
- We talked about the section.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
