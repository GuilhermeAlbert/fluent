# Sector

## Meaning

Sector is a plane figure bounded by two radii and the included arc of a circle.

## Examples

- We talked about the sector.
- The sector matters here.
- I noticed the sector right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
