# Sell

## Meaning

To sell means to exchange or deliver for money or its equivalent.

## Examples

- They sell the food to the people.
- They sell the people the food.
- He sold his house in January.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
