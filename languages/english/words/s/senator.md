# Senator

## Meaning

Senator is a member of a senate.

## Examples

- The senator asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the senator yesterday.
- The senator joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
