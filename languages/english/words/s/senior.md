# Senior

## Meaning

Senior is an undergraduate student during the year preceding graduation.

## Examples

- The senior asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the senior yesterday.
- The senior joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
