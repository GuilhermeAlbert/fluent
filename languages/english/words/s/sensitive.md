# Sensitive

## Meaning

Sensitive describes someone or something that is responsive to physical stimuli.

## Examples

- A mimosa's leaves are sensitive to touch.
- A sensitive voltmeter.
- Sensitive skin.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
