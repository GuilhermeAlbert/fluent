# Sentence

## Meaning

Sentence is a string of words satisfying the grammatical rules of a language.

## Examples

- He always spoke in grammatical sentences.
- We talked about the sentence.
- The sentence matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
