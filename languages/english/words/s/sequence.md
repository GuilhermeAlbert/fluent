# Sequence

## Meaning

Sequence means serial arrangement in which things follow in logical order or a recurrent pattern.

## Examples

- The sequence of names was alphabetical.
- He invented a technique to determine the sequence of base pairs in DNA.
- We talked about the sequence.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
