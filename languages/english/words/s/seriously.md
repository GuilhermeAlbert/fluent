# Seriously

## Meaning

Seriously means doing something in a way that is in a serious manner.

## Examples

- Talking earnestly with his son.
- She started studying snakes in earnest.
- A play dealing seriously with the question of divorce.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
