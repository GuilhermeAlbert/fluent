# Setting

## Meaning

Setting is the context and environment in which something is set.

## Examples

- The perfect setting for a ghost story.
- We met near the setting.
- The setting was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
