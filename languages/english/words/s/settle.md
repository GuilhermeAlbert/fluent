# Settle

## Meaning

To settle means to settle into a position, usually on a surface or ground.

## Examples

- Dust settled on the roofs.
- We need to settle soon.
- They started to settle after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
