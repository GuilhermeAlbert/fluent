# Settlement

## Meaning

Settlement is a body of people who settle far from home but maintain ties with their homeland.

## Examples

- The American colony in Paris.
- We talked about the settlement.
- The settlement matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
