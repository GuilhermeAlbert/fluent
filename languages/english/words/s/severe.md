# Severe

## Meaning

Severe describes someone or something that is intensely or extremely bad or unpleasant in degree or quality.

## Examples

- Severe pain.
- A severe case of flu.
- A terrible cough.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
