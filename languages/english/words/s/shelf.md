# Shelf

## Meaning

Shelf is a support that consists of a horizontal surface for holding objects.

## Examples

- I left the shelf on the table.
- She picked up the shelf carefully.
- The shelf is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
