# Shift

## Meaning

To shift means to make a shift in or exchange of.

## Examples

- First Joe led; then we switched.
- I need to shift this today.
- They plan to shift it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
