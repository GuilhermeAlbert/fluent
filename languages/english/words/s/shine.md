# Shine

## Meaning

To shine means to be bright by reflecting or casting light.

## Examples

- The coins shine.
- Drive carefully - the wet road reflects.
- I need to shine soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
