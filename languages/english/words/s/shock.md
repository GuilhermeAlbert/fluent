# Shock

## Meaning

Shock is the feeling of distress and disbelief that you have when something bad happens accidentally.

## Examples

- His mother's death left him in a daze.
- He was numb with shock.
- We talked about the shock.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
