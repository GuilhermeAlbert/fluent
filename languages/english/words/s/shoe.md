# Shoe

## Meaning

Shoe means footwear shaped to fit the foot with a flexible upper of leather or plastic and a sole and heel of heavier material.

## Examples

- I left the shoe on the table.
- She picked up the shoe carefully.
- The shoe is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
