# Shoot

## Meaning

To shoot means to hit with a missile from a weapon.

## Examples

- I need to shoot soon.
- They want to shoot together.
- Please shoot when you are ready.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
