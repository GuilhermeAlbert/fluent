# Shopping

## Meaning

Shopping means searching for or buying goods or services.

## Examples

- Went shopping for a reliable plumber.
- Does her shopping at the mall rather than down town.
- The shopping starts at nine.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
