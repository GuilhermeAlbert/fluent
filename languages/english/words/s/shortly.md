# Shortly

## Meaning

Shortly means doing something in a way that is for a short time.

## Examples

- He was at the airport shortly before she was expected to arrive.
- She visited him briefly.
- Was briefly associated with IBM.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
