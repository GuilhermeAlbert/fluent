# Shoulder

## Meaning

Shoulder is the part of the body between the neck and the upper arm.

## Examples

- My shoulder hurts a little.
- She touched her shoulder.
- Keep your shoulder relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
