# Show

## Meaning

To show means to give an exhibition of to an interested audience.

## Examples

- The parents show a French poem to the children.
- The parents show the children a French poem.
- She shows her dogs frequently.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
