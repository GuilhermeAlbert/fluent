# Sick

## Meaning

Sick describes someone or something that is affected by an impairment of normal physical or mental function.

## Examples

- Ill from the monotony of his suffering.
- It feels sick.
- That was a sick choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
