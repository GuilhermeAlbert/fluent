# Sigh

## Meaning

Sigh is an utterance made by exhaling audibly.

## Examples

- We talked about the sigh.
- The sigh matters here.
- I noticed the sigh right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
