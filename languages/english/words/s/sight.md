# Sight

## Meaning

Sight is an instance of visual perception.

## Examples

- The sight of his wife brought him back to reality.
- The train was an unexpected sight.
- We talked about the sight.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
