# Signal

## Meaning

Signal describes someone or something that is notably out of the ordinary.

## Examples

- The year saw one signal triumph for the Labour party.
- It feels signal.
- That was a signal choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
