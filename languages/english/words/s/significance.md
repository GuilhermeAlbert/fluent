# Significance

## Meaning

Significance is the quality of being significant.

## Examples

- Do not underestimate the significance of nuclear power.
- We talked about the significance.
- The significance matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
