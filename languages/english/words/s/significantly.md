# Significantly

## Meaning

Significantly means doing something in a way that is in a statistically significant way.

## Examples

- The two groups differed significantly.
- She answered significantly.
- It happened significantly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
