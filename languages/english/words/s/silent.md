# Silent

## Meaning

Silent describes someone or something that is marked by absence of sound.

## Examples

- A silent house.
- Soundless footsteps on the grass.
- The night was still.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
