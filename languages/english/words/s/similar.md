# Similar

## Meaning

Similar describes someone or something that is marked by correspondence or resemblance.

## Examples

- Similar food at similar prices.
- Problems similar to mine.
- They wore similar coats.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
