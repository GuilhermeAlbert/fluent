# Similarly

## Meaning

Similarly means doing something in a way that is in like or similar manner.

## Examples

- He was similarly affected.
- Some people have little power to do good, and have likewise little strength to resist evil.
- She answered similarly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
