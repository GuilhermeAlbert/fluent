# Singer

## Meaning

Singer means someone who sings.

## Examples

- The singer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the singer yesterday.
- The singer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
