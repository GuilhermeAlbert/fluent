# Sink

## Meaning

To sink means to fall or descend to a lower place or level.

## Examples

- He sank to his knees.
- We need to sink soon.
- They started to sink after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
