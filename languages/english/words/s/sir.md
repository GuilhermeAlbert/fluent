# Sir

## Meaning

Sir means term of address for a man.

## Examples

- The sir asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the sir yesterday.
- The sir joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
