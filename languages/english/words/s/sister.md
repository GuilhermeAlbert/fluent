# Sister

## Meaning

Sister is a female person who has the same parents as another person.

## Examples

- My sister married a musician.
- The sister asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the sister yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
