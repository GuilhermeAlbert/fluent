# Sit

## Meaning

To sit means to be in a position in which one's upper body is largely upright and supported by one's backside.

## Examples

- The children sit in the rocking chair.
- There sit some children in the rocking chair.
- I need to sit this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
