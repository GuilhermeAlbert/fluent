# Situation

## Meaning

Situation is the general state of things.

## Examples

- The present international situation is dangerous.
- Wondered how such a state of affairs had come about.
- We talked about the situation.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
