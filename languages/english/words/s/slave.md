# Slave

## Meaning

Slave means someone who is owned by someone.

## Examples

- The slave asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the slave yesterday.
- The slave joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
