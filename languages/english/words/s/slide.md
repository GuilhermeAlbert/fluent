# Slide

## Meaning

Slide is a small flat rectangular piece of glass on which specimens can be mounted for microscopic study.

## Examples

- I left the slide on the table.
- She picked up the slide carefully.
- The slide is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
