# Slight

## Meaning

Slight describes someone or something that is small in quantity or degree.

## Examples

- Little rain fell in May.
- Gave it little thought.
- Little time is left.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
