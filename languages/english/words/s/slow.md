# Slow

## Meaning

Slow describes someone or something that is not moving quickly.

## Examples

- A slow walker.
- The slow lane of traffic.
- Her steps were slow.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
