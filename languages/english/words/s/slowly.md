# Slowly

## Meaning

Slowly means doing something in a way that is without speed.

## Examples

- He spoke slowly.
- Go easy here - the road is slippery.
- Glaciers move tardily.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
