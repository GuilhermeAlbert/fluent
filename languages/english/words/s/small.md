# Small

## Meaning

Small describes someone or something that is limited or below average in number or quantity or magnitude or extent.

## Examples

- A little dining room.
- A little house.
- A small car.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
