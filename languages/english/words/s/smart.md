# Smart

## Meaning

Smart describes someone or something that is showing mental alertness and calculation and resourcefulness.

## Examples

- It feels smart.
- That was a smart choice.
- The room looked smart.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
