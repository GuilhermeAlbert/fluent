# Smile

## Meaning

To smile means to change one's facial expression by spreading the lips, often to signal pleasure.

## Examples

- I need to smile soon.
- They want to smile together.
- Please smile when you are ready.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
