# Smoke

## Meaning

Smoke is a cloud of fine particles suspended in a gas.

## Examples

- We talked about the smoke.
- The smoke matters here.
- I noticed the smoke right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
