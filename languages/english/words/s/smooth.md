# Smooth

## Meaning

Smooth describes someone or something that is having a surface free from roughness or bumps or ridges or irregularities.

## Examples

- Smooth skin.
- A smooth tabletop.
- Smooth fabric.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
