# Snow

## Meaning

Snow means precipitation falling from clouds in the form of ice crystals.

## Examples

- We talked about the snow.
- The snow matters here.
- I noticed the snow right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
