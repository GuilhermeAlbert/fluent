# So-Called

## Meaning

So-Called describes someone or something that is doubtful or suspect.

## Examples

- These so-called experts are no help.
- It feels so-called.
- That was a so-called choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
