# Soft

## Meaning

Soft describes someone or something that is yielding readily to pressure or weight.

## Examples

- It feels soft.
- That was a soft choice.
- The room looked soft.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
