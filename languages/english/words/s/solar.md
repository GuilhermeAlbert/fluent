# Solar

## Meaning

Solar describes something connected with or derived from the sun or utilizing the energies of the sun.

## Examples

- Solar eclipse.
- Solar energy.
- It feels solar.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
