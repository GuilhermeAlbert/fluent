# Soldier

## Meaning

Soldier is an enlisted man or woman who serves in an army.

## Examples

- The soldiers stood at attention.
- The soldier asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the soldier yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
