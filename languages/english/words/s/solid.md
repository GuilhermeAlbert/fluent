# Solid

## Meaning

Solid describes someone or something that is characterized by good substantial quality.

## Examples

- Solid comfort.
- A solid base hit.
- It feels solid.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
