# Some

## Meaning

Some describes someone or something that is used with either mass nouns or plural count nouns to indicate an unspecified number or quantity.

## Examples

- Have some milk.
- Some roses were still blooming.
- Having some friends over.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
