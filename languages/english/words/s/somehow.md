# Somehow

## Meaning

Somehow means doing something in a way that is in some unspecified way or manner.

## Examples

- They managed somehow.
- He expected somehow to discover a woman who would love him.
- He tried to make it someway acceptable.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
