# Sometimes

## Meaning

Sometimes means doing something in a way that is on certain occasions or in certain cases but not always.

## Examples

- Sometimes she wished she were back in England.
- Sometimes her photography is breathtaking.
- Sometimes they come for a month; at other times for six months.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
