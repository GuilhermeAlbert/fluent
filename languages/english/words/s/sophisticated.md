# Sophisticated

## Meaning

Sophisticated describes someone or something that is having or appealing to those having worldly knowledge and refinement and savoir-faire.

## Examples

- Sophisticated young socialites.
- A sophisticated audience.
- A sophisticated lifestyle.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
