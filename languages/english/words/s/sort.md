# Sort

## Meaning

Sort is a category of things distinguished by some common characteristic or quality.

## Examples

- Sculpture is a form of art.
- What kinds of desserts are there?
- We talked about the sort.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
