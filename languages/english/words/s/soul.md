# Soul

## Meaning

Soul is the immaterial part of a person.

## Examples

- The soul asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the soul yesterday.
- The soul joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
