# Soup

## Meaning

Soup means liquid food especially of meat or fish or vegetable stock often containing pieces of solid food.

## Examples

- I had soup with breakfast.
- She bought soup at the store.
- This soup tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
