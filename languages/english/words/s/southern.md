# Southern

## Meaning

Southern describes someone or something that is in or characteristic of a region of the United States south of the Mason-Dixon line.

## Examples

- Southern hospitality.
- Southern cooking.
- Southern plantations.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
