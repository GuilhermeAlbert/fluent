# Speaker

## Meaning

Speaker means someone who expresses in language.

## Examples

- The speaker at commencement.
- An utterer of useful maxims.
- The speaker asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
