# Specialist

## Meaning

Specialist is an expert who is devoted to one occupation or branch of learning.

## Examples

- The specialist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the specialist yesterday.
- The specialist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
