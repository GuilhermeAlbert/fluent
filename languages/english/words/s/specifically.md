# Specifically

## Meaning

Specifically means doing something in a way that is in distinction from others.

## Examples

- A program specifically for teenagers.
- He is interested specifically in poisonous snakes.
- She answered specifically.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
