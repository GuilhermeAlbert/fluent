# Speech

## Meaning

Speech is the action of delivering a formal spoken communication to an audience.

## Examples

- He listened to an address on minor Roman poets.
- We talked about the speech.
- The speech matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
