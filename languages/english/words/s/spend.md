# Spend

## Meaning

To spend means to use up a period of time in a specific way.

## Examples

- They spend a long time.
- How are you spending your summer vacation?
- I need to spend soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
