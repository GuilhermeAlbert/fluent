# Spirit

## Meaning

Spirit is the vital principle or animating force within living things.

## Examples

- The spirit asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the spirit yesterday.
- The spirit joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
