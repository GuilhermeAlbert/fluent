# Spiritual

## Meaning

Spiritual describes someone or something that is concerned with sacred matters or religion or the church.

## Examples

- Religious texts.
- A member of a religious order.
- Lords temporal and spiritual.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
