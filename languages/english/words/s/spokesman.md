# Spokesman

## Meaning

Spokesman is a male spokesperson.

## Examples

- The spokesman asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the spokesman yesterday.
- The spokesman joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
