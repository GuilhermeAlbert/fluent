# Spread

## Meaning

To spread means to distribute or disperse widely.

## Examples

- The invaders spread their language all over the country.
- I need to spread this today.
- They plan to spread it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
