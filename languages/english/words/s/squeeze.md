# Squeeze

## Meaning

To squeeze means to compress with violence, out of natural shape or condition.

## Examples

- Crush an aluminum can.
- Squeeze a lemon.
- I need to squeeze this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
