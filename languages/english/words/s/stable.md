# Stable

## Meaning

Stable describes someone or something that is resistant to change of position or condition.

## Examples

- A stable ladder.
- A stable peace.
- A stable relationship.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
