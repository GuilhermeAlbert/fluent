# Stair

## Meaning

Stair means support consisting of a place to rest the foot while ascending or descending a stairway.

## Examples

- He paused on the bottom step.
- I left the stair on the table.
- She picked up the stair carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
