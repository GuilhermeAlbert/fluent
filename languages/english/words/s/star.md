# Star

## Meaning

Star is a celestial body of hot gases that radiates energy derived from thermonuclear reactions in the interior.

## Examples

- We talked about the star.
- The star matters here.
- I noticed the star right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
