# Statement

## Meaning

Statement is a message that is stated or declared.

## Examples

- According to his statement he was in London on that day.
- We talked about the statement.
- The statement matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
