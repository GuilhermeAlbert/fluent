# Station

## Meaning

Station is a facility equipped with special equipment and personnel for a particular purpose.

## Examples

- He started looking for a gas station.
- The train pulled into the station.
- I left the station on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
