# Steady

## Meaning

Steady describes someone or something that is not subject to change or variation especially in behavior.

## Examples

- A steady beat.
- A steady job.
- A steady breeze.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
