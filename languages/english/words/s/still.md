# Still

## Meaning

Still describes someone or something that is not in physical motion.

## Examples

- She remained motionless, yet poised to take flight.
- It feels still.
- That was a still choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
