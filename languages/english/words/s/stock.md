# Stock

## Meaning

Stock is the capital raised by a corporation through the issue of shares entitling holders to an ownership interest.

## Examples

- He owns a controlling share of the company's stock.
- We talked about the stock.
- The stock matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
