# Stranger

## Meaning

Stranger means anyone who does not belong in the environment in which they are found.

## Examples

- The stranger asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the stranger yesterday.
- The stranger joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
