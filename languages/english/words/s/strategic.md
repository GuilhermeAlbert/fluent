# Strategic

## Meaning

Strategic describes something connected with or concerned with strategy.

## Examples

- Strategic weapon.
- The islands are of strategic importance.
- Strategic considerations.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
