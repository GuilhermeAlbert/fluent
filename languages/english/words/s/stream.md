# Stream

## Meaning

To stream means to extend, wave or float outward, as if in the wind.

## Examples

- Their manes streamed like stiff black pennants in the wind.
- We need to stream soon.
- They started to stream after lunch.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
