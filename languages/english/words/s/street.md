# Street

## Meaning

Street is a thoroughfare that is lined with buildings.

## Examples

- They walked the streets of the small town.
- He lives on Nassau Street.
- I left the street on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
