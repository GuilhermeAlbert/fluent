# Strength

## Meaning

Strength is the property of being physically or mentally strong.

## Examples

- Fatigue sapped his strength.
- We talked about the strength.
- The strength matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
