# Strengthen

## Meaning

To strengthen means to make strong or stronger.

## Examples

- This exercise will strengthen your upper body.
- Strengthen the relations between the two countries.
- I need to strengthen this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
