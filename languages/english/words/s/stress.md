# Stress

## Meaning

Stress is the relative prominence of a syllable or musical note.

## Examples

- He put the stress on the wrong syllable.
- We talked about the stress.
- The stress matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
