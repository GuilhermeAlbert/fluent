# Stretch

## Meaning

To stretch means to occupy a large, elongated area.

## Examples

- The park stretched beneath the train line.
- I need to stretch soon.
- They want to stretch together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
