# Strike

## Meaning

To strike means to deliver a sharp blow, as with the hand, fist, or weapon.

## Examples

- The fighter managed to strike his opponent.
- The teacher struck the child.
- The opponent refused to strike.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
