# Strong

## Meaning

Strong describes someone or something that is having strength or power greater than average or expected.

## Examples

- A strong radio signal.
- Strong medicine.
- A strong man.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
