# Strongly

## Meaning

Strongly means doing something in a way that is with strength or in a strong manner.

## Examples

- Argues very strongly for his proposal.
- He was strongly opposed to the government.
- She answered strongly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
