# Student

## Meaning

Student is a learner who is enrolled in an educational institution.

## Examples

- The student asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the student yesterday.
- The student joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
