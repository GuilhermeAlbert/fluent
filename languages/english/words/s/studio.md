# Studio

## Meaning

Studio means workplace for the teaching or practice of an art.

## Examples

- She ran a dance studio.
- The music department provided studios for their students.
- You don't need a studio to make a passport photograph.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
