# Stuff

## Meaning

To stuff means to cram into a cavity.

## Examples

- They stuff the cart with boxes.
- The child stuffed candy into his pockets.
- I need to stuff this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
