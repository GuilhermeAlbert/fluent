# Stupid

## Meaning

Stupid describes someone or something that is lacking or marked by lack of intellectual acuity.

## Examples

- It feels stupid.
- That was a stupid choice.
- The room looked stupid.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
