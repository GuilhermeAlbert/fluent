# Submit

## Meaning

To submit means to refer for judgment or consideration.

## Examples

- The lawyers submitted the material to the court.
- I need to submit this clearly.
- She tried to submit the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
