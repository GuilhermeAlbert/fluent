# Subsequent

## Meaning

Subsequent describes someone or something that is following in time or order.

## Examples

- Subsequent developments.
- It feels subsequent.
- That was a subsequent choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
