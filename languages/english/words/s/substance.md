# Substance

## Meaning

Substance is the real physical matter of which a person or thing consists.

## Examples

- DNA is the substance of our genes.
- We talked about the substance.
- The substance matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
