# Substantial

## Meaning

Substantial describes someone or something that is of considerable importance, size, or worth.

## Examples

- Won by a substantial margin.
- It feels substantial.
- That was a substantial choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
