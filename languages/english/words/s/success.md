# Success

## Meaning

Success is an event that accomplishes its intended purpose.

## Examples

- Let's call heads a success and tails a failure.
- The election was a remarkable success for the Whigs.
- The success starts at nine.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
