# Successful

## Meaning

Successful describes someone or something that is having succeeded or being marked by a favorable outcome.

## Examples

- A successful architect.
- A successful business venture.
- It feels successful.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
