# Successfully

## Meaning

Successfully means doing something in a way that is with success.

## Examples

- She performed the surgery successfully.
- She answered successfully.
- It happened successfully.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
