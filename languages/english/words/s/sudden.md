# Sudden

## Meaning

Sudden describes someone or something that is happening without warning or in a short space of time.

## Examples

- A sudden storm.
- A sudden decision.
- A sudden cure.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
