# Suddenly

## Meaning

Suddenly means doing something in a way that is quickly and without warning.

## Examples

- He stopped suddenly.
- Suddenly she felt a sharp pain in her side.
- He decided to go to Chicago on the spur of the moment.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
