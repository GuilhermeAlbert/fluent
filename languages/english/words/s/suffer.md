# Suffer

## Meaning

To suffer means to undergo or be subjected to.

## Examples

- He suffered the penalty.
- Many saints suffered martyrdom.
- I need to suffer soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
