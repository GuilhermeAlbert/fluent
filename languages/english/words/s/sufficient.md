# Sufficient

## Meaning

Sufficient describes someone or something that is of a quantity that can fulfill a need or requirement but without being abundant.

## Examples

- Sufficient food.
- It feels sufficient.
- That was a sufficient choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
