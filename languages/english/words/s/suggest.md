# Suggest

## Meaning

To suggest means to make a proposal, declare a plan for something.

## Examples

- They suggest him to write the letter.
- They suggest to move.
- The senator proposed to abolish the sales tax.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
