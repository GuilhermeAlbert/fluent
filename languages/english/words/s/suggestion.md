# Suggestion

## Meaning

Suggestion is an idea that is suggested.

## Examples

- The picnic was her suggestion.
- We talked about the suggestion.
- The suggestion matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
