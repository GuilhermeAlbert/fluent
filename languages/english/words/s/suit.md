# Suit

## Meaning

Suit is a set of garments for outerwear all of the same fabric and color.

## Examples

- They buried him in his best suit.
- I left the suit on the table.
- She picked up the suit carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
