# Supporter

## Meaning

Supporter means someone who backs a politician or a team etc.

## Examples

- All their supporters came out for the game.
- They are friends of the library.
- The supporter asked a clear question.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
