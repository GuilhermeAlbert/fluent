# Supposed

## Meaning

Supposed describes someone or something that is required or under orders.

## Examples

- I'm supposed to be there at ten.
- He was supposed to go to the store.
- It feels supposed.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
