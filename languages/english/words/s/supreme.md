# Supreme

## Meaning

Supreme describes someone or something that is final or last in your life or progress.

## Examples

- The supreme sacrifice.
- The supreme judgment.
- It feels supreme.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
