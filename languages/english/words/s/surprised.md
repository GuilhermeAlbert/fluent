# Surprised

## Meaning

Surprised describes someone or something that is taken unawares or suddenly and feeling wonder or astonishment.

## Examples

- Surprised by her student's ingenuity.
- Surprised that he remembered my name.
- A surprised expression.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
