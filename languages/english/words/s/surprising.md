# Surprising

## Meaning

Surprising describes someone or something that is causing surprise or wonder or amazement.

## Examples

- The report shows a surprising lack of hard factual data.
- Leaped up with surprising agility.
- She earned a surprising amount of money.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
