# Surprisingly

## Meaning

Surprisingly means doing something in a way that is in a surprising manner.

## Examples

- He was surprisingly friendly.
- She answered surprisingly.
- It happened surprisingly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
