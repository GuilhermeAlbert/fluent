# Surround

## Meaning

To surround means to extend on all sides of simultaneously.

## Examples

- The forest surrounds my property.
- I need to surround this today.
- They plan to surround it together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
