# Survey

## Meaning

To survey means to consider in a comprehensive way.

## Examples

- He appraised the situation carefully before acting.
- I need to survey the problem.
- They tried to survey what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
