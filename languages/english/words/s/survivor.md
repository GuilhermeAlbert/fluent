# Survivor

## Meaning

Survivor means one who lives through affliction.

## Examples

- The survivors of the fire were taken to a hospital.
- The survivor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the survivor yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
