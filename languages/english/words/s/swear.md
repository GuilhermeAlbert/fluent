# Swear

## Meaning

To swear means to utter obscenities or profanities.

## Examples

- The drunken men were cursing loudly in the street.
- I need to swear this clearly.
- She tried to swear the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
