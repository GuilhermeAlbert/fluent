# Sweep

## Meaning

To sweep means to sweep across or over.

## Examples

- Her long skirt brushed the floor.
- A gasp swept cross the audience.
- We need to sweep soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
