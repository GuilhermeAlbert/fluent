# Sweet

## Meaning

Sweet describes someone or something that is having or denoting the characteristic taste of sugar.

## Examples

- It feels sweet.
- That was a sweet choice.
- The room looked sweet.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
