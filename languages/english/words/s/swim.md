# Swim

## Meaning

To swim means to travel through water.

## Examples

- These men swim across the river.
- These men swim the river.
- We had to swim for 20 minutes to reach the shore.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
