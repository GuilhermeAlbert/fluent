# Swing

## Meaning

To swing means to move in a curve or arc, usually with the intent of hitting.

## Examples

- The girls swing the wooden sticks.
- He swung his left fist.
- Swing a bat.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
