# Switch

## Meaning

Switch means control consisting of a mechanical or electrical or electronic device for making or breaking or changing the connections in a circuit.

## Examples

- I left the switch on the table.
- She picked up the switch carefully.
- The switch is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
