# Symbol

## Meaning

Symbol is an arbitrary sign that has acquired a conventional significance.

## Examples

- We talked about the symbol.
- The symbol matters here.
- I noticed the symbol right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
