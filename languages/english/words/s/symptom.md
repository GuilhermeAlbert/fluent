# Symptom

## Meaning

Symptom means any sensation or change in bodily function that is experienced by a patient and is associated with a particular disease.

## Examples

- We talked about the symptom.
- The symptom matters here.
- I noticed the symptom right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
