# System

## Meaning

System means instrumentality that combines interrelated interacting artifacts designed to work as a coherent entity.

## Examples

- He bought a new stereo system.
- The system consists of a motor and a small computer.
- I left the system on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
