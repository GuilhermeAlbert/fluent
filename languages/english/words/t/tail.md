# Tail

## Meaning

Tail is the posterior part of the body of a vertebrate especially when elongated and extending beyond the trunk or main part of the body.

## Examples

- The tail moved across the road.
- We saw a tail near the house.
- The tail looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
