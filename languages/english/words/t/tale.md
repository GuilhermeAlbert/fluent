# Tale

## Meaning

Tale is a message that tells the particulars of an act or occurrence or course of events.

## Examples

- His narrative was interesting.
- Disney's stories entertain adults as well as children.
- We talked about the tale.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
