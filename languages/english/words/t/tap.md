# Tap

## Meaning

To tap means to cut a female screw thread with a tap.

## Examples

- I need to tap this today.
- They plan to tap it together.
- Can you tap this for me?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
