# Tape

## Meaning

Tape is a long thin piece of cloth or paper as used for binding or fastening.

## Examples

- He used a piece of tape for a belt.
- He wrapped a tape around the package.
- I left the tape on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
