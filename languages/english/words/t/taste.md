# Taste

## Meaning

Taste is the sensation that results when taste buds in the tongue and throat convey information about the chemical composition of a soluble stimulus.

## Examples

- The candy left him with a bad taste.
- The melon had a delicious taste.
- We talked about the taste.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
