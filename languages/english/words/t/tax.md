# Tax

## Meaning

To tax means to levy a tax on.

## Examples

- The State taxes alcohol heavily.
- Clothing is not taxed in our state.
- I need to tax this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
