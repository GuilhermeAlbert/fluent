# Taxpayer

## Meaning

Taxpayer means someone who pays taxes.

## Examples

- The taxpayer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the taxpayer yesterday.
- The taxpayer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
