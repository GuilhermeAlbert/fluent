# Tea

## Meaning

Tea is a beverage made by steeping tea leaves in water.

## Examples

- Iced tea is a cooling drink.
- I had tea with breakfast.
- She bought tea at the store.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
