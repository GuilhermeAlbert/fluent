# Teach

## Meaning

To teach means to impart skills or knowledge to.

## Examples

- The parents teach a French poem to the children.
- The parents teach the children a French poem.
- I taught them French.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
