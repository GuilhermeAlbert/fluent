# Teacher

## Meaning

Teacher means someone whose occupation is teaching.

## Examples

- The teacher asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the teacher yesterday.
- The teacher joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
