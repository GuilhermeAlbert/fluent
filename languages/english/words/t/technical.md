# Technical

## Meaning

Technical describes something connected with technique or proficiency in a practical skill.

## Examples

- His technical innovation was his brushwork.
- The technical dazzle of her dancing.
- It feels technical.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
