# Technique

## Meaning

Technique is a practical method or art applied to some particular task.

## Examples

- We talked about the technique.
- The technique matters here.
- I noticed the technique right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
