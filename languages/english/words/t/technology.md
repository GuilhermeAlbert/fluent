# Technology

## Meaning

Technology is the application of the knowledge and usage of tools and techniques to control one's environment.

## Examples

- The mastery of fire was a huge advance in human technology.
- The technology starts at nine.
- We planned the technology together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
