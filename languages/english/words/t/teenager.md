# Teenager

## Meaning

Teenager is a juvenile between the onset of puberty and maturity.

## Examples

- The teenager asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the teenager yesterday.
- The teenager joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
