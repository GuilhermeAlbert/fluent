# Temperature

## Meaning

Temperature is the degree of hotness or coldness of a body or environment.

## Examples

- We talked about the temperature.
- The temperature matters here.
- I noticed the temperature right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
