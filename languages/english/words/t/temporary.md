# Temporary

## Meaning

Temporary describes someone or something that is not permanent.

## Examples

- Politics is an impermanent factor of life.
- Impermanent palm cottages.
- A temporary arrangement.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
