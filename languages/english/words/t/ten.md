# Ten

## Meaning

Ten is the cardinal number that is the sum of nine and one.

## Examples

- We talked about the ten.
- The ten matters here.
- I noticed the ten right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
