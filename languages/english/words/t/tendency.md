# Tendency

## Meaning

Tendency is an attitude of mind especially one that favors one alternative over others.

## Examples

- He had an inclination to give up too easily.
- A tendency to be too strict.
- We talked about the tendency.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
