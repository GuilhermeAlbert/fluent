# Terms

## Meaning

Terms means status with respect to the relations between people or groups.

## Examples

- On good terms with her in-laws.
- On a friendly footing.
- We talked about the terms.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
