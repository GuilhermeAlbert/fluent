# Terrorist

## Meaning

Terrorist is a radical who employs terror as a political weapon.

## Examples

- The terrorist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the terrorist yesterday.
- The terrorist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
