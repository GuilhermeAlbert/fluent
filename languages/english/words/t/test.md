# Test

## Meaning

To test means to put to the test, as for its quality, or give experimental use to.

## Examples

- This approach has been tried with good results.
- Test this recipe.
- I need to test soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
