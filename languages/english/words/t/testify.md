# Testify

## Meaning

To testify means to give testimony in a court of law.

## Examples

- They testify that there was a traffic accident.
- I need to testify this clearly.
- She tried to testify the idea.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
