# Testimony

## Meaning

Testimony is a solemn statement made under oath.

## Examples

- We talked about the testimony.
- The testimony matters here.
- I noticed the testimony right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
