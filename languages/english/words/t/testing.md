# Testing

## Meaning

Testing is the action of subjecting to experimental test in order to determine how well something works.

## Examples

- They agreed to end the testing of atomic weapons.
- The testing starts at nine.
- We planned the testing together.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
