# Text

## Meaning

Text is the words of something written.

## Examples

- There were more than a thousand words of text.
- They handed out the printed text of the mayor's speech.
- He wants to reconstruct the original text.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
