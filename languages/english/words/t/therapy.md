# Therapy

## Meaning

Therapy is the action of caring for someone.

## Examples

- The quarterback is undergoing treatment for a knee injury.
- He tried every treatment the doctors suggested.
- Heat therapy gave the best relief.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
