# Thin

## Meaning

Thin describes someone or something that is of relatively small extent from one surface to the opposite or in cross section.

## Examples

- Thin wire.
- A thin chiffon blouse.
- A thin book.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
