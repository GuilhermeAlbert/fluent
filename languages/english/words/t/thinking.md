# Thinking

## Meaning

Thinking is the process of using your mind to consider something carefully.

## Examples

- Thinking always made him frown.
- She paused for thought.
- We talked about the thinking.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
