# Third

## Meaning

Third means one of three equal parts of a divisible whole.

## Examples

- It contains approximately a third of the minimum daily requirement.
- We talked about the third.
- The third matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
