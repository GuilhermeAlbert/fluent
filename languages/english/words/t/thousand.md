# Thousand

## Meaning

Thousand is the cardinal number that is the product of 10 and 100.

## Examples

- We talked about the thousand.
- The thousand matters here.
- I noticed the thousand right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
