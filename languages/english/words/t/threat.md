# Threat

## Meaning

Threat means something that is a source of danger.

## Examples

- Earthquakes are a constant threat in Japan.
- We talked about the threat.
- The threat matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
