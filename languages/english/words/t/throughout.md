# Throughout

## Meaning

Throughout means doing something in a way that is from first to last.

## Examples

- The play was excellent end-to-end.
- She answered throughout.
- It happened throughout.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
