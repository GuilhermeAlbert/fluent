# Throw

## Meaning

To throw means to propel through the air.

## Examples

- Throw a frisbee.
- The rocket lofted the space shuttle into the air.
- I need to throw this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
