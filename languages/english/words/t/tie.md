# Tie

## Meaning

Tie means neckwear consisting of a long narrow piece of material worn under a collar and tied in knot at the front.

## Examples

- He stood in front of the mirror tightening his necktie.
- He wore a vest and tie.
- I left the tie on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
