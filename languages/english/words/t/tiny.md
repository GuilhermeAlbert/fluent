# Tiny

## Meaning

Tiny describes someone or something that is very small.

## Examples

- Diminutive in stature.
- A lilliputian chest of drawers.
- Her petite figure.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
