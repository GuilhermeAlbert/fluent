# Tire

## Meaning

To tire means to lose interest or become bored with something or somebody.

## Examples

- I'm so tired of your mother and her complaints about my food.
- I need to tire soon.
- They want to tire together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
