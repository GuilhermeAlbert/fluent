# Tired

## Meaning

Tired describes someone or something that is depleted of strength or energy.

## Examples

- Tired mothers with crying babies.
- Too tired to eat.
- It feels tired.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
