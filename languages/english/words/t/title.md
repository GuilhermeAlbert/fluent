# Title

## Meaning

Title is a heading that names a statute or legislative bill.

## Examples

- Title 8 provided federal help for schools.
- We talked about the title.
- The title matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
