# Together

## Meaning

Together means doing something in a way that is in contact with each other or in proximity.

## Examples

- The leaves stuck together.
- She answered together.
- It happened together.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
