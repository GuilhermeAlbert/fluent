# Tomato

## Meaning

Tomato means mildly acid red or yellow pulpy fruit eaten as a vegetable.

## Examples

- I had tomato with breakfast.
- She bought tomato at the store.
- This tomato tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
