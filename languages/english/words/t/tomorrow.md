# Tomorrow

## Meaning

Tomorrow is the day after today.

## Examples

- What are our tasks for tomorrow?
- The tomorrow went by quickly.
- We chose a better tomorrow.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
