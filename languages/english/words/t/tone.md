# Tone

## Meaning

Tone is the quality of a person's voice.

## Examples

- He began in a conversational tone.
- He spoke in a nervous tone of voice.
- We talked about the tone.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
