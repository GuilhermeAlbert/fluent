# Tongue

## Meaning

Tongue is a mobile mass of muscular tissue covered with mucous membrane and located in the oral cavity.

## Examples

- My tongue hurts a little.
- She touched her tongue.
- Keep your tongue relaxed.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
