# Total

## Meaning

Total describes someone or something that is constituting the full quantity or extent.

## Examples

- An entire town devastated by an earthquake.
- Gave full attention.
- A total failure.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
