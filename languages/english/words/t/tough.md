# Tough

## Meaning

Tough describes someone or something that is not given to gentleness or sentimentality.

## Examples

- A tough character.
- It feels tough.
- That was a tough choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
