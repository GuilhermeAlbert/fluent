# Tourist

## Meaning

Tourist means someone who travels for pleasure.

## Examples

- The tourist asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the tourist yesterday.
- The tourist joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
