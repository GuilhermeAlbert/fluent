# Town

## Meaning

Town is an urban area with a fixed boundary that is smaller than a city.

## Examples

- They drive through town on their way to work.
- We met near the town.
- The town was quiet this morning.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
