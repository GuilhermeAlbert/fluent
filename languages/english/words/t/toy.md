# Toy

## Meaning

Toy is an artifact designed to be played with.

## Examples

- I left the toy on the table.
- She picked up the toy carefully.
- The toy is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
