# Tradition

## Meaning

Tradition is an inherited pattern of thought or action.

## Examples

- We talked about the tradition.
- The tradition matters here.
- I noticed the tradition right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
