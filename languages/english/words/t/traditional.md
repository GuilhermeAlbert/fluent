# Traditional

## Meaning

Traditional describes someone or something that is consisting of or derived from tradition.

## Examples

- Traditional history.
- Traditional morality.
- It feels traditional.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
