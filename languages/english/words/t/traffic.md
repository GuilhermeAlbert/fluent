# Traffic

## Meaning

Traffic is the aggregation of things coming and going in a particular locality during a specified period of time.

## Examples

- I need more traffic before I decide.
- We talked about traffic after lunch.
- That traffic really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
