# Training

## Meaning

Training means activity leading to skilled behavior.

## Examples

- I need more training before I decide.
- We talked about training after lunch.
- That training really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
