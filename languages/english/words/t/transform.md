# Transform

## Meaning

To transform means to subject to a mathematical transformation.

## Examples

- I need to transform this today.
- They plan to transform it together.
- Can you transform this for me?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
