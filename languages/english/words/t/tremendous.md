# Tremendous

## Meaning

Tremendous describes someone or something that is extraordinarily large in size or extent or amount or power or degree.

## Examples

- An enormous boulder.
- Enormous expenses.
- Tremendous sweeping plains.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
