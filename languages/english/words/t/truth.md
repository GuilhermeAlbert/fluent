# Truth

## Meaning

Truth is a fact that has been verified.

## Examples

- At last he knew the truth.
- The truth is that he didn't want to do it.
- We talked about the truth.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
