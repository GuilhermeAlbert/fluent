# Tube

## Meaning

Tube means conduit consisting of a long hollow object used to hold and conduct objects or liquids or gases.

## Examples

- I left the tube on the table.
- She picked up the tube carefully.
- The tube is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
