# Twelve

## Meaning

Twelve is the cardinal number that is the sum of eleven and one.

## Examples

- We talked about the twelve.
- The twelve matters here.
- I noticed the twelve right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
