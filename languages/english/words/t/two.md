# Two

## Meaning

Two is the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one or a numeral representing this number.

## Examples

- We talked about the two.
- The two matters here.
- I noticed the two right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
