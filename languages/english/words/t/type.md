# Type

## Meaning

Type is a subdivision of a particular kind of thing.

## Examples

- What type of sculpture do you prefer?
- We talked about the type.
- The type matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
