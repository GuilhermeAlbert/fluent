# Typical

## Meaning

Typical describes someone or something that is exhibiting the qualities or characteristics that identify a group or kind or category.

## Examples

- A typical American girl.
- A typical suburban community.
- The typical car owner drives 10,000 miles a year.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
