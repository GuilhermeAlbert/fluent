# Typically

## Meaning

Typically means doing something in a way that is in a typical manner.

## Examples

- Tom was typically hostile.
- She answered typically.
- It happened typically.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
