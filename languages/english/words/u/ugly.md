# Ugly

## Meaning

Ugly describes someone or something that is displeasing to the senses.

## Examples

- An ugly face.
- Ugly furniture.
- It feels ugly.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
