# Ultimately

## Meaning

Ultimately means doing something in a way that is as the end result of a succession or process.

## Examples

- Ultimately he had to give in.
- At long last the winter was over.
- She answered ultimately.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
