# Unable

## Meaning

Unable describes someone or something that is not having the necessary means or skill or know-how.

## Examples

- Unable to get to town without a car.
- Unable to obtain funds.
- It feels unable.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
