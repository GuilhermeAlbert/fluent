# Uncle

## Meaning

Uncle is the brother of your father or mother.

## Examples

- The uncle asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the uncle yesterday.
- The uncle joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
