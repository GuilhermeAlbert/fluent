# Under

## Meaning

Under means doing something in a way that is down to defeat, death, or ruin.

## Examples

- Their competitors went under.
- She answered under.
- It happened under.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
