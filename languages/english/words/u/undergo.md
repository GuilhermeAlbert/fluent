# Undergo

## Meaning

To undergo means to go through.

## Examples

- The chemical undergoes a sudden change.
- The fluid undergoes shear.
- Undergo a strange sensation.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
