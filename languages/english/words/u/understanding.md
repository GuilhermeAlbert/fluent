# Understanding

## Meaning

Understanding is the cognitive condition of someone who understands.

## Examples

- He has virtually no understanding of social cause and effect.
- We talked about the understanding.
- The understanding matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
