# Unfortunately

## Meaning

Unfortunately means doing something in a way that is by bad luck.

## Examples

- Unfortunately it rained all day.
- Alas, I cannot stay.
- She answered unfortunately.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
