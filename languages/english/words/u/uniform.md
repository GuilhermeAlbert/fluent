# Uniform

## Meaning

Uniform describes someone or something that is always the same.

## Examples

- A street of uniform tall white buildings.
- It feels uniform.
- That was an uniform choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
