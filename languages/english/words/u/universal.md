# Universal

## Meaning

Universal describes someone or something that is of worldwide scope or applicability.

## Examples

- An issue of cosmopolitan import.
- The shrewdest political and ecumenical comment of our time.
- Universal experience.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
