# Unlike

## Meaning

Unlike describes someone or something that is marked by dissimilarity.

## Examples

- For twins they are very unlike.
- It feels unlike.
- That was an unlike choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
