# Unlikely

## Meaning

Unlikely describes someone or something that is not likely to be true or to occur or to have occurred.

## Examples

- Legislation on the question is highly unlikely.
- An improbable event.
- It feels unlikely.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
