# Unusual

## Meaning

Unusual describes someone or something that is not usual or common or ordinary.

## Examples

- A scene of unusual beauty.
- A man of unusual ability.
- Cruel and unusual punishment.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
