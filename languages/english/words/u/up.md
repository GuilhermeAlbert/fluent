# Up

## Meaning

Up describes someone or something that is being or moving higher in position or greater in some value.

## Examples

- The anchor is up.
- The sun is up.
- He lay face up.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
