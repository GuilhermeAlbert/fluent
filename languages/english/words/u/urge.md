# Urge

## Meaning

To urge means to force or impel in an indicated direction.

## Examples

- They urge him to write the letter.
- I urged him to finish his studies.
- I need to urge this clearly.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
