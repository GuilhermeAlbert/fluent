# Used

## Meaning

Used describes someone or something that is employed in accomplishing something.

## Examples

- The principle of surprise is the most used and misused of all the principles of war.
- It feels used.
- That was an used choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
