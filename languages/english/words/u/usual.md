# Usual

## Meaning

Usual describes someone or something that is occurring or encountered or experienced or observed frequently or in accordance with regular practice or procedure.

## Examples

- Grew the usual vegetables.
- The usual summer heat.
- Came at the usual time.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
