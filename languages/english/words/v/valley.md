# Valley

## Meaning

Valley is a long depression in the surface of the land that usually contains a river.

## Examples

- We talked about the valley.
- The valley matters here.
- I noticed the valley right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
