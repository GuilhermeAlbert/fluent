# Valuable

## Meaning

Valuable describes someone or something that is having great material or monetary value especially for use or exchange.

## Examples

- A valuable diamond.
- It feels valuable.
- That was a valuable choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
