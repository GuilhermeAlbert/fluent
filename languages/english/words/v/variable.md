# Variable

## Meaning

Variable describes someone or something that is liable to or capable of change.

## Examples

- Rainfall in the tropics is notoriously variable.
- Variable winds.
- Variable expenses.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
