# Various

## Meaning

Various describes someone or something that is of many different kinds purposefully arranged but lacking any uniformity.

## Examples

- Assorted sizes.
- His disguises are many and various.
- Various experiments have failed to disprove the theory.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
