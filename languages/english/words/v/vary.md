# Vary

## Meaning

To vary means to become different in some particular way, without permanently losing one's or its former characteristics or essence.

## Examples

- Her mood changes in accordance with the weather.
- The supermarket's selection of vegetables varies according to the season.
- I need to vary this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
