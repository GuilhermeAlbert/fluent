# Vegetable

## Meaning

Vegetable means edible seeds or roots or stems or leaves or bulbs or tubers or nonsweet fruits of any of numerous herbaceous plant.

## Examples

- I had vegetable with breakfast.
- She bought vegetable at the store.
- This vegetable tastes fresh.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
