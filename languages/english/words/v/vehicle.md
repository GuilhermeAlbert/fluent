# Vehicle

## Meaning

Vehicle is a conveyance that transports people or objects.

## Examples

- I left the vehicle on the table.
- She picked up the vehicle carefully.
- The vehicle is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
