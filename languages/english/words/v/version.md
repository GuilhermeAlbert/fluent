# Version

## Meaning

Version is an interpretation of a matter from a particular viewpoint.

## Examples

- His version of the fight was different from mine.
- We talked about the version.
- The version matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
