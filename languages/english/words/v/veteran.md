# Veteran

## Meaning

Veteran is a serviceman who has seen considerable active service.

## Examples

- The veterans laughed at the new recruits.
- The veteran asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the veteran yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
