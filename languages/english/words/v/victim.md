# Victim

## Meaning

Victim is an unfortunate person who suffers from some adverse circumstance.

## Examples

- The victim asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the victim yesterday.
- The victim joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
