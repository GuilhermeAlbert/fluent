# Victory

## Meaning

Victory is a successful ending of a struggle or contest.

## Examples

- A narrow victory.
- The general always gets credit for his army's victory.
- Clinched a victory.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
