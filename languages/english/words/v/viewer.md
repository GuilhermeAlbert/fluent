# Viewer

## Meaning

Viewer is a close observer.

## Examples

- The spectators applauded the performance.
- Television viewers.
- Sky watchers discovered a new star.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
