# Violent

## Meaning

Violent describes someone or something that is acting with or marked by or resulting from great force or energy or emotional intensity.

## Examples

- A violent attack.
- A violent person.
- Violent feelings.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
