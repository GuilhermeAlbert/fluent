# Virtually

## Meaning

Virtually means doing something in a way that is in essence or effect but not in fact.

## Examples

- The strike virtually paralyzed the city.
- I'm virtually broke.
- She answered virtually.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
