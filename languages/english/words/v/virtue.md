# Virtue

## Meaning

Virtue is the quality of doing what is right and avoiding what is wrong.

## Examples

- We talked about the virtue.
- The virtue matters here.
- I noticed the virtue right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
