# Virus

## Meaning

Virus means ultramicroscopic infectious agent that replicates itself only within cells of living hosts.

## Examples

- The virus moved across the road.
- We saw a virus near the house.
- The virus looked calm.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
