# Visit

## Meaning

To visit means to go to see a place, as for entertainment.

## Examples

- We went to see the Eiffel Tower in the morning.
- I need to visit soon.
- They want to visit together.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
