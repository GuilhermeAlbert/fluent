# Visitor

## Meaning

Visitor means someone who visits.

## Examples

- The visitor asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the visitor yesterday.
- The visitor joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
