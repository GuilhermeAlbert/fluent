# Voice

## Meaning

Voice is the distinctive quality or pitch or condition of a person's speech.

## Examples

- A shrill voice sounded behind us.
- We talked about the voice.
- The voice matters here.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
