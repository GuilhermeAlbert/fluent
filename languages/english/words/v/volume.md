# Volume

## Meaning

Volume is the amount of 3-dimensional space occupied by an object.

## Examples

- The gas expanded to twice its original volume.
- We talked about the volume.
- The volume matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
