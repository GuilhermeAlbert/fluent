# Volunteer

## Meaning

Volunteer means someone who freely enlists for service.

## Examples

- The volunteer asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the volunteer yesterday.
- The volunteer joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
