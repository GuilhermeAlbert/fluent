# Voter

## Meaning

Voter is a citizen who has a legal right to vote.

## Examples

- The voter asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the voter yesterday.
- The voter joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
