# Vs

## Meaning

Vs means "against" or "compared with."

## Examples

- Tonight is Brazil vs Argentina.
- We compared cost vs speed.
- The debate was privacy vs convenience.

## Notes

Often written as "vs." in notes, schedules, and comparisons.
