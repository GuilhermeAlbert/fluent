# Vulnerable

## Meaning

Vulnerable describes someone or something that is susceptible to attack.

## Examples

- A vulnerable bridge.
- It feels vulnerable.
- That was a vulnerable choice.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
