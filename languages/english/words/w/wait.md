# Wait

## Meaning

To wait means to stay in one place and anticipate or expect something.

## Examples

- They wait a long time.
- I had to wait on line for an hour to get the tickets.
- I need to wait soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
