# Wall

## Meaning

Wall is an architectural partition with a height and length greater than its thickness.

## Examples

- The south wall had a small window.
- The walls were covered with pictures.
- I left the wall on the table.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
