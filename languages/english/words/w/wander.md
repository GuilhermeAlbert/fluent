# Wander

## Meaning

To wander means to move about aimlessly or without any destination, often in search of food or employment.

## Examples

- They wander the countryside.
- They wander in the countryside.
- The gypsies roamed the woods.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
