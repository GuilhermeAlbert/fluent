# Warm

## Meaning

Warm describes someone or something that is having or producing a comfortable and agreeable degree of heat or imparting or maintaining heat.

## Examples

- A warm body.
- A warm room.
- A warm climate.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
