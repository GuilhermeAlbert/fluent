# Waste

## Meaning

To waste means to spend thoughtlessly.

## Examples

- He wasted his inheritance on his insincere friends.
- You squandered the opportunity to get an advanced degree.
- I need to waste this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
