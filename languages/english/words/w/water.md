# Water

## Meaning

Water means binary compound that occurs at room temperature as a clear colorless odorless tasteless liquid.

## Examples

- I need more water before I decide.
- We talked about water after lunch.
- That water really helped me.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
