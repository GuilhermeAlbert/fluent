# Wave

## Meaning

Wave means one of a series of ridges that moves across the surface of a liquid.

## Examples

- The wave starts at nine.
- We planned the wave together.
- That wave changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
