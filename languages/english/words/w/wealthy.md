# Wealthy

## Meaning

Wealthy describes someone or something that is having an abundant supply of money or haveions of value.

## Examples

- An affluent banker.
- A speculator flush with cash.
- Not merely rich but loaded.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
