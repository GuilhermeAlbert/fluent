# Weapon

## Meaning

Weapon means any instrument or instrumentality used in fighting or hunting.

## Examples

- He was licensed to carry a weapon.
- I left the weapon on the table.
- She picked up the weapon carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
