# Wedding

## Meaning

Wedding is the social event at which the ceremony of marriage is performed.

## Examples

- The wedding starts at nine.
- We planned the wedding together.
- That wedding changed everything.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
