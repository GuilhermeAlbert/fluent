# Weekend

## Meaning

Weekend is a time period usually extending from Friday night through Sunday.

## Examples

- The weekend went by quickly.
- We chose a better weekend.
- That weekend worked well for us.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
