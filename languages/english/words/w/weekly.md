# Weekly

## Meaning

Weekly means doing something in a way that is without missing a week.

## Examples

- She visited her aunt weekly.
- She answered weekly.
- It happened weekly.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
