# Weight

## Meaning

Weight is the vertical force exerted by a mass as a result of gravity.

## Examples

- We talked about the weight.
- The weight matters here.
- I noticed the weight right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
