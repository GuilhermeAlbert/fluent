# Well

## Meaning

Well means doing something in a way that is in a good or proper or satisfactory manner or to a high standard.

## Examples

- The children behaved well.
- A task well done.
- The party went well.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
