# Western

## Meaning

Western describes something connected with or characteristic of the western parts of the world or the West as opposed to the eastern or oriental parts.

## Examples

- The western world.
- Western thought.
- It feels western.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
