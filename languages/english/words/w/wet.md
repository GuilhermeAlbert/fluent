# Wet

## Meaning

Wet describes someone or something that is covered or soaked with a liquid such as water.

## Examples

- A wet bathing suit.
- Wet sidewalks.
- Wet weather.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
