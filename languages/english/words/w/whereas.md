# Whereas

## Meaning

Whereas introduces a contrast between two facts.

## Examples

- I like tea, whereas she prefers coffee.
- The old plan was slow, whereas this one is faster.
- Some people stayed, whereas others left.

## Notes

More formal than "while" for contrast.
