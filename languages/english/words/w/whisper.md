# Whisper

## Meaning

Whisper means speaking softly without vibration of the vocal cords.

## Examples

- We talked about the whisper.
- The whisper matters here.
- I noticed the whisper right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
