# White

## Meaning

White describes someone or something that is being of the achromatic color of maximum lightness.

## Examples

- As white as fresh snow.
- A bride's white dress.
- It feels white.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
