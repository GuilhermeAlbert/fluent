# Whole

## Meaning

Whole describes someone or something that is including all components without exception.

## Examples

- Gave his whole attention.
- A whole wardrobe for the tropics.
- The whole hog.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
