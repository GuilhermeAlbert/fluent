# Why

## Meaning

Why is the cause or intention underlying an action or situation, especially in the phrase "the whys and wherefores".

## Examples

- We talked about the why.
- The why matters here.
- I noticed the why right away.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
