# Widely

## Meaning

Widely means doing something in a way that is to or over a great extent or range.

## Examples

- Wandered wide through many lands.
- He traveled widely.
- Her work is widely known.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
