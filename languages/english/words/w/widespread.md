# Widespread

## Meaning

Widespread describes someone or something that is widely circulated or diffused.

## Examples

- A widespread doctrine.
- Widespread fear of nuclear war.
- It feels widespread.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
