# Wife

## Meaning

Wife is a married woman.

## Examples

- The wife asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the wife yesterday.
- The wife joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
