# Wild

## Meaning

Wild describes someone or something that is marked by extreme lack of restraint or control.

## Examples

- Wild talk.
- Wild parties.
- It feels wild.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
