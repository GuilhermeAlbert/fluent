# Window

## Meaning

Window is a framework of wood or metal that contains a glass windowpane and is built into a wall or roof to admit light or air.

## Examples

- I left the window on the table.
- She picked up the window carefully.
- The window is in the kitchen.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
