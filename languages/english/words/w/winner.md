# Winner

## Meaning

Winner is the contestant who wins the contest.

## Examples

- The winner asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the winner yesterday.
- The winner joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
