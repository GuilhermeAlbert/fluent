# Wisdom

## Meaning

Wisdom means accumulated knowledge or erudition or enlightenment.

## Examples

- We talked about the wisdom.
- The wisdom matters here.
- I noticed the wisdom right away.

## Notes

Often used for abstract ideas, feelings, qualities, or information rather than physical objects.
