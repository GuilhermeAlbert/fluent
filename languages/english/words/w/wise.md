# Wise

## Meaning

Wise describes someone or something that is having or prompted by wisdom or discernment.

## Examples

- A wise leader.
- A wise and perceptive comment.
- It feels wise.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
