# Wish

## Meaning

To wish means to hope, to desire or to prefer to have something, or to do something.

## Examples

- They wish him to write the letter.
- They wish to move.
- Do you care to try this dish?

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
