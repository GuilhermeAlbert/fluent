# Withdraw

## Meaning

To withdraw means to pull back or move away or backward.

## Examples

- The enemy withdrew.
- The limo pulled away from the curb.
- We need to withdraw soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
