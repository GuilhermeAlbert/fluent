# Witness

## Meaning

Witness means someone who sees an event and reports what happened.

## Examples

- The witness asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the witness yesterday.
- The witness joined the meeting.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
