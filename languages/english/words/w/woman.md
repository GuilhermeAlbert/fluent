# Woman

## Meaning

Woman is an adult female person.

## Examples

- The woman kept house while the man hunted.
- The woman asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the woman yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
