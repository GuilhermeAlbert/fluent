# Wonder

## Meaning

To wonder means to have a wish or desire to know something.

## Examples

- He wondered who had built this beautiful church.
- I need to wonder the problem.
- They tried to wonder what happened.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
