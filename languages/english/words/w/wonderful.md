# Wonderful

## Meaning

Wonderful describes someone or something that is extraordinarily good or great.

## Examples

- A fantastic trip to the Orient.
- The film was fantastic!
- A howling success.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
