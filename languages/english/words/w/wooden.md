# Wooden

## Meaning

Wooden describes someone or something that is made or consisting of or employing wood.

## Examples

- A wooden box.
- An ancient cart with wooden wheels.
- It feels wooden.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
