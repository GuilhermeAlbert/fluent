# Work

## Meaning

To work means to exert oneself by doing mental or physical work for a purpose or out of necessity.

## Examples

- I will work hard to improve my grades.
- She worked hard for better living conditions for the poor.
- I need to work soon.

## Notes

Usually uncountable; use "some" or "more" instead of "a" in most everyday contexts.
