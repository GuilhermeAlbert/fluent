# Worker

## Meaning

Worker means someone who works at a specific occupation.

## Examples

- He is a good worker.
- The worker asked a clear question.
- I spoke with the worker yesterday.

## Notes

Usually used for a person or role. Add "a," "an," or "the" when talking about one specific person.
