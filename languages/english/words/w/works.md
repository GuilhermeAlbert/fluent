# Works

## Meaning

Works means buildings for carrying on industrial labor.

## Examples

- They built a large plant to manufacture automobiles.
- I left the works on the table.
- She picked up the works carefully.

## Notes

Usually used as a noun for a physical object. Add "a," "an," or "the" when needed.
