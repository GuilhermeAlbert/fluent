# Worry

## Meaning

To worry means to be worried, concerned, anxious, troubled, or uneasy.

## Examples

- They worry that there was a traffic accident.
- I worry about my job.
- I need to worry soon.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
