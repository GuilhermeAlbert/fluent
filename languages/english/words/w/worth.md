# Worth

## Meaning

Worth is an indefinite quantity of something having a specified value.

## Examples

- 10 dollars worth of gasoline.
- We talked about the worth.
- The worth matters here.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
