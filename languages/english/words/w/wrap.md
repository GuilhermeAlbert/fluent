# Wrap

## Meaning

To wrap means to arrange or fold as a cover or protection.

## Examples

- Wrap the baby before taking her out.
- Wrap the present.
- I need to wrap this today.

## Notes

Most often used as a verb. It can appear after "to," "can," "should," or a subject.
