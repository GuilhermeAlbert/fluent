# Yellow

## Meaning

Yellow describes someone or something that is of the color intermediate between green and orange in the color spectrum.

## Examples

- It feels yellow.
- That was a yellow choice.
- The room looked yellow.

## Notes

Usually used before a noun or after verbs like "be," "feel," "seem," or "look."
