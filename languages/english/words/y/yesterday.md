# Yesterday

## Meaning

Yesterday is the day immediately before today.

## Examples

- It was in yesterday's newspapers.
- The yesterday went by quickly.
- We chose a better yesterday.

## Notes

Commonly used as a noun. The exact article or plural form depends on the sentence.
