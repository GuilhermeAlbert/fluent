# A

## Meaning

A means "to" or "at," and it often marks direction, time, or a person as the object.

## Examples

- Voy a casa.
- Llegamos a las ocho.
- Llamé a mi madre.

## Notes

Very common as a preposition. It can combine with "el" to form "al."
