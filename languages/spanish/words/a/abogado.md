# Abogado

## Meaning

Abogado means "lawyer or solicitor."

## Examples

- Hablamos del abogado.
- El abogado es importante.
- No entiendo el abogado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
