# Abrazo

## Meaning

Abrazo means "hug, embrace."

## Examples

- Hablamos del abrazo.
- El abrazo es importante.
- No entiendo el abrazo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
