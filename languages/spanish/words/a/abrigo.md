# Abrigo

## Meaning

Abrigo means "overcoat or warm clothes."

## Examples

- Hablamos del abrigo.
- El abrigo es importante.
- No entiendo el abrigo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
