# Abril

## Meaning

Abril means "April or years of age."

## Examples

- John ha cumplido 20 abriles.
- Hablamos del abril.
- El abril es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
