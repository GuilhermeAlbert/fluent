# Absolutamente

## Meaning

Absolutamente means "absolutely or completely."

## Examples

- Lo hice absolutamente.
- Llegó absolutamente.
- Funciona absolutamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
