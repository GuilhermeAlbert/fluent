# Abuela

## Meaning

Abuela means "grandmother or female equivalent of abuelo."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la abuela.
- La abuela es importante.
- No entiendo la abuela.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
