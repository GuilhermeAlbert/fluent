# Abuelo

## Meaning

Abuelo means "grandfather or an elderly person."

## Examples

- Su abuelo es simpático.
- Hablamos del abuelo.
- El abuelo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
