# Accidente

## Meaning

Accidente means "accident or accidental."

## Examples

- Hablamos del accidente.
- El accidente es importante.
- No entiendo el accidente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
