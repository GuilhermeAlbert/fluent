# Actitud

## Meaning

Actitud means "posture or pose."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la actitud.
- La actitud es importante.
- No entiendo la actitud.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
