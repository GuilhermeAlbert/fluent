# Actividad

## Meaning

Actividad means "activity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la actividad.
- La actividad es importante.
- No entiendo la actividad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
