# Adiós

## Meaning

Adiós means "farewell; goodbye."

## Examples

- Hablamos del adiós.
- El adiós es importante.
- No entiendo el adiós.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
