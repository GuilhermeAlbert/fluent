# Adolescente

## Meaning

Adolescente means "adolescent or teenager."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la adolescente.
- La adolescente es importante.
- No entiendo la adolescente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
