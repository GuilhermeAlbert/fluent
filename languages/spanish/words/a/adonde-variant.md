# Adonde

## Meaning

Adonde means "to where or whither."

## Examples

- Adonde voy tú no puedes ir.
- Lo hice adonde.
- Llegó adonde.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
