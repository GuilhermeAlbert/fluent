# Advertencia

## Meaning

Advertencia means "warning or advice."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la advertencia.
- La advertencia es importante.
- No entiendo la advertencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
