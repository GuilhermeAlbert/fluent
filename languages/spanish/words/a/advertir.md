# Advertir

## Meaning

Advertir means "to warn or to notice."

## Examples

- Te advierto que no lo hagas otra vez.
- Le advertí del peligro.
- Advertí algunos errores en su informe.

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
