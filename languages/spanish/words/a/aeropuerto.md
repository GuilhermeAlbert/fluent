# Aeropuerto

## Meaning

Aeropuerto means "airport or a Peruvian dish based on fried rice with chicken eggs and various other ingredients."

## Examples

- Hablamos del aeropuerto.
- El aeropuerto es importante.
- No entiendo el aeropuerto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
