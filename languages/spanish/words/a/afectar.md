# Afectar

## Meaning

Afectar means "to affect or to have an effect on."

## Examples

- El alcohol puede afectar su juicio.
- Quiero afectar hoy.
- Vamos a afectar mañana.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
