# Afortunado

## Meaning

Afortunado means "lucky, fortunate."

## Examples

- Me considero afortunado.
- Es muy afortunado.
- Parece afortunado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
