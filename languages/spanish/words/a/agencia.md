# Agencia

## Meaning

Agencia means "agency."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la agencia.
- La agencia es importante.
- No entiendo la agencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
