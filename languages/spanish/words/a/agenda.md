# Agenda

## Meaning

Agenda means "agenda, planner or agenda."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la agenda.
- La agenda es importante.
- No entiendo la agenda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
