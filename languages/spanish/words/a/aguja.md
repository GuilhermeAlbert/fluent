# Aguja

## Meaning

Aguja means "needle or hand."

## Examples

- ¿Tiene usted una aguja para coser estos botones?
- Hablamos de la aguja.
- La aguja es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
