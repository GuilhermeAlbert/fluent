# Agujero

## Meaning

Agujero means "hole or pincushion."

## Examples

- Agujero negro.
- Hablamos del agujero.
- El agujero es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
