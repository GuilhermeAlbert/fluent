# Alcalde

## Meaning

Alcalde means "mayor or an official such as an administrator."

## Examples

- Hablamos del alcalde.
- El alcalde es importante.
- No entiendo el alcalde.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
