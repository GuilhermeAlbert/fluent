# Aldea

## Meaning

Aldea means "village."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la aldea.
- La aldea es importante.
- No entiendo la aldea.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
