# Alegría

## Meaning

Alegría means "joy or happiness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la alegría.
- La alegría es importante.
- No entiendo la alegría.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
