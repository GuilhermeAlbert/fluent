# Alianza

## Meaning

Alianza means "alliance or wedding ring."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la alianza.
- La alianza es importante.
- No entiendo la alianza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
