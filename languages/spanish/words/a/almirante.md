# Almirante

## Meaning

Almirante means "admiral."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la almirante.
- La almirante es importante.
- No entiendo la almirante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
