# Alta

## Meaning

Alta means "certificate of discharge or entry into a profession."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la alta.
- La alta es importante.
- No entiendo la alta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
