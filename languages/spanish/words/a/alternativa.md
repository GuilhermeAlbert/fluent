# Alternativa

## Meaning

Alternativa means "alternative or option."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la alternativa.
- La alternativa es importante.
- No entiendo la alternativa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
