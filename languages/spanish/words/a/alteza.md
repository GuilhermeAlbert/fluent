# Alteza

## Meaning

Alteza means "highness or height."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la alteza.
- La alteza es importante.
- No entiendo la alteza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
