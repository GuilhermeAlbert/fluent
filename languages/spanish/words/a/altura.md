# Altura

## Meaning

Altura means "height or altitude."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la altura.
- La altura es importante.
- No entiendo la altura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
