# Alumno

## Meaning

Alumno means "pupil or student."

## Examples

- Hablamos del alumno.
- El alumno es importante.
- No entiendo el alumno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
