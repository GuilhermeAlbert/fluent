# Ama

## Meaning

Ama means "lady of the house or proprietress."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ama.
- La ama es importante.
- No entiendo la ama.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
