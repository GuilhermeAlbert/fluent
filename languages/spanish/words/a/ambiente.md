# Ambiente

## Meaning

Ambiente means "atmosphere or environment."

## Examples

- Es importante proteger el medio ambiente.
- Hablamos del ambiente.
- El ambiente es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
