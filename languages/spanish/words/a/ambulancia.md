# Ambulancia

## Meaning

Ambulancia means "ambulance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ambulancia.
- La ambulancia es importante.
- No entiendo la ambulancia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
