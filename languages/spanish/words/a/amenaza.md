# Amenaza

## Meaning

Amenaza means "threat or menace."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la amenaza.
- La amenaza es importante.
- No entiendo la amenaza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
