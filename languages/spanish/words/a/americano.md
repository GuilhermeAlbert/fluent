# Americano

## Meaning

Americano means "American or American, US-American."

## Examples

- Hablamos del americano.
- El americano es importante.
- No entiendo el americano.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
