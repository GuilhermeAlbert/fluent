# Amistad

## Meaning

Amistad means "friendship."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la amistad.
- La amistad es importante.
- No entiendo la amistad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
