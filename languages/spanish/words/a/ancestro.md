# Ancestro

## Meaning

Ancestro means "ancestor, forefather."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ancestro.
- El ancestro es importante.
- No entiendo el ancestro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
