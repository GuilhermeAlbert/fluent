# Anillo

## Meaning

Anillo means "ring or annulus."

## Examples

- Hablamos del anillo.
- El anillo es importante.
- No entiendo el anillo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
