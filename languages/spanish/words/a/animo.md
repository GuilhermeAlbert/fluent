# Ánimo

## Meaning

Ánimo means "soul or spirit."

## Examples

- No estoy de ánimo para….
- Ánimo y fuerza.
- Hablamos del ánimo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
