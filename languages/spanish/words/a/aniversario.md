# Aniversario

## Meaning

Aniversario means "anniversary or birthday."

## Examples

- Hablamos del aniversario.
- El aniversario es importante.
- No entiendo el aniversario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
