# Anteriormente

## Meaning

Anteriormente means "previously."

## Examples

- Lo hice anteriormente.
- Llegó anteriormente.
- Funciona anteriormente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
