# Antes

## Meaning

Antes means "earlier or before."

## Examples

- Poco antes.
- Nos conocimos antes, ¿te acuerdas?
- La embajadora llegó antes de lo esperado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
