# Anuncio

## Meaning

Anuncio means "announcement or notice."

## Examples

- Hablamos del anuncio.
- El anuncio es importante.
- No entiendo el anuncio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
