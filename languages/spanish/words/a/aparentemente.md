# Aparentemente

## Meaning

Aparentemente means "apparently or seemingly."

## Examples

- Lo hice aparentemente.
- Llegó aparentemente.
- Funciona aparentemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
