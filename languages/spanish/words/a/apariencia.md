# Apariencia

## Meaning

Apariencia means "appearance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la apariencia.
- La apariencia es importante.
- No entiendo la apariencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
