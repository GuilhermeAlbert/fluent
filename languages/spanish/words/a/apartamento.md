# Apartamento

## Meaning

Apartamento means "flat, apartment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del apartamento.
- El apartamento es importante.
- No entiendo el apartamento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
