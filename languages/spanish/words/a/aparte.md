# Aparte

## Meaning

Aparte means "separately or apart."

## Examples

- Buenas intenciones aparte, aún su comportamiento era imprudente.
- Lo hice aparte.
- Llegó aparte.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
