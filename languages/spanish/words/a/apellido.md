# Apellido

## Meaning

Apellido means "surname or last name."

## Examples

- «Rápido» es mi apellido.
- Hablamos del apellido.
- El apellido es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
