# Apetecer

## Meaning

Apetecer means "to feel like or to fancy."

## Examples

- No me apetece salir esta noche.
- ¿Te apetece ayudarme con esto?
- Me apetece una pizza con champiñones.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
