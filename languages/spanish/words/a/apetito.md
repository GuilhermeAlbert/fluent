# Apetito

## Meaning

Apetito means "appetite."

## Examples

- Hablamos del apetito.
- El apetito es importante.
- No entiendo el apetito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
