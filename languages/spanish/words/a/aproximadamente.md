# Aproximadamente

## Meaning

Aproximadamente means "approximately."

## Examples

- Es aproximadamente diez dólares.
- Lo hice aproximadamente.
- Llegó aproximadamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
