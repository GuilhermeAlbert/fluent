# Araña

## Meaning

Araña means "spider or chandelier."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la araña.
- La araña es importante.
- No entiendo la araña.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
