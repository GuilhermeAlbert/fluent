# Arma

## Meaning

Arma means "weapon, arm."

## Examples

- El arma secreta.
- Las armas secretas.
- Hablamos de la arma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
