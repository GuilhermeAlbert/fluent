# Armario

## Meaning

Armario means "cupboard or storage cabinet."

## Examples

- Hablamos del armario.
- El armario es importante.
- No entiendo el armario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
