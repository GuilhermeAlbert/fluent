# Arroz

## Meaning

Arroz means "rice."

## Examples

- Hablamos del arroz.
- El arroz es importante.
- No entiendo el arroz.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
