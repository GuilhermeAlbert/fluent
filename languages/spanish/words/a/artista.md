# Artista

## Meaning

Artista means "artist or entertainer."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la artista.
- La artista es importante.
- No entiendo la artista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
