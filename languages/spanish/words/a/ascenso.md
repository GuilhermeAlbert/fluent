# Ascenso

## Meaning

Ascenso means "promotion or ascent."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ascenso.
- El ascenso es importante.
- No entiendo el ascenso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
