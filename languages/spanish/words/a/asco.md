# Asco

## Meaning

Asco means "disgust or nausea."

## Examples

- ¡Qué asco!
- Hablamos del asco.
- El asco es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
