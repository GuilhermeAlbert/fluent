# Asesino

## Meaning

Asesino means "murderer or killer."

## Examples

- Hablamos del asesino.
- El asesino es importante.
- No entiendo el asesino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
