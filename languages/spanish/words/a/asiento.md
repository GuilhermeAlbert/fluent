# Asiento

## Meaning

Asiento means "seat or sediment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del asiento.
- El asiento es importante.
- No entiendo el asiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
