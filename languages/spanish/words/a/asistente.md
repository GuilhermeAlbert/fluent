# Asistente

## Meaning

Asistente means "attendee or assistant."

## Examples

- Hablamos del asistente.
- El asistente es importante.
- No entiendo el asistente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
