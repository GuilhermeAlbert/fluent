# Asunto

## Meaning

Asunto means "matter or issue."

## Examples

- Hablamos del asunto.
- El asunto es importante.
- No entiendo el asunto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
