# Ataque

## Meaning

Ataque means "attack or attack, offensive."

## Examples

- Ataque sorpresa.
- Hablamos del ataque.
- El ataque es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
