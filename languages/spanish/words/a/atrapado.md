# Atrapado

## Meaning

Atrapado means "caught or captured."

## Examples

- ¡Estamos atrapados!
- Es muy atrapado.
- Parece atrapado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
