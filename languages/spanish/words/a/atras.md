# Atrás

## Meaning

Atrás means "behind or back in time."

## Examples

- Miles de años atrás.
- Lo hice atrás.
- Llegó atrás.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
