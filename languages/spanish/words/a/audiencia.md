# Audiencia

## Meaning

Audiencia means "audience or a tribunal of justice of a defined territory which hears its problems."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la audiencia.
- La audiencia es importante.
- No entiendo la audiencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
