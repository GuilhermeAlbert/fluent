# Aumento

## Meaning

Aumento means "increase or raise."

## Examples

- Hablamos del aumento.
- El aumento es importante.
- No entiendo el aumento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
