# Ausencia

## Meaning

Ausencia means "absence or absence seizure."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ausencia.
- La ausencia es importante.
- No entiendo la ausencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
