# Auto

## Meaning

Auto means "a public deed or ceremony."

## Examples

- Hablamos del auto.
- El auto es importante.
- No entiendo el auto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
