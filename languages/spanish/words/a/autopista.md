# Autopista

## Meaning

Autopista means "motorway, freeway."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la autopista.
- La autopista es importante.
- No entiendo la autopista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
