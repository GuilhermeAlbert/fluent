# Autoridad

## Meaning

Autoridad means "authority."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la autoridad.
- La autoridad es importante.
- No entiendo la autoridad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
