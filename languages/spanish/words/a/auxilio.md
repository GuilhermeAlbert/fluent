# Auxilio

## Meaning

Auxilio means "aid, help."

## Examples

- Hablamos del auxilio.
- El auxilio es importante.
- No entiendo el auxilio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
