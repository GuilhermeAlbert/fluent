# Ave

## Meaning

Ave means "bird or fowl."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ave.
- La ave es importante.
- No entiendo la ave.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
