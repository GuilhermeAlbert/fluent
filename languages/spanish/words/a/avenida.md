# Avenida

## Meaning

Avenida means "avenue or flood."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la avenida.
- La avenida es importante.
- No entiendo la avenida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
