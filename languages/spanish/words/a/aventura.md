# Aventura

## Meaning

Aventura means "adventure or affair."

## Examples

- Aventura de una noche.
- Hablamos de la aventura.
- La aventura es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
