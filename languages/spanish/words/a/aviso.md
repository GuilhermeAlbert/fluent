# Aviso

## Meaning

Aviso means "warning or announcement."

## Examples

- Hablamos del aviso.
- El aviso es importante.
- No entiendo el aviso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
