# Ayer

## Meaning

Ayer means "yesterday or days gone by."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ayer.
- El ayer es importante.
- No entiendo el ayer.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
