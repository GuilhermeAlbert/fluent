# Bahía

## Meaning

Bahía means "bay."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bahía.
- La bahía es importante.
- No entiendo la bahía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
