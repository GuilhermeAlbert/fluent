# Bailarina

## Meaning

Bailarina means "ballet flat or flat."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bailarina.
- La bailarina es importante.
- No entiendo la bailarina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
