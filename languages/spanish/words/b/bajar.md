# Bajar

## Meaning

Bajar means "to go down or come down."

## Examples

- (Me) bajé del taxi.
- (Se) bajaron del árbol.
- Quiero bajar hoy.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
