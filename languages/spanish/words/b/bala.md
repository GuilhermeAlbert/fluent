# Bala

## Meaning

Bala means "bullet."

## Examples

- La bala atravesó la pared de la casa.
- Encontraron una bala en la esceña del crimen.
- Hablamos de la bala.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
