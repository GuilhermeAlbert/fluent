# Balón

## Meaning

Balón means "ball or balloon."

## Examples

- Hablamos del balón.
- El balón es importante.
- No entiendo el balón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
