# Bandera

## Meaning

Bandera means "flag, banner."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bandera.
- La bandera es importante.
- No entiendo la bandera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
