# Baño

## Meaning

Baño means "bath or bathroom."

## Examples

- Hablamos del baño.
- El baño es importante.
- No entiendo el baño.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
