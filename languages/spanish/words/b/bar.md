# Bar

## Meaning

Bar means "bar or coffee shop."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bar.
- El bar es importante.
- No entiendo el bar.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
