# Barba

## Meaning

Barba means "beardy or bearded man."

## Examples

- Hablamos del barba.
- El barba es importante.
- No entiendo el barba.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
