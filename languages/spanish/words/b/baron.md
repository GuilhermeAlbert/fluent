# Barón

## Meaning

Barón means "baron or baron, bigwig."

## Examples

- Hablamos del barón.
- El barón es importante.
- No entiendo el barón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
