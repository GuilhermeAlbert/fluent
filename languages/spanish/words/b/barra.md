# Barra

## Meaning

Barra means "bar, rod or bar."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la barra.
- La barra es importante.
- No entiendo la barra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
