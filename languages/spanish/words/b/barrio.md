# Barrio

## Meaning

Barrio means "neighbourhood or any neighbourhood of the original."

## Examples

- Un barrio de clase media.
- Hablamos del barrio.
- El barrio es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
