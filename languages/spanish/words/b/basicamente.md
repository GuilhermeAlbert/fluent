# Básicamente

## Meaning

Básicamente means "basically."

## Examples

- Lo hice básicamente.
- Llegó básicamente.
- Funciona básicamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
