# Bastante

## Meaning

Bastante means "quite or rather."

## Examples

- Es bastante claro.
- He trabajado bastante.
- Lo conozco bastante.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
