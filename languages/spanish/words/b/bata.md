# Bata

## Meaning

Bata means "dressing gown or robe."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bata.
- La bata es importante.
- No entiendo la bata.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
