# Batalla

## Meaning

Batalla means "battle."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la batalla.
- La batalla es importante.
- No entiendo la batalla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
