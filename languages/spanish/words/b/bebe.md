# Bebé

## Meaning

Bebé means "baby."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bebé.
- La bebé es importante.
- No entiendo la bebé.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
