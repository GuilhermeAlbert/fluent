# Bebida

## Meaning

Bebida means "drink or beverage."

## Examples

- ¿Quieres una bebida?
- Hablamos de la bebida.
- La bebida es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
