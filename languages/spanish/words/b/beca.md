# Beca

## Meaning

Beca means "grant or stipend."

## Examples

- Una beca Erasmus.
- Hablamos de la beca.
- La beca es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
