# Belleza

## Meaning

Belleza means "beauty."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la belleza.
- La belleza es importante.
- No entiendo la belleza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
