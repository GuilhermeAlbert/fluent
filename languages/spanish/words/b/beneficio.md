# Beneficio

## Meaning

Beneficio means "benefit or profit."

## Examples

- Hablamos del beneficio.
- El beneficio es importante.
- No entiendo el beneficio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
