# Beso

## Meaning

Beso means "kiss."

## Examples

- Los besos son la cura para todo.
- Hablamos del beso.
- El beso es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
