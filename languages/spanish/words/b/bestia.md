# Bestia

## Meaning

Bestia means "beast or animal."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bestia.
- La bestia es importante.
- No entiendo la bestia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
