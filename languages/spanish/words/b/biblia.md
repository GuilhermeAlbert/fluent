# Biblia

## Meaning

Biblia means "bible or a wall of text."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la biblia.
- La biblia es importante.
- No entiendo la biblia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
