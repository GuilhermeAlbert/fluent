# Biblioteca

## Meaning

Biblioteca means "library or bookcase."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la biblioteca.
- La biblioteca es importante.
- No entiendo la biblioteca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
