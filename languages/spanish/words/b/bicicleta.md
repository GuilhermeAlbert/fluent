# Bicicleta

## Meaning

Bicicleta means "bicycle or pushbike."

## Examples

- Andar/montar en bicicleta.
- Hablamos de la bicicleta.
- La bicicleta es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
