# Bienvenida

## Meaning

Bienvenida means "welcome or greeting."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bienvenida.
- La bienvenida es importante.
- No entiendo la bienvenida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
