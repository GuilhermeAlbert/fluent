# Billete

## Meaning

Billete means "banknote or bill."

## Examples

- ¿Ya reservaste los billetes del tren?
- Hablamos del billete.
- El billete es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
