# Boca

## Meaning

Boca means "mouth or oral cavity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la boca.
- La boca es importante.
- No entiendo la boca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
