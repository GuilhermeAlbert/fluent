# Bola

## Meaning

Bola means "ball or testicle."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bola.
- La bola es importante.
- No entiendo la bola.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
