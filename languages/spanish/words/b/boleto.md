# Boleto

## Meaning

Boleto means "porcini or any bolete mushroom."

## Examples

- Hablamos del boleto.
- El boleto es importante.
- No entiendo el boleto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
