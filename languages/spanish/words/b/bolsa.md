# Bolsa

## Meaning

Bolsa means "bag or coin purse."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bolsa.
- La bolsa es importante.
- No entiendo la bolsa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
