# Bolsillo

## Meaning

Bolsillo means "pocket or pocketbook."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bolsillo.
- El bolsillo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
