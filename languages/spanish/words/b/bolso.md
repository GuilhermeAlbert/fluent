# Bolso

## Meaning

Bolso means "purse or bag."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bolso.
- El bolso es importante.
- No entiendo el bolso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
