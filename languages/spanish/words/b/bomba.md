# Bomba

## Meaning

Bomba means "bomb or pump."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bomba.
- La bomba es importante.
- No entiendo la bomba.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
