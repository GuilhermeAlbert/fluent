# Bondad

## Meaning

Bondad means "goodness or good deed."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bondad.
- La bondad es importante.
- No entiendo la bondad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
