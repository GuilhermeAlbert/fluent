# Bordo

## Meaning

Bordo means "side or board."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bordo.
- El bordo es importante.
- No entiendo el bordo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
