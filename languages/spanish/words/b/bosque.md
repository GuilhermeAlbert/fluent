# Bosque

## Meaning

Bosque means "forest."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bosque.
- El bosque es importante.
- No entiendo el bosque.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
