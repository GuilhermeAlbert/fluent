# Bote

## Meaning

Bote means "boat or dinghy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del bote.
- El bote es importante.
- No entiendo el bote.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
