# Botella

## Meaning

Botella means "bottle or tank."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la botella.
- La botella es importante.
- No entiendo la botella.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
