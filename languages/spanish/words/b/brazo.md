# Brazo

## Meaning

Brazo means "arm or branch."

## Examples

- Hablamos del brazo.
- El brazo es importante.
- No entiendo el brazo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
