# Broma

## Meaning

Broma means "joke or prank."

## Examples

- Hizo una broma y todos se rieron.
- Hablamos de la broma.
- La broma es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
