# Bruja

## Meaning

Bruja means "witch or sorceress."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la bruja.
- La bruja es importante.
- No entiendo la bruja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
