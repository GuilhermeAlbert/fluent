# Burro

## Meaning

Burro means "donkey or especially one used as a pack animal."

## Examples

- Hablamos del burro.
- El burro es importante.
- No entiendo el burro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
