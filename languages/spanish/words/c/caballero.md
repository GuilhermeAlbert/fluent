# Caballero

## Meaning

Caballero means "horseman or knight."

## Examples

- Hablamos del caballero.
- El caballero es importante.
- No entiendo el caballero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
