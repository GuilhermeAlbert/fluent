# Caballo

## Meaning

Caballo means "horse or knight."

## Examples

- Hablamos del caballo.
- El caballo es importante.
- No entiendo el caballo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
