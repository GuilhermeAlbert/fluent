# Cabaña

## Meaning

Cabaña means "cabin or cottage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cabaña.
- La cabaña es importante.
- No entiendo la cabaña.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
