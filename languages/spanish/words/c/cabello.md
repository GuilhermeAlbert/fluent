# Cabello

## Meaning

Cabello means "hair or hair strand."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cabello.
- El cabello es importante.
- No entiendo el cabello.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
