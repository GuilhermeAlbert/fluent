# Cabeza

## Meaning

Cabeza means "head or mind."

## Examples

- Perder la cabeza.
- No puedo quitarme a esa chica de la cabeza.
- Hablamos de la cabeza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
