# Cabina

## Meaning

Cabina means "cabin."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cabina.
- La cabina es importante.
- No entiendo la cabina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
