# Cabo

## Meaning

Cabo means "end or edge."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cabo.
- El cabo es importante.
- No entiendo el cabo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
