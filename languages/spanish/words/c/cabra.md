# Cabra

## Meaning

Cabra means "female goat or goat."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cabra.
- La cabra es importante.
- No entiendo la cabra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
