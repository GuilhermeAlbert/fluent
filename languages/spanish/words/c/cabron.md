# Cabrón

## Meaning

Cabrón means "coward or bastard."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cabrón.
- El cabrón es importante.
- No entiendo el cabrón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
