# Cadena

## Meaning

Cadena means "chain or necklace."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cadena.
- La cadena es importante.
- No entiendo la cadena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
