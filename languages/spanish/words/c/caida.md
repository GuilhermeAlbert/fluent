# Caída

## Meaning

Caída means "fall or crash."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la caída.
- La caída es importante.
- No entiendo la caída.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
