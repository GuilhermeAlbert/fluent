# Calidad

## Meaning

Calidad means "quality or kind."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la calidad.
- La calidad es importante.
- No entiendo la calidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
