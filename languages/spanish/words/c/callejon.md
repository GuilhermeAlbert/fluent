# Callejón

## Meaning

Callejón means "alley or side street."

## Examples

- Hablamos del callejón.
- El callejón es importante.
- No entiendo el callejón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
