# Calma

## Meaning

Calma means "calm or stillness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la calma.
- La calma es importante.
- No entiendo la calma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
