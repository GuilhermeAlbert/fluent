# Calzón

## Meaning

Calzón means "underpants or female undergarment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del calzón.
- El calzón es importante.
- No entiendo el calzón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
