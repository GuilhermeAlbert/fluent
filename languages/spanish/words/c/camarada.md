# Camarada

## Meaning

Camarada means "comrade."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la camarada.
- La camarada es importante.
- No entiendo la camarada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
