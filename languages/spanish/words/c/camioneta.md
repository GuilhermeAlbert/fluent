# Camioneta

## Meaning

Camioneta means "pick-up truck or station wagon."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la camioneta.
- La camioneta es importante.
- No entiendo la camioneta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
