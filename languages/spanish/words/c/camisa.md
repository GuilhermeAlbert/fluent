# Camisa

## Meaning

Camisa means "shirt or gas mantle."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la camisa.
- La camisa es importante.
- No entiendo la camisa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
