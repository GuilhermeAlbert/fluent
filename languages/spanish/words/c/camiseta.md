# Camiseta

## Meaning

Camiseta means "T-shirt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la camiseta.
- La camiseta es importante.
- No entiendo la camiseta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
