# Campamento

## Meaning

Campamento means "camping or campsite."

## Examples

- Campamento de verano.
- Hablamos del campamento.
- El campamento es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
