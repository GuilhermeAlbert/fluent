# Campana

## Meaning

Campana means "bell or a bell-shaped object."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la campana.
- La campana es importante.
- No entiendo la campana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
