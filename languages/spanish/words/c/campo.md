# Campo

## Meaning

Campo means "countryside or country."

## Examples

- En el campo, es tranquilo.
- Campo de fútbol.
- Campo de golf.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
