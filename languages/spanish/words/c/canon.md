# Cañón

## Meaning

Cañón means "canyon or cannon."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cañón.
- El cañón es importante.
- No entiendo el cañón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
