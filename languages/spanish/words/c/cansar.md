# Cansar

## Meaning

Cansar means "to tire or to get tired."

## Examples

- Se me cansan los brazos.
- Nunca me canso de escucharla cantar.
- Quiero cansar hoy.

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
