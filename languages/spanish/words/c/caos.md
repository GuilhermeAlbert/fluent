# Caos

## Meaning

Caos means "chaos or mayhem."

## Examples

- Hablamos del caos.
- El caos es importante.
- No entiendo el caos.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
