# Capa

## Meaning

Capa means "cloak or cape."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la capa.
- La capa es importante.
- No entiendo la capa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
