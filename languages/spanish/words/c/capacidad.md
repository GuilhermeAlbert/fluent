# Capacidad

## Meaning

Capacidad means "capacity or capability."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la capacidad.
- La capacidad es importante.
- No entiendo la capacidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
