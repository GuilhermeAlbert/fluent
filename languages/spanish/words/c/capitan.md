# Capitán

## Meaning

Capitán means "captain."

## Examples

- Hablamos del capitán.
- El capitán es importante.
- No entiendo el capitán.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
