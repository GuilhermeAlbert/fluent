# Capullo

## Meaning

Capullo means "cocoon or bud."

## Examples

- Hablamos del capullo.
- El capullo es importante.
- No entiendo el capullo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
