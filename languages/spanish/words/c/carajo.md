# Carajo

## Meaning

Carajo is a vulgar word used in strong expressions of anger, surprise, or frustration.

## Examples

- ¿Qué carajo pasó?
- No me importa un carajo.
- ¡Vete al carajo!

## Notes

Vulgar and informal. It appears often in speech, but it is not polite.
