# Caridad

## Meaning

Caridad means "charity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la caridad.
- La caridad es importante.
- No entiendo la caridad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
