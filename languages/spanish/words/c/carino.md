# Cariño

## Meaning

Cariño means "affection or fondness."

## Examples

- Cariño, ya estoy en casa.
- Hablamos del cariño.
- El cariño es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
