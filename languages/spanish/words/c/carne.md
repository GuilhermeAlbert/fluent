# Carne

## Meaning

Carne means "flesh, the soft part of a body which covers the bones."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la carne.
- La carne es importante.
- No entiendo la carne.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
