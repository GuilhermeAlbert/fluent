# Cartel

## Meaning

Cartel means "poster or placard."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cartel.
- El cartel es importante.
- No entiendo el cartel.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
