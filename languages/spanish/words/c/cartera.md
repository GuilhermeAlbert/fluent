# Cartera

## Meaning

Cartera means "wallet or billfold."

## Examples

- Ministro sin cartera.
- Hablamos de la cartera.
- La cartera es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
