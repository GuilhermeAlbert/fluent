# Casi

## Meaning

Casi means "almost."

## Examples

- Casi casi que me parece que ocurriese ayer mismo.
- Lo hice casi.
- Llegó casi.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
