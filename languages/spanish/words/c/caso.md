# Caso

## Meaning

Caso means "case."

## Examples

- En mi caso.
- Es un caso especial.
- Aumentan los casos de diabetes entre niños.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
