# Castillo

## Meaning

Castillo means "castle."

## Examples

- Hablamos del castillo.
- El castillo es importante.
- No entiendo el castillo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
