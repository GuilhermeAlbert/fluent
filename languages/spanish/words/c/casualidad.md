# Casualidad

## Meaning

Casualidad means "chance or coincidence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la casualidad.
- La casualidad es importante.
- No entiendo la casualidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
