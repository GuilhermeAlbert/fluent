# Causa

## Meaning

Causa means "cause or lawsuit."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la causa.
- La causa es importante.
- No entiendo la causa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
