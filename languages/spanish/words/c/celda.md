# Celda

## Meaning

Celda means "prison cell or cell."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la celda.
- La celda es importante.
- No entiendo la celda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
