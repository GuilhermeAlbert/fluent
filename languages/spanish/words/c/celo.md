# Celo

## Meaning

Celo means "zeal or heat."

## Examples

- Está en celo.
- Su novio tiene celos de mí.
- Hablamos del celo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
