# Cena

## Meaning

Cena means "supper, dinner."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cena.
- La cena es importante.
- No entiendo la cena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
