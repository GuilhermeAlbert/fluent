# Ceniza

## Meaning

Ceniza means "ash or ashes."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ceniza.
- La ceniza es importante.
- No entiendo la ceniza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
