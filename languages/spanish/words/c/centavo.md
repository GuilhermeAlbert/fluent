# Centavo

## Meaning

Centavo means "hundredth or cent."

## Examples

- Siete centavos.
- Hablamos del centavo.
- El centavo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
