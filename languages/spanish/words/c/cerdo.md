# Cerdo

## Meaning

Cerdo means "pig or hog."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cerdo.
- El cerdo es importante.
- No entiendo el cerdo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
