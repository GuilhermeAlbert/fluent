# Cerebro

## Meaning

Cerebro means "brain or nerd."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cerebro.
- El cerebro es importante.
- No entiendo el cerebro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
