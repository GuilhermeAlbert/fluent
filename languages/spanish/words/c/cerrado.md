# Cerrado

## Meaning

Cerrado means "shut or closed."

## Examples

- El baúl está cerrado.
- Gracias por mantener la boca cerrada.
- Es muy cerrado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
