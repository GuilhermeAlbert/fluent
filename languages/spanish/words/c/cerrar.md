# Cerrar

## Meaning

Cerrar means "to close or to shut."

## Examples

- No olvides cerrar la puerta.
- Cierra la ventana, por favor.
- Quieren cerrar tu negocio.

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
