# Certeza

## Meaning

Certeza means "certainty."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la certeza.
- La certeza es importante.
- No entiendo la certeza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
