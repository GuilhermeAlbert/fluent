# Cerveza

## Meaning

Cerveza means "beer."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cerveza.
- La cerveza es importante.
- No entiendo la cerveza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
