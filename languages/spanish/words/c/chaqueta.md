# Chaqueta

## Meaning

Chaqueta means "jacket or piece of clothing worn on the upper body outside a shirt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la chaqueta.
- La chaqueta es importante.
- No entiendo la chaqueta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
