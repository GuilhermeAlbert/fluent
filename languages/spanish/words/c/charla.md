# Charla

## Meaning

Charla means "talk, chat."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la charla.
- La charla es importante.
- No entiendo la charla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
