# Chica

## Meaning

Chica means "gal or chick."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la chica.
- La chica es importante.
- No entiendo la chica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
