# Chimenea

## Meaning

Chimenea means "chimney or smokestack."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la chimenea.
- La chimenea es importante.
- No entiendo la chimenea.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
