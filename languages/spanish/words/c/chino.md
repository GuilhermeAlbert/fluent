# Chino

## Meaning

Chino means "gobbledygook or a Chinese-run establishment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del chino.
- El chino es importante.
- No entiendo el chino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
