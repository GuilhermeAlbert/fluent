# Cicatriz

## Meaning

Cicatriz means "scar, cicatrix."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cicatriz.
- La cicatriz es importante.
- No entiendo la cicatriz.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
