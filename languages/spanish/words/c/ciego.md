# Ciego

## Meaning

Ciego means "blind or very drunk."

## Examples

- Ser ciego de nacimiento.
- Es muy ciego.
- Parece ciego.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
