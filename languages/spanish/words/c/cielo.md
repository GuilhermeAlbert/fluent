# Cielo

## Meaning

Cielo means "sky or heaven."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cielo.
- El cielo es importante.
- No entiendo el cielo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
