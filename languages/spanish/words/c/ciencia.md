# Ciencia

## Meaning

Ciencia means "science or erudition."

## Examples

- El árbol de la ciencia.
- Hablamos de la ciencia.
- La ciencia es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
