# Ciento

## Meaning

Ciento means "hundred or hundreds."

## Examples

- Compré dos cientos de manzanas.
- Hablamos del ciento.
- El ciento es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
