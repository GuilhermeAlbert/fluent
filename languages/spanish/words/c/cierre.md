# Cierre

## Meaning

Cierre means "shutting or closing."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cierre.
- El cierre es importante.
- No entiendo el cierre.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
