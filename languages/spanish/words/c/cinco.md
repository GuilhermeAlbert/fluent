# Cinco

## Meaning

Cinco means "a five."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cinco.
- El cinco es importante.
- No entiendo el cinco.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
