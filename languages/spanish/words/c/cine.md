# Cine

## Meaning

Cine means "cinema or moviehouse."

## Examples

- Un cine mudo.
- Esta ilustración es cine.
- Hablamos del cine.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
