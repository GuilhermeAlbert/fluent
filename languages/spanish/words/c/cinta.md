# Cinta

## Meaning

Cinta means "ribbon or worn by girls in the hair."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cinta.
- La cinta es importante.
- No entiendo la cinta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
