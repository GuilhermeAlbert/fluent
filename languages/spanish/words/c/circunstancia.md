# Circunstancia

## Meaning

Circunstancia means "circumstance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la circunstancia.
- La circunstancia es importante.
- No entiendo la circunstancia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
