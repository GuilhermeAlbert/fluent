# Clase

## Meaning

Clase means "lecture or class."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la clase.
- La clase es importante.
- No entiendo la clase.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
