# Claudia

## Meaning

Claudia means "greengage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la claudia.
- La claudia es importante.
- No entiendo la claudia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
