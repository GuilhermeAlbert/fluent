# Clínica

## Meaning

Clínica means "clinic."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la clínica.
- La clínica es importante.
- No entiendo la clínica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
