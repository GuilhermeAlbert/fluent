# Cobra

## Meaning

Cobra means "cobra or the act of hacer la cobra."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cobra.
- La cobra es importante.
- No entiendo la cobra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
