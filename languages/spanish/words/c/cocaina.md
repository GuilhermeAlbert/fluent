# Cocaína

## Meaning

Cocaína means "cocaine."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cocaína.
- La cocaína es importante.
- No entiendo la cocaína.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
