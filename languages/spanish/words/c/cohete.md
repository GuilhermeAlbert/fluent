# Cohete

## Meaning

Cohete means "skyrocket or rocket."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cohete.
- El cohete es importante.
- No entiendo el cohete.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
