# Coincidencia

## Meaning

Coincidencia means "coincidence or agreement."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la coincidencia.
- La coincidencia es importante.
- No entiendo la coincidencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
