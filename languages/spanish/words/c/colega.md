# Colega

## Meaning

Colega means "colleague or buddy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la colega.
- La colega es importante.
- No entiendo la colega.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
