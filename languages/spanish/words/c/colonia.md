# Colonia

## Meaning

Colonia means "colony or neighbourhood."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la colonia.
- La colonia es importante.
- No entiendo la colonia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
