# Columna

## Meaning

Columna means "a column or a circular support."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la columna.
- La columna es importante.
- No entiendo la columna.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
