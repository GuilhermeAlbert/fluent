# Coma

## Meaning

Coma means "comma or misericord."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la coma.
- La coma es importante.
- No entiendo la coma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
