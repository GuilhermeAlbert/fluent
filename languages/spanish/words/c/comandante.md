# Comandante

## Meaning

Comandante means "commander."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la comandante.
- La comandante es importante.
- No entiendo la comandante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
