# Comedor

## Meaning

Comedor means "dining room or eatery."

## Examples

- Hablamos del comedor.
- El comedor es importante.
- No entiendo el comedor.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
