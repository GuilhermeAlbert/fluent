# Comentario

## Meaning

Comentario means "comment or commentary."

## Examples

- Hablamos del comentario.
- El comentario es importante.
- No entiendo el comentario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
