# Cometa

## Meaning

Cometa means "kite."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cometa.
- La cometa es importante.
- No entiendo la cometa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
