# Comida

## Meaning

Comida means "food or lunch."

## Examples

- Yo no quiero comida.
- Hablamos de la comida.
- La comida es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
