# Comienzo

## Meaning

Comienzo means "beginning."

## Examples

- A comienzos del siglo.
- Hablamos del comienzo.
- El comienzo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
