# Cómoda

## Meaning

Cómoda means "chest of drawers."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cómoda.
- La cómoda es importante.
- No entiendo la cómoda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
