# Compañera

## Meaning

Compañera means "female equivalent of compañero fellow or companion."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la compañera.
- La compañera es importante.
- No entiendo la compañera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
