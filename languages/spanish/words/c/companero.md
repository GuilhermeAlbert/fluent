# Compañero

## Meaning

Compañero means "companion or mate."

## Examples

- Compañero de viaje.
- Compañero de clase.
- Compañero de piso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
