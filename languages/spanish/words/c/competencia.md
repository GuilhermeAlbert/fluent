# Competencia

## Meaning

Competencia means "competence or expertise."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la competencia.
- La competencia es importante.
- No entiendo la competencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
