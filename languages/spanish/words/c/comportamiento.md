# Comportamiento

## Meaning

Comportamiento means "behavior."

## Examples

- Hablamos del comportamiento.
- El comportamiento es importante.
- No entiendo el comportamiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
