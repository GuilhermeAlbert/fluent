# Compra

## Meaning

Compra means "purchase or buying."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la compra.
- La compra es importante.
- No entiendo la compra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
