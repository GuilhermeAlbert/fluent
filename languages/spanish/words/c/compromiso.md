# Compromiso

## Meaning

Compromiso means "commitment or engagement."

## Examples

- Sin compromiso.
- Hablamos del compromiso.
- El compromiso es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
