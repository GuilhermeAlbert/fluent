# Concepto

## Meaning

Concepto means "concept or conceit."

## Examples

- Hablamos del concepto.
- El concepto es importante.
- No entiendo el concepto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
