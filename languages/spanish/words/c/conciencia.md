# Conciencia

## Meaning

Conciencia means "conscience or consciousness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la conciencia.
- La conciencia es importante.
- No entiendo la conciencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
