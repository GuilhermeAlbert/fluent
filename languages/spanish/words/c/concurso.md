# Concurso

## Meaning

Concurso means "concourse or competition."

## Examples

- Hablamos del concurso.
- El concurso es importante.
- No entiendo el concurso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
