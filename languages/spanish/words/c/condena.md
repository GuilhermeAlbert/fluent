# Condena

## Meaning

Condena means "sentence, punishment."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la condena.
- La condena es importante.
- No entiendo la condena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
