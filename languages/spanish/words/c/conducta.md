# Conducta

## Meaning

Conducta means "behaviour, conduct."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la conducta.
- La conducta es importante.
- No entiendo la conducta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
