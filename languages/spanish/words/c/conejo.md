# Conejo

## Meaning

Conejo means "rabbit or cony."

## Examples

- Hablamos del conejo.
- El conejo es importante.
- No entiendo el conejo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
