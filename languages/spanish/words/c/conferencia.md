# Conferencia

## Meaning

Conferencia means "conference or meeting."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la conferencia.
- La conferencia es importante.
- No entiendo la conferencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
