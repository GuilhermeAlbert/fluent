# Confianza

## Meaning

Confianza means "confidence, trust."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la confianza.
- La confianza es importante.
- No entiendo la confianza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
