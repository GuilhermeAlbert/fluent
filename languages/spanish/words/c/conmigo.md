# Conmigo

## Meaning

Conmigo means "with me or with myself."

## Examples

- Lo hice conmigo.
- Llegó conmigo.
- Funciona conmigo.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
