# Coño

## Meaning

Coño means "Spaniard or a person who belongs to wealthy Spanish-."

## Examples

- Hablamos del coño.
- El coño es importante.
- No entiendo el coño.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
