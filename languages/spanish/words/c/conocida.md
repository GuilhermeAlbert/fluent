# Conocida

## Meaning

Conocida means "female equivalent of conocido."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la conocida.
- La conocida es importante.
- No entiendo la conocida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
