# Consejero

## Meaning

Consejero means "adviser or counsellor."

## Examples

- Hablamos del consejero.
- El consejero es importante.
- No entiendo el consejero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
