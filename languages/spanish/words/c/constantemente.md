# Constantemente

## Meaning

Constantemente means "constantly."

## Examples

- Lo hice constantemente.
- Llegó constantemente.
- Funciona constantemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
