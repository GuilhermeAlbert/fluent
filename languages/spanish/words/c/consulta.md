# Consulta

## Meaning

Consulta means "consultation or query."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la consulta.
- La consulta es importante.
- No entiendo la consulta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
