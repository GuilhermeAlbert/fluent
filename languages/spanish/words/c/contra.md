# Contra

## Meaning

Contra means "antidote or counterpunch."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la contra.
- La contra es importante.
- No entiendo la contra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
