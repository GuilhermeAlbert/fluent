# Contrato

## Meaning

Contrato means "contract, agreement."

## Examples

- Hablamos del contrato.
- El contrato es importante.
- No entiendo el contrato.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
