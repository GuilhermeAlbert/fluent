# Coordenada

## Meaning

Coordenada means "coordinate."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la coordenada.
- La coordenada es importante.
- No entiendo la coordenada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
