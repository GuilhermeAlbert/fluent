# Copa

## Meaning

Copa means "stemmed glass or glass."

## Examples

- Una copa de vino.
- Hablamos de la copa.
- La copa es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
