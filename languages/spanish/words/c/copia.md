# Copia

## Meaning

Copia means "copy or abundance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la copia.
- La copia es importante.
- No entiendo la copia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
