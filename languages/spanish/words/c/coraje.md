# Coraje

## Meaning

Coraje means "anger or rage."

## Examples

- Hablamos del coraje.
- El coraje es importante.
- No entiendo el coraje.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
