# Corazón

## Meaning

Corazón means "heart or emotions."

## Examples

- Hablamos del corazón.
- El corazón es importante.
- No entiendo el corazón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
