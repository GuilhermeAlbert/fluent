# Cortesía

## Meaning

Cortesía means "courtesy or politeness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cortesía.
- La cortesía es importante.
- No entiendo la cortesía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
