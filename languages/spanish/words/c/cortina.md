# Cortina

## Meaning

Cortina means "curtain."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cortina.
- La cortina es importante.
- No entiendo la cortina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
