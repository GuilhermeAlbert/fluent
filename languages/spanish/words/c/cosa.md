# Cosa

## Meaning

Cosa means "thing."

## Examples

- *ve un gato* ay, qué cosita tan linda!
- Hablamos de la cosa.
- La cosa es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
