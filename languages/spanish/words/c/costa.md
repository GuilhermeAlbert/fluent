# Costa

## Meaning

Costa means "coast or coastline."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la costa.
- La costa es importante.
- No entiendo la costa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
