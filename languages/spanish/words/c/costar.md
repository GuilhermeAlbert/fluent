# Costar

## Meaning

Costar means "to cost or to find something very difficult."

## Examples

- Cuesta catorce pesos.
- Cuando estoy de pie, me cuesta respirar.
- Le cuesta mucho pronunciar esa palabra.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
