# Costilla

## Meaning

Costilla means "rib or a type of biscuit."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la costilla.
- La costilla es importante.
- No entiendo la costilla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
