# Costo

## Meaning

Costo means "cost or hashish."

## Examples

- Hablamos del costo.
- El costo es importante.
- No entiendo el costo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
