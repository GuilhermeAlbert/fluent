# Costumbre

## Meaning

Costumbre means "habit or custom."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la costumbre.
- La costumbre es importante.
- No entiendo la costumbre.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
