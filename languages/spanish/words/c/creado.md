# Creado

## Meaning

Creado means "created or vested."

## Examples

- Ella tiene intereses creados en esas nuevas reformas.
- Es muy creado.
- Parece creado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
