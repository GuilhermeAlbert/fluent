# Crédito

## Meaning

Crédito means "credit."

## Examples

- Hablamos del crédito.
- El crédito es importante.
- No entiendo el crédito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
