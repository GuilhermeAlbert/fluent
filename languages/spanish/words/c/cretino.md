# Cretino

## Meaning

Cretino means "a person affected by cretinism or an idiot."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cretino.
- El cretino es importante.
- No entiendo el cretino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
