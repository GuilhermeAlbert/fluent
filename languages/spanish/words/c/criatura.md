# Criatura

## Meaning

Criatura means "creature or critter."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la criatura.
- La criatura es importante.
- No entiendo la criatura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
