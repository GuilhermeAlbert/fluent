# Crisis

## Meaning

Crisis means "crisis or attack."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la crisis.
- La crisis es importante.
- No entiendo la crisis.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
