# Cristal

## Meaning

Cristal means "crystal or glass."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cristal.
- El cristal es importante.
- No entiendo el cristal.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
