# Cristo

## Meaning

Cristo means "Jesus or hassle."

## Examples

- ¡Es un cristo!
- Hablamos del cristo.
- El cristo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
