# Crítica

## Meaning

Crítica means "criticism or flack."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la crítica.
- La crítica es importante.
- No entiendo la crítica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
