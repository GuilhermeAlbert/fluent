# Cruce

## Meaning

Cruce means "crossroads or crossing."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cruce.
- El cruce es importante.
- No entiendo el cruce.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
