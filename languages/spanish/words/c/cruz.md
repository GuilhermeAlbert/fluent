# Cruz

## Meaning

Cruz means "cross."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cruz.
- La cruz es importante.
- No entiendo la cruz.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
