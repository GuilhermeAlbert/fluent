# Cuba

## Meaning

Cuba means "barrel."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cuba.
- La cuba es importante.
- No entiendo la cuba.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
