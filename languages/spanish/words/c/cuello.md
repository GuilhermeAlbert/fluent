# Cuello

## Meaning

Cuello means "neck or collar."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cuello.
- El cuello es importante.
- No entiendo el cuello.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
