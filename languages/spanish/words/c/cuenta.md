# Cuenta

## Meaning

Cuenta means "count or tally."

## Examples

- Nos trae la cuenta, por favor.
- Hablamos de la cuenta.
- La cuenta es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
