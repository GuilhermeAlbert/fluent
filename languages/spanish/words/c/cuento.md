# Cuento

## Meaning

Cuento means "story or tale."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cuento.
- El cuento es importante.
- No entiendo el cuento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
