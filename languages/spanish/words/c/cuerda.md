# Cuerda

## Meaning

Cuerda means "rope or cord."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cuerda.
- La cuerda es importante.
- No entiendo la cuerda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
