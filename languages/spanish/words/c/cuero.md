# Cuero

## Meaning

Cuero means "leather or animal skin."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cuero.
- El cuero es importante.
- No entiendo el cuero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
