# Cuerpo

## Meaning

Cuerpo means "body or body, torso."

## Examples

- Hablamos del cuerpo.
- El cuerpo es importante.
- No entiendo el cuerpo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
