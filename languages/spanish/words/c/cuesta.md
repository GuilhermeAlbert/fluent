# Cuesta

## Meaning

Cuesta means "slope or cuesta."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cuesta.
- La cuesta es importante.
- No entiendo la cuesta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
