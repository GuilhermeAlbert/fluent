# Cueva

## Meaning

Cueva means "cave or basement."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cueva.
- La cueva es importante.
- No entiendo la cueva.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
