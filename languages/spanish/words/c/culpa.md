# Culpa

## Meaning

Culpa means "fault or guilt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la culpa.
- La culpa es importante.
- No entiendo la culpa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
