# Culpable

## Meaning

Culpable means "guilty, culpable or guilty."

## Examples

- Hacerle sentir culpable.
- Una conciencia culpable.
- Es muy culpable.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
