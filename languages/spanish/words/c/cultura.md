# Cultura

## Meaning

Cultura means "culture or knowledge."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cultura.
- La cultura es importante.
- No entiendo la cultura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
