# Cura

## Meaning

Cura means "cure or drunkenness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la cura.
- La cura es importante.
- No entiendo la cura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
