# Curiosidad

## Meaning

Curiosidad means "curiosity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la curiosidad.
- La curiosidad es importante.
- No entiendo la curiosidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
