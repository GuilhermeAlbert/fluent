# Curso

## Meaning

Curso means "course or trajectory."

## Examples

- Un curso intensivo.
- Hablamos del curso.
- El curso es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
