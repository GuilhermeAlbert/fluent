# Custodia

## Meaning

Custodia means "custody or safekeeping."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la custodia.
- La custodia es importante.
- No entiendo la custodia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
