# Dama

## Meaning

Dama means "lady or dame."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dama.
- La dama es importante.
- No entiendo la dama.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
