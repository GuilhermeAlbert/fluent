# Dar

## Meaning

Dar means "to give or to give out."

## Examples

- Dame mi celular.
- Te daré tiempo para jugar si terminas las tareas a tiempo.
- Deme su teléfono.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
