# Debilidad

## Meaning

Debilidad means "debility or weakness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la debilidad.
- La debilidad es importante.
- No entiendo la debilidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
