# Dedo

## Meaning

Dedo means "finger or digit."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dedo.
- El dedo es importante.
- No entiendo el dedo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
