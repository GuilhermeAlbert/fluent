# Defensa

## Meaning

Defensa means "defense or retaining wall."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la defensa.
- La defensa es importante.
- No entiendo la defensa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
