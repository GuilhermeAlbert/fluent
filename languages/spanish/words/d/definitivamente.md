# Definitivamente

## Meaning

Definitivamente means "definitely or definitively."

## Examples

- Lo hice definitivamente.
- Llegó definitivamente.
- Funciona definitivamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
