# Del

## Meaning

Del is the contraction of "de" + "el," meaning "of the" or "from the."

## Examples

- Vengo del trabajo.
- Es amigo del profesor.
- Salió del cuarto.

## Notes

Use "del" before a masculine singular noun that would take "el."
