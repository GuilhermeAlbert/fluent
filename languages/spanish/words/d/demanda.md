# Demanda

## Meaning

Demanda means "demand or formal complaint."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la demanda.
- La demanda es importante.
- No entiendo la demanda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
