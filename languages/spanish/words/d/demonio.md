# Demonio

## Meaning

Demonio means "demon, devil."

## Examples

- Hablamos del demonio.
- El demonio es importante.
- No entiendo el demonio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
