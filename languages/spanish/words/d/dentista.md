# Dentista

## Meaning

Dentista means "dentist."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dentista.
- La dentista es importante.
- No entiendo la dentista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
