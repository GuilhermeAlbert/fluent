# Dentro

## Meaning

Dentro means "inside or indoors."

## Examples

- Dentro de la caja.
- Dentro de cinco minutos.
- Lo hice dentro.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
