# Departamento

## Meaning

Departamento means "department or apartment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del departamento.
- El departamento es importante.
- No entiendo el departamento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
