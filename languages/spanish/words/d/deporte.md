# Deporte

## Meaning

Deporte means "sport."

## Examples

- Hablamos del deporte.
- El deporte es importante.
- No entiendo el deporte.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
