# Derecha

## Meaning

Derecha means "right."

## Examples

- Tuerza a la derecha.
- Hablamos de la derecha.
- La derecha es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
