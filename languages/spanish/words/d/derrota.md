# Derrota

## Meaning

Derrota means "path or route."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la derrota.
- La derrota es importante.
- No entiendo la derrota.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
