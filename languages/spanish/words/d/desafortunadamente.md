# Desafortunadamente

## Meaning

Desafortunadamente means "unfortunately; regrettably."

## Examples

- Desafortunadamente, soy adulto.
- Lo hice desafortunadamente.
- Llegó desafortunadamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
