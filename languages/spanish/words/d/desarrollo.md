# Desarrollo

## Meaning

Desarrollo means "development."

## Examples

- Hablamos del desarrollo.
- El desarrollo es importante.
- No entiendo el desarrollo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
