# Desayuno

## Meaning

Desayuno means "breakfast."

## Examples

- Para mi desayuno, tomo pan.
- Hablamos del desayuno.
- El desayuno es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
