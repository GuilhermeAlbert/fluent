# Descanso

## Meaning

Descanso means "rest or break."

## Examples

- Hablamos del descanso.
- El descanso es importante.
- No entiendo el descanso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
