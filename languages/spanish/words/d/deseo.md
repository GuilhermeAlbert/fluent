# Deseo

## Meaning

Deseo means "desire, wish."

## Examples

- Hablamos del deseo.
- El deseo es importante.
- No entiendo el deseo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
