# Desgracia

## Meaning

Desgracia means "disgrace or misfortune."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la desgracia.
- La desgracia es importante.
- No entiendo la desgracia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
