# Desorden

## Meaning

Desorden means "disorder or untidiness."

## Examples

- Hablamos del desorden.
- El desorden es importante.
- No entiendo el desorden.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
