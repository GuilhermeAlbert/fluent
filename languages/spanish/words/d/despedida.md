# Despedida

## Meaning

Despedida means "farewell or farewell party."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la despedida.
- La despedida es importante.
- No entiendo la despedida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
