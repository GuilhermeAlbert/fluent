# Destino

## Meaning

Destino means "destiny or fate."

## Examples

- Destino cruel.
- Un capricho del destino.
- Hablamos del destino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
