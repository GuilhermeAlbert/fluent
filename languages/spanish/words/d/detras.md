# Detrás

## Meaning

Detrás means "behind or at the rear."

## Examples

- Lo hice detrás.
- Llegó detrás.
- Funciona detrás.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
