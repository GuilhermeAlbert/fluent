# Deuda

## Meaning

Deuda means "debt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la deuda.
- La deuda es importante.
- No entiendo la deuda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
