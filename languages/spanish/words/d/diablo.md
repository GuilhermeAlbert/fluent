# Diablo

## Meaning

Diablo means "devil or crowbar."

## Examples

- Hablamos del diablo.
- El diablo es importante.
- No entiendo el diablo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
