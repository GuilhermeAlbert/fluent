# Diamante

## Meaning

Diamante means "diamond."

## Examples

- Hablamos del diamante.
- El diamante es importante.
- No entiendo el diamante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
