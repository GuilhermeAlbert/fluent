# Diana

## Meaning

Diana means "bullseye or archery target."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la diana.
- La diana es importante.
- No entiendo la diana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
