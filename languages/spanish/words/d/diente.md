# Diente

## Meaning

Diente means "tooth or tooth, cog."

## Examples

- Hablamos del diente.
- El diente es importante.
- No entiendo el diente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
