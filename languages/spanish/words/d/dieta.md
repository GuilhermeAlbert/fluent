# Dieta

## Meaning

Dieta means "diet."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dieta.
- La dieta es importante.
- No entiendo la dieta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
