# Dignidad

## Meaning

Dignidad means "dignity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dignidad.
- La dignidad es importante.
- No entiendo la dignidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
