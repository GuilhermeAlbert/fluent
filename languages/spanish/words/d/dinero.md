# Dinero

## Meaning

Dinero means "money or wealth."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dinero.
- El dinero es importante.
- No entiendo el dinero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
