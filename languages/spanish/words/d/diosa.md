# Diosa

## Meaning

Diosa means "goddess."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la diosa.
- La diosa es importante.
- No entiendo la diosa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
