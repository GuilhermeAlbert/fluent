# Directamente

## Meaning

Directamente means "directly or firsthand."

## Examples

- Lo hice directamente.
- Llegó directamente.
- Funciona directamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
