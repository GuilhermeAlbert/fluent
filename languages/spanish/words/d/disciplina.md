# Disciplina

## Meaning

Disciplina means "discipline or discipline, subject."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la disciplina.
- La disciplina es importante.
- No entiendo la disciplina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
