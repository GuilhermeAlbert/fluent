# Disculpa

## Meaning

Disculpa means "excuse or apology."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la disculpa.
- La disculpa es importante.
- No entiendo la disculpa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
