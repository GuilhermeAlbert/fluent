# Disparo

## Meaning

Disparo means "shot or gunfire."

## Examples

- ¡Acabamos de oír disparos!
- Hablamos del disparo.
- El disparo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
