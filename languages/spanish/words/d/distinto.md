# Distinto

## Meaning

Distinto means "distinct, different."

## Examples

- El libro tiene distintas interpretaciones.
- Es muy distinto.
- Parece distinto.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
