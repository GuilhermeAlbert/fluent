# Distrito

## Meaning

Distrito means "district."

## Examples

- Hablamos del distrito.
- El distrito es importante.
- No entiendo el distrito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
