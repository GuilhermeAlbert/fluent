# Dj

## Meaning

Dj means "DJ or deejay."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dj.
- La dj es importante.
- No entiendo la dj.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
