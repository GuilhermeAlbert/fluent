# Doctor

## Meaning

Doctor means "doctor or physician."

## Examples

- Hablamos del doctor.
- El doctor es importante.
- No entiendo el doctor.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
