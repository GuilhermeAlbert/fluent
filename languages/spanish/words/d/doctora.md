# Doctora

## Meaning

Doctora means "doctor's wife."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la doctora.
- La doctora es importante.
- No entiendo la doctora.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
