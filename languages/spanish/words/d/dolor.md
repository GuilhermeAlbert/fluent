# Dolor

## Meaning

Dolor means "pain or ache."

## Examples

- Dolores de crecimiento.
- Dolor de espalda.
- Dolor de garganta.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
