# Domingo

## Meaning

Domingo means "Sunday."

## Examples

- Hablamos del domingo.
- El domingo es importante.
- No entiendo el domingo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
