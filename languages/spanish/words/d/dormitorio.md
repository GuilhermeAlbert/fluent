# Dormitorio

## Meaning

Dormitorio means "bedroom or dormitory."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dormitorio.
- El dormitorio es importante.
- No entiendo el dormitorio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
