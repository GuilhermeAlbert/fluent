# Dos

## Meaning

Dos means "dung, excrement."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dos.
- El dos es importante.
- No entiendo el dos.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
