# Dosis

## Meaning

Dosis means "dose."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la dosis.
- La dosis es importante.
- No entiendo la dosis.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
