# Dragón

## Meaning

Dragón means "dragon or dragoon."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dragón.
- El dragón es importante.
- No entiendo el dragón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
