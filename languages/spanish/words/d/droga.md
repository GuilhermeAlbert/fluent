# Droga

## Meaning

Droga means "drug or indebtedness."

## Examples

- Has usado drogas antes?
- Hablamos de la droga.
- La droga es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
