# Ducha

## Meaning

Ducha means "shower."

## Examples

- Voy a pegarme una ducha.
- Hablamos de la ducha.
- La ducha es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
