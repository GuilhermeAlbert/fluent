# Duda

## Meaning

Duda means "doubt or question."

## Examples

- No ofrece dudas.
- No admite dudas.
- Me quedé con una duda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
