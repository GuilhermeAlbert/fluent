# Dueño

## Meaning

Dueño means "owner or proprietor."

## Examples

- Hablamos del dueño.
- El dueño es importante.
- No entiendo el dueño.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
