# Duro

## Meaning

Duro means "hard or firm."

## Examples

- Ponlo sobre una superficie dura.
- Le traté de advertir que el posgrado iba a ser duro.
- Es muy duro.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
