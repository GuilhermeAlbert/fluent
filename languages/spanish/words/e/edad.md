# Edad

## Meaning

Edad means "age or time since an event."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la edad.
- La edad es importante.
- No entiendo la edad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
