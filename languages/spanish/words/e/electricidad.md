# Electricidad

## Meaning

Electricidad means "electricity or electrical current."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la electricidad.
- La electricidad es importante.
- No entiendo la electricidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
