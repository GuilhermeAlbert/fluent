# Elefante

## Meaning

Elefante means "elephant."

## Examples

- Hablamos del elefante.
- El elefante es importante.
- No entiendo el elefante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
