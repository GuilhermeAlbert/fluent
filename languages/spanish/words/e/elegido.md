# Elegido

## Meaning

Elegido means "chosenness, selectness."

## Examples

- Hablamos del elegido.
- El elegido es importante.
- No entiendo el elegido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
