# Elemento

## Meaning

Elemento means "element."

## Examples

- Hablamos del elemento.
- El elemento es importante.
- No entiendo el elemento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
