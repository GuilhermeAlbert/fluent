# Embajada

## Meaning

Embajada means "embassy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la embajada.
- La embajada es importante.
- No entiendo la embajada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
