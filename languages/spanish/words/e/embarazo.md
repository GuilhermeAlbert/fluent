# Embarazo

## Meaning

Embarazo means "pregnancy or childbearing."

## Examples

- Hablamos del embarazo.
- El embarazo es importante.
- No entiendo el embarazo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
