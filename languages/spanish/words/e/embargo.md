# Embargo

## Meaning

Embargo means "embargo or trade embargo."

## Examples

- Hablamos del embargo.
- El embargo es importante.
- No entiendo el embargo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
