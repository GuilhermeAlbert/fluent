# Emergencia

## Meaning

Emergencia means "emergency or emergence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la emergencia.
- La emergencia es importante.
- No entiendo la emergencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
