# Empleo

## Meaning

Empleo means "job or employment."

## Examples

- Hablamos del empleo.
- El empleo es importante.
- No entiendo el empleo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
