# Empresa

## Meaning

Empresa means "company or enterprise."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la empresa.
- La empresa es importante.
- No entiendo la empresa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
