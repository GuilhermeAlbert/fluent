# Enamorado

## Meaning

Enamorado means "infatuated or having a crush on."

## Examples

- Ella está enamorada de mi hermano.
- Estoy enamorado de tu hermana.
- Es muy enamorado.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
