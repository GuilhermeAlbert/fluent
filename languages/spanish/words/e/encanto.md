# Encanto

## Meaning

Encanto means "charm or captivation."

## Examples

- Hablamos del encanto.
- El encanto es importante.
- No entiendo el encanto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
