# Encargo

## Meaning

Encargo means "order or errand."

## Examples

- Hablamos del encargo.
- El encargo es importante.
- No entiendo el encargo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
