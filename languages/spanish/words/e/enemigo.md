# Enemigo

## Meaning

Enemigo means "foe; enemy."

## Examples

- Los enemigos de nuestros enemigos son nuestros amigos.
- Hablamos del enemigo.
- El enemigo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
