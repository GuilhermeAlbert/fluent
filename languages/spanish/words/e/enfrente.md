# Enfrente

## Meaning

Enfrente means "in front of or across from."

## Examples

- Enfrente de la tienda.
- Lo hice enfrente.
- Llegó enfrente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
