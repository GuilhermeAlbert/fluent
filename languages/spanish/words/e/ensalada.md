# Ensalada

## Meaning

Ensalada means "salad or a genre of secular polyphonic music popular in 16th century Spain."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ensalada.
- La ensalada es importante.
- No entiendo la ensalada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
