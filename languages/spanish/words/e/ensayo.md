# Ensayo

## Meaning

Ensayo means "essay or paper."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ensayo.
- El ensayo es importante.
- No entiendo el ensayo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
