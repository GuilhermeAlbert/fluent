# Enterado

## Meaning

Enterado means "know-it-all."

## Examples

- Hablamos del enterado.
- El enterado es importante.
- No entiendo el enterado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
