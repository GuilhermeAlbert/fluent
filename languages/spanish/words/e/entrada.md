# Entrada

## Meaning

Entrada means "entrance or entry."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la entrada.
- La entrada es importante.
- No entiendo la entrada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
