# Entrega

## Meaning

Entrega means "delivery or handover."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la entrega.
- La entrega es importante.
- No entiendo la entrega.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
