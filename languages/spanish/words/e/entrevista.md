# Entrevista

## Meaning

Entrevista means "interview."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la entrevista.
- La entrevista es importante.
- No entiendo la entrevista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
