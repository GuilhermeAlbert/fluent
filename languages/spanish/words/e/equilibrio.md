# Equilibrio

## Meaning

Equilibrio means "balance, equilibrium."

## Examples

- Hablamos del equilibrio.
- El equilibrio es importante.
- No entiendo el equilibrio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
