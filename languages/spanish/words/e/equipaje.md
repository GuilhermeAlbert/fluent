# Equipaje

## Meaning

Equipaje means "luggage or baggage."

## Examples

- Hablamos del equipaje.
- El equipaje es importante.
- No entiendo el equipaje.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
