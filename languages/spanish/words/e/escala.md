# Escala

## Meaning

Escala means "ladder or scale."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la escala.
- La escala es importante.
- No entiendo la escala.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
