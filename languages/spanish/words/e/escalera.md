# Escalera

## Meaning

Escalera means "stairs or staircase."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la escalera.
- La escalera es importante.
- No entiendo la escalera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
