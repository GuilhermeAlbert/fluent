# Escena

## Meaning

Escena means "scene or stage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la escena.
- La escena es importante.
- No entiendo la escena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
