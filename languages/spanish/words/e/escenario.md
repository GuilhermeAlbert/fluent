# Escenario

## Meaning

Escenario means "scenario or scene."

## Examples

- El peor escenario posible.
- Hablamos del escenario.
- El escenario es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
