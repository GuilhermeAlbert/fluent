# Esclavo

## Meaning

Esclavo means "slave."

## Examples

- Hablamos del esclavo.
- El esclavo es importante.
- No entiendo el esclavo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
