# Escopeta

## Meaning

Escopeta means "shotgun."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la escopeta.
- La escopeta es importante.
- No entiendo la escopeta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
