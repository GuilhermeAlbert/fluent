# Escudo

## Meaning

Escudo means "shield or emblem."

## Examples

- Hablamos del escudo.
- El escudo es importante.
- No entiendo el escudo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
