# Escuela

## Meaning

Escuela means "school."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la escuela.
- La escuela es importante.
- No entiendo la escuela.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
