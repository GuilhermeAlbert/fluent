# Esencia

## Meaning

Esencia means "essence or gist."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la esencia.
- La esencia es importante.
- No entiendo la esencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
