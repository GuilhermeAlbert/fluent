# Espada

## Meaning

Espada means "sword or which is designed to cut."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la espada.
- La espada es importante.
- No entiendo la espada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
