# Espalda

## Meaning

Espalda means "back or backstroke."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la espalda.
- La espalda es importante.
- No entiendo la espalda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
