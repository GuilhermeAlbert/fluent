# Especialista

## Meaning

Especialista means "specialist or stuntman."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la especialista.
- La especialista es importante.
- No entiendo la especialista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
