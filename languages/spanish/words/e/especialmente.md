# Especialmente

## Meaning

Especialmente means "specially or especially."

## Examples

- Lo hice especialmente.
- Llegó especialmente.
- Funciona especialmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
