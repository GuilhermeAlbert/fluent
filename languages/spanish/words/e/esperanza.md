# Esperanza

## Meaning

Esperanza means "hope or hopefulness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la esperanza.
- La esperanza es importante.
- No entiendo la esperanza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
