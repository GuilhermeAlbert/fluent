# Espíritu

## Meaning

Espíritu means "spirit or soul."

## Examples

- Hablamos del espíritu.
- El espíritu es importante.
- No entiendo el espíritu.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
