# Esposa

## Meaning

Esposa means "wife."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la esposa.
- La esposa es importante.
- No entiendo la esposa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
