# Esposas

## Meaning

Esposas means "handcuffs."

## Examples

- Hablamos de las esposas.
- Las esposas es importante.
- No entiendo las esposas.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
