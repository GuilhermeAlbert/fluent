# Esposo

## Meaning

Esposo means "husband."

## Examples

- Hablamos del esposo.
- El esposo es importante.
- No entiendo el esposo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
