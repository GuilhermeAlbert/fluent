# Esquina

## Meaning

Esquina means "corner."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la esquina.
- La esquina es importante.
- No entiendo la esquina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
