# Estatua

## Meaning

Estatua means "statue."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la estatua.
- La estatua es importante.
- No entiendo la estatua.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
