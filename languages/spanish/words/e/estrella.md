# Estrella

## Meaning

Estrella means "star."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la estrella.
- La estrella es importante.
- No entiendo la estrella.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
