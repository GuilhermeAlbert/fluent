# Estudiante

## Meaning

Estudiante means "student."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la estudiante.
- La estudiante es importante.
- No entiendo la estudiante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
