# Estudio

## Meaning

Estudio means "study or studio."

## Examples

- Hablamos del estudio.
- El estudio es importante.
- No entiendo el estudio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
