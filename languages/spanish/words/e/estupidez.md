# Estupidez

## Meaning

Estupidez means "stupidity or foolishness."

## Examples

- ¡No digas tantas estupideces!
- Hablamos de la estupidez.
- La estupidez es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
