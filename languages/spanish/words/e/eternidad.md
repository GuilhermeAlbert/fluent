# Eternidad

## Meaning

Eternidad means "eternity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la eternidad.
- La eternidad es importante.
- No entiendo la eternidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
