# Evidencia

## Meaning

Evidencia means "evidence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la evidencia.
- La evidencia es importante.
- No entiendo la evidencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
