# Exactamente

## Meaning

Exactamente means "exactly."

## Examples

- Lo hice exactamente.
- Llegó exactamente.
- Funciona exactamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
