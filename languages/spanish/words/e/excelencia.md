# Excelencia

## Meaning

Excelencia means "excellency, brilliance or Excellency."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la excelencia.
- La excelencia es importante.
- No entiendo la excelencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
