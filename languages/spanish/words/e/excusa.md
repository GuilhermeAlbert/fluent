# Excusa

## Meaning

Excusa means "excuse or cop-out."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la excusa.
- La excusa es importante.
- No entiendo la excusa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
