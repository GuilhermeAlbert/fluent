# Existencia

## Meaning

Existencia means "existence or stock."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la existencia.
- La existencia es importante.
- No entiendo la existencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
