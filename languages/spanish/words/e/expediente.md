# Expediente

## Meaning

Expediente means "case or file."

## Examples

- Hablamos del expediente.
- El expediente es importante.
- No entiendo el expediente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
