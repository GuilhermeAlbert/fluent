# Experiencia

## Meaning

Experiencia means "experience or expertise."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la experiencia.
- La experiencia es importante.
- No entiendo la experiencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
