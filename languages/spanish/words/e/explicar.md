# Explicar

## Meaning

Explicar means "to explain or to explain oneself."

## Examples

- Near-synonyms: aclarar, describir, detallar, desglosar, simplificar.
- Quiero explicar hoy.
- Vamos a explicar mañana.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
