# Exterior

## Meaning

Exterior means "outside or abroad."

## Examples

- Hablamos del exterior.
- El exterior es importante.
- No entiendo el exterior.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
