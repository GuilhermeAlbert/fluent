# Extranjero

## Meaning

Extranjero means "A foreign country or countries."

## Examples

- Vamos al extranjero por primera vez.
- Hablamos del extranjero.
- El extranjero es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
