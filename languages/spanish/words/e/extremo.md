# Extremo

## Meaning

Extremo means "extreme or end."

## Examples

- Hablamos del extremo.
- El extremo es importante.
- No entiendo el extremo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
