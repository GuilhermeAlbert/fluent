# Fábrica

## Meaning

Fábrica means "factory."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fábrica.
- La fábrica es importante.
- No entiendo la fábrica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
