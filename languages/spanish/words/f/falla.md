# Falla

## Meaning

Falla means "flaw or failure."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la falla.
- La falla es importante.
- No entiendo la falla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
