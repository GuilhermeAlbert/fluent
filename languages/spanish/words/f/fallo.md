# Fallo

## Meaning

Fallo means "mistake or failure."

## Examples

- Hablamos del fallo.
- El fallo es importante.
- No entiendo el fallo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
