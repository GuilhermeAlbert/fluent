# Falta

## Meaning

Falta means "lack or shortage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la falta.
- La falta es importante.
- No entiendo la falta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
