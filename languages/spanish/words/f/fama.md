# Fama

## Meaning

Fama means "fame or reputation."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fama.
- La fama es importante.
- No entiendo la fama.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
