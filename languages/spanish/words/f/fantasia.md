# Fantasía

## Meaning

Fantasía means "fantasy or costume jewellery."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fantasía.
- La fantasía es importante.
- No entiendo la fantasía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
