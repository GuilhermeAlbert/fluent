# Fantasma

## Meaning

Fantasma means "ghost or phantom."

## Examples

- El fantasma de la ópera.
- Hablamos del fantasma.
- El fantasma es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
