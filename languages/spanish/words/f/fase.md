# Fase

## Meaning

Fase means "phase or stage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fase.
- La fase es importante.
- No entiendo la fase.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
