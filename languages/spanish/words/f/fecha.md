# Fecha

## Meaning

Fecha means "date."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fecha.
- La fecha es importante.
- No entiendo la fecha.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
