# Felicidad

## Meaning

Felicidad means "happiness or something that brings happiness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la felicidad.
- La felicidad es importante.
- No entiendo la felicidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
