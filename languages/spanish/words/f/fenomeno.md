# Fenómeno

## Meaning

Fenómeno means "phenomenon or freak."

## Examples

- Hablamos del fenómeno.
- El fenómeno es importante.
- No entiendo el fenómeno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
