# Fianza

## Meaning

Fianza means "suretyship or bail."

## Examples

- Salió bajo fianza.
- Pagar una fianza.
- Hablamos de la fianza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
