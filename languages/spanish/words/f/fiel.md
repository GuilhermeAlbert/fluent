# Fiel

## Meaning

Fiel means "faithful or true."

## Examples

- Irene siempre ha sido fiel a su marido.
- Sé fiel a quien eres.
- Los perros son fieles.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
