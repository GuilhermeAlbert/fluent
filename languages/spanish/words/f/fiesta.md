# Fiesta

## Meaning

Fiesta means "party or feast."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fiesta.
- La fiesta es importante.
- No entiendo la fiesta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
