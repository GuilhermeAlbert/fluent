# Figura

## Meaning

Figura means "figure or charge."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la figura.
- La figura es importante.
- No entiendo la figura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
