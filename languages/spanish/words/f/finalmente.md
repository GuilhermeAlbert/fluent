# Finalmente

## Meaning

Finalmente means "finally, in the end."

## Examples

- Lo hice finalmente.
- Llegó finalmente.
- Funciona finalmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
