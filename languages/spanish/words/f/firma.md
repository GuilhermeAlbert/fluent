# Firma

## Meaning

Firma means "signature or seal."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la firma.
- La firma es importante.
- No entiendo la firma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
