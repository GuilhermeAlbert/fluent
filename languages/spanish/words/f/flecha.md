# Flecha

## Meaning

Flecha means "arrow."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la flecha.
- La flecha es importante.
- No entiendo la flecha.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
