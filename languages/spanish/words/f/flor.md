# Flor

## Meaning

Flor means "flower or bloom."

## Examples

- Flor de harina.
- En la flor de la vida.
- Hablamos de la flor.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
