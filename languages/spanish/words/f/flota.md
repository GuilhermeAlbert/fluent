# Flota

## Meaning

Flota means "fleet or crowd."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la flota.
- La flota es importante.
- No entiendo la flota.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
