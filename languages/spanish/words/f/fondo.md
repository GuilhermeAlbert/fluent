# Fondo

## Meaning

Fondo means "bottom or back."

## Examples

- Al fondo.
- Hablamos del fondo.
- El fondo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
