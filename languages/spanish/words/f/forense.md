# Forense

## Meaning

Forense means "forensic examiner or coroner."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la forense.
- La forense es importante.
- No entiendo la forense.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
