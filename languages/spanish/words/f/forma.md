# Forma

## Meaning

Forma means "shape or form."

## Examples

- ¡Tiene que haber otra forma!
- Hablamos de la forma.
- La forma es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
