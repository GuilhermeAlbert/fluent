# Fortaleza

## Meaning

Fortaleza means "fortress or fortitude."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fortaleza.
- La fortaleza es importante.
- No entiendo la fortaleza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
