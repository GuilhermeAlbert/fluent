# Fortuna

## Meaning

Fortuna means "fortune or prophecy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fortuna.
- La fortuna es importante.
- No entiendo la fortuna.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
