# Foto

## Meaning

Foto means "photo, photograph."

## Examples

- ¿Puede sacar una foto por favor?
- Hablamos de la foto.
- La foto es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
