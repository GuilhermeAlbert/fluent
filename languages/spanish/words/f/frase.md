# Frase

## Meaning

Frase means "phrase or sentence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la frase.
- La frase es importante.
- No entiendo la frase.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
