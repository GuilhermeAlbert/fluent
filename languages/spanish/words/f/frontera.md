# Frontera

## Meaning

Frontera means "border or frontier."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la frontera.
- La frontera es importante.
- No entiendo la frontera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
