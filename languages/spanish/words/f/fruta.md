# Fruta

## Meaning

Fruta means "fruit."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fruta.
- La fruta es importante.
- No entiendo la fruta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
