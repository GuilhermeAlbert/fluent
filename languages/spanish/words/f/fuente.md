# Fuente

## Meaning

Fuente means "spring or fountain."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fuente.
- La fuente es importante.
- No entiendo la fuente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
