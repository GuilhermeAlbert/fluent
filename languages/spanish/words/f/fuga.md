# Fuga

## Meaning

Fuga means "escape or flight."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la fuga.
- La fuga es importante.
- No entiendo la fuga.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
