# Furia

## Meaning

Furia means "fury or rage."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la furia.
- La furia es importante.
- No entiendo la furia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
