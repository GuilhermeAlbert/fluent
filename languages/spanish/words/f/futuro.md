# Futuro

## Meaning

Futuro means "future or future, future tense."

## Examples

- Hablamos del futuro.
- El futuro es importante.
- No entiendo el futuro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
