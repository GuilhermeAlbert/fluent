# Galaxia

## Meaning

Galaxia means "galaxy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la galaxia.
- La galaxia es importante.
- No entiendo la galaxia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
