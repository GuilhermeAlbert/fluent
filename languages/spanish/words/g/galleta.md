# Galleta

## Meaning

Galleta means "cookie / biscuit or slap."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la galleta.
- La galleta es importante.
- No entiendo la galleta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
