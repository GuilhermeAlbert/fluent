# Gallina

## Meaning

Gallina means "hen or chicken."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la gallina.
- La gallina es importante.
- No entiendo la gallina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
