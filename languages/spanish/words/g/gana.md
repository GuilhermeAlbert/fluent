# Gana

## Meaning

Gana means "desire."

## Examples

- Tengo ganas de irme.
- Lo que me da la gana.
- Hablamos de la gana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
