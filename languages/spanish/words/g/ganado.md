# Ganado

## Meaning

Ganado means "livestock or cattle."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ganado.
- El ganado es importante.
- No entiendo el ganado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
