# Ganancia

## Meaning

Ganancia means "profit or gain."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ganancia.
- La ganancia es importante.
- No entiendo la ganancia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
