# Gasto

## Meaning

Gasto means "expenditure or cost."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gasto.
- El gasto es importante.
- No entiendo el gasto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
