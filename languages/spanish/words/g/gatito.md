# Gatito

## Meaning

Gatito means "kitten."

## Examples

- Tenemos un gatito que se llama Colin.
- Hablamos del gatito.
- El gatito es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
