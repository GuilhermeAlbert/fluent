# Gato

## Meaning

Gato means "cat or tomcat."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gato.
- El gato es importante.
- No entiendo el gato.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
