# Generalmente

## Meaning

Generalmente means "generally, normally."

## Examples

- Lo hice generalmente.
- Llegó generalmente.
- Funciona generalmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
