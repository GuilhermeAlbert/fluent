# Genial

## Meaning

Genial means "genial or pleasant."

## Examples

- ¡Espero que hoy te vaya genial!
- Es muy genial.
- Parece genial.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
