# Gesto

## Meaning

Gesto means "gesture or expression."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gesto.
- El gesto es importante.
- No entiendo el gesto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
