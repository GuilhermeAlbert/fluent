# Gigante

## Meaning

Gigante means "giant or juggernaut."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gigante.
- El gigante es importante.
- No entiendo el gigante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
