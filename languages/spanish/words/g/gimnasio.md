# Gimnasio

## Meaning

Gimnasio means "gym, gymnasium."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gimnasio.
- El gimnasio es importante.
- No entiendo el gimnasio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
