# Giro

## Meaning

Giro means "turn or spin."

## Examples

- Navegación giro a giro.
- Hablamos del giro.
- El giro es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
