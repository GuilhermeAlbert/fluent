# Globo

## Meaning

Globo means "balloon or globe."

## Examples

- Hablamos del globo.
- El globo es importante.
- No entiendo el globo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
