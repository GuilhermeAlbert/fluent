# Gloria

## Meaning

Gloria means "glory."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la gloria.
- La gloria es importante.
- No entiendo la gloria.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
