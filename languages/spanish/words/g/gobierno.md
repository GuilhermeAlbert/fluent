# Gobierno

## Meaning

Gobierno means "government."

## Examples

- Hablamos del gobierno.
- El gobierno es importante.
- No entiendo el gobierno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
