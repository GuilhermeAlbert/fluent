# Gordo

## Meaning

Gordo means "fat man or the grand prize in a lottery."

## Examples

- ¿Vamos al cine, gordo?
- Hablamos del gordo.
- El gordo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
