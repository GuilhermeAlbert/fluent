# Gota

## Meaning

Gota means "a drop or small amount of."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la gota.
- La gota es importante.
- No entiendo la gota.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
