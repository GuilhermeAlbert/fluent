# Gracia

## Meaning

Gracia means "grace or charm."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la gracia.
- La gracia es importante.
- No entiendo la gracia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
