# Grado

## Meaning

Grado means "degree or grade."

## Examples

- El agua suele hervir a cien grados centígrados.
- Conocí a mi primera novia en octavo grado.
- Hablamos del grado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
