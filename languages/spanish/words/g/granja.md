# Granja

## Meaning

Granja means "farm, grange."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la granja.
- La granja es importante.
- No entiendo la granja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
