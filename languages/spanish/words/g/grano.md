# Grano

## Meaning

Grano means "grain or seed."

## Examples

- Un grano de arroz.
- Un grano de maíz.
- Un grano de arena.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
