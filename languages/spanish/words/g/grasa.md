# Grasa

## Meaning

Grasa means "fat or grease used in cooking."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la grasa.
- La grasa es importante.
- No entiendo la grasa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
