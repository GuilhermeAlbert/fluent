# Gravedad

## Meaning

Gravedad means "seriousness or severity."

## Examples

- Herido de gravedad.
- Hablamos de la gravedad.
- La gravedad es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
