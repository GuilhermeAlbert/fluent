# Griego

## Meaning

Griego means "Greek or an unintelligible language."

## Examples

- Es griego para mí.
- Hablamos del griego.
- El griego es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
