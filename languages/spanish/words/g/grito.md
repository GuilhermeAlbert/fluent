# Grito

## Meaning

Grito means "a cry or a yell."

## Examples

- Hablamos del grito.
- El grito es importante.
- No entiendo el grito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
