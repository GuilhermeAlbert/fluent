# Guante

## Meaning

Guante means "glove or gauntlet."

## Examples

- Me gustan mucho tus guantes.
- Hablamos del guante.
- El guante es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
