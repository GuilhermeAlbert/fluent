# Guardia

## Meaning

Guardia means "watch or guard."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la guardia.
- La guardia es importante.
- No entiendo la guardia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
