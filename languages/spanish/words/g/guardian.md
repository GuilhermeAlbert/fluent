# Guardián

## Meaning

Guardián means "guardian, warden or guardian."

## Examples

- Hablamos del guardián.
- El guardián es importante.
- No entiendo el guardián.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
