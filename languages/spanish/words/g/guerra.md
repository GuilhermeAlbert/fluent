# Guerra

## Meaning

Guerra means "war, warfare."

## Examples

- La guerra entre los Estados Unidos e Irak.
- Hablamos de la guerra.
- La guerra es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
