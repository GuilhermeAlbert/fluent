# Guía

## Meaning

Guía means "guidebook or directory."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la guía.
- La guía es importante.
- No entiendo la guía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
