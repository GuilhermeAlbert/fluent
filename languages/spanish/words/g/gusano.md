# Gusano

## Meaning

Gusano means "worm or insect larva."

## Examples

- Gusano de seda.
- Hablamos del gusano.
- El gusano es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
