# Gustar

## Meaning

Gustar means "to like," but it works more like "to please."

## Examples

- Me gusta este lugar.
- ¿Te gusta la música?
- Nos gusta aprender juntos.

## Notes

The thing liked is usually the subject: "Me gusta el café" literally works like "Coffee pleases me."
