# Gusto

## Meaning

Gusto means "taste."

## Examples

- El gusto es uno de los cinco sentidos.
- Es un gusto que nos visites.
- Lo haré con gusto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
