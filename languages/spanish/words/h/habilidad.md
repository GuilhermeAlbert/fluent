# Habilidad

## Meaning

Habilidad means "ability, skill."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la habilidad.
- La habilidad es importante.
- No entiendo la habilidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
