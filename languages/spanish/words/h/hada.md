# Hada

## Meaning

Hada means "fairy or one of the Fates."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la hada.
- La hada es importante.
- No entiendo la hada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
