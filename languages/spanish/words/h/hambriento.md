# Hambriento

## Meaning

Hambriento means "very hungry or famished."

## Examples

- Es muy hambriento.
- Parece hambriento.
- Fue una decisión hambriento.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
