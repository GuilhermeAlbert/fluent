# Hermana

## Meaning

Hermana means "sister; female equivalent of hermano."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la hermana.
- La hermana es importante.
- No entiendo la hermana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
