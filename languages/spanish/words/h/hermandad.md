# Hermandad

## Meaning

Hermandad means "brotherhood."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la hermandad.
- La hermandad es importante.
- No entiendo la hermandad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
