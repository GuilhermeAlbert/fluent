# Hermanito

## Meaning

Hermanito means "diminutive of hermano; little brother."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hermanito.
- El hermanito es importante.
- No entiendo el hermanito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
