# Hermano

## Meaning

Hermano means "brother or sibling."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hermano.
- El hermano es importante.
- No entiendo el hermano.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
