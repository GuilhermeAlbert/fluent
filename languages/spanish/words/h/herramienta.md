# Herramienta

## Meaning

Herramienta means "a tool, an implement."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la herramienta.
- La herramienta es importante.
- No entiendo la herramienta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
