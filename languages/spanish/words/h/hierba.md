# Hierba

## Meaning

Hierba means "grass or herb."

## Examples

- Near-synonyms: césped, pasto.
- Hablamos de la hierba.
- La hierba es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
