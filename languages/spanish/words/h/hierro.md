# Hierro

## Meaning

Hierro means "iron or branding iron."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hierro.
- El hierro es importante.
- No entiendo el hierro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
