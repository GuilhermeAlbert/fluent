# Hija

## Meaning

Hija means "daughter; female equivalent of hijo."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la hija.
- La hija es importante.
- No entiendo la hija.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
