# Hogar

## Meaning

Hogar means "home or homeland."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hogar.
- El hogar es importante.
- No entiendo el hogar.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
