# Hoja

## Meaning

Hoja means "leaf or petal."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la hoja.
- La hoja es importante.
- No entiendo la hoja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
