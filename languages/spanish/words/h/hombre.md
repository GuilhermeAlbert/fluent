# Hombre

## Meaning

Hombre means "man or or man."

## Examples

- Sé (un) hombre!
- Hablamos del hombre.
- El hombre es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
