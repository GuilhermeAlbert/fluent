# Hombro

## Meaning

Hombro means "shoulder."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hombro.
- El hombro es importante.
- No entiendo el hombro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
