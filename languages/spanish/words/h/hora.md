# Hora

## Meaning

Hora means "hour or time."

## Examples

- Hay veinticuatro horas por el día.
- ¿Qué hora es?
- Ya es hora de ir.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
