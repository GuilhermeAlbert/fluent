# Horario

## Meaning

Horario means "timetable or schedule."

## Examples

- Hablamos del horario.
- El horario es importante.
- No entiendo el horario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
