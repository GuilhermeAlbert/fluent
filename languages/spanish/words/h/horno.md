# Horno

## Meaning

Horno means "oven or furnace."

## Examples

- Hablamos del horno.
- El horno es importante.
- No entiendo el horno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
