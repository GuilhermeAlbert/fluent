# Hoy

## Meaning

Hoy means "today."

## Examples

- Hoy es Sunday, 10 de May de 2026.
- Lo hice hoy.
- Llegó hoy.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
