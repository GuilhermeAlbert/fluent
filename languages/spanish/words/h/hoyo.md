# Hoyo

## Meaning

Hoyo means "hole."

## Examples

- Hablamos del hoyo.
- El hoyo es importante.
- No entiendo el hoyo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
