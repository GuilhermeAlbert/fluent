# Hueco

## Meaning

Hueco means "hole or gap."

## Examples

- Te llamo si tengo un hueco.
- Hablamos del hueco.
- El hueco es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
