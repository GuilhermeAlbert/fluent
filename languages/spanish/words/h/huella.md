# Huella

## Meaning

Huella means "footprint or fingerprint."

## Examples

- Huella dactilar.
- Huella digital.
- Hablamos de la huella.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
