# Hueso

## Meaning

Hueso means "bone or stone."

## Examples

- El perro enterró un hueso.
- Los adultos tienen 206 huesos.
- Hablamos del hueso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
