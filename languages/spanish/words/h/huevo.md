# Huevo

## Meaning

Huevo means "egg or spawn."

## Examples

- Poner un huevo.
- Tener huevos.
- Hablamos del huevo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
