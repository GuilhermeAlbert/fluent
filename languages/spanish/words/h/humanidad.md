# Humanidad

## Meaning

Humanidad means "humanity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la humanidad.
- La humanidad es importante.
- No entiendo la humanidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
