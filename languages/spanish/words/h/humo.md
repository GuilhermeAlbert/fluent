# Humo

## Meaning

Humo means "smoke or steam."

## Examples

- Hablamos del humo.
- El humo es importante.
- No entiendo el humo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
