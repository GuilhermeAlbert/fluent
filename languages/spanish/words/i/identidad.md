# Identidad

## Meaning

Identidad means "identity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la identidad.
- La identidad es importante.
- No entiendo la identidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
