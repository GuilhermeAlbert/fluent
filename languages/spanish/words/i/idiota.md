# Idiota

## Meaning

Idiota means "idiot or moron."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la idiota.
- La idiota es importante.
- No entiendo la idiota.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
