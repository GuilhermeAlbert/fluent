# Iglesia

## Meaning

Iglesia means "church."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la iglesia.
- La iglesia es importante.
- No entiendo la iglesia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
