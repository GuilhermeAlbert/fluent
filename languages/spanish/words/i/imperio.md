# Imperio

## Meaning

Imperio means "empire or imperium."

## Examples

- Heredar el imperio.
- Hablamos del imperio.
- El imperio es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
