# Importancia

## Meaning

Importancia means "importance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la importancia.
- La importancia es importante.
- No entiendo la importancia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
