# Imposible

## Meaning

Imposible means "impossible or unable."

## Examples

- Su acento me es casi imposible de entender.
- Si le resulta imposible usar una computadora...
- Es muy imposible.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
