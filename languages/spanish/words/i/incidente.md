# Incidente

## Meaning

Incidente means "incident or dispute."

## Examples

- Hablamos del incidente.
- El incidente es importante.
- No entiendo el incidente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
