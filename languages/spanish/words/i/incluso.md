# Incluso

## Meaning

Incluso means "even or including."

## Examples

- Incluso yo perdí.
- Incluso ahora, no sería posible.
- Lo hice incluso.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
