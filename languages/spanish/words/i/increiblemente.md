# Increíblemente

## Meaning

Increíblemente means "incredibly."

## Examples

- Lo hice increíblemente.
- Llegó increíblemente.
- Funciona increíblemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
