# Inevitable

## Meaning

Inevitable means "inevitable or inescapable."

## Examples

- Es muy inevitable.
- Parece inevitable.
- Fue una decisión inevitable.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
