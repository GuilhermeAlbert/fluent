# Infierno

## Meaning

Infierno means "hell."

## Examples

- Hablamos del infierno.
- El infierno es importante.
- No entiendo el infierno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
