# Influencia

## Meaning

Influencia means "influence or clout."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la influencia.
- La influencia es importante.
- No entiendo la influencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
