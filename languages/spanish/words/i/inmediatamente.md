# Inmediatamente

## Meaning

Inmediatamente means "immediately."

## Examples

- Lo hice inmediatamente.
- Llegó inmediatamente.
- Funciona inmediatamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
