# Instinto

## Meaning

Instinto means "instinct or gut."

## Examples

- Tienes que confiar en tu instinto.
- Hablamos del instinto.
- El instinto es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
