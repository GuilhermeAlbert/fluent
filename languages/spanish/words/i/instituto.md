# Instituto

## Meaning

Instituto means "institute or secondary school."

## Examples

- Hablamos del instituto.
- El instituto es importante.
- No entiendo el instituto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
