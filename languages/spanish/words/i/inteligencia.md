# Inteligencia

## Meaning

Inteligencia means "intelligence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la inteligencia.
- La inteligencia es importante.
- No entiendo la inteligencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
