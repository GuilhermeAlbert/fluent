# Intentar

## Meaning

Intentar means "to try or to attempt."

## Examples

- Intento correr pero me canso mucho.
- Vale la pena intentarlo.
- Quiero intentar hoy.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
