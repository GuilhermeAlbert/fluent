# Intercambio

## Meaning

Intercambio means "exchange or interchange."

## Examples

- Estudiante de intercambio.
- Hablamos del intercambio.
- El intercambio es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
