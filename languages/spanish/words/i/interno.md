# Interno

## Meaning

Interno means "patient or prisoner."

## Examples

- Hablamos del interno.
- El interno es importante.
- No entiendo el interno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
