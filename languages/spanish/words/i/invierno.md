# Invierno

## Meaning

Invierno means "winter."

## Examples

- Hablamos del invierno.
- El invierno es importante.
- No entiendo el invierno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
