# Ir

## Meaning

Ir means "to go or to be suitable."

## Examples

- Nos gusta ir al cine.
- Una blusa negra no le va a esa falda.
- Este camino va a la aldea.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
