# Ira

## Meaning

Ira means "anger or ire."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ira.
- La ira es importante.
- No entiendo la ira.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
