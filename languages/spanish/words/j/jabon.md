# Jabón

## Meaning

Jabón means "soap; a bar of soap."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jabón.
- El jabón es importante.
- No entiendo el jabón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
