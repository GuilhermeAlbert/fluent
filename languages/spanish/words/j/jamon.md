# Jamón

## Meaning

Jamón means "ham or ham, gammon."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jamón.
- El jamón es importante.
- No entiendo el jamón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
