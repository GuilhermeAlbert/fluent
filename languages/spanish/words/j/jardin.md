# Jardín

## Meaning

Jardín means "garden or rabbit hole."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jardín.
- El jardín es importante.
- No entiendo el jardín.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
