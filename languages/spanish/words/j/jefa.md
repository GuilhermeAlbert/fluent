# Jefa

## Meaning

Jefa means "female equivalent of jefe, boss."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la jefa.
- La jefa es importante.
- No entiendo la jefa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
