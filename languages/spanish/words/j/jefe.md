# Jefe

## Meaning

Jefe means "chief or president."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jefe.
- El jefe es importante.
- No entiendo el jefe.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
