# Joya

## Meaning

Joya means "jewellery or jewelry."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la joya.
- La joya es importante.
- No entiendo la joya.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
