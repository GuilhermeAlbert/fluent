# Jueves

## Meaning

Jueves means "Thursday or the letter J in the Spanish spelling alphabet."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jueves.
- El jueves es importante.
- No entiendo el jueves.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
