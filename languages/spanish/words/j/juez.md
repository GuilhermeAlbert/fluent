# Juez

## Meaning

Juez means "judge or umpire."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la juez.
- La juez es importante.
- No entiendo la juez.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
