# Jugo

## Meaning

Jugo means "juice or substance."

## Examples

- Hablamos del jugo.
- El jugo es importante.
- No entiendo el jugo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
