# Juicio

## Meaning

Juicio means "good judgement or reason."

## Examples

- No estar en su sano juicio.
- Mantener el juicio.
- Hablamos del juicio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
