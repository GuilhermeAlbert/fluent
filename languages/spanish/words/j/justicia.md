# Justicia

## Meaning

Justicia means "justice or righteousness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la justicia.
- La justicia es importante.
- No entiendo la justicia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
