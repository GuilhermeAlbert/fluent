# Juventud

## Meaning

Juventud means "youth or a youth."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la juventud.
- La juventud es importante.
- No entiendo la juventud.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
