# Labio

## Meaning

Labio means "lip or labium."

## Examples

- Labios rojos.
- Labios menores y labios mayores.
- Hablamos del labio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
