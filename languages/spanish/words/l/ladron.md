# Ladrón

## Meaning

Ladrón means "thief or robber."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ladrón.
- El ladrón es importante.
- No entiendo el ladrón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
