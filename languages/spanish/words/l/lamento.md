# Lamento

## Meaning

Lamento means "lament or plaint."

## Examples

- Hablamos del lamento.
- El lamento es importante.
- No entiendo el lamento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
