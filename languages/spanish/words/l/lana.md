# Lana

## Meaning

Lana means "wool or cash."

## Examples

- Sin variación en los precios de las lanas de merina.
- Hablamos de la lana.
- La lana es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
