# Lanza

## Meaning

Lanza means "lance or spear."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lanza.
- La lanza es importante.
- No entiendo la lanza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
