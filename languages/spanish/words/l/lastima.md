# Lástima

## Meaning

Lástima means "pity."

## Examples

- ¡Qué lástima!
- Hablamos de la lástima.
- La lástima es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
