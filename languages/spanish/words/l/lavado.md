# Lavado

## Meaning

Lavado means "laundry or laundering."

## Examples

- Hablamos del lavado.
- El lavado es importante.
- No entiendo el lavado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
