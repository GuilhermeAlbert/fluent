# Leal

## Meaning

Leal means "adhering to the rules of propriety or fair."

## Examples

- Es muy leal.
- Parece leal.
- Fue una decisión leal.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
