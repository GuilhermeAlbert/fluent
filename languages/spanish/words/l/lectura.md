# Lectura

## Meaning

Lectura means "reading or lecture."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lectura.
- La lectura es importante.
- No entiendo la lectura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
