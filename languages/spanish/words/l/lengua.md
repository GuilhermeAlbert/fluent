# Lengua

## Meaning

Lengua means "tongue or language."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lengua.
- La lengua es importante.
- No entiendo la lengua.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
