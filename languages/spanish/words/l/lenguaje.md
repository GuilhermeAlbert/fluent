# Lenguaje

## Meaning

Lenguaje means "language."

## Examples

- Lenguaje sencillo.
- Lenguaje corporal.
- Hablamos del lenguaje.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
