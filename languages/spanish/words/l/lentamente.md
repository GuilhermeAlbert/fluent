# Lentamente

## Meaning

Lentamente means "slowly."

## Examples

- Lo hice lentamente.
- Llegó lentamente.
- Funciona lentamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
