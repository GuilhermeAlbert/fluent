# Letra

## Meaning

Letra means "letter or handwriting."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la letra.
- La letra es importante.
- No entiendo la letra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
