# Libertad

## Meaning

Libertad means "liberty or freedom."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la libertad.
- La libertad es importante.
- No entiendo la libertad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
