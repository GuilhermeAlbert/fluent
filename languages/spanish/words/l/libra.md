# Libra

## Meaning

Libra means "English or American pound avoirdupois."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la libra.
- La libra es importante.
- No entiendo la libra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
