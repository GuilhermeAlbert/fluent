# Licencia

## Meaning

Licencia means "license or permission."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la licencia.
- La licencia es importante.
- No entiendo la licencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
