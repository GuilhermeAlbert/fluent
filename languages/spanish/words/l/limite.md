# Límite

## Meaning

Límite means "limit or threshold."

## Examples

- Hablamos del límite.
- El límite es importante.
- No entiendo el límite.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
