# Limpieza

## Meaning

Limpieza means "cleanliness or cleaning."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la limpieza.
- La limpieza es importante.
- No entiendo la limpieza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
