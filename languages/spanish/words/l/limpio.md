# Limpio

## Meaning

Limpio means "clean or broke."

## Examples

- ¿Viste lo limpia que quedaba su habitación?
- Es muy limpio.
- Parece limpio.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
