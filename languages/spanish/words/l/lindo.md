# Lindo

## Meaning

Lindo means "pretty or cute."

## Examples

- Near-synonyms: bello, hermoso, guapo.
- México es muy lindo y querido.
- Near-synonyms: bonito, precioso.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
