# Linterna

## Meaning

Linterna means "lantern or flashlight."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la linterna.
- La linterna es importante.
- No entiendo la linterna.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
