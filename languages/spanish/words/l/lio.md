# Lío

## Meaning

Lío means "bundle or trouble."

## Examples

- Hablamos del lío.
- El lío es importante.
- No entiendo el lío.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
