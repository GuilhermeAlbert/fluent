# Literalmente

## Meaning

Literalmente means "literally or verbatim."

## Examples

- Lo hice literalmente.
- Llegó literalmente.
- Funciona literalmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
