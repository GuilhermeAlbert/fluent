# Llamada

## Meaning

Llamada means "call."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la llamada.
- La llamada es importante.
- No entiendo la llamada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
