# Lluvia

## Meaning

Lluvia means "rain or shower."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lluvia.
- La lluvia es importante.
- No entiendo la lluvia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
