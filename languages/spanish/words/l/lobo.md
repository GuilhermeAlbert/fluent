# Lobo

## Meaning

Lobo means "wolf or drunkenness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lobo.
- La lobo es importante.
- No entiendo la lobo.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
