# Loco

## Meaning

Loco means "crazy or insane."

## Examples

- Estoy loco por ti.
- David está muy loco.
- Lorena se pone algo loca cuando bebe.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
