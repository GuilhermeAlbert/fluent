# Lógica

## Meaning

Lógica means "logic or rationale, logic."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lógica.
- La lógica es importante.
- No entiendo la lógica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
