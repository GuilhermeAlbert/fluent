# Lola

## Meaning

Lola means "girl or breasts."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lola.
- La lola es importante.
- No entiendo la lola.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
