# Lucha

## Meaning

Lucha means "fight or fighting."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la lucha.
- La lucha es importante.
- No entiendo la lucha.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
