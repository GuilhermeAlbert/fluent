# Lugar

## Meaning

Lugar means "place."

## Examples

- Hablamos del lugar.
- El lugar es importante.
- No entiendo el lugar.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
