# Lujo

## Meaning

Lujo means "luxury, extravagance or luxury."

## Examples

- Hablamos del lujo.
- El lujo es importante.
- No entiendo el lujo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
