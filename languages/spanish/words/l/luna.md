# Luna

## Meaning

Luna means "moon or windshield."

## Examples

- A veces puedo ver la luna en la mañana.
- Hablamos de la luna.
- La luna es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
