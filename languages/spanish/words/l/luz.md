# Luz

## Meaning

Luz means "light or lumen."

## Examples

- La luz extinguida.
- Vas a llegar con menos luces.
- Debes verlo bajo una nueva luz.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
