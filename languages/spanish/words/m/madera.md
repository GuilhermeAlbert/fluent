# Madera

## Meaning

Madera means "wood or wooden plank."

## Examples

- No tiene madera de instigador.
- Hablamos de la madera.
- La madera es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
