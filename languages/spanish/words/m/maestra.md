# Maestra

## Meaning

Maestra means "queen bee."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la maestra.
- La maestra es importante.
- No entiendo la maestra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
