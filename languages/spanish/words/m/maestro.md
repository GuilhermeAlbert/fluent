# Maestro

## Meaning

Maestro means "master or craftsman."

## Examples

- Hablamos del maestro.
- El maestro es importante.
- No entiendo el maestro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
