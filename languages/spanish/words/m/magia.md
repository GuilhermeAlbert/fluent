# Magia

## Meaning

Magia means "magic or spell."

## Examples

- Los cineastas usaron su magia tecnológica para crear efectos especiales.
- Hablamos de la magia.
- La magia es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
