# Mago

## Meaning

Mago means "magician or sorcerer."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mago.
- El mago es importante.
- No entiendo el mago.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
