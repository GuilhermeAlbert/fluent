# Maíz

## Meaning

Maíz means "corn, maize."

## Examples

- Hablamos del maíz.
- El maíz es importante.
- No entiendo el maíz.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
