# Mal

## Meaning

Mal means "badly or poorly."

## Examples

- No hables mal de los muertos.
- Qué mal.
- Tu plan maestro salió mal bastante rápido.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
