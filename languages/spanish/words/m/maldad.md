# Maldad

## Meaning

Maldad means "badness or evilness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la maldad.
- La maldad es importante.
- No entiendo la maldad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
