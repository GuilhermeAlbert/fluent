# Maleta

## Meaning

Maleta means "suitcase or schoolbag."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la maleta.
- La maleta es importante.
- No entiendo la maleta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
