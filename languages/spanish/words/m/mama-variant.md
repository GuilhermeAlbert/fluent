# Mama

## Meaning

Mama means "mamma, breast."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mama.
- La mama es importante.
- No entiendo la mama.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
