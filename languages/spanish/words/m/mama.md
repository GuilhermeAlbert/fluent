# Mamá

## Meaning

Mamá means "mum or mom."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mamá.
- La mamá es importante.
- No entiendo la mamá.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
