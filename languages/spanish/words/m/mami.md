# Mami

## Meaning

Mami means "mommy or mother."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mami.
- La mami es importante.
- No entiendo la mami.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
