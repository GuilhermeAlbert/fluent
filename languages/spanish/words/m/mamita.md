# Mamita

## Meaning

Mamita means "diminutive of mamá; mummy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mamita.
- La mamita es importante.
- No entiendo la mamita.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
