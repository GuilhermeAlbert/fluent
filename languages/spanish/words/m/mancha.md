# Mancha

## Meaning

Mancha means "spot or stain."

## Examples

- Mancha urbana.
- Salí a nadar con la mancha en Villa Nueva.
- Hablamos de la mancha.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
