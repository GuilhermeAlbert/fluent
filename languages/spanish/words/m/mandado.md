# Mandado

## Meaning

Mandado means "errand or errand boy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mandado.
- El mandado es importante.
- No entiendo el mandado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
