# Mano

## Meaning

Mano means "hand or front foot."

## Examples

- Lávate las manos.
- Hablamos de la mano.
- La mano es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
