# Máquina

## Meaning

Máquina means "machine."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la máquina.
- La máquina es importante.
- No entiendo la máquina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
