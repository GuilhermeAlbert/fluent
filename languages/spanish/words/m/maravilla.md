# Maravilla

## Meaning

Maravilla means "wonder or marvel."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la maravilla.
- La maravilla es importante.
- No entiendo la maravilla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
