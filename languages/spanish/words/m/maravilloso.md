# Maravilloso

## Meaning

Maravilloso means "wonderful, marvelous."

## Examples

- Un mundo maravilloso.
- Es muy maravilloso.
- Parece maravilloso.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
