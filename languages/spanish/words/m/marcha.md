# Marcha

## Meaning

Marcha means "march or departure."

## Examples

- Marcha Real.
- Hablamos de la marcha.
- La marcha es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
