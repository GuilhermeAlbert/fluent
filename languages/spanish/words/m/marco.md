# Marco

## Meaning

Marco means "marco or Spanish mark."

## Examples

- Hablamos del marco.
- El marco es importante.
- No entiendo el marco.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
