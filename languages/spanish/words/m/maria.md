# María

## Meaning

María means "magpie or Former coin of Spain in the 17th century."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la maría.
- La maría es importante.
- No entiendo la maría.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
