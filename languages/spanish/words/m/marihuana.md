# Marihuana

## Meaning

Marihuana means "marijuana."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la marihuana.
- La marihuana es importante.
- No entiendo la marihuana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
