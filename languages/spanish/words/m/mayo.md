# Mayo

## Meaning

Mayo means "May."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mayo.
- El mayo es importante.
- No entiendo el mayo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
