# Mayor

## Meaning

Mayor means "major or boss."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mayor.
- El mayor es importante.
- No entiendo el mayor.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
