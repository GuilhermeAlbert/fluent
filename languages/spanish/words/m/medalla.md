# Medalla

## Meaning

Medalla means "medal."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la medalla.
- La medalla es importante.
- No entiendo la medalla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
