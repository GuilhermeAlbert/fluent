# Media

## Meaning

Media means "half or synonym of cuarto."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la media.
- La media es importante.
- No entiendo la media.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
