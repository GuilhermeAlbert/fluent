# Medianoche

## Meaning

Medianoche means "midnight or a small meat pie."

## Examples

- Me voy a dormir a medianoche.
- Hablamos de la medianoche.
- La medianoche es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
