# Médica

## Meaning

Médica means "female equivalent of médico."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la médica.
- La médica es importante.
- No entiendo la médica.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
