# Medicina

## Meaning

Medicina means "medicine."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la medicina.
- La medicina es importante.
- No entiendo la medicina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
