# Médico

## Meaning

Médico means "doctor or physician."

## Examples

- Hablamos del médico.
- El médico es importante.
- No entiendo el médico.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
