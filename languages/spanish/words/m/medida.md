# Medida

## Meaning

Medida means "measurement or measure."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la medida.
- La medida es importante.
- No entiendo la medida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
