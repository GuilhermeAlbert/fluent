# Mediodía

## Meaning

Mediodía means "noon or midday."

## Examples

- Pleno mediodía.
- Hacer mediodía.
- Hablamos del mediodía.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
