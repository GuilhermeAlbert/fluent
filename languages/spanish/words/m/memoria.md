# Memoria

## Meaning

Memoria means "memory or report."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la memoria.
- La memoria es importante.
- No entiendo la memoria.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
