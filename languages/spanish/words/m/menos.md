# Menos

## Meaning

Menos means "less or least."

## Examples

- Camina menos rápido, por favor.
- Él come el menos de todos en la familia.
- Lo hice menos.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
