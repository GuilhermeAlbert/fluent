# Mente

## Meaning

Mente means "mind, intellect."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mente.
- La mente es importante.
- No entiendo la mente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
