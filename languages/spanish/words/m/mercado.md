# Mercado

## Meaning

Mercado means "market."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mercado.
- El mercado es importante.
- No entiendo el mercado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
