# Mercancía

## Meaning

Mercancía means "ware or merchandise."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mercancía.
- La mercancía es importante.
- No entiendo la mercancía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
