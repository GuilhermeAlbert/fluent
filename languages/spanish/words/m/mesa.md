# Mesa

## Meaning

Mesa means "table or dinner table."

## Examples

- ¡A la mesa!
- Hablamos de la mesa.
- La mesa es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
