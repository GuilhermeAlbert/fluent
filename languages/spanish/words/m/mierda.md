# Mierda

## Meaning

Mierda means "shit" and is used literally or as a strong insult or complaint.

## Examples

- Esto es una mierda.
- No me gusta esta mierda.
- ¡Vete a la mierda!

## Notes

Vulgar and informal. Common in movies and casual speech, but not polite.
