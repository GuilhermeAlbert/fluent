# Mil

## Meaning

Mil means "thousand."

## Examples

- Gané muchos miles de dólares.
- Hablamos del mil.
- El mil es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
