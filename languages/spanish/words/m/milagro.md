# Milagro

## Meaning

Milagro means "miracle or wonder."

## Examples

- Este relato se trata de un milagro de la Virgen de Guadalupe.
- Hablamos del milagro.
- El milagro es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
