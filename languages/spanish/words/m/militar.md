# Militar

## Meaning

Militar means "soldier or any person serving in the military."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la militar.
- La militar es importante.
- No entiendo la militar.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
