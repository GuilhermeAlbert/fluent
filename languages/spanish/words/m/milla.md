# Milla

## Meaning

Milla means "English or American mile."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la milla.
- La milla es importante.
- No entiendo la milla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
