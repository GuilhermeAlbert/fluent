# Ministerio

## Meaning

Ministerio means "ministry."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ministerio.
- El ministerio es importante.
- No entiendo el ministerio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
