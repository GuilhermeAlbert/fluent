# Ministro

## Meaning

Ministro means "minister."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ministro.
- El ministro es importante.
- No entiendo el ministro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
