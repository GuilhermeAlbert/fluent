# Minuto

## Meaning

Minuto means "minute."

## Examples

- Hablamos del minuto.
- El minuto es importante.
- No entiendo el minuto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
