# Mirada

## Meaning

Mirada means "look, glance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mirada.
- La mirada es importante.
- No entiendo la mirada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
