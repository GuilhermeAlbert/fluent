# Mirar

## Meaning

Mirar means "to look at or to watch."

## Examples

- Estábamos mirando la televisión.
- Quiero mirar hoy.
- Vamos a mirar mañana.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
