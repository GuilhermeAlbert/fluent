# Misericordia

## Meaning

Misericordia means "misericord or mercy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la misericordia.
- La misericordia es importante.
- No entiendo la misericordia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
