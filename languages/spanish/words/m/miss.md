# Miss

## Meaning

Miss means "Miss or beauty queen."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la miss.
- La miss es importante.
- No entiendo la miss.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
