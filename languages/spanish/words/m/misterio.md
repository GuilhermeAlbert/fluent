# Misterio

## Meaning

Misterio means "mystery or mysteriousness."

## Examples

- Hablamos del misterio.
- El misterio es importante.
- No entiendo el misterio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
