# Mochila

## Meaning

Mochila means "backpack or schoolbag."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mochila.
- La mochila es importante.
- No entiendo la mochila.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
