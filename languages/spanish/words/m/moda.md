# Moda

## Meaning

Moda means "fashion or fad."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la moda.
- La moda es importante.
- No entiendo la moda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
