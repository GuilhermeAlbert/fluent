# Molestia

## Meaning

Molestia means "trouble or nuisance."

## Examples

- Perdona las molestias.
- Hablamos de la molestia.
- La molestia es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
