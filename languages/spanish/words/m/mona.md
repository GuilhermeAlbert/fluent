# Mona

## Meaning

Mona means "copycat or doll."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mona.
- La mona es importante.
- No entiendo la mona.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
