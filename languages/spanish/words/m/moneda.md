# Moneda

## Meaning

Moneda means "coin or currency."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la moneda.
- La moneda es importante.
- No entiendo la moneda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
