# Mono

## Meaning

Mono means "monkey or boiler suit."

## Examples

- Hablamos del mono.
- El mono es importante.
- No entiendo el mono.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
