# Montaña

## Meaning

Montaña means "mountain or a lot."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la montaña.
- La montaña es importante.
- No entiendo la montaña.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
