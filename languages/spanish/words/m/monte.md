# Monte

## Meaning

Monte means "mountain or mount."

## Examples

- Hablamos del monte.
- El monte es importante.
- No entiendo el monte.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
