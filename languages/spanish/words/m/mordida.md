# Mordida

## Meaning

Mordida means "bite or bribe."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la mordida.
- La mordida es importante.
- No entiendo la mordida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
