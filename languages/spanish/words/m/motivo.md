# Motivo

## Meaning

Motivo means "reason or ground."

## Examples

- No sé por qué motivo.
- Hablamos del motivo.
- El motivo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
