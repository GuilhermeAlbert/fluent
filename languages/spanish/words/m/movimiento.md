# Movimiento

## Meaning

Movimiento means "movement."

## Examples

- Hablamos del movimiento.
- El movimiento es importante.
- No entiendo el movimiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
