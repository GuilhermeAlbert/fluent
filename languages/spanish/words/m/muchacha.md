# Muchacha

## Meaning

Muchacha means "female equivalent of muchacho: girl, young lady."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la muchacha.
- La muchacha es importante.
- No entiendo la muchacha.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
