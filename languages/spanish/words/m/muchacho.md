# Muchacho

## Meaning

Muchacho means "boy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del muchacho.
- El muchacho es importante.
- No entiendo el muchacho.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
