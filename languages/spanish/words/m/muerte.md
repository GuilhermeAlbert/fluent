# Muerte

## Meaning

Muerte means "death."

## Examples

- Mi esposa dice que será mi muerte.
- Hablamos de la muerte.
- La muerte es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
