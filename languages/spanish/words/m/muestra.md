# Muestra

## Meaning

Muestra means "sample or sampling."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la muestra.
- La muestra es importante.
- No entiendo la muestra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
