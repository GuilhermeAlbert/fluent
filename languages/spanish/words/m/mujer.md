# Mujer

## Meaning

Mujer means "woman or wife."

## Examples

- Había una mujer extraña en el autobús.
- Hablamos de la mujer.
- La mujer es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
