# Multitud

## Meaning

Multitud means "multitude or crowd."

## Examples

- Hay multitud de libros.
- Hablamos de la multitud.
- La multitud es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
