# Mundial

## Meaning

Mundial means "related to all the parts of the world; global."

## Examples

- Es muy mundial.
- Parece mundial.
- Fue una decisión mundial.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
