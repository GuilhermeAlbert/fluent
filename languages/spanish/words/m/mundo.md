# Mundo

## Meaning

Mundo means "world or earth."

## Examples

- El mundo cambia rápidamente.
- Ella es la persona más feliz del mundo.
- Hablamos del mundo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
