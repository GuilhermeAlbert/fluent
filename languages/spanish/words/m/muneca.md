# Muñeca

## Meaning

Muñeca means "wrist."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la muñeca.
- La muñeca es importante.
- No entiendo la muñeca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
