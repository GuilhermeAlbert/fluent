# Muralla

## Meaning

Muralla means "large wall built to protect cities or territories."

## Examples

- La Gran Muralla China.
- Hablamos de la muralla.
- La muralla es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
