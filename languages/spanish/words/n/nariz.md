# Nariz

## Meaning

Nariz means "nose or nose; smell."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nariz.
- La nariz es importante.
- No entiendo la nariz.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
