# Naturaleza

## Meaning

Naturaleza means "nature."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la naturaleza.
- La naturaleza es importante.
- No entiendo la naturaleza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
