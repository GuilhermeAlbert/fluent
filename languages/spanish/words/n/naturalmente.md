# Naturalmente

## Meaning

Naturalmente means "naturally, of course."

## Examples

- Lo hice naturalmente.
- Llegó naturalmente.
- Funciona naturalmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
