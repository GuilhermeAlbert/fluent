# Nave

## Meaning

Nave means "ship or vessel."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nave.
- La nave es importante.
- No entiendo la nave.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
