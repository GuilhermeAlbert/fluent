# Negativo

## Meaning

Negativo means "minus sign or negative."

## Examples

- Hablamos del negativo.
- El negativo es importante.
- No entiendo el negativo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
