# Negocio

## Meaning

Negocio means "business or enterprise."

## Examples

- Hablamos del negocio.
- El negocio es importante.
- No entiendo el negocio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
