# Negro

## Meaning

Negro means "a black person or a person of black African descent."

## Examples

- Hablamos del negro.
- El negro es importante.
- No entiendo el negro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
