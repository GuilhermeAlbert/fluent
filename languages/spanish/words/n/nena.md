# Nena

## Meaning

Nena means "babe, dear."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nena.
- La nena es importante.
- No entiendo la nena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
