# Nene

## Meaning

Nene means "young child or baby."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nene.
- La nene es importante.
- No entiendo la nene.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
