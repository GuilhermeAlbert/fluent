# Nervioso

## Meaning

Nervioso means "nerve or nervous."

## Examples

- Ponerse nervioso.
- Es muy nervioso.
- Parece nervioso.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
