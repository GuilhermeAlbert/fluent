# Nido

## Meaning

Nido means "nest or edible bird's nest."

## Examples

- Hablamos del nido.
- El nido es importante.
- No entiendo el nido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
