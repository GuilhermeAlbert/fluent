# Nieto

## Meaning

Nieto means "grandchild; grandson."

## Examples

- Hablamos del nieto.
- El nieto es importante.
- No entiendo el nieto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
