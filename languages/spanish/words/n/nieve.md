# Nieve

## Meaning

Nieve means "snow or ice cream."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nieve.
- La nieve es importante.
- No entiendo la nieve.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
