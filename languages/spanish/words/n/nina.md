# Niña

## Meaning

Niña means "girl or female equivalent of niño."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la niña.
- La niña es importante.
- No entiendo la niña.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
