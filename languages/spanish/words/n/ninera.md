# Niñera

## Meaning

Niñera means "babysitter."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la niñera.
- La niñera es importante.
- No entiendo la niñera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
