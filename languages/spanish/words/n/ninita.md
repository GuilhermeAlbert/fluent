# Niñita

## Meaning

Niñita means "a boy or man considered effeminate."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la niñita.
- La niñita es importante.
- No entiendo la niñita.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
