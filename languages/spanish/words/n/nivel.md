# Nivel

## Meaning

Nivel means "level or tier."

## Examples

- Hablamos del nivel.
- El nivel es importante.
- No entiendo el nivel.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
