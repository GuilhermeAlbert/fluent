# Norma

## Meaning

Norma means "rule or norm."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la norma.
- La norma es importante.
- No entiendo la norma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
