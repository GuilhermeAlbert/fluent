# Norte

## Meaning

Norte means "north or north wind."

## Examples

- Vamos al norte.
- Entrada la noche, el norte sopla.
- Hablamos del norte.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
