# Nota

## Meaning

Nota means "note, memo or note."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nota.
- La nota es importante.
- No entiendo la nota.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
