# Noticia

## Meaning

Noticia means "notice or news."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la noticia.
- La noticia es importante.
- No entiendo la noticia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
