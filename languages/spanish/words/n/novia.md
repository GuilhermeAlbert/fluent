# Novia

## Meaning

Novia means "a type of sweet roll."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la novia.
- La novia es importante.
- No entiendo la novia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
