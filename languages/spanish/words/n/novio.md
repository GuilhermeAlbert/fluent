# Novio

## Meaning

Novio means "boyfriend or fiancé."

## Examples

- Hablamos del novio.
- El novio es importante.
- No entiendo el novio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
