# Nube

## Meaning

Nube means "cloud or multitude."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la nube.
- La nube es importante.
- No entiendo la nube.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
