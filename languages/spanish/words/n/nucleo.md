# Núcleo

## Meaning

Núcleo means "nucleus."

## Examples

- Hablamos del núcleo.
- El núcleo es importante.
- No entiendo el núcleo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
