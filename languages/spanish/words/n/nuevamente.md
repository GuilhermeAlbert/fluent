# Nuevamente

## Meaning

Nuevamente means "newly or again."

## Examples

- Lo hice nuevamente.
- Llegó nuevamente.
- Funciona nuevamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
