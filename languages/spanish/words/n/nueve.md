# Nueve

## Meaning

Nueve means "the digit or figure 9."

## Examples

- Hablamos del nueve.
- El nueve es importante.
- No entiendo el nueve.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
