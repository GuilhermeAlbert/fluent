# Nuevo

## Meaning

Nuevo means "new or brand new."

## Examples

- Un nuevo comienzo.
- Es muy nuevo.
- Parece nuevo.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
