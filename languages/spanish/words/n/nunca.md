# Nunca

## Meaning

Nunca means "never or ever."

## Examples

- Nunca he probado la langosta.
- Estamos mejor que nunca.
- Lo hice nunca.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
