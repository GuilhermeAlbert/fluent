# Obispo

## Meaning

Obispo means "bishop."

## Examples

- Hablamos del obispo.
- El obispo es importante.
- No entiendo el obispo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
