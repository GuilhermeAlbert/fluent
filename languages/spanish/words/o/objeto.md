# Objeto

## Meaning

Objeto means "object or objective."

## Examples

- Hablamos del objeto.
- El objeto es importante.
- No entiendo el objeto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
