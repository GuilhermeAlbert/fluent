# Obviamente

## Meaning

Obviamente means "obviously."

## Examples

- Lo hice obviamente.
- Llegó obviamente.
- Funciona obviamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
