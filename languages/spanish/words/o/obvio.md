# Obvio

## Meaning

Obvio means "obvious or clear."

## Examples

- ¿No es obvio que no me gusta?
- Es muy obvio.
- Parece obvio.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
