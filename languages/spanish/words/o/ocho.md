# Ocho

## Meaning

Ocho means "eight or figure of eight."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ocho.
- El ocho es importante.
- No entiendo el ocho.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
