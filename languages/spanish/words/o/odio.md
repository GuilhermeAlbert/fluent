# Odio

## Meaning

Odio means "hate or hatred."

## Examples

- Hablamos del odio.
- El odio es importante.
- No entiendo el odio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
