# Oeste

## Meaning

Oeste means "west."

## Examples

- Hablamos del oeste.
- El oeste es importante.
- No entiendo el oeste.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
