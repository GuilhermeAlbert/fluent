# Oferta

## Meaning

Oferta means "offer."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la oferta.
- La oferta es importante.
- No entiendo la oferta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
