# Ojalá

## Meaning

Ojalá means "hopefully or God willing: inshallah."

## Examples

- Lo hice ojalá.
- Llegó ojalá.
- Funciona ojalá.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
