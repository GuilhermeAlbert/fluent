# Ojo

## Meaning

Ojo means "eye or keyhole."

## Examples

- Dora tiene (los) ojos azules.
- Hay que andar con ojo.
- Hablamos del ojo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
