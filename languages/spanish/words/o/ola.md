# Ola

## Meaning

Ola means "wave or sudden appearance of a large amount of something."

## Examples

- Ola de calor.
- Hablamos de la ola.
- La ola es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
