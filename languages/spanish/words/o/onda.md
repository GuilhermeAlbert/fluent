# Onda

## Meaning

Onda means "wave or any other wave."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la onda.
- La onda es importante.
- No entiendo la onda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
