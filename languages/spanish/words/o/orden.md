# Orden

## Meaning

Orden means "order."

## Examples

- Es una orden, no una pregunta.
- A sus órdenes.
- La Orden del Toisón de Oro.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
