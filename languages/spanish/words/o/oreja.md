# Oreja

## Meaning

Oreja means "outer ear or auricle."

## Examples

- Me duelen las orejas.
- Hablamos de la oreja.
- La oreja es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
