# Oso

## Meaning

Oso means "bear or boar."

## Examples

- Tengo un amigo delgado al que le gustan solo los osos barrigudos y velludos.
- Hablamos del oso.
- El oso es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
