# Otoño

## Meaning

Otoño means "autumn, fall."

## Examples

- Hablamos del otoño.
- El otoño es importante.
- No entiendo el otoño.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
