# Paciencia

## Meaning

Paciencia means "patience, forbearance."

## Examples

- Tener paciencia con.
- Hablamos de la paciencia.
- La paciencia es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
