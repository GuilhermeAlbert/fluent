# Paga

## Meaning

Paga means "payment or payoff."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la paga.
- La paga es importante.
- No entiendo la paga.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
