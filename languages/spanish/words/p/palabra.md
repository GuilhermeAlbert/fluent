# Palabra

## Meaning

Palabra means "word or wording."

## Examples

- Las palabras de amor.
- Te doy mi palabra.
- Hablamos de la palabra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
