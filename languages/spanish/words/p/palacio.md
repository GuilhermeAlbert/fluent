# Palacio

## Meaning

Palacio means "palace or mansion."

## Examples

- Hablamos del palacio.
- El palacio es importante.
- No entiendo el palacio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
