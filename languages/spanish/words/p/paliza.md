# Paliza

## Meaning

Paliza means "beating or bashing."

## Examples

- Le di una paliza.
- Hablamos de la paliza.
- La paliza es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
