# Palo

## Meaning

Palo means "stake or stick."

## Examples

- Mamá, me pegó con un palo.
- Las barajas suelen tener cuatro palos.
- Hablamos del palo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
