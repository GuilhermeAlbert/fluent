# Pan

## Meaning

Pan means "bread."

## Examples

- Para mi desayuno, tomo pan y leche.
- Hablamos del pan.
- El pan es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
