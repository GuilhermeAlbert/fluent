# Pánico

## Meaning

Pánico means "panic or fear."

## Examples

- Le tengo pánico.
- Hablamos del pánico.
- El pánico es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
