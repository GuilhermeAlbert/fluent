# Pantalla

## Meaning

Pantalla means "screen or display screen."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pantalla.
- La pantalla es importante.
- No entiendo la pantalla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
