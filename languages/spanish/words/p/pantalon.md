# Pantalón

## Meaning

Pantalón means "trousers or pants."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pantalón.
- El pantalón es importante.
- No entiendo el pantalón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
