# Pantano

## Meaning

Pantano means "swamp or marsh."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pantano.
- El pantano es importante.
- No entiendo el pantano.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
