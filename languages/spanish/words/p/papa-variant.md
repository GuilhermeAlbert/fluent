# Papa

## Meaning

Papa means "very bland soup or or more broadly."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la papa.
- La papa es importante.
- No entiendo la papa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
