# Papi

## Meaning

Papi means "daddy or daddy, baby."

## Examples

- Hablamos del papi.
- El papi es importante.
- No entiendo el papi.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
