# Papito

## Meaning

Papito means "diminutive of papá; daddy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del papito.
- El papito es importante.
- No entiendo el papito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
