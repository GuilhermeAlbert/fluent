# Paraíso

## Meaning

Paraíso means "paradise or heaven."

## Examples

- Hablamos del paraíso.
- El paraíso es importante.
- No entiendo el paraíso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
