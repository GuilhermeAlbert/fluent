# Pared

## Meaning

Pared means "wall or especially of a house."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pared.
- La pared es importante.
- No entiendo la pared.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
