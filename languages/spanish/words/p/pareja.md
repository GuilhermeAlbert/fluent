# Pareja

## Meaning

Pareja means "couple or pair."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pareja.
- La pareja es importante.
- No entiendo la pareja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
