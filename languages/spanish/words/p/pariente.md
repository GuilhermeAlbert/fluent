# Pariente

## Meaning

Pariente means "relative or relation."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pariente.
- La pariente es importante.
- No entiendo la pariente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
