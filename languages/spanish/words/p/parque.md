# Parque

## Meaning

Parque means "park or parking lot."

## Examples

- Hablamos del parque.
- El parque es importante.
- No entiendo el parque.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
