# Pasada

## Meaning

Pasada means "transit or crossing."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pasada.
- La pasada es importante.
- No entiendo la pasada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
