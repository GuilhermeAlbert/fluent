# Paseo

## Meaning

Paseo means "walk or stroll."

## Examples

- Hablamos del paseo.
- El paseo es importante.
- No entiendo el paseo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
