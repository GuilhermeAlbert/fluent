# Pasta

## Meaning

Pasta means "pasta or paste."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pasta.
- La pasta es importante.
- No entiendo la pasta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
