# Pastel

## Meaning

Pastel means "pastry or cake."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pastel.
- El pastel es importante.
- No entiendo el pastel.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
