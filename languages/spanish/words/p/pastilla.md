# Pastilla

## Meaning

Pastilla means "pill or cake."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pastilla.
- La pastilla es importante.
- No entiendo la pastilla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
