# Pastor

## Meaning

Pastor means "shepherd or herder."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pastor.
- El pastor es importante.
- No entiendo el pastor.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
