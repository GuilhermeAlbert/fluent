# Pata

## Meaning

Pata means "paw or foot."

## Examples

- Acabo de trapear y ya dejaste tus patas.
- Hablamos de la pata.
- La pata es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
