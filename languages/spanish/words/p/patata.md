# Patata

## Meaning

Patata means "potato or piece of rubbish."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la patata.
- La patata es importante.
- No entiendo la patata.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
