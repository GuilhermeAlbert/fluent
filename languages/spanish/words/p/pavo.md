# Pavo

## Meaning

Pavo means "turkey or peacock."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pavo.
- El pavo es importante.
- No entiendo el pavo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
