# Peligro

## Meaning

Peligro means "peril, danger."

## Examples

- Hablamos del peligro.
- El peligro es importante.
- No entiendo el peligro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
