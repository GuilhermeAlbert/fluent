# Pelo

## Meaning

Pelo means "hair."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pelo.
- El pelo es importante.
- No entiendo el pelo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
