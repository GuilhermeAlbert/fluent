# Pelota

## Meaning

Pelota means "ball or ball game."

## Examples

- Jugar a (lanzar) la pelota.
- Hablamos de la pelota.
- La pelota es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
