# Pelotón

## Meaning

Pelotón means "group or team."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pelotón.
- El pelotón es importante.
- No entiendo el pelotón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
