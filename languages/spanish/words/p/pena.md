# Pena

## Meaning

Pena means "punishment or pain."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pena.
- La pena es importante.
- No entiendo la pena.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
