# Pensamiento

## Meaning

Pensamiento means "thought or thinking."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pensamiento.
- El pensamiento es importante.
- No entiendo el pensamiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
