# Pensar

## Meaning

Pensar means "to think or ponder."

## Examples

- Pensar en/sobre algo.
- Pienso, luego existo.
- ¿Por qué no pensé en eso antes?

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
