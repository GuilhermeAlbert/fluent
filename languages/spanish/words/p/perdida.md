# Pérdida

## Meaning

Pérdida means "loss or leak."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pérdida.
- La pérdida es importante.
- No entiendo la pérdida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
