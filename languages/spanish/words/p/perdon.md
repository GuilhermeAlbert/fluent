# Perdón

## Meaning

Perdón means "pardon or mercy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del perdón.
- El perdón es importante.
- No entiendo el perdón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
