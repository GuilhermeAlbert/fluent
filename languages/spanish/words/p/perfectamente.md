# Perfectamente

## Meaning

Perfectamente means "perfectly."

## Examples

- Lo hice perfectamente.
- Llegó perfectamente.
- Funciona perfectamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
