# Periodista

## Meaning

Periodista means "journalist."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la periodista.
- La periodista es importante.
- No entiendo la periodista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
