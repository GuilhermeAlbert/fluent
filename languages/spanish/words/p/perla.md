# Perla

## Meaning

Perla means "pearl or bead."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la perla.
- La perla es importante.
- No entiendo la perla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
