# Permanente

## Meaning

Permanente means "perm or permanent wave."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la permanente.
- La permanente es importante.
- No entiendo la permanente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
