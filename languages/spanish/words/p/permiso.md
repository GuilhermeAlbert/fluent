# Permiso

## Meaning

Permiso means "permission or license."

## Examples

- Hablamos del permiso.
- El permiso es importante.
- No entiendo el permiso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
