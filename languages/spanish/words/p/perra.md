# Perra

## Meaning

Perra means "bitch or prostitute."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la perra.
- La perra es importante.
- No entiendo la perra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
