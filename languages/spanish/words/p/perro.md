# Perro

## Meaning

Perro means "dog, domesticated for thousands of years and of highly variable appearance because of h."

## Examples

- Perro callejero.
- Hablamos del perro.
- El perro es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
