# Persecución

## Meaning

Persecución means "persecution or chase."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la persecución.
- La persecución es importante.
- No entiendo la persecución.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
