# Persona

## Meaning

Persona means "person or a socially distinguished person."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la persona.
- La persona es importante.
- No entiendo la persona.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
