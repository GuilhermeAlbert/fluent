# Personalidad

## Meaning

Personalidad means "personality."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la personalidad.
- La personalidad es importante.
- No entiendo la personalidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
