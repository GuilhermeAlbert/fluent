# Perspectiva

## Meaning

Perspectiva means "perspective or prospect."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la perspectiva.
- La perspectiva es importante.
- No entiendo la perspectiva.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
