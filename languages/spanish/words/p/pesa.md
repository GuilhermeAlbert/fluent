# Pesa

## Meaning

Pesa means "weight or dumbbell."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pesa.
- La pesa es importante.
- No entiendo la pesa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
