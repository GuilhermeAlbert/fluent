# Pesadilla

## Meaning

Pesadilla means "nightmare or bane."

## Examples

- La pesadilla de mi existencia/vida.
- Hablamos de la pesadilla.
- La pesadilla es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
