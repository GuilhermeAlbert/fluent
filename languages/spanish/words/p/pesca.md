# Pesca

## Meaning

Pesca means "fishing."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pesca.
- La pesca es importante.
- No entiendo la pesca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
