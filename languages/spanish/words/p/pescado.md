# Pescado

## Meaning

Pescado means "fish that has been caught; food fish."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pescado.
- El pescado es importante.
- No entiendo el pescado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
