# Pez

## Meaning

Pez means "fish or idiot."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pez.
- El pez es importante.
- No entiendo el pez.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
