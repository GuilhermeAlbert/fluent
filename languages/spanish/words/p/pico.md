# Pico

## Meaning

Pico means "beak or sharp point."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pico.
- El pico es importante.
- No entiendo el pico.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
