# Piedad

## Meaning

Piedad means "piety or pity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la piedad.
- La piedad es importante.
- No entiendo la piedad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
