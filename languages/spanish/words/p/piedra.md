# Piedra

## Meaning

Piedra means "stone or flint."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la piedra.
- La piedra es importante.
- No entiendo la piedra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
