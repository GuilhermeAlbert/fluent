# Piel

## Meaning

Piel means "skin or pelt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la piel.
- La piel es importante.
- No entiendo la piel.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
