# Pierna

## Meaning

Pierna means "leg."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pierna.
- La pierna es importante.
- No entiendo la pierna.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
