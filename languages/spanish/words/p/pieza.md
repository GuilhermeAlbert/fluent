# Pieza

## Meaning

Pieza means "piece or part."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pieza.
- La pieza es importante.
- No entiendo la pieza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
