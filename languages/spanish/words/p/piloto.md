# Piloto

## Meaning

Piloto means "pilot or driver."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la piloto.
- La piloto es importante.
- No entiendo la piloto.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
