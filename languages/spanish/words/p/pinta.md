# Pinta

## Meaning

Pinta means "look or appearance."

## Examples

- Tener buena pinta.
- Hablamos de la pinta.
- La pinta es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
