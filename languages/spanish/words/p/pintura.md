# Pintura

## Meaning

Pintura means "painting or paint."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pintura.
- La pintura es importante.
- No entiendo la pintura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
