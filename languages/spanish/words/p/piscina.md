# Piscina

## Meaning

Piscina means "swimming pool or fishpond."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la piscina.
- La piscina es importante.
- No entiendo la piscina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
