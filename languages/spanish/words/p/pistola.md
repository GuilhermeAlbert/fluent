# Pistola

## Meaning

Pistola means "pistol or handgun."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pistola.
- La pistola es importante.
- No entiendo la pistola.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
