# Pito

## Meaning

Pito means "whistle or fife."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pito.
- El pito es importante.
- No entiendo el pito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
