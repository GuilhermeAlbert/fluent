# Placa

## Meaning

Placa means "plate or shield."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la placa.
- La placa es importante.
- No entiendo la placa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
