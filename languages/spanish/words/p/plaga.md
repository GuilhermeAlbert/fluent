# Plaga

## Meaning

Plaga means "plague or nuisance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la plaga.
- La plaga es importante.
- No entiendo la plaga.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
