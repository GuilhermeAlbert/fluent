# Planeta

## Meaning

Planeta means "planet."

## Examples

- Hablamos del planeta.
- El planeta es importante.
- No entiendo el planeta.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
