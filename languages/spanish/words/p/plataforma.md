# Plataforma

## Meaning

Plataforma means "platform."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la plataforma.
- La plataforma es importante.
- No entiendo la plataforma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
