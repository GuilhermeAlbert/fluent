# Playa

## Meaning

Playa means "beach or car park."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la playa.
- La playa es importante.
- No entiendo la playa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
