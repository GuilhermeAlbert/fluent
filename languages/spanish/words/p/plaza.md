# Plaza

## Meaning

Plaza means "plaza or town square."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la plaza.
- La plaza es importante.
- No entiendo la plaza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
