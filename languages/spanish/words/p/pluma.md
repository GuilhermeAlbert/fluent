# Pluma

## Meaning

Pluma means "feather or quill."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la pluma.
- La pluma es importante.
- No entiendo la pluma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
