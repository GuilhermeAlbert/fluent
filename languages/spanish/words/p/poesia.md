# Poesía

## Meaning

Poesía means "poetry or poem."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la poesía.
- La poesía es importante.
- No entiendo la poesía.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
