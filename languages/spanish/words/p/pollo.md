# Pollo

## Meaning

Pollo means "chicken or young chicken."

## Examples

- Pollo frito.
- Montar un pollo.
- Hablamos del pollo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
