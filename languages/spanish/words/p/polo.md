# Polo

## Meaning

Polo means "polo or polo shirt."

## Examples

- Hablamos del polo.
- El polo es importante.
- No entiendo el polo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
