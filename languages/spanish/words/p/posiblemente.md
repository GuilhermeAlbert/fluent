# Posiblemente

## Meaning

Posiblemente means "possibly."

## Examples

- Lo hice posiblemente.
- Llegó posiblemente.
- Funciona posiblemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
