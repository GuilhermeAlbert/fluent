# Potencia

## Meaning

Potencia means "power or potency."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la potencia.
- La potencia es importante.
- No entiendo la potencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
