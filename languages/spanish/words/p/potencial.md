# Potencial

## Meaning

Potencial means "potential or conditional tense."

## Examples

- Hablamos del potencial.
- El potencial es importante.
- No entiendo el potencial.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
