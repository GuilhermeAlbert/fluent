# Pozo

## Meaning

Pozo means "well."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pozo.
- El pozo es importante.
- No entiendo el pozo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
