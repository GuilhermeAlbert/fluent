# Premio

## Meaning

Premio means "prize, award or prize."

## Examples

- Hablamos del premio.
- El premio es importante.
- No entiendo el premio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
