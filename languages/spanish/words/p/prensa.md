# Prensa

## Meaning

Prensa means "press."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la prensa.
- La prensa es importante.
- No entiendo la prensa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
