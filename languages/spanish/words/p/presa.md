# Presa

## Meaning

Presa means "reservoir or dam."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la presa.
- La presa es importante.
- No entiendo la presa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
