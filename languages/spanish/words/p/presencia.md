# Presencia

## Meaning

Presencia means "presence or appearance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la presencia.
- La presencia es importante.
- No entiendo la presencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
