# Presentimiento

## Meaning

Presentimiento means "presentiment or foreboding."

## Examples

- Hablamos del presentimiento.
- El presentimiento es importante.
- No entiendo el presentimiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
