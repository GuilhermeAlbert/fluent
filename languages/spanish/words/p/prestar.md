# Prestar

## Meaning

Prestar means "to lend or to give."

## Examples

- Préstame tu bicicleta.
- ¡Prestad atención, por favor!
- El alcohol se presta al abuso.

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
