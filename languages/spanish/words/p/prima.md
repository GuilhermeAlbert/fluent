# Prima

## Meaning

Prima means "bonus or premium."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la prima.
- La prima es importante.
- No entiendo la prima.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
