# Primavera

## Meaning

Primavera means "spring or year of age."

## Examples

- Mi primo tiene cuarenta primaveras.
- Hablamos de la primavera.
- La primavera es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
