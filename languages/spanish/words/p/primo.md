# Primo

## Meaning

Primo means "cousin or cuz."

## Examples

- ¿Quieres problemas, primo?
- Hablamos del primo.
- El primo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
