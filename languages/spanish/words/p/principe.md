# Príncipe

## Meaning

Príncipe means "prince or sovereign."

## Examples

- Hablamos del príncipe.
- El príncipe es importante.
- No entiendo el príncipe.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
