# Prioridad

## Meaning

Prioridad means "priority or right of way."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la prioridad.
- La prioridad es importante.
- No entiendo la prioridad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
