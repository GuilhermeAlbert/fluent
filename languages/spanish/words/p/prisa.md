# Prisa

## Meaning

Prisa means "hurry or rush."

## Examples

- Me voy, que tengo prisa.
- ¡Date prisa!
- Hablamos de la prisa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
