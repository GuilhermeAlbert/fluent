# Probabilidad

## Meaning

Probabilidad means "probability or likelihood."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la probabilidad.
- La probabilidad es importante.
- No entiendo la probabilidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
