# Probablemente

## Meaning

Probablemente means "probably, likely."

## Examples

- Lo hice probablemente.
- Llegó probablemente.
- Funciona probablemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
