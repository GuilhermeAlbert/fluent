# Profesor

## Meaning

Profesor means "professor or teacher."

## Examples

- Profesores y personal.
- Hablamos del profesor.
- El profesor es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
