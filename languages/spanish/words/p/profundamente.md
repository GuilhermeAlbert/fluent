# Profundamente

## Meaning

Profundamente means "profoundly, deeply."

## Examples

- Lo hice profundamente.
- Llegó profundamente.
- Funciona profundamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
