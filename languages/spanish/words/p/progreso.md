# Progreso

## Meaning

Progreso means "progress or headway."

## Examples

- Hablamos del progreso.
- El progreso es importante.
- No entiendo el progreso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
