# Promesa

## Meaning

Promesa means "promise."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la promesa.
- La promesa es importante.
- No entiendo la promesa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
