# Propiedad

## Meaning

Propiedad means "propriety or proprietary."

## Examples

- Propiedad intelectual.
- Hablamos de la propiedad.
- La propiedad es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
