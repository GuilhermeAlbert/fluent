# Propósito

## Meaning

Propósito means "purpose or resolution."

## Examples

- Propósito de Año Nuevo.
- Hablamos del propósito.
- El propósito es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
