# Prostituta

## Meaning

Prostituta means "female equivalent of prostituto."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la prostituta.
- La prostituta es importante.
- No entiendo la prostituta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
