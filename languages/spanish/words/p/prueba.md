# Prueba

## Meaning

Prueba means "proof or evidence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la prueba.
- La prueba es importante.
- No entiendo la prueba.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
