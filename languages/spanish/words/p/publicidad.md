# Publicidad

## Meaning

Publicidad means "advertisement or publicity."

## Examples

- Publicidad directa.
- Dar publicidad a algo.
- No queremos publicidad negativa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
