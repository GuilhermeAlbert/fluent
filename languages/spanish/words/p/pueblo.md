# Pueblo

## Meaning

Pueblo means "town or village."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pueblo.
- El pueblo es importante.
- No entiendo el pueblo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
