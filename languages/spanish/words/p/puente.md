# Puente

## Meaning

Puente means "bridge or long weekend."

## Examples

- Hablamos del puente.
- El puente es importante.
- No entiendo el puente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
