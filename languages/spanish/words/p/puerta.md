# Puerta

## Meaning

Puerta means "door or gate."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la puerta.
- La puerta es importante.
- No entiendo la puerta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
