# Puerto

## Meaning

Puerto means "port, harbour or port."

## Examples

- Hablamos del puerto.
- El puerto es importante.
- No entiendo el puerto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
