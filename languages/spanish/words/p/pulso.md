# Pulso

## Meaning

Pulso means "pulse or arm-wrestle."

## Examples

- Hablamos del pulso.
- El pulso es importante.
- No entiendo el pulso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
