# Puta

## Meaning

Puta means "whore or slut."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la puta.
- La puta es importante.
- No entiendo la puta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
