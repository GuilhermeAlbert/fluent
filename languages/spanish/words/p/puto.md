# Puto

## Meaning

Puto means "man-whore or faggot."

## Examples

- Hablamos del puto.
- El puto es importante.
- No entiendo el puto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
