# Que

## Meaning

Que usually means "that," and it also connects ideas in a sentence.

## Examples

- Creo que sí.
- Dijo que venía.
- Quiero que escuches.

## Notes

Do not confuse "que" with "qué," which has an accent in questions and exclamations.
