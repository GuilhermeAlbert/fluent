# Queja

## Meaning

Queja means "complaint or grievance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la queja.
- La queja es importante.
- No entiendo la queja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
