# Querida

## Meaning

Querida means "dear or honey."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la querida.
- La querida es importante.
- No entiendo la querida.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
