# Querido

## Meaning

Querido means "sweetheart or lover."

## Examples

- Hablamos del querido.
- El querido es importante.
- No entiendo el querido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
