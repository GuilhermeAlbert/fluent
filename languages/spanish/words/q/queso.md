# Queso

## Meaning

Queso means "cheese or foot."

## Examples

- Hablamos del queso.
- El queso es importante.
- No entiendo el queso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
