# Quizás

## Meaning

Quizás means "maybe, perhaps."

## Examples

- Quizás luego.
- Quizás no vaya allá este verano.
- Quizás tenga que irme temprano.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
