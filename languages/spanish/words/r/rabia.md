# Rabia

## Meaning

Rabia means "rage or anger."

## Examples

- Me da rabia que tomes.
- Hablamos de la rabia.
- La rabia es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
