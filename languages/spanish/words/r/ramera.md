# Ramera

## Meaning

Ramera means "harlot; prostitute."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ramera.
- La ramera es importante.
- No entiendo la ramera.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
