# Rancho

## Meaning

Rancho means "ranch or shed."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rancho.
- El rancho es importante.
- No entiendo el rancho.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
