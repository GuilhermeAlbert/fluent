# Rastro

## Meaning

Rastro means "sign or trace."

## Examples

- Un rastro de sangre.
- Hablamos del rastro.
- El rastro es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
