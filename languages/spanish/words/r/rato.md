# Rato

## Meaning

Rato means "a while or bit."

## Examples

- Pasó un buen rato viendo la película.
- Me hizo pasar un mal rato.
- Hablamos del rato.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
