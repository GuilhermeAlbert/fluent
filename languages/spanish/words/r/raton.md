# Ratón

## Meaning

Ratón means "mouse."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ratón.
- El ratón es importante.
- No entiendo el ratón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
