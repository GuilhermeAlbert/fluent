# Rayo

## Meaning

Rayo means "beam or ray."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rayo.
- El rayo es importante.
- No entiendo el rayo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
