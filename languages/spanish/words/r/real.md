# Real

## Meaning

Real means "real or a quarter of a peseta."

## Examples

- Hablamos del real.
- El real es importante.
- No entiendo el real.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
