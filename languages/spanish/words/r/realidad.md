# Realidad

## Meaning

Realidad means "reality or commodity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la realidad.
- La realidad es importante.
- No entiendo la realidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
