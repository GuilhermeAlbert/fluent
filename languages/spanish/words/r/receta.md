# Receta

## Meaning

Receta means "prescription or recipe."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la receta.
- La receta es importante.
- No entiendo la receta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
