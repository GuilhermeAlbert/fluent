# Recibo

## Meaning

Recibo means "receipt or bill."

## Examples

- Hablamos del recibo.
- El recibo es importante.
- No entiendo el recibo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
