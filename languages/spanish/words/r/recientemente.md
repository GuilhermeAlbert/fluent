# Recientemente

## Meaning

Recientemente means "recently."

## Examples

- Lo hice recientemente.
- Llegó recientemente.
- Funciona recientemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
