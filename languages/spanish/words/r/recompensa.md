# Recompensa

## Meaning

Recompensa means "reward or recompense."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la recompensa.
- La recompensa es importante.
- No entiendo la recompensa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
