# Recuerdo

## Meaning

Recuerdo means "memory or souvenir."

## Examples

- Hablamos del recuerdo.
- El recuerdo es importante.
- No entiendo el recuerdo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
