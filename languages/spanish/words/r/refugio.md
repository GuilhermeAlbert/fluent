# Refugio

## Meaning

Refugio means "refuge or safety."

## Examples

- Hablamos del refugio.
- El refugio es importante.
- No entiendo el refugio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
