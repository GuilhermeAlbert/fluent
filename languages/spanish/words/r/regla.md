# Regla

## Meaning

Regla means "rule or ruler."

## Examples

- Tiene la regla.
- Hablamos de la regla.
- La regla es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
