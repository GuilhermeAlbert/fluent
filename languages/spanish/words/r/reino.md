# Reino

## Meaning

Reino means "kingdom."

## Examples

- Entrar al reino de la fantasía.
- Hablamos del reino.
- El reino es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
