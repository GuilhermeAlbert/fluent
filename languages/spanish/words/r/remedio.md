# Remedio

## Meaning

Remedio means "remedy or solution."

## Examples

- Hablamos del remedio.
- El remedio es importante.
- No entiendo el remedio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
