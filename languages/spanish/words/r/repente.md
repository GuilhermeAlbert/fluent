# Repente

## Meaning

Repente means "attack or upsurge."

## Examples

- Hablamos del repente.
- El repente es importante.
- No entiendo el repente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
