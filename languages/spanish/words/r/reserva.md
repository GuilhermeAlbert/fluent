# Reserva

## Meaning

Reserva means "reservation or booking."

## Examples

- Los agentes de viajes cancelaron todas las reservas para el vuelo de la tarde.
- Hablamos de la reserva.
- La reserva es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
