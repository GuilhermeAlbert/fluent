# Resistencia

## Meaning

Resistencia means "resistance."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la resistencia.
- La resistencia es importante.
- No entiendo la resistencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
