# Respecto

## Meaning

Respecto means "respect or regard."

## Examples

- Hablamos del respecto.
- El respecto es importante.
- No entiendo el respecto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
