# Respiro

## Meaning

Respiro means "breath or break."

## Examples

- ¡Dame un respiro!
- Dale un respiro.
- Dale un respiro. Ella está realmente tratando de cambiar.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
