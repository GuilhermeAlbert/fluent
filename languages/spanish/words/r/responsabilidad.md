# Responsabilidad

## Meaning

Responsabilidad means "responsibility or responsibleness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la responsabilidad.
- La responsabilidad es importante.
- No entiendo la responsabilidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
