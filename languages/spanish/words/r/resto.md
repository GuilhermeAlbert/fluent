# Resto

## Meaning

Resto means "rest, remainder or remainder."

## Examples

- El resto de mi vida.
- Hablamos del resto.
- El resto es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
