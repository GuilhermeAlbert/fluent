# Retiro

## Meaning

Retiro means "retirement or retirement pension."

## Examples

- Hablamos del retiro.
- El retiro es importante.
- No entiendo el retiro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
