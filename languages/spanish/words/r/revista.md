# Revista

## Meaning

Revista means "magazine or revue."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la revista.
- La revista es importante.
- No entiendo la revista.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
