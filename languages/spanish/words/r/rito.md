# Rito

## Meaning

Rito means "rite or ritual."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rito.
- El rito es importante.
- No entiendo el rito.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
