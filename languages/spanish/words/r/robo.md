# Robo

## Meaning

Robo means "robbery, theft."

## Examples

- Hablamos del robo.
- El robo es importante.
- No entiendo el robo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
