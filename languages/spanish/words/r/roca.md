# Roca

## Meaning

Roca means "rock or synonym of incoherencia."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la roca.
- La roca es importante.
- No entiendo la roca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
