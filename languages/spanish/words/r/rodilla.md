# Rodilla

## Meaning

Rodilla means "knee."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la rodilla.
- La rodilla es importante.
- No entiendo la rodilla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
