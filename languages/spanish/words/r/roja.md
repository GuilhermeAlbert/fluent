# Roja

## Meaning

Roja means "the Chile national football team or the Spain national football team."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la roja.
- La roja es importante.
- No entiendo la roja.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
