# Rojo

## Meaning

Rojo means "red or a 1000 colón bill."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rojo.
- El rojo es importante.
- No entiendo el rojo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
