# Rollo

## Meaning

Rollo means "roll or coil."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rollo.
- El rollo es importante.
- No entiendo el rollo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
