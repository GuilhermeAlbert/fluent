# Ronda

## Meaning

Ronda means "round or iteration."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ronda.
- La ronda es importante.
- No entiendo la ronda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
