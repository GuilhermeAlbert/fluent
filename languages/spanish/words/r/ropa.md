# Ropa

## Meaning

Ropa means "garment or clothing."

## Examples

- Me gustan estas ropas.
- Hablamos de la ropa.
- La ropa es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
