# Rosa

## Meaning

Rosa means "rose or pink."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la rosa.
- La rosa es importante.
- No entiendo la rosa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
