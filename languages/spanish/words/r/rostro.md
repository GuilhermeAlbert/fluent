# Rostro

## Meaning

Rostro means "face."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rostro.
- El rostro es importante.
- No entiendo el rostro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
