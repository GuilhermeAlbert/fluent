# Rubio

## Meaning

Rubio means "a blond person or gurnard."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rubio.
- El rubio es importante.
- No entiendo el rubio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
