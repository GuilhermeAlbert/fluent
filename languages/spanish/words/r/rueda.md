# Rueda

## Meaning

Rueda means "wheel or circle."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la rueda.
- La rueda es importante.
- No entiendo la rueda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
