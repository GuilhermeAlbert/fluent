# Rumbo

## Meaning

Rumbo means "course or heading."

## Examples

- Hablamos del rumbo.
- El rumbo es importante.
- No entiendo el rumbo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
