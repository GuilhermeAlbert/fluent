# Ruso

## Meaning

Ruso means "Russian or an Ashkenazic Jew."

## Examples

- Hablamos del ruso.
- El ruso es importante.
- No entiendo el ruso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
