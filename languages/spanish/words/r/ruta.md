# Ruta

## Meaning

Ruta means "highway or route."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ruta.
- La ruta es importante.
- No entiendo la ruta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
