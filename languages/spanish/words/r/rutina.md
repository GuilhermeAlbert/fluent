# Rutina

## Meaning

Rutina means "routine or grind."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la rutina.
- La rutina es importante.
- No entiendo la rutina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
