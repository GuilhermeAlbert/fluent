# Sábana

## Meaning

Sábana means "sheet; bedsheet."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sábana.
- La sábana es importante.
- No entiendo la sábana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
