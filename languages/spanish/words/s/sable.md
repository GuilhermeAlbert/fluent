# Sable

## Meaning

Sable means "saber, cutlass or saber."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sable.
- El sable es importante.
- No entiendo el sable.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
