# Sacar

## Meaning

Sacar means "to put out or to get out."

## Examples

- Vamos a sacar un álbum.
- Sacaron un anuncio importante.
- Él no sacará ninguna canción.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
