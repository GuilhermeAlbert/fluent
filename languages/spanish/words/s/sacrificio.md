# Sacrificio

## Meaning

Sacrificio means "sacrifice or slaughter."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sacrificio.
- El sacrificio es importante.
- No entiendo el sacrificio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
