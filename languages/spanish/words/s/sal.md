# Sal

## Meaning

Sal means "salt; table salt or salt."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sal.
- La sal es importante.
- No entiendo la sal.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
