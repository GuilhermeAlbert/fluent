# Salida

## Meaning

Salida means "exit or way out."

## Examples

- Un trabajo con salida.
- Hablamos de la salida.
- La salida es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
