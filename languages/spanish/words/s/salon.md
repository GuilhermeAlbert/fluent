# Salón

## Meaning

Salón means "living room or sitting room."

## Examples

- Veo la tele en mi salón.
- Hablamos del salón.
- El salón es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
