# Salsa

## Meaning

Salsa means "sauce or gravy."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la salsa.
- La salsa es importante.
- No entiendo la salsa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
