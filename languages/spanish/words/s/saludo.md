# Saludo

## Meaning

Saludo means "greeting or greetings."

## Examples

- Hablamos del saludo.
- El saludo es importante.
- No entiendo el saludo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
