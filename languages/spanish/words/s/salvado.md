# Salvado

## Meaning

Salvado means "bran."

## Examples

- Hablamos del salvado.
- El salvado es importante.
- No entiendo el salvado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
