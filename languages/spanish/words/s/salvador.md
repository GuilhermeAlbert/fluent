# Salvador

## Meaning

Salvador means "savior."

## Examples

- Hablamos del salvador.
- El salvador es importante.
- No entiendo el salvador.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
