# Salvo

## Meaning

Salvo means "except, apart from."

## Examples

- Todos fueron a la fiesta, salvo Ana.
- Lo hice salvo.
- Llegó salvo.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
