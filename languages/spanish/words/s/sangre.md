# Sangre

## Meaning

Sangre means "blood."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sangre.
- La sangre es importante.
- No entiendo la sangre.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
