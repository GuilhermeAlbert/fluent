# Santo

## Meaning

Santo means "male saint or name day."

## Examples

- Hablamos del santo.
- El santo es importante.
- No entiendo el santo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
