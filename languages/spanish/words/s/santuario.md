# Santuario

## Meaning

Santuario means "sanctuary or shrine."

## Examples

- Hablamos del santuario.
- El santuario es importante.
- No entiendo el santuario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
