# Secretaria

## Meaning

Secretaria means "female equivalent of secretario."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la secretaria.
- La secretaria es importante.
- No entiendo la secretaria.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
