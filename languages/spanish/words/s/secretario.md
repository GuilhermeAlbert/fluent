# Secretario

## Meaning

Secretario means "secretary."

## Examples

- Hablamos del secretario.
- El secretario es importante.
- No entiendo el secretario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
