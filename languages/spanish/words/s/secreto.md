# Secreto

## Meaning

Secreto means "a secret or secrecy."

## Examples

- Guardar el secreto.
- Hablamos del secreto.
- El secreto es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
