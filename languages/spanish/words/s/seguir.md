# Seguir

## Meaning

Seguir means "to follow or to continue."

## Examples

- ¡Sígueme!
- Ahora siguen los postres.
- Este tío sigue hablando sin parar.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
