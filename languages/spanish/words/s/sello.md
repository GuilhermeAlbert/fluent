# Sello

## Meaning

Sello means "seal or sigil."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sello.
- El sello es importante.
- No entiendo el sello.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
