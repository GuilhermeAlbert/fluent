# Senado

## Meaning

Senado means "senate or assembly."

## Examples

- Hablamos del senado.
- El senado es importante.
- No entiendo el senado.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
