# Sencillo

## Meaning

Sencillo means "single."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sencillo.
- El sencillo es importante.
- No entiendo el sencillo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
