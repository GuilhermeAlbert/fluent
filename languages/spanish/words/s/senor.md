# Señor

## Meaning

Señor means "mister or sir."

## Examples

- Señor, cierre la puerta.
- Hablamos del señor.
- El señor es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
