# Sentar

## Meaning

Sentar means "to seat or to set."

## Examples

- Sin duda, esto va a sentar un precedente para eventos futuros.
- ¿Puedo sentarme?
- ¡Siéntate!

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
