# Sentencia

## Meaning

Sentencia means "sentence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sentencia.
- La sentencia es importante.
- No entiendo la sentencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
