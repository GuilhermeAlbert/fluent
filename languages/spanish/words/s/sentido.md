# Sentido

## Meaning

Sentido means "sense or sense, way."

## Examples

- Lo dije en un buen sentido.
- El sentido de la palabra es...
- No tiene sentido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
