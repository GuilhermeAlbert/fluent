# Sentimiento

## Meaning

Sentimiento means "feeling."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sentimiento.
- El sentimiento es importante.
- No entiendo el sentimiento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
