# Seriamente

## Meaning

Seriamente means "seriously."

## Examples

- Lo hice seriamente.
- Llegó seriamente.
- Funciona seriamente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
