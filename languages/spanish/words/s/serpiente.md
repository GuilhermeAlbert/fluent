# Serpiente

## Meaning

Serpiente means "snake or serpent."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la serpiente.
- La serpiente es importante.
- No entiendo la serpiente.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
