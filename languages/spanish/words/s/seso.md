# Seso

## Meaning

Seso means "brain or brainwork."

## Examples

- Volarle los sesos.
- Hablamos de los seso.
- Los seso es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
