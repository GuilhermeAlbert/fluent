# Sexo

## Meaning

Sexo means "sex."

## Examples

- Near-synonyms: varones ("men"), mujeres ("women").
- Hablamos del sexo.
- El sexo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
