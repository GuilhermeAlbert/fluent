# Sierra

## Meaning

Sierra means "saw or mountain range."

## Examples

- Sierra Madre Occidental.
- Hablamos de la sierra.
- La sierra es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
