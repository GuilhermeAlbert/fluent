# Siete

## Meaning

Siete means "seven or butthole."

## Examples

- Hablamos del siete.
- El siete es importante.
- No entiendo el siete.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
