# Siglo

## Meaning

Siglo means "century or age."

## Examples

- Siglo XXI.
- Hablamos del siglo.
- El siglo es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
