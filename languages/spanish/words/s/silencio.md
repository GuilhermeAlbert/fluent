# Silencio

## Meaning

Silencio means "silence or rest."

## Examples

- Silencio de corchea.
- Hablamos del silencio.
- El silencio es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
