# Silla

## Meaning

Silla means "chair or saddle."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la silla.
- La silla es importante.
- No entiendo la silla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
