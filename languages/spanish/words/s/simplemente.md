# Simplemente

## Meaning

Simplemente means "simply."

## Examples

- Lo hice simplemente.
- Llegó simplemente.
- Funciona simplemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
