# Sinceramente

## Meaning

Sinceramente means "sincerely or honestly."

## Examples

- Lo hice sinceramente.
- Llegó sinceramente.
- Funciona sinceramente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
