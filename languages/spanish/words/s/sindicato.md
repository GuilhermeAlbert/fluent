# Sindicato

## Meaning

Sindicato means "trade union or labor union."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sindicato.
- El sindicato es importante.
- No entiendo el sindicato.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
