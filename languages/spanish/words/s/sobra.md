# Sobra

## Meaning

Sobra means "surplus or excess."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sobra.
- La sobra es importante.
- No entiendo la sobra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
