# Sobrina

## Meaning

Sobrina means "female equivalent of sobrino: niece."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sobrina.
- La sobrina es importante.
- No entiendo la sobrina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
