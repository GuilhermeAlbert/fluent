# Sobrino

## Meaning

Sobrino means "nephew."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sobrino.
- El sobrino es importante.
- No entiendo el sobrino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
