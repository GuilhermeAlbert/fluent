# Sociedad

## Meaning

Sociedad means "society or social order."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sociedad.
- La sociedad es importante.
- No entiendo la sociedad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
