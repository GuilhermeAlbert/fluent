# Socorro

## Meaning

Socorro means "relief or help."

## Examples

- Hablamos del socorro.
- El socorro es importante.
- No entiendo el socorro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
