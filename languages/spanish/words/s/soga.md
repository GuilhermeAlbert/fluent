# Soga

## Meaning

Soga means "rope or noose."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la soga.
- La soga es importante.
- No entiendo la soga.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
