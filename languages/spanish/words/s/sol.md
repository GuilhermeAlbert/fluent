# Sol

## Meaning

Sol means "sun or sunlight."

## Examples

- Quítate del sol.
- Hablamos del sol.
- El sol es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
