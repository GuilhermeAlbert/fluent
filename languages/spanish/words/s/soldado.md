# Soldado

## Meaning

Soldado means "soldier."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la soldado.
- La soldado es importante.
- No entiendo la soldado.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
