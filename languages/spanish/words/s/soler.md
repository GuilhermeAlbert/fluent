# Soler

## Meaning

Soler means "to be accustomed to doing something or to do something on a regular basis."

## Examples

- Suele llegar tarde.
- Aquí solía estar la tele.
- Quiero soler hoy.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
