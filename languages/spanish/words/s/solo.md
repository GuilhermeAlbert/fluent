# Sólo

## Meaning

Sólo means "superseded spelling of solo."

## Examples

- Lo hice sólo.
- Llegó sólo.
- Funciona sólo.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
