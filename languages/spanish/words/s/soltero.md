# Soltero

## Meaning

Soltero means "single or bachelor."

## Examples

- Hablamos del soltero.
- El soltero es importante.
- No entiendo el soltero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
