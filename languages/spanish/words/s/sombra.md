# Sombra

## Meaning

Sombra means "shadow is blocked by the shade of an object) or shade."

## Examples

- Sin sombra de duda.
- Hablamos de la sombra.
- La sombra es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
