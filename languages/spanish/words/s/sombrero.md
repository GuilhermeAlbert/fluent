# Sombrero

## Meaning

Sombrero means "hat or butterfly pea."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sombrero.
- El sombrero es importante.
- No entiendo el sombrero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
