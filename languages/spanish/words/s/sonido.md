# Sonido

## Meaning

Sonido means "sound or noise."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sonido.
- El sonido es importante.
- No entiendo el sonido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
