# Sopa

## Meaning

Sopa means "soup or noodles."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la sopa.
- La sopa es importante.
- No entiendo la sopa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
