# Submarino

## Meaning

Submarino means "submarine or waterboarding."

## Examples

- Hablamos del submarino.
- El submarino es importante.
- No entiendo el submarino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
