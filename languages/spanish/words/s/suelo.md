# Suelo

## Meaning

Suelo means "dirt or earth."

## Examples

- Hablamos del suelo.
- El suelo es importante.
- No entiendo el suelo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
