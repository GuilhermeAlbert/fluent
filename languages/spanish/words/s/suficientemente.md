# Suficientemente

## Meaning

Suficientemente means "sufficiently."

## Examples

- Lo hice suficientemente.
- Llegó suficientemente.
- Funciona suficientemente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
