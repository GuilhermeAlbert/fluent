# Sujeto

## Meaning

Sujeto means "subject or matter."

## Examples

- Hablamos del sujeto.
- El sujeto es importante.
- No entiendo el sujeto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
