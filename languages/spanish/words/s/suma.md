# Suma

## Meaning

Suma means "sum or addition."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la suma.
- La suma es importante.
- No entiendo la suma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
