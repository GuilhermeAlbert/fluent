# Superficie

## Meaning

Superficie means "surface or area."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la superficie.
- La superficie es importante.
- No entiendo la superficie.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
