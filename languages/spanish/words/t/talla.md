# Talla

## Meaning

Talla means "size or clothing size."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la talla.
- La talla es importante.
- No entiendo la talla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
