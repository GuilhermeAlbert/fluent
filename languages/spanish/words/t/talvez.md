# Talvez

## Meaning

Talvez means "alternative form of tal vez."

## Examples

- Lo hice talvez.
- Llegó talvez.
- Funciona talvez.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
