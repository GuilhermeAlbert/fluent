# Tampoco

## Meaning

Tampoco means "neither or nor."

## Examples

- Tampoco puedo explicarlo.
- Tampoco soy canadiense.
- Lo hice tampoco.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
