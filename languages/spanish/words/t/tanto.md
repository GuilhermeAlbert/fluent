# Tanto

## Meaning

Tanto means "copy or poker chip."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tanto.
- El tanto es importante.
- No entiendo el tanto.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
