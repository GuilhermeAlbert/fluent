# Tapa

## Meaning

Tapa means "lid or cap."

## Examples

- De tapa blanda.
- De tapa dura.
- Hablamos de la tapa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
