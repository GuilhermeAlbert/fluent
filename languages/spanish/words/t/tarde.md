# Tarde

## Meaning

Tarde means "afternoon or evening."

## Examples

- Por la tarde.
- Buenas tardes.
- Hablamos de la tarde.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
