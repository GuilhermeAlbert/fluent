# Tarea

## Meaning

Tarea means "task or homework."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tarea.
- La tarea es importante.
- No entiendo la tarea.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
