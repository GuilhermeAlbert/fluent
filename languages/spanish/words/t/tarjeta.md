# Tarjeta

## Meaning

Tarjeta means "card."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tarjeta.
- La tarjeta es importante.
- No entiendo la tarjeta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
