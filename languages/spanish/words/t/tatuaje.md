# Tatuaje

## Meaning

Tatuaje means "tattoo."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tatuaje.
- El tatuaje es importante.
- No entiendo el tatuaje.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
