# Taza

## Meaning

Taza means "cup or mug."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la taza.
- La taza es importante.
- No entiendo la taza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
