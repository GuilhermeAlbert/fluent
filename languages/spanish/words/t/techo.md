# Techo

## Meaning

Techo means "ceiling or roof."

## Examples

- Hablamos del techo.
- El techo es importante.
- No entiendo el techo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
