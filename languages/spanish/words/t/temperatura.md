# Temperatura

## Meaning

Temperatura means "temperature."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la temperatura.
- La temperatura es importante.
- No entiendo la temperatura.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
