# Temporada

## Meaning

Temporada means "season or period."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la temporada.
- La temporada es importante.
- No entiendo la temporada.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
