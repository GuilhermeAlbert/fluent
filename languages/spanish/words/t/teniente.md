# Teniente

## Meaning

Teniente means "lieutenant or deputy."

## Examples

- Hablamos del teniente.
- El teniente es importante.
- No entiendo el teniente.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
