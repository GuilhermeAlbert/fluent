# Tercero

## Meaning

Tercero means "third party or mediator."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tercero.
- El tercero es importante.
- No entiendo el tercero.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
