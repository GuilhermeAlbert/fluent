# Terreno

## Meaning

Terreno means "land or ground."

## Examples

- Hablamos del terreno.
- El terreno es importante.
- No entiendo el terreno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
