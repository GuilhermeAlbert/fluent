# Tesoro

## Meaning

Tesoro means "treasure or trove."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tesoro.
- El tesoro es importante.
- No entiendo el tesoro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
