# Testimonio

## Meaning

Testimonio means "testimony or witness."

## Examples

- Hablamos del testimonio.
- El testimonio es importante.
- No entiendo el testimonio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
