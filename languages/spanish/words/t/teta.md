# Teta

## Meaning

Teta means "boob or tit."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la teta.
- La teta es importante.
- No entiendo la teta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
