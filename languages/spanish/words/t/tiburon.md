# Tiburón

## Meaning

Tiburón means "shark or raider."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tiburón.
- El tiburón es importante.
- No entiendo el tiburón.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
