# Tienda

## Meaning

Tienda means "tent or shop."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tienda.
- La tienda es importante.
- No entiendo la tienda.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
