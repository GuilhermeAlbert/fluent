# Tierra

## Meaning

Tierra means "Earth or land."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tierra.
- La tierra es importante.
- No entiendo la tierra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
