# Tigre

## Meaning

Tigre means "tiger or jaguar."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tigre.
- El tigre es importante.
- No entiendo el tigre.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
