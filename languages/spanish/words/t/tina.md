# Tina

## Meaning

Tina means "vat or large earthenware jar."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tina.
- La tina es importante.
- No entiendo la tina.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
