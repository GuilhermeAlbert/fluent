# Tinta

## Meaning

Tinta means "ink."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tinta.
- La tinta es importante.
- No entiendo la tinta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
