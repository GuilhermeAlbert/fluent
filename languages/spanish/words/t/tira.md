# Tira

## Meaning

Tira means "tie or ribbon."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tira.
- La tira es importante.
- No entiendo la tira.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
