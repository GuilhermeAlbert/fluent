# Toalla

## Meaning

Toalla means "towel."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la toalla.
- La toalla es importante.
- No entiendo la toalla.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
