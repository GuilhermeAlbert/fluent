# Toma

## Meaning

Toma means "conquest or capture."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la toma.
- La toma es importante.
- No entiendo la toma.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
