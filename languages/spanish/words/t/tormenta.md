# Tormenta

## Meaning

Tormenta means "storm or thunderstorm."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tormenta.
- La tormenta es importante.
- No entiendo la tormenta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
