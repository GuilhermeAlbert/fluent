# Torneo

## Meaning

Torneo means "tournament or tourney."

## Examples

- Hablamos del torneo.
- El torneo es importante.
- No entiendo el torneo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
