# Torre

## Meaning

Torre means "tower or rook."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la torre.
- La torre es importante.
- No entiendo la torre.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
