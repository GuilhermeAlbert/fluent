# Totalmente

## Meaning

Totalmente means "totally."

## Examples

- Lo hice totalmente.
- Llegó totalmente.
- Funciona totalmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
