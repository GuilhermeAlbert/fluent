# Tragedia

## Meaning

Tragedia means "tragedy or catastrophe."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tragedia.
- La tragedia es importante.
- No entiendo la tragedia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
