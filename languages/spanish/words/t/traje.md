# Traje

## Meaning

Traje means "suit or dress."

## Examples

- Quiero este traje.
- El traje es de color azul.
- Hablamos del traje.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
