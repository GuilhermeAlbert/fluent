# Trampa

## Meaning

Trampa means "trap or snare."

## Examples

- Caer en una trampa.
- Hacer trampas.
- ¡Eso es trampa!

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
