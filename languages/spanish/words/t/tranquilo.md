# Tranquilo

## Meaning

Tranquilo means "calm or peaceful."

## Examples

- El campo es tranquilo.
- Es muy tranquilo.
- Parece tranquilo.

## Notes

Usually used as an adjective. In Spanish, many adjectives change for gender and number.
