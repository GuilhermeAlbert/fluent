# Transferencia

## Meaning

Transferencia means "transfer."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la transferencia.
- La transferencia es importante.
- No entiendo la transferencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
