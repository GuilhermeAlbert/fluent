# Tren

## Meaning

Tren means "train or extravagance."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tren.
- El tren es importante.
- No entiendo el tren.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
