# Tribu

## Meaning

Tribu means "tribe."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tribu.
- La tribu es importante.
- No entiendo la tribu.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
