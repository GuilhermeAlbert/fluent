# Tristeza

## Meaning

Tristeza means "sadness or gloom."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tristeza.
- La tristeza es importante.
- No entiendo la tristeza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
