# Tropa

## Meaning

Tropa means "troop or crowd."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tropa.
- La tropa es importante.
- No entiendo la tropa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
