# Truco

## Meaning

Truco means "trick."

## Examples

- Bolsa de trucos.
- ¿Cuál es el truco?
- Hablamos del truco.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
