# Tubo

## Meaning

Tubo means "tube or receiver."

## Examples

- Hablamos del tubo.
- El tubo es importante.
- No entiendo el tubo.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
