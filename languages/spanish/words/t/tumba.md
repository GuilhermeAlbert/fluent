# Tumba

## Meaning

Tumba means "grave, tomb."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la tumba.
- La tumba es importante.
- No entiendo la tumba.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
