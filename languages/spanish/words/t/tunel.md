# Túnel

## Meaning

Túnel means "tunnel or nutmeg."

## Examples

- Hablamos del túnel.
- El túnel es importante.
- No entiendo el túnel.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
