# Uña

## Meaning

Uña means "fingernail or toenail."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la uña.
- La uña es importante.
- No entiendo la uña.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
