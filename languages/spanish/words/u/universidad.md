# Universidad

## Meaning

Universidad means "university, college."

## Examples

- Ir a la universidad.
- Hablamos de la universidad.
- La universidad es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
