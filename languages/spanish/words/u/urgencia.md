# Urgencia

## Meaning

Urgencia means "urgency or emergency."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la urgencia.
- La urgencia es importante.
- No entiendo la urgencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
