# Usualmente

## Meaning

Usualmente means "usually."

## Examples

- Lo hice usualmente.
- Llegó usualmente.
- Funciona usualmente.

## Notes

Usually used as an adverb to change the meaning of a verb, adjective, or whole sentence.
