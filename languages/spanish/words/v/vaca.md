# Vaca

## Meaning

Vaca means "cow or beef."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la vaca.
- La vaca es importante.
- No entiendo la vaca.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
