# Vacío

## Meaning

Vacío means "vacuum or void."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vacío.
- El vacío es importante.
- No entiendo el vacío.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
