# Vago

## Meaning

Vago means "wanderer or vagabond."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vago.
- El vago es importante.
- No entiendo el vago.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
