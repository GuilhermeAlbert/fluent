# Vampiro

## Meaning

Vampiro means "vampire or vampire bat."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vampiro.
- El vampiro es importante.
- No entiendo el vampiro.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
