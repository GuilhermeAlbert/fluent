# Vaso

## Meaning

Vaso means "drinking glass or glassful."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vaso.
- El vaso es importante.
- No entiendo el vaso.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
