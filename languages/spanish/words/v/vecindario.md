# Vecindario

## Meaning

Vecindario means "neighbourhood."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vecindario.
- El vecindario es importante.
- No entiendo el vecindario.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
