# Vecino

## Meaning

Vecino means "neighbour or a resident."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vecino.
- El vecino es importante.
- No entiendo el vecino.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
