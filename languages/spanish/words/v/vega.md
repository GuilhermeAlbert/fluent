# Vega

## Meaning

Vega means "meadow or fertile lowland."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la vega.
- La vega es importante.
- No entiendo la vega.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
