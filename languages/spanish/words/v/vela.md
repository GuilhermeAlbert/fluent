# Vela

## Meaning

Vela means "candle or wakefulness."

## Examples

- Una noche en vela.
- Hablamos de la vela.
- La vela es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
