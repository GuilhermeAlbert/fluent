# Velocidad

## Meaning

Velocidad means "speed or velocity."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la velocidad.
- La velocidad es importante.
- No entiendo la velocidad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
