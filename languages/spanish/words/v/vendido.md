# Vendido

## Meaning

Vendido means "sellout."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vendido.
- El vendido es importante.
- No entiendo el vendido.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
