# Veneno

## Meaning

Veneno means "poison or venom."

## Examples

- Hablamos del veneno.
- El veneno es importante.
- No entiendo el veneno.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
