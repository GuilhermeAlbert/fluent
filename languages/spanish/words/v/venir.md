# Venir

## Meaning

Venir means "to come or to arrive."

## Examples

- Ven aquí / ven acá.
- El tren viene a las ocho.
- Lo que venga, lo acepto.

## Notes

Usually used as a verb. Learn it with common phrases and conjugated forms.
