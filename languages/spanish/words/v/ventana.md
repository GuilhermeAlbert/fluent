# Ventana

## Meaning

Ventana means "window or window frame."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la ventana.
- La ventana es importante.
- No entiendo la ventana.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
