# Veras

## Meaning

Veras means "truth or reality."

## Examples

- Hablamos de las veras.
- Las veras es importante.
- No entiendo las veras.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
