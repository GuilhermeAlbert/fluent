# Verdad

## Meaning

Verdad means "truth or fact."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la verdad.
- La verdad es importante.
- No entiendo la verdad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
