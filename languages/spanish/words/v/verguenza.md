# Vergüenza

## Meaning

Vergüenza means "shame or embarrassment."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la vergüenza.
- La vergüenza es importante.
- No entiendo la vergüenza.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
