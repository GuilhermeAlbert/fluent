# Viaje

## Meaning

Viaje means "voyage or journey."

## Examples

- Hay un viaje de gente.
- Hablamos del viaje.
- El viaje es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
