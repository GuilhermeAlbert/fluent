# Victoria

## Meaning

Victoria means "victory or triumph."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la victoria.
- La victoria es importante.
- No entiendo la victoria.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
