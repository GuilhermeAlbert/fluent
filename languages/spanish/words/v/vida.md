# Vida

## Meaning

Vida means "life or lifestyle."

## Examples

- ¡Me salvó la vida!
- Hablamos de la vida.
- La vida es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
