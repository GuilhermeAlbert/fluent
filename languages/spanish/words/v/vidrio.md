# Vidrio

## Meaning

Vidrio means "glass or window."

## Examples

- Hablamos del vidrio.
- El vidrio es importante.
- No entiendo el vidrio.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
