# Viento

## Meaning

Viento means "wind or woodwind section."

## Examples

- Hablamos del viento.
- El viento es importante.
- No entiendo el viento.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
