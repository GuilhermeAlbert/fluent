# Vigilancia

## Meaning

Vigilancia means "vigilance or watchfulness."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la vigilancia.
- La vigilancia es importante.
- No entiendo la vigilancia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
