# Villa

## Meaning

Villa means "small town or villa."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la villa.
- La villa es importante.
- No entiendo la villa.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
