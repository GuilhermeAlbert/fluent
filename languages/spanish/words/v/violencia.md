# Violencia

## Meaning

Violencia means "violence or act of violence."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la violencia.
- La violencia es importante.
- No entiendo la violencia.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
