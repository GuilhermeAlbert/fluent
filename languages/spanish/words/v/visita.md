# Visita

## Meaning

Visita means "visit or visitor."

## Examples

- Tenemos visita.
- Hablamos de la visita.
- La visita es importante.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
