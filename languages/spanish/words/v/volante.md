# Volante

## Meaning

Volante means "flyer or circular."

## Examples

- Hablamos del volante.
- El volante es importante.
- No entiendo el volante.

## Notes

Usually used as a masculine noun. Pair it with "el" or "un" when the sentence needs an article.
