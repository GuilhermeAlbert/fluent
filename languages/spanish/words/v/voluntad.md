# Voluntad

## Meaning

Voluntad means "will or wish."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la voluntad.
- La voluntad es importante.
- No entiendo la voluntad.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
