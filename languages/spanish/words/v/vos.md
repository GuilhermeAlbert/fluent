# Vos

## Meaning

Vos means "you or familiar form of the second-person singular pronoun."

## Examples

- ¿Cómo te va? Bien, ¿y vos?
- Vos, doña Juana, sois caritativa.
- Uso "vos" todos los días.

## Notes

Commonly used as a pronoun. Small words like this change meaning depending on context.
