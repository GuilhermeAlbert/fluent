# Vuelta

## Meaning

Vuelta means "turn or return."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la vuelta.
- La vuelta es importante.
- No entiendo la vuelta.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
