# Zorra

## Meaning

Zorra means "slut or prostitute."

## Examples

- Hablamos de la zorra.
- La zorra es importante.
- No entiendo la zorra.

## Notes

Usually used as a feminine noun. Pair it with "la" or "una" when the sentence needs an article.
